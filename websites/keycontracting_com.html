About US

Teamwork. The number one reason Key Contracting is the best in its class. Key’s top 10 employees and owners have been together for more than 15 years. That level of dedication to one another only develops with the passage of time and common experience.

Key Contracting has provided exceptional specialty contracting services since 2002. If you are having a trouble finding a contractor to take on a difficult construction or facility problem, check us out because our talents are as diverse as your problems.

Key’s personnel are innovative, safety conscious and take great pride in quickly pushing your difficult project to completion. The faster our company safely completes your project, the faster you are on the road to success.

Key is seriously committed to safety. We have hired an outside safety officer that routinely visits are construction sites to perform safety audits and mini-training sessions. An outside safety officer does not feel the pressure to complete the job, his only concern is worker safety.
