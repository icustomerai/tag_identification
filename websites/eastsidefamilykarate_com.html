WHO WE ARE

Eastside Family Karate offers a fun family environment to learn traditional karate, self-defense, and life skills while achieving self-confidence, focus, and physical fitness. Our goal is to provide the necessary instruction and discipline to help you experience all the benefits of karate. We offer karate classes for children, teens, and adults; serving the Bellevue, Redmond, and Kirkland area, located near the Microsoft campus in Redmond, Washington.

Great for all ages!  The discipline bonds families and friends in a unique way of self-discovery and control.  Increased strength, movement, concentration, focus, and determination are just a few of the benefits that are enjoyed while learning karate.  Our instructors are dedicated to teaching and developing all ages to be stronger people.
