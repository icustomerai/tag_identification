Grow & Share in Faith

Are you interested in growing and sharing in faith with others who are named and claimed through the love of Jesus? Sundays are great opportunities to join in worship and fellowship with one another and discover ways God is revealed among us. Through the grace of Jesus Christ, we are connected within by love, reach out in hope to share the Gospel of Jesus Christ with one another, the community, and the world.

This is a personal invitation to come to the house of the Lord to be among God’s people, pray, hear the word proclaimed, and receive the forgiving presence of Jesus from the Lord’s table of grace. There is joy in the house of the Lord, even more when we are together. Salem Lutheran Church looks forward to seeing you in worship at 9:15am. Please stay for a time of fellowship and refreshments in the social hall following worship where we support and encourage one another around tables and through conversation. All are welcome!

Psalm 67:3 - Let the peoples praise you, O God;   let all the peoples praise you.

In Isaiah the people are advised to look to their spiritual ancestors as the rock from which they were hewn. Jesus declares that the church will be built on the rock of Peter’s bold confession of faith. God’s word of reconciliation and God’s mercy are keys to the church’s mission. Paul urges us to not be conformed to this world but to offer our bodies as a living sacrifice, using our individual gifts to build up the body of Christ. From the table we go forth to offer our spiritual worship through word and deed.

JOURNEY TOGETHER

Rally Sunday is September 10th!

Rally Sunday is September 10th! Plans are underway for Rally Day – when Salem Lutheran Church transitions to the fall schedule of two worship services. On September 10, at 9:30am, join the cross-generational team to assemble the items collected into kits to assist Phoenix Community Development Services (CDS) in their work.

Click here to learn more about Phoenix Community Development Services (CDS) and items being collected.​

FAITH FORMATION - EDUCATION HOUR

SUNDAY SCHOOL - Sunday, Sept 10

Sunday School classes (ages 3 through high school) will kick-off the new school year by participating in the cross-generational service project and then parents and students will have time to go to the classrooms and meet teachers. There will be a shortened class time on Sunday, September 10. Sunday School students and parents plan to meet in the Atrium at 9:30am for a blessing and prayer for the people receiving the items we have collected.

SUNDAY ADULT EDUCATION - Sunday, Sept 17

Adult classes such as Man to Man and Women in the Word will officially begin on September 17 during Education Hour at 9:30am. Adults are encouraged to participate in the service project on September 10, working in partnership with our young people to do God’s work. 

Please pause for prayer each day at 3pm seeking God’s direction for Salem Lutheran Church’s future together. Salem Lutheran Church has some intentional work ahead of us, and the hope is to receive your support for council and all Salem members to work through budget challenges while also engaging God’s Mission for a hopeful future through Salem. God’s blessings!
