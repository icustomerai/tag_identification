3-Wheel EFLV20 - ARRIVING SOON

The ePicker EFLV20 is one of the products in our lineup of 3-wheeled Forklift trucks. With a load capacity of 4,000 lbs., the 3-wheel design accommodates a smaller turning radius and enables the forklift to turn in even more restricted spaces than an equivalent sized 4 wheel forklift.
These units are highly maneuverable, operating comfortably in a wide range of applications and conditions. Dual AC drive motor delivers smooth torque and speed curves matched to vehicle needs in real-time for peak efficiency and performance.
