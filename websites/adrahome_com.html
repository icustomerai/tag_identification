Adra is where the heart is

Here at Adra - which in Welsh means 'home' - we believe that home is where the heart is. Founded in 2007 by Angharad Gwyn, all our products are either made in Wales, designed by Welsh designers or feature the Welsh language. They're made by independent designers and makers from across Wales to a high standard, and many of our products are created here at Adra so that you can enjoy ranges exclusive to us.

The Adra Story

Parc Glynllifon

Adra is located at the beautiful grounds of Parc Glynllifon near Caernarfon. With miles of rustic woodland walks, charming historic features and contemporary sculptures - Parc Glynllifon is perfect for relaxing picnics, leisurely walks and spending quality time with the family.
