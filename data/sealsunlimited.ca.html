Welcome to Seals Unlimited

Seals Unlimited has over 40 years of sealing experience and expertise. Our extensive knowledge of applications and engineering design has allowed us to become the foremost supplier of seals and gaskets in a wide range of solutions.

Our broad inventory of nearly 30,000 individual products that represent only the top names in seal manufacturing, together with our unique customer support services, positions Seals Unlimited as true “Sealing Experts.”

With our commitment to quality assurance, our entire operation adheres to strict ISO requirements and has been CANPAC audited to CSAZ299.2 standards. This dedication places Seals Unlimited as a unique leader in the MRO market.


Combining on site manufacturing with the industry leading partnerships we have built and maintained over the last 40 years ensures that we can literally provide unlimited options for any sealing application that exists. No matter how unique your application and the challenges that surround it, we can get you what you need to solve the problem. The quality that we offer you is backed by these well-established partners and ensures that your requirements are met right…the FIRST time.

The collective 125+ years of seal experience in our Sales Department positions us uniquely to service your company’s requirements with extensive design expertise, industry contacts, and information on product trends and ever-evolving new technology. Our entire organization from sales to shipping to billing has the unparalleled commitment to you as our customer. Customer Service is our attitude and it is how we approach every step along the way in processing your order.
