Welcome To Celgard

Celgard is a global leader in the development and production of high-performance membrane technology. Our products are used in a broad range of energy storage and other barrier-type applications, including lithium-ion batteries, lithium primary and select specialty battery solutions. They are also used in technical textiles such as waterproof breathable apparel and medical personal protective equipment (PPE) and other specialty applications.

Careers at Celgard

A career at Celgard offers opportunities for challenge and advancement.
