    Tredegar Corporation
    1100 Boulders Parkway
    North Chesterfield, Virginia 23225
    Phone: 804-330-1000
    Toll Free: 855-330-1001

    Tredegar Corporation is an industrial manufacturer with three primary businesses: custom aluminum extrusions for the North American building and construction, automotive and specialty end-use markets; surface protection films for high-technology applications in the global electronics industry; and specialized polyester films primarily for the Latin American flexible packaging market. Tredegar had 2022 sales from continuing operations of $939 million. With approximately 2,300 employees, the Company operates manufacturing facilities in North America, South America and Asia.
