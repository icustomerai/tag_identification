Cold Chain Solutions

Pharmaceuticals and other temperature sensitive shipments require an industry leader in safe, secure and compliant transportation. At Landstar, we understand the requirements of the healthcare and biotech industries and consistently meet the challenges associated with transporting high value, high security, temperature critical shipments.

All shipment temperatures are monitored and trucks are tracked throughout the shipment lifecycle to maintain product stability and integrity.

To achieve a high level of quality assurance, Landstar offers:

  • Temperature Controlled and Thermal Mapped Equipment
  • Service Available 24/7/365
  • 24-Hour Temperature Monitoring and Equipment Tracking
  • Hazardous Materials and TSA Certified
  • Layered Security Measures
  • Quality Assurance Processes to Comply with Pharmaceutical Guidelines
  • Skilled Team Owner-Operators with a vested interest in getting your shipment delivered safely and on-time
  • Single Point of Contact
Cargo hold will go thru annual thermal mapping and NIST testing at three different industry standard set points:
  • -15 to -25 C (set point -20 C)
  • +15 to +25 C (set point +20 C)
  • +2 to + 8 C (set point + 5 C)
