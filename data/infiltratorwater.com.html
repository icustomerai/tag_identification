Our Story

Infiltrator Water Technologies is a market leader within the onsite wastewater treatment industry, Infiltrator manufactures a variety of revolutionary products and innovative, environmentally friendly alternatives to traditional stone and pipe leachfield and concrete septic wastewater system components. We sell our products through wholesale distribution across the United States and Canada for use on properties with onsite wastewater treatment systems.

Environmentally Friendly Wastewater Treatment Solutions

  • Chambers

    Chambers

    Infiltrator chambers offer design flexibility and a smaller footprint compared to traditional stone and pipe systems.

  • EZflow Septic

    EZflow Septic

    An eco-friendly engineered geosynthetic aggregate and pipe system to replace traditional stone and pipe drainfields.

  • Infiltrator Tanks

    Infiltrator Tanks

    Available for both septic and potable water storage, IM-Series tanks are injection molded for strength and durability.

  • Tank Risers

    Tank Risers

    The EZsnap and EZset riser series are compatible with our IM-Series tanks as well as other plastic and precast tanks.

  • EZflow Drainage System

    EZflow Drainage System

    An eco-friendly, gravel-free drainage system consisting of engineered geosynthetic aggregate in bundles with pipe.

  • ATL: Advanced Treatment Leachfield

    ATL: Advanced Treatment Leachfield

    A sand-lined treatment and dispersal leachfield system offering passive advanced wastewater treatment.

  • Delta Treatment Systems

    Delta Treatment Systems

    Advanced treatment solutions for residential, commercial, and community wastewater management from Delta.

  • Pumping

    Pumping

    Infiltrator offers a complete line of pump tanks, pump vaults, and pumping control systems.

  • Presby Environmental

    Presby Environmental

    Scalable, cost-effective solutions for treating effluent from commercial, residential, and community sources.
