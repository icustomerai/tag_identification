Fort Drum Mountain Community Homes

We have the location you want with the amenities you deserve.

We'll Take Care of You

Youll love calling Fort Drum Mountain Community Homes your home. In our gated community, we aim to provide the ultimate living experience and are proud to serve active-duty military and retirees, reservists, DOD employees, and civilian residents. Our community has been designed with our residents in mind offering spacious two- to four-bedroom single-family homes in Fort Drum. 

Residents enjoy the comforts of reduced commute times, easy access to Watertown, Highway 11, Interstate 81, and other local attractions. All are welcome to enjoy our free resident events, community gardens, fitness center, and basketball and tennis courts. When you live with Fort Drum Mountain Community Homes, youll also have access to superb community amenities for pets like dog parks with dog stations for your furry friends to enjoy!

  • As a pet-friendly community, we allow our residents to have up to three domesticated pets (cats or dogs). The third pet must be a cat or dog under 35 lbs. at breed maturity (upstairs apartments excluded from having three dogs).

    All dogs must be registered with the Fort Drum Veterinary Clinic, up-to-date with vaccinations, micro chipped, and licensed with the Town of Leray. Dog breeds (including mixed breeds) deemed aggressive or potentially aggressive are not permitted unless the dog is a certified military working dog that is being boarded by its handler/trainer. The following breeds are deemed aggressive or potentially aggressive:

    Pit bull (also known as: American Staffordshire Bull Terrier or English Staffordshire Bull Terrier), Rottweiler, Doberman Pinscher, Chow, Presa Canario, Cane Corso, Neapolitan Mastiff, Wolf and Wolf hybrids
