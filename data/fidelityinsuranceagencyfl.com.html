Auto Dealer bonds serve to guarantee that a dealer operates according to federal laws, terms and regulations of the state, local laws, and tax and judgment guidelines that relate to motor vehicles.

The Bond also ensures that if the dealer or any of his or her employees commit fraud or other actions that are wrongful to the consumer, the consumer is protected from any consequences of those actions.

Fidelity Insurance Agency can help you obtain a Dealer Bond (also known as a DMV Bond, Used Car Dealer Bond or MVD Bond) at a competitive rate. We have the experience and access to surety companies to facilitate the process. We know what is required to obtain this type of Bond. Call us to help you with your Bonds.
