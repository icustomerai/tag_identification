We are Helmitin

Since day one, we have been devoted to researching and refining our craft, creating innovative products, and developing our team to provide the best customer service that anyone has ever experienced. It is because of our commitment and passion that we have become an industry leader in all manner of specialty adhesives, for more than 100 years.

At Helmitin we lead the way through uncharted territory with our creative vision, determination, and our extensive network of research and manufacturing facilities. It is through our resources that we have been able to stay on top of industry trends and requirements, providing some of the best products on the market.

In the end, we see ourselves as more than just a supplier and manufacturer. We are a partner in the success of every one of our valued customers, and we see it as our job to ensure your needs are met, from start to finish. Are you ready? Let’s get to work!
