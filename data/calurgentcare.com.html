Welcome to California Urgent Care Center. Don’t put your health on hold when our friendly practitioners at California Urgent Care Center are here to help you feel well again. We value your, your time, and your health, and that’s why we offer a full spectrum of urgent care services and treat a variety of conditions for non-critical health problems. 

You don’t need to go to the ER if you have a non-life-threatening health problem, and we encourage you to come to our urgent care facilities for your injuries or ailments that can be capably treated by our team in our well-equipped clinics. We can meet your needs and help you get back to your everyday life ASAP.

    Services We Offer

    At California Urgent Care Center we can often provide the same level of quality care as an ER, but for non-life-threatening conditions. There are many reasons why receiving treatment from an urgent care center is preferable to the emergency room. Hospital emergency rooms often have longer waiting periods and the cost is more expensive. Since urgent care centers are designed to handle minor injuries and illnesses, patients are less likely to be exposed to more dangerous infectious diseases that are common in an ER. At California Urgent Care Center, we provide services for a variety of medical problems including:

      Frequently Ask Questions We're Here to Help
      • Do I Need Insurance for Urgent Care?
        You do not need insurance to receive care at California Urgent Care Center. We accept patients from all walks of life, regardless of insurance coverage. We accept cash and credit card payments.
      • What Insurance Policies Do You Take?
        We accept certain insurance policies. You can find more information on the insurance companies we accept on our Payments and Information Page. Feel free to contact us if you have more specific questions about insurance.
      • Do You Offer Telehealth Appointments?

        Patient safety is our primary concern. We realize that during the pandemic, many people may not be able to make an appointment in person. At California Urgent Care Center, we offer telemedicine appointments. These are appointments that take place through a secure online platform or by telephone.

        By offering this service, we can provide you with a medical consultation from the comfort of your residence. We have more information on our telehealth appointments here.

      • Does Urgent Care Do Lab Testing?

        We offer limited lab testing at our facilities. To receive more information on lab testing at California Urgent Care Center, please contact us.

      • How Do I Pay My Bill?
        Visit our Payments and Insurance Page to make a bill payment online. We have a payment portal. You can also make a payment in person at the location where you received services.
