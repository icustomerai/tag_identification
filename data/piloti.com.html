ABOUT PILOTI

At Piloti, our motto is performance driven design.


With over 20 years of history gearing championship racers for the world's toughest tracks, it's our mission to combine the performance of professional racing gear with the impeccable quality, design and comfort of your favorite footwear styles.


Handmade in Europe from supple leathers and suedes, our collection of driving shoes offer the versatility you need for your daily adventures, both behind the wheel and on foot.
