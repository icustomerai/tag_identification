Ershigs

Your turnkey, single-source solution for design, manufacturing, construction and installation of FRP corrosion-resistant products.

Building Inspiring Products

The Ershigs Brand is recognized for delivering the highest quality of corrosion resistant products and service worldwide. Maintaining a long history of unmatched products and services, Ershigs tanks, ducting, and scrubbers serve many industries:

  • Chemical
  • Brine
  • Food & Beverage
  • Mining
  • Lithium
  • Carbon Capture
  • Biogas
  • Water
  • Pulp & Paper
  • Pharmaceuticals
  • Semi Conductors
  • Other Industrial

As technology changes, Ershigs FRP products continue to innovate and lead the pack in industrial product design, manufacturing and installation. The scale of products & projects can be massive and manufacturing can be arranged on site or large scale modules & equipment can be barged or shipped to your facility, as needed.
