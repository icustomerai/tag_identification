Experienced and knowledgeable services

Musselman Lumber started in 1911 as Musselman Bros. offering coal, feed and lumber. We have been serving New Holland, Ephrata and the surrounding communities for years with top quality materials and service. We offer a wide variety of products including: lumber, engineered wood products, trusses, windows, doors, siding, roofing, pre-built stairs, pre-hung doors, trim packages and a full line of hardware.
