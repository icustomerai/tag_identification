Software Features to Make Your Gym Run Smoothly

Simplify business processes into one system you’ll love to use. eFit gives you more time to focus on the relationships that grow your business.

Gym Billing Software

eFit delivers more on-time payments, happier members, and higher profits. Our team manages all your membership payments and resolves billing-related issues. Automatically process credit card and EFT payments through our secure Level I PCI compliant system.

POS System

Sell anything from retail items to gift certificates to paid-in-full gym memberships with our point of sale software. Quickly handle transactions to create a pleasant checkout experience. Use automatic inventory tracking to keep up with your best-selling items.

Membership Contracts

Simplify sign-up with custom contract templates that populate automatically within your gym management software. Configure options for memberships, session packages, family accounts, and more. Go paperless with e-signatures for terms and waivers.

Online Booking

Sell more sessions and memberships through eFit’s online scheduling software and payment portal. Members can do everything from one convenient location: book their favorite classes, sign up for additional packages, view transactions, and update payment methods.

Business Reports

Get the numbers you need to make the right decisions for your business. Use eFit’s gym management software to find out which classes are profitable, which marketing efforts drive growth, and how best to allocate your budget. Measure success in real-time reports.

Member Check-in

Keep your front desk moving during peak hours with rapid self check-in. Automatically alert members when it’s time to renew or purchase additional sessions. Staff up for popular classes and times based on attendance tracking and reports.

Gym Marketing

Use automatic reminders and email blasts to make sure members never miss an appointment, membership renewal, or special offer. Show you care with a note after each training session and special birthday messages.

Customer Service

Your business is about the people you serve. So is ours. That’s why our skilled team is trained to resolve any problem for you or your members—from software support to gym membership payments—in a single call.

24-Hour Gym Access

Gain a competitive edge with premium around-the-clock access that doesn’t require extra staffing. Remotely monitor alerts and real-time security through seamless integration with Brivo’s industry-leading access control solutions.
