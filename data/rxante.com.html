How it Works

Program Design & Simulation

We want to hear your performance goals, current strategies, and budget constraints. Then we’ll use your plan’s data to simulate multiple scenarios to help you design your program with confidence.

Program Activation, Driven by Patented Analytics

RxAnte configures our patented analytics and software to your unique population, then selects the intervention most likely to benefit each at-risk individual.

Tech-Enabled Interventions

Engage at-risk members with your existing care managers, activate your provider network, or use our curated network of patient engagement vendors. For your most complex and vulnerable members, we recommend our value-based pharmacy service, Mosaic.

Performance Measurement & Continuous Improvement

Ongoing performance measurement, available in your RxAnte portal and discussed in regular business reviews, is how we engineer success.
