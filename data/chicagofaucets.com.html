Why
Chicago Faucets?

Chicago Faucets has been a trusted manufacturer of industry-leading fittings for over 120 years. We create the most durable faucets and fittings in the industry that solve some of the biggest water delivery system challenges in commercial buildings.

Meet with an Expert

Quality

For over 100 years, our commitment to quality has been the benchmark in the industry, with foundry, assembly, and distribution right here in the USA.

Durability

Get the dependability you need with faucets and fittings that work like new, for years to come. Chicago Faucets are built for high-use, high-abuse environments.

Design

Chicago Faucets are available in a range of classic and contemporary styles. Check out our manual and touchless faucets and upgrade the look and function of your restroom.
