Welcome to SKB Environmental!

With over 35 years of recycling experience, SKB Recycling is Minnesota's longtime leader in innovative processing, recycling, and total waste management solutions. We provide hauling, waste processing, recycling, and environmentally sound waste disposal services.

Due to our stringent and continual focus on environmental protection, we have never received a solid waste violation. With this company philosophy, SKB Environmental is able to provide unmatched environmental safeguards to protect our customers' interests. Our facilities are state-of-the-art, designed to maximize environmental protection.
