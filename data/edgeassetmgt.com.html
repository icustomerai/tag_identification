Edge Asset Management is a leading manager of equity strategies, managing approximately $16.2 billion as of March 31, 2023. Edge is located in Seattle, Washington.

Edge is at the forefront of innovation and has a foundation dating back to 1939, managing one of the first mutual funds in the industry. A specialized investment boutique of Principal Global Investors, Edge enjoys the focus of a boutique firm while leveraging the resources of a global leader.
