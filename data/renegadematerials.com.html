BISMALEIMIDES
(BMI’S)

Renegade Materials Corporation offers a full line of aerospace approved BMI products to support applications up to 450°F. Autoclave Cure RM-3002 and Out of Autoclave (OOA) Cure RM-3004 prepreg systems have excellent handling characteristics and offer processing flexibility. Infusion resins RM-3000 and RM-3010 are uniquely formulated for ease of use in Resin Transfer Molding (RTM) and Vacuum Assisted Resin Transfer Molding (VARTM) processes respectively. Renegade Materials’ BMI adhesive product line includes RM-3011 film adhesive and two paste products RM-3006 and RM-3007 developed for use with our prepregs and resins. Many of these products are qualified at key aerospace OEM’s and have extensive databases. Contact us to learn more about our exciting BMI product line.

RM-1100
POLYIMIDE PREPREG

Renegade Materials highest temperature polyimide prepreg system, RM-1100, exhibits unparalleled hot-wet properties and thermal oxidative stability (TOS) in harsh environments. RM-1100 is powered by a proprietary Maverick Corp resin and can support continuous service up to 650°F (343°C) with excursions to 700°F (370°C). In addition to offering a non-carcinogenic replacement for legacy PMR-15 applications, RM-1100 is approved for use on advanced commercial and military engine and aircraft programs. RM-1100 is a NON-ITAR system available for global export. It can be cured using autoclave or compression-molding processes, and is available in fabric and unidirectional tape prepreg forms, on carbon, quartz, ceramic and glass fibers. Contact us for more information about RM-1100 or any of our polyimide products.

LOW DIELECTRIC
PREPREGS

Renegade Materials Corporation has supplied Cyanate Ester Prepregs (RM-5001 and RM-5003) as well as our BMI (RM-3002) to the radome industry since its inception. In 2015, Renegade launched RM-2014-LDk-TK, a low dielectric modified epoxy prepreg system. The objective was to offer a product with dielectric performance similar to cyanate esters without the cost. RM-2014-LDk-TK processes like an epoxy with a straight forward 300°F (150°C) cure cycle and exhibits excellent handle-ability. RM-2014-LDk-TK is available on a variety of fabric reinforcement types including glass and quartz up to 60” wide. Contact us for more information about this game changing product.
