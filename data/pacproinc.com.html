Who We Help

Pacproinc® solutions are sanitary, washdown-duty, stainless steel systems. The streamlined designs incorporate leading-edge technology that ensures low maintenance and a long machine life. With compact or large designs available, our products can fit into any floor plan and are engineered to exceed the American Meat Industry’s 10 Principles of Sanitary Design.
