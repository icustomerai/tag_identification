FEATURED PRODUCTS

The inline Alignment Station (iAS) and Litho Booster

iAS is a high speed, pre-measurement module integrated between the coater/developer and NSR-S635E immersion scanners. iAS provides feed forward alignment results for all shots on every wafer. iAS capabilities can be extended to other scanners with the Litho Booster Standalone Alignment Station.
