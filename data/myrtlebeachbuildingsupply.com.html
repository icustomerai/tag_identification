Supplying high quality building materials since 2002.

Myrtle Beach Building Supply, located in Murrells Inlet and Little River, S.C., has been supplying high quality building materials and tools since 2002. U.S. LBM ACQUIRES MYRTLE BEACH BUILDING SUPPLY IN SOUTH CAROLINA

U.S. LBM Holdings, LLC (“U.S. LBM”), a leading distributor of specialty building materials in the United States, announced today that it has acquired Myrtle Beach Building Supply. With this acquisition, U.S. LBM now operates eight locations across North and South Carolina.
