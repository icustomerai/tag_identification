Meet the Author: Tyler Thrasher

TYLER THRASHER is an artist, chemist, botanist, and explorer who aims to help catalyze curiosity and share his enthusiasm about combining art and science and the joys of experimentation. His work has been featured in Juxtapoz magazine, The Smithsonian Folklife Festival, and Vox’s “The Future Of” series on Netflix. He is the author of Grow a Damn Plant Journal and can be found sharing his mad scientist experiments with the world on Instagram @tylerthrasherart!
