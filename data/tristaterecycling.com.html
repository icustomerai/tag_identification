At Tri-State Recycling, we are a family owned business offering a streamlined recycling service to help in diverting valuable resources from solid waste streams.  Diverting waste flow means supporting the environment while putting cash in your pocket.

We purchase all forms of Ferrous and Non-Ferrous metals on site using our state certified scales.  Pricing is reflective of the current metal market, meaning our rates are constantly being updated to provide you with the best possible pricing for your material.

Whether Industrial, Commercial or Residential, we specialize in a complete recycling service and offer fast, dependable removal.  We supply and maintain containers in every size and can even offer full on-site recycling evaluations for your location. From home owner walk-in traffic, commercial business to industrial scrap, TRI-STATE RECYCLING offers a service that you can rely upon.
