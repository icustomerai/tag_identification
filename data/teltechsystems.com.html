Who We Are?

TelTech Systems has been an innovator in Healthcare Patient Communication for over 20 years with a clear and critical mission – to provide medical practices with a simple platform to improve patient engagement and increase practice efficiency. Then and now, our focus has been on developing solutions to accomplish that mission. By helping practices automate patient messaging in multiple formats, we offer practices, hospitals, and other health care organizations more effective and efficient communications in the method preferred by their patient population – this provides organizations with a true patient-centered approach.

What We Do?

TelTech System’s MediCall Service offers a patient communication platform that provides the ideal solution to help practices communicate efficiently reducing cancellations and “no-shows” while positively impacting your bottom line! MediCall is a true cloud-based system with custom voice calling, text & email notification options.
