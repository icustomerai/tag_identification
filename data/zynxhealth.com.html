For example, evidence demonstrating the link between hand-washing and reduced infection rates has existed for more than 100 years. Yet, clinicians wash their hands properly only about 30 to 40 percent of the time.

About Zynx Health

What We Believe

Evidence is at the heart of all we do at Zynx Health.

In 1996, Zynx Health began with a mission to help practitioners deliver better care by following evidence-based practices. We pioneered electronic, evidence-based clinical decision support solutions. More than twenty-five years later, ensuring that healthcare teams have access to solutions that can measurably improve patient care remains central to our mission.

We are widely recognized as the market leader in evidence-based clinical decision support and have worked with over 1,500 hospitals across the United States.
