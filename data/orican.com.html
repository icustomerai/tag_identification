      Your insurance specialists...

      Old Republic Canada is focused on bringing its expertise and superior service to insurance products designed for companies and individuals.

      Old Republic Canada started as a small Hamilton, Ontario association in 1887 with a mission to provide the best insurance solutions to Canadians. A lot has changed since then, but our mission has stayed the same. Through the years, we have stayed true to our roots and culture; and this is what has made us successful in our market for over 130 years. We take pride in being a long-standing insurance organization in Canada as we have both wisdom of the past and an eye on the future.
