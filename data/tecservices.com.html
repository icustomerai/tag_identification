Construction Materials Testing Laboratory
& Engineering Services

Located in Atlanta, Georgia, U.S.A., we are an independent materials testing laboratory including a professional staff of registered engineers, geologists, petrographers, chemists, ACI special inspectors, consultants, and laboratory specialists. We serve clients around the world.

Testing Laboratory

Our construction materials testing laboratory is an AMRL R18 AASHTO accredited lab meeting ASTM C1077 requirements. We test CCRL and AMRL samples, concrete mix designs, metals, and a multitude of products.

Engineering Services

Our experienced professional staff consists of Structural, Metallurgical, Concrete, and Materials Engineers and Construction Consultants – many of whom are registered, with multiple years of broad experience.

Consulting

Difficult Engineering and problems arise on most construction projects.  SGS TEC Services has the reputation for taking on these problems and determining adequate and practical repair
