NJR Clean Energy Ventures

Commitment to Clean Energy

Creating Sustainable Advantages with Clean Energy Solutions

NJR Clean Energy Ventures (NJRCEV) develops, invests in, owns and operates energy projects throughout the Northeast that generate clean power, provide low carbon energy solutions and help our customers save energy and money in a sustainable way. For well over a decade, NJRCEV has successfully operated solar energy infrastructure to serve its customers. Project types include utility-scale, commercial and residential solar systems. 

National carbon reduction targets and mandates will continue to provide NJRCEV with investment opportunities in the renewable space. These opportunities offer valuable environmental benefits and align us with New Jersey’s sustainability goals. In addition, they promote economic development and job creation.

As a leading renewable energy company, NJRCEV continually seeks to grow its portfolio and bring the benefits of clean energy solutions to its customers and the communities it proudly serves.
