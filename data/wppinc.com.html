A MESSAGE FROM OUR CEO, DON CAMP

Western Power Products has been serving customers here in California, Arizona, Nevada, Utah and Hawaii with quality engines and factory-trained employees for over 40 years. We furnish John Deere diesel engines, parts and components, as well as Funk Drivetrain Components and Reliable Horsepower gaseous engines for those applications requiring an alternative to diesel. We also provide custom services, from complete power unit builds to upfitting engines into machines with the related hardware. We offer a very capable engineering and technical assistance group able to provide solutions to your power needs, build custom units to your specifications, and much more. We repower a wide variety of machines, and as the “solution provider,” we offer the fabrication, welding, painting and testing capabilities to complete a project in our facilities. We also build irrigation power units on skids or on trailers for ease of mobility. We build enclosures and offer many other specialty products and services to better serve the off-road markets in agriculture, construction, oilfield, power generation, commercial turf, mining, and much more. With over 80,000 square feet of value-added production, storage and office space to serve a wide scope of market needs, we are a full-service operation. We carry a deep inventory of engines, cooling systems, electrical components, pumps, generators, trailers and skids, along with engine and related component parts to serve our customer needs. We look forward to providing your power solutions and send our best wishes for everyone to have a successful and enjoyable 2023. Sincerely, Don Camp and Everyone at Western Power Products
