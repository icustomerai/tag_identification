Where to Find Your Trim

You can usually find your vehicles trim/version in your:

  • Drivers Door Jamb
  • OwnersManual
  • Back or Side of your Vehicle
  • Under your Vehicle Hood

Where to Find Your VIN

You can usually find your vehicle identification number on your:

  • Vehicle Insurance Card
  • Drivers Side Dashboard
  • Drivers Door Jamb
  • Vehicle Title

Where to Find Your Tire Size

There are two places to find your tire size. Look directly on your tire sidewall or inside your drivers side door jamb for your vehicles tire size.

  • Passenger Tire Sidewall

  • High Flotation Tire Sidewall

  • Inside Your Door Jamb
