Sunrise Banks - a Social Engine for Good

Sunrise is a socially responsible community bank in Minnesota with locations in Minneapolis and St. Paul. Sunrise Banks is focused on creating financial empowerment for all. Learn more about our mission and history of community.

Come join one of the planet’s most socially responsible companies and be surrounded by caring teams and people who challenge and inspire you to greatness.
