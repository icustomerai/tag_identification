Company snapshot

Maximus Canada focuses exclusively on providing services to government. Across health and human services, we transform public policy into programs that change lives.

We guide clients through the change process using:

  • Deep program knowledge
  • Vision for operational and client support
  • Design thinking to simplify complex problems
  • Citizen-centric approach to delivering services

Through government-sponsored program insights and modern technical solutions, we move people forward.

Proudly delivering Canadian solutions with global expertise

At Maximus Canada, we play a critical role in helping governments achieve their goals by providing essential services to the most vulnerable citizens in the communities we serve. We understand that our efforts make a difference in people's lives. We are passionate about what we do, because we care.

Everywhere we serve, we foster economic development by staffing our operations with local employees. Our diverse team communicates in more than 110 languages, helping to eliminate barriers and reduce disparities to match citizens with the services they need.
