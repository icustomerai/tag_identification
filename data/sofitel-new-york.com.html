Bienvenue au Sofitel New York — Welcome to Sofitel New York
The hotel with French soul and a New York City edge.

Live the French Way in luxurious New York City! An address of distinction, Sofitel New York is perfectly situated in midtown Manhattan, just off 5th Avenue’s luxury shops, the beautiful Bryant Park, Broadway theaters, stunning Rockefeller Center and energetic Times Square area. Enjoy luxurious, spacious accommodations with extra-large bathrooms, ample closet space, floor to ceiling windows that open for fresh air, French Balmain bath products, plush towels, and more.

Often called ‘a quiet oasis’, Sofitel New York provides a magnifique respite with all the wonderful energy of the city just outside our doors. Inquire about our Skyscraper and Iconic Building One Bedroom Suites on our top floors; all boast spectacular skyline views, along with our 24th floor four spectacular Terrace Suites. Each provides unparalleled views of the Chrysler or Empire state buildings from your private outdoor terrace. Ideal for a romantic celebration or entertaining friends.

As the proud, Official Hotel Partner of the Tony Awards, Sofitel New York offers two unique suites, our Tony Award terrace suite and Broadway Juliette balcony suite. Both pay homage to the many talented artists who help make the Broadway experience so special. Just two blocks from our front door, you’ll marvel in all the sights and sounds that Broadway theaters offer.

It’s no wonder Sofitel New York is consistently awarded one of the best hotels in NYC by Conde Nast Traveler Reader’s Choice. We look forward to welcoming you.

A bientôt, see you soon.


„
Simon Antoine
