About

Qorvis is a global advisory and strategic communications firm headquartered in Washington, D.C., with primary offices in Geneva and Dubai.

Standing at the nexus of influence and capital, we leverage our rich 20-year history to help the worlds most recognizable brands navigate the headwinds of a rapidly changing stakeholder economy and anticipate whats next in the increasingly volatile geopolitical landscape.

We have the reach and resources to deliver clear and actionable strategies to optimize brand performance, accelerate growth, secure capital and influence outcomes in the markets that matter most.

Locations
