Who we are

Fresenius Medical Care is the world's leading provider of products and services for individuals with renal diseases. We are the leading provider of dialysis products such as dialysis machines or dialyzers. We aim to continuously improve our patients’ quality of life by offering them high-quality products as well as innovative technologies and treatment concepts.
