OUR STORY

Founded in 2005, NOMAC, the First National Operation & Maintenance Company, provides high-quality O&M (Operation & Maintenance) services for the production of electricity and desalinated water worldwide. NOMAC is a wholly-owned subsidiary of ACWA Power.
