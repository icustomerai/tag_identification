New SPC3000 Particle Counter Family

Setra is proud to announce the expansion of our particle counter family with the NEW SPC3000! The Setra SPC3000 particle counters provide remote monitoring with both 0.1 CFM (2.83) & 1.0 CFM (28.3 LPM) flow rate options and provide a simple compact solution for remote monitoring of critical processes in your cleanroom environments.
