About

Lampros Steel, Inc. was founded in 1983 by Milt and Marcus Lampros. For over 35 years, Lampros Steel has provided structural steel to the Northwest and beyond. Lampros Steel is one of the first specialty structural steel warehouses in the Northwest, and operates the largest saw shop in the region.

Processes

Lampros Steel prides itself on its commitment to value added processing and short lead times. Combining our large inventory with value-added processing gives customers the benefit of a one-stop shop. Processing capabilities include sawing, cambering, mitering and a multitude of outside services.
