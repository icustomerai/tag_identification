1031 CORP. Exchange Services

For over 100 years, astute taxpayers have taken advantage of a tax strategy referred to as a 1031 exchange. Section 1031 of the Internal Revenue Code allows the deferral of gain on the sale and purchase of property held for use in a trade or business or for investment. Most taxpayers that take advantage of 1031 exchanges have one objective: deferring the gain. Unfortunately, many do not realize the many other short and long term benefits of a 1031 exchange.
