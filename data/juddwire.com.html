  • High-Performance Wire & Cable Solutions for the Automotive Industry

    Judd Wire’s Automotive products have been supplied to the industry for over 25 years and are used on all major light-vehicle platforms. Our products span a wide range of applications,including wheel speed sensor cables, engine, transmission and fuel systems wiring as well as shielded and unshielded battery cables at 60V, 600V and 1kV ratings.

    AUTOMOTIVE PRODUCTS

  • Superior Insulation Systems for Electrical Aerospace Wire and Cable

    Judd Wire’s Aerospace wiring is currently used in a wide range of commercial and military airframes, avionics, satellites, missile guidance systems, fly-by cables, in-flight communication and entertainment cables, and military electronics ground equipment.

    AEROSPACE PRODUCTS

  • Electronics & Rail Transit Products

    Judd Wire manufactures an extensive line of coaxial cable,crosslinked electronic hook up wire, OEM power and data cables with more than 200 UL and CSA approvals. Our broad offering of high performance radiation crosslinked insulators and enhanced thermoplastic compounds enables us to provide product solutions for the most demanding customer requirements.

    ELECTRONICS / RAIL TRANSIT PRODUCTS

  • Flexible Flat Cable Products

    Flexible Flat Cable (FFC) is the main Flat Component product line. FFC offers lightweight and space savings over traditional wire harness designs. SUMI-CARD® is produced in five factories located in four worldwide locations to support our customers globally. Judd Wire services North and South America by supplying and supporting SUMI-CARD® consumer electronics and automotive electronics customers.

    FLEXIBLE FLAT CABLE PRODUCTS

Insulated Wire and Cable Innovation — Define, Design, Deliver

Judd Wire is a high technology wire company, specializing in the use of electron beam crosslinked thermosets for wire insulation. By applying irradiation crosslinked technology to insulating materials, Judd Wire meets the needs of the wire and cable industry for economical, high-performance products.

Judd Wire manufactures electronic wire and cable in over 100 styles recognized by Underwriters Laboratory, Canadian Standards and/or Military Specifications.
