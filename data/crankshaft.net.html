CRANKSHAFT MACHINE

Established in 1916, Crankshaft Machine Company became a prominent name within manufacturing for building high quality, rough end crankshaft and camshaft equipment known for capacity, quality, and longevity. CMG is now the umbrella company for US Broach Tool, US Broach Machine, SFS Grinders, and LFP Hydraulics.
