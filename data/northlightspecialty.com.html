About Us

North Light Specialty Insurance Company ("NLSIC") provides specialized, consumer-focused insurance products and services designed to help protect individuals as well as families and their assets today and in the future. NLSIC is an AM Best A+ rated (Excellent) insurance company.

NLSIC is a wholly-owned subsidiary of Allstate Insurance Company. We are financially strong and have an experienced management team and employee group with substantial business history and experience.

NLSIC distributes its products through licensed Surplus Lines Brokers.
