Groundbreaking new lining designed to withstand high abrasion and high temperature conditions

The V627BNI formula is a unique black, soft rubber lining. It is 100% resistant to oil exposure, making it perfect for high abrasion and temperature applications when petroleum oils are present. Its known for its physical strength and retention of properties, even after long-term exposure to heat and oil.
