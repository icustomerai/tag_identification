                        Physicians Regional Medical Group

                        Physicians Regional Medical Group is the multi-specialty group practice affiliated with Physicians Regional Healthcare System. Comprised of more than 50 providers with experience in more than 20 different specialties, Physicians Regional Medical Group is part of a fully integrated medical campus, including six modern clinics, two hospitals, labs and much more.
