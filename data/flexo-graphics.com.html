We are an innovative Flexographic label printing company that provides state-of-the-art pressure-sensitive label solutions, complete label design, and program management services to a broad range of industries with a strong focus on being highly responsive to our customers needs.

Get In Touch

FLEXOGRAPHIC LABEL PRINTING, DESIGN & MANAGEMENT

Our mission is to be the most customer-focused provider of product branding and identification solutions by designing, printing, and delivering top quality, cost-effective, flexographic label products on time every time. The company was established in 1983 in Milwaukee, WI with the goal of exceeding customers expectations. Today that goal remains unchanged as we build true partnerships with our customers.

With over 30 years of experience serving the industrial, consumer, food & beverage, automotive, and appliance sectors, Flexo-Graphics understands the ever-changing needs of its customers. We are flexible, responsive, innovative, and committed to mutual success with our customers.

Our Products

Pressure Sensitive Labels

Labels accommodating a diverse range of products printed on a wide range of materials. We produce eye-catching labels that help your brand stand out as well as highly engineered labels that function in difficult applications and environments.

Industrial/Consumer Durables

Flexo-Graphics has been serving the battery industry and industrial markets for over 30 years. With industry-proven label constructions and 1000s of dies in stock, Flexo-Graphics is perfectly positioned to help with your unique requirements.

Digital Labels

Flexo-Graphics offers state of the art digital solutions for labels. Digital printing is a cost-effective method for printing custom labels with photographic-quality graphics, crisp text, and vibrant colors. It is ideal for small to medium-sized runs and Smaller Projects with Multiple Designs.

Peel & Reseal Labels

Flexo-Graphics highly engineered peel and reseal labels work in a wide variety of applications. Food applications like tomatoes, cookies, deli meats are common uses. Also, wet wipe packs are used for general cleaning, more intense higher concentrate industrial cleaning, or cleanroom medical wipes.

Extended Content Labels

Any business that manufactures and packages goods that require additional instructions or safety information can benefit from the use of Extended Content Labels to deliver their message without having to drastically alter the packaging design. If the package simply uses a standard label it is hard to provide all of the necessary information in the available space.

Specialty Label Products

Flexo-Graphics also makes a number of specialty label products such as Coupons and Labels for bulk mail kits, Security-Based Label Applications, Tamper Proof Seals, Specialty Inks, Metallic labels, Instant Rebate Coupons, Window Clings, Peel and Reveal labels, Variable Print, UL approved constructions, Preprint and Imprint Programs, Serialization, Thermal Imprinting, and on-press inkjet variable Imprinting.

Markets We Serve

Flexo-Graphics has a diverse set of vertical markets that we have created expertise in over the past 35 years. Our roots started in the production of flexographic labels for the automotive industry. Through significant capital investments in technology and people, our markets served has expanded greatly.
