﻿﻿Design Group | Who We Are﻿﻿

Barry-Wehmiller Design Group is a fully integrated engineering, architecture, construction, system integration, and consulting firm showing what’s possible at the intersection of great business strategy and profound care for people. Serving the world’s leading companies across the food and beverage, life sciences, industrial and semiconductor industries, we pair deep industry experience with multidisciplinary subject matter expertise across our 45+ offices to deliver fully customized solutions to our clients. Rooted in our distinct commitment to caring deeply for people, we’ve built a firm uniquely equipped to create unparalleled partnerships and lasting value for our clients.
