Serving Producers in the DJ Basin

Scroll to learn more

Black Diamond Gathering is a joint venture between Chevron and Greenfield Midstream established in January 2018 to provide crude oil gathering and storage services to producers in the Denver Julesburg Basin. Black Diamond is the only midstream service provider in the DJ Basin with the ability to deliver simultaneously to all major long-haul crude outlets.

The Black Diamond system includes approximately 335 miles of pipeline in operation, 300,000 barrels per day of delivery capacity and approximately 420,000 barrels of crude oil storage capacity. The system is connected to every major takeaway pipeline in the basin, including the White Cliffs Pipeline, the Saddlehorn Pipeline, the Grand Mesa Pipeline, and the Pony Express Pipeline.
