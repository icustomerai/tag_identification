Our Services

We partner with colleges and universities across the country to deliver an off-campus housing website that provides students, faculty, and staff with the ability to easily find housing, roommates, and educational resources, all in one place. Our service is provided at no cost to our university partners or students and includes revenue sharing to invest back into student programming and initiatives. We supply the software and support for the service, and work together with you to market the website to your students to ensure a successful program.

Websites that engage & connect

Our sleek, modern, university-branded websites are lightning fast, work perfectly on any device, and make it easy for students to find their next home, roommates, and more. Features include:

We build

engaging websites

Our websites are supported by custom imagery, university-specific announcements and a property listing preview that can easily be accessed through the homepage. 

Our websites come equipped with a

powerful housing search

Our map-based search shows a variety of housing options (e.g., apartments, houses, sublets, etc.), off-campus points of interest, and transit shuttle routes and stops. Students also have the option to search by neighborhood, distance to campus, and other critical features such as price, number of bedrooms, and move-in date.

Learn more about vacancies on our

comprehensive listing detail page

Students can view property photos, 3D tours, videos, floorplans, unit amenities, property ratings, commute options, as well as walk scores, bike scores, transit scores, sound scores, internet options, and much more.

Explore the

secure roommate finder

Our roommate finder is only accessible to authenticated university affiliates. It contains detailed student profiles, as well as the ability to filter the search by timeline needed, pets, social/study habits and programs. Students also have the option to share photos, social media handles, and sublet listing information.

Make use of our

educational resources

The resource section has an engaging, modern layout with quick search functionality and is fully customizable to allow you to include educational information such as living-guides, how-to's, and videos that your students will find beneficial as they navigate off-campus living.
