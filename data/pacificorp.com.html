Energy plan advances a

net-zero future

Our long-term plan advances a net-zero energy future with safe, low-cost, reliable and sustainable power for our customers and communities. The plan envisions a 78% reduction in greenhouse gas emissions from 2005 levels by 2030 and a 90% reduction by 2035.
