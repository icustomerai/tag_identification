Palmetto Corp

Palmetto Corp is a fully integrated complete site development and construction company serving central and eastern South Carolina for over 30 years. Palmetto Corp is in its third generation of ownership and is led by President Shawn Godwin, who has taken the company from what has traditionally been known as a paving company to a complete site development and vertical construction company. Palmetto Corp is the leader in Horry and surrounding counties for complete site development, company leadership and is dedicated to continuing a legacy of greatness.

Palmetto Corp’s services include: asphalt paving and maintenance, grading, concrete, crushing, surveying and hauling. From repairing a pothole, paving a parking lot, or managing large-scale infrastructure, Palmetto Corp’s team of experts can handle any construction job from start to finish. The company encourages all of its potential and existing customers to use them as their resource, particularly when it comes to compiling budget numbers and making sure they are educated on what services are needed and why. We care about your bottom line.

Palmetto Corp uses state of the art technology and employs highly trained workers on every job to ensure that all work is completed safely, efficiently, and of the highest quality. Certified employees staff the company’s three asphalt laboratories and provide ongoing testing that exceeds all regulatory standard.

The company was recently named one of South Carolina’s largest private companies by the Grant Thornton 100 and one of the country’s largest paving contractors by Pavement Magazine.
