ABOUT SMC

SMC was founded in 1987 in response to a shortage of trained technologists able to perform competent cardiovascular services. SMC strives to provide a comprehensive range of products and services while maintaining an excellent reputation for quality
​and dependability. SMC currently has mobile ultrasound operations in Louisiana, Mississippi and Texas.

SMC has employment opportunities for individuals looking to become part of a dynamic, expanding company that offers tremendous professional growth opportunities and highly competitive compensation packages.

For more information, please ﻿contact﻿ SMC.
