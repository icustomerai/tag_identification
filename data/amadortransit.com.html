Welcome to Amador Transit

(209) 267-9395

Amador Transit is committed to providing safe, reliable service to all. We provide Monday-Friday fixed route service within Amador County from our Sutter Hill Transit Center to Jackson, Sutter Creek, Plymouth, Ione, and along the Highway 88 corridor through Pine Grove and Pioneer ending at Amador Station. We also travel twice a day to downtown Sacramento with connections to the Sacramento International Airport. ADA Paratransit “Dial-a-Ride” curb-to-curb service operates in the Jackson-Sutter Creek and now, Pine Grove area. Call for details!

DIAL-A-RIDE
