Transmission

Sumter Utilities specializes in all aspects of electrical transmission work, including new construction, maintenance, upgrading, and energized services. Our efficiency and specialized skills keep the power flowing to substations and distribution networks.

Substation

Substations are a critical piece of any utility system. Sumter Utilities constructs and modifies high voltage substations, offering start-to-finish support and project management. Our experience provides efficiency and quality delivery for our customers.

Renewable Energy

The utility industry is ever-changing, and Sumter is at the forefront. We completed our first solar field project in 2009. Our experience in this area gives Sumter Utilities the ability to be the renewable energy leader in the Southeast.

Emergency Restoration

When disaster strikes, experience is the best preparation. Year after year, Sumter Utilities’ crews mobilize to respond to emergencies and restore power to the communities in need. Our modern fleet of equipment and skilled crews, along with tried-and-true processes enable us to respond at a moment’s notice to any crisis situation.
