Making responsible precision medicine possible

Med fusion, acquired by Quest Diagnostics on July 21, 2017, is an integrated Molecular Center of Excellence and clinical trials organization delivering support to healthcare providers and biotech/pharmaceutical companies to consistently meet the needs of patients. An industry leader in advanced laboratory diagnostics and support, our full-service clinical laboratory includes a dedicated test development and validation team, as well as reference lab services such as pathology and molecular diagnostics.
