What are probiotics?

Probiotics are important for your gut health. As you age, your population of friendly bacteria naturally starts to decline. This can be due to antibiotics, stress, infections, and/or poor diet. Unbalanced gut flora can lead to uncomfortable occasional digestive upsets such as gas, bloating, constipation, and diarrhea. By promoting an ideal balance of ‘friendly bacteria’, probiotics can help set you up for optimal digestive health.
