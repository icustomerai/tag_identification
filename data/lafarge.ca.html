WHAT WE DO

Solutions & Products
Solutions & Products

Making buildings smarter from rooftop to foundation, our advanced building solutions range from roofing and waterproofing to insulation and renovation for energy efficient, enduring, and resilient construction.

Cement
Cement

With leading market positions across the globe, our specialty cements enable sustainable building. With products like ECOPlanet, we deliver low-carbon and circular benefits with no compromise on performance.

Aggregates
Aggregates

Our range of aggregates, from sustainably sourced materials to recycled construction & demolition waste, provide the raw materials for concrete, masonry and asphalt, as well as the foundation for buildings, roads and landfills.

Concrete
Concrete

As the green concrete company, we offer Canada's broadest range of low-carbon and circular concrete with ECOPact, available in all regions of the world, making sustainable construction happen at scale.

ECOPACT, THE GREEN CONCRETE

ECOPact is the industry's broadest range of green concrete with products that provide 30% to 100% less carbon emissions compared to standard (OPC) concrete, with no compromise in performance.

Lafarge is Canada’s largest provider of sustainable and innovative building solutions including Aggregates, Cement, Ready Mix and Precast Concrete, Asphalt and Paving, and Road and Civil Construction. With over 6,900 employees and 400 sites across the country, we provide green products to build the infrastructure and communities where Canadians live and work.

As a member of Holcim Group, our purpose is to build progress for people and the planet.
