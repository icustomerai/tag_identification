Yachiyo of America

A Tier 1 supplier to the automotive industry since 1997

As a world-class manufacturer of automotive parts, Yachiyo of America is committed to meeting every expectation of our customers. Our specialty is designing and producing plastic fuel tanks and sunroofs. At every turn, we work to improve our processes as we achieve the ideal outcome for our customers.

Yachiyo was established in 1947 by Eiichi Otake in Japan. Since then the company has grown its facilities worldwide. Yachiyo of America was founded in 1997 to serve a new demographic in our global capabilities.

As a company, our goal is to become one of the world’s top-level manufacturers of automotive body parts and seeks to supply superior quality, while maintaining economical products in direct response to our customers’ needs.
