LINETEC DELIVERS SPECIALIZED ARCHITECTURAL COATINGS & FINISHES, LARGE AND SMALL

Linetec is the nations largest independent architectural metals finishing company. We offer the highest quality and reliability in high-performance architectural coatings, including fluoropolymer PVDF (Kynar) paint, powder paint and eco-friendly acid etch anodize. In addition to the 50,000 colors in our library, we offer custom options and specialty finishes such as Brushed Anodize, Copper Anodize, Wood Grain finishes and textured Terra Cotta coatings. As your single source solution, we also provide thermal improvement services, stretch forming and repair/restoration services.
