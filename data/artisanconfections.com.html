At Artisan Confections, we know the best things in life take time. That’s why our dedicated team of chocolatiers works for 3 days to handcraft each small batch of our fine chocolates.

Who We Are

Artisan Confections specializes in high-quality, hand-made chocolates that are elegant in both design and flavor. We thoughtfully select the best ingredients – from French Valrhona chocolate to organic mint grown on a Maryland farm – to craft smooth, delicate confections that are so fresh, they have a shelf life.

Order our truffles and confections online for delivery nationwide, or visit our boutique just outside of Washington, DC for a glimpse at our time- honored manufacturing techniques.
