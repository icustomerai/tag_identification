We Keep Industry in America Producing

Southern Industrial Constructors (SIC) has been proud to help install and maintain the manufacturing and industrial base that has fueled growth across the Southeast and beyond. In doing so, we have grown from a Carolinas-based rigging company into a leading provider of turnkey industrial construction and plant maintenance services.
