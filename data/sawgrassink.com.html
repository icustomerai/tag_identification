The Story of Sawgrass Ink

Our Ink is different. Our ink is created purely for sublimation and purely for our printers. With a passion to perfect dye sublimation ink for more than 30 years, our team of elite chemists based in Mount Pleasant, South Carolina, carefully formulates every batch of ink to ensure consistent color and maximum efficiency.

We take ink seriously and we craft it to perform better, helping product decorators worldwide create personalized products that customers love, with minimum costs.
