A Global Operation with Personalized Service

 

Seattle Shrimp & Seafood offers both the “big buy” savings you need and the “local supplier” service you want. By leveraging our worldwide sourcing network and extensive industry knowledge, we continuously supply quality shrimp and shellfish to large volume retail and food service chains, club warehouse outlets, broad line distributors and local distributors. Since our start in 2006, our company has grown to exceed 130 million dollars in annual sales. We have accomplished this by consistently delivering products and services that exceed our customers’ expectations. Let us put our resources and dependability to work for you.

We invite you to explore our website to learn more; we look forward to working with you.
