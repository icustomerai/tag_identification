Franciscan Cabernet Sauvignon V21 750ML

Dark Cherry, Toasted Caramel
Sourced from some of California’s finest vineyards, our Franciscan Cabernet Sauvignon is full bodied with notes of dark cherry and toasted caramel. While it is bold and plush, our Cabernet is not overpowered by oak or tannins which allows the fruit to shine through. It also allows the wine to pairs well with a wide variety of cuisines - anything from pho to your favorite gourmet burger.

Franciscan Chardonnay V21 750ML

Pear, Pineapple, Honey
Sourced from some of California’s finest vineyards, our Franciscan Chardonnay is bright and bursting with notes of with pear, pineapple & hint of honey. We used no oak aging to allow for the fruit character to come through. It also allows the wine to pairs well with a wide variety of cuisines - anything from creamy pasta to BBQ chicken.
