    MOECO and Chevron to explore advanced closed loop geothermal pilot in Hokkaido Japan

    Mitsui Oil Exploration Co., Ltd, (“MOECO”), a wholly owned subsidiary of Mitsui & Co., Ltd. ("Mitsui"; Head Office: Chiyoda-ku, Tokyo; President: Kenichi Hori) and Chevron New Energies Japan GK. (“Chevron”) have agreed to conduct pilot tests of a developing novel geothermal technology known as advanced closed loop (ACL).

    Chevron Corporation (NYSE: CVX) today provided an overview of the company’s business plans and operations at its annual meeting of stockholders.

  • May 22, 2023

    chevron announces agreement to acquire PDC energy

    Chevron Corporation (NYSE: CVX) announced today that it has entered into a definitive agreement with PDC Energy, Inc. (NASDAQ: PDCE) to acquire all of the outstanding shares of PDC in an all-stock transaction valued at $6.3 billion, or $72 per share.

  • April 28, 2023

    chevron announces first quarter 2023 results

    Chevron Corporation (NYSE: CVX) today reported earnings of $6.6 billion ($3.46 per share - diluted) for first quarter 2023, compared with $6.3 billion ($3.22 per share - diluted) in first quarter 2022.

what drives us

our people

We believe in the power of humanity to solve any challenge. Our greatest resources are ingenuity, creativity and collaboration. We develop generations of problem solvers who have met the most complex challenges of energy’s past. Together, we will innovate toward the future. 

our work

We define the energy challenge in human terms. Our work has made human progress a reality for millions by driving social and economic development and enabling the benefits of modern society. Life depends on reliable, affordable and ever-cleaner energy. 

our culture

We deliver results the right and responsible way. Our culture is built upon the principles of responsibility, trust and integrity. Our leadership is a profound source of pride and we will lead in the future of energy.
