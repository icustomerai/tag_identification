Our Services

Helping clients design, build, maintain, and protect their grid investments for today and the future.

Full-service Engineering Consulting

UtilityEngineering provides traditional electrical and civil engineering services as well as turnkey (EPC) project completion to electric utility clients and others who require our specialized knowledge of design, construction, operations, and maintenance of utility class electric systems. We are focused not only on the project at hand, but also the short- and long-term business goals of our clients. Our services are comprehensive from foundations and structures to complex electrical system analysis to rate and cost of service studies to assisting regulatory and legal entities.
