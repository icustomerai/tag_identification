OUR STORY

In 1981, Walker Engineering, Inc was formed in Dallas, Texas. The company started with only three employees, and during the last 42 years we have transformed into one of the largest and most qualified electrical construction companies in Texas with offices in Dallas/Fort Worth, Houston, Austin, and San Antonio.
