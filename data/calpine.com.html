Right Fleet, Right Place, Right Time 

Environmental regulations. Integration of intermittent renewable resources. Plentiful, clean, affordable natural gas. The retirement of inefficient generation units. As these trends shape the nation’s power industry, Calpine’s modern fleet stands as a model of flexibility and reliability to meet America’s power needs today – and tomorrow.

Powering America

With 76 power plants in operation and 1 under construction, Calpine’s fleet has the capacity to generate nearly 26,000 megawatts of electricity – enough to power approximately 20 million homes. Our geographically diverse fleet capitalizes on trends in the nation’s most robust power markets.
