Freight and Logistics Services

Online Freight Services (OFS) offers a wide variety of services to seamlessly deliver freight across the country or around the world. Combined with our expert logistics professionals, proprietary technology, and commitment to quality, we offer you the transportation management that best fits your unique needs. With OFS, you will enjoy the personal service of a local office backed by the resources of a 2-billion-dollar Fortune 1000 publicly traded company.

To start streamlining your transportation management, contact an OFS representative today at (800) 284-2603 or ofs@onlinefreight.com.

  • Full Truckload (Van)

    We leverage our network of over 50,000 carriers to secure the right equipment for you, when you need it, and for the price that fits your budget. Whether you require frequent truckload shipments or just a few a year, we are committed to providing the best solutions for you. We understand the importance of just in time (JIT) schedules and are qualified to handle the most difficult situations.

    OFS is able to provide you with 48’ and 53’ x 102” vans that have the following options, depending on your needs:

    • Air ride
    • Team drivers
    • Driver assist/unload
    • Curtain vans
    • Vented/heated

  • Flatbed, Over Dimensional, and Heavy Haul

    With our expertise and exceptional service, we are equipped to handle your most complex shipments, ensuring they arrive on time and on budget. For large freight that does not fit within the dimensions of standard truck trailers, we offer a wide range of specialized services, including flatbed, over dimensional, and heavy haul. We use only the most reliable carriers in the industry to handle these sensitive shipments. Whether you need tarps, side kits, permits, or escorts, we can meet all your specialized needs.

    We offer equipment including:

    • Flats 45', 48', and 53' x 102"
    • Drop decks, double drops, and low boys
    • Stretch trailers

  • Temperature-controlled

    At OFS, we realize the importance of reliable, cost-effective service when handling temperature-sensitive freight. With our extensive experience and our wide network of carriers that specialize in refrigerated shipping, we provide you with service you can depend on.

    We also offer the following options to meet your unique temperature-controlled shipping needs:

    • Air ride
    • Pallet Exchange
    • Driver assist/unload
    • 48' & 53' x 102" space saver

  • Less Than Truckload (LTL)/Partial Shipments

    For your freight that does not require the use of an entire trailer, OFS offers LTL and partial truckload shipping that helps you reduce costs without sacrificing speed and efficiency. By working with our network of consolidators, our team secures the right carrier for your freight. Whether you need to move one pallet or half of a truckload, we have a large complement of carriers to provide the most competitive pricing and best service possible.

    Additional service options include:

    • Lift gates
    • Driver assist/unload
    • Residential service

  • Specialized Equipment

    If you have a need for a specialized trailer or piece of equipment, we have expertise to help.

    We offer a wide range of solutions, including:

    • Hot shot
    • Car haulers
    • Tankers
    • Belly dump/end dump
    • Boat haulers

  • Intermodal Rail

    For long-distance shipments that have a flexible transit schedule, OFS offers intermodal rail services. Easily ship anywhere in the United States, Canada, and Mexico. OFS always has a train for your shipments; our North American network includes all U.S. Class 1 railroads, plus access to more than 100,000 dry-van and temperature-controlled intermodal containers.

    Intermodal trains can move one ton of freight 500 miles on a single gallon of gas—using less fuel and emitting less CO2 than trucks. Talk with OFS, a certified EPA SmartWay partner, about over-the-road shipping alternatives that help protect the environment and simplify transportation.

    This cost-effective shipping method is available for:

    • 40', 45', or 48' containers
    • Door-to-door service
    • 48' or 53' piggyback trailers

  • International Air and Ocean

    OFS offers international shipping options to move your freight anywhere across the globe.

    Our international services include:

    • Air
    • Barge
    • Container Freight
