Clinic

Our rural health family practice clinic cares for people of all ages and is open Monday - Friday 8:30 a.m. to 5 p.m.

Hospital

Our critical access hospital offers inpatient, outpatient and 24/7 emergency care.

Public Health

Our public health department promotes healthy behaviors in our community and coordinates WIC, family health, school nursing, child/teen check-ups, foot and blood pressure clinics and influenza vaccination clinics.

Senior Living

Our long-term care and assisted residential community give area seniors a place to call home.
