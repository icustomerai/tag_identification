We Help People Love the Way They Look and Feel for Their Most Important Moments

Tailored Brands provides a personal, convenient, one-of-a-kind shopping experience with compelling products and world-class service. Tailored Brands features leading menswear retailers Men’s Wearhouse, Jos. A. Bank and Moores; and family retailer K&G Fashion Superstore.

About Us

Our Brands

We are energized by going above and beyond for our customers, because a shopping experience with us is more than just that – it’s gaining the confidence to express your true self. That’s what our brands strive to achieve through our exceptional service and compelling products for your diverse tastes and preferences.
