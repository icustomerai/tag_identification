19 Apr.2021

Tideland Signal Manufacturing LLC continues production of Tidelands product portfolio

Tideland Signal Manufacturing LLC, part of the privately held Orga group of companies, is pleased to announce that it will set up production and continue to produce the Tideland product range from the original production facility in Houston. Tideland products will be supplied via Orga Signal regional offices in Houston, Schiedam and Singapore.

In September 2020, Orga Signal already acquired the product design rights and production tooling of Tideland Signal Corporation.

With this opportunity Tideland Signal Manufacturing will be able to prolong the quality of products and experience of the people, to continue the renowned product portfolio.

Rob van Beest, founder and Managing Director of the Orga group of companies, commented: “The addition of the renowned Tideland brand and product portfolio complements and enhances Orga’s leading position in the Aids to Marine Navigation market. The continuation of production at the original facility allows us to supply and support our existing and new client base worldwide with Tideland products and systems in addition to Orga’s own well established Aids to Navigation product range.”
