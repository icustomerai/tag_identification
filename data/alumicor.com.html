At Alumicor, it’s our mission to provide quality products, on-time delivery and exceptional service for contract glazing across North America. Through our line of architectural aluminum curtainwall, storefront, entrance and window systems, Alumicor has positioned itself as a leading manufacturer for sustainable and innovative building solutions in the architectural and construction community.

Our commitment to the success of Alumicor clients is supported by corporate offices in Toronto, Canada, and manufacturing facilities and service centers across the country. We hold our dedicated employees, distributors and suppliers to the highest standard as we continue to be a resource for technical expertise and provide a performance-focused product line that responds to the evolving needs of market.

Delivering an exceptional customer experience for over 60 years.
