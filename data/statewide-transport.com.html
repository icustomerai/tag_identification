About Us

Statewide Transport, LLC is a subsidiary of Vulcan Materials Company, the nation's largest producer of construction aggregates. Whether it is delivery or removal, Statewide has the ability to haul construction-related materials, including stone, sand, gravel, dirt, asphalt, broken concrete and other bulk materials to and from your jobsite. Statewide has access to a broad range of construction material operations within Vulcan and other construction material suppliers. This provides Statewide the ability to quote transportation from the location closest to your jobsite.

Our distinct focus on customer service is what makes us different. Our network of terminals and owner-operators have you covered for your project.
