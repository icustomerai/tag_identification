A Full Range of Food Packaging Solutions and Services

For nearly a century, Viskase has been revolutionizing the meat processing industry. We are committed to meeting our customers’ needs by providing best-in-class, safe and affordable protein sources within a broad array of innovative casing solutions. As a global leader, quality and value are our highest priorities for our customers. 

Cellulose Casings

NOJAX® and LLC are regenerated cellulose casings that are typically presented in the form of shirred sticks and designed for automated production of tubular sausages.

Plastic Casings

High performance casings tailored to maximize product yield and shelf stability of sliced and retail items such as formed processed meat and poultry.

Fibrous Casings

Fibrous Casings are a unique combination of regenerated cellulose applied to a special paper for exceptional strength and diameter uniformity. These casings can be used for a wide range of applications including pepperoni, salami, luncheon meats, boneless hams and other deli-style processed meats. They are ideal for slicing products requiring precise diameter control. Fibrous Casings can also be used for processed and smoked cheeses.

Netting & Fabric

With a full range of sizes, patterns, colors and applications available, Viskase provides you with the ability and flexibility to tailor your own all-in-one “netting and casing” product to suit your individual requirements.

Transfer Casings

Add value and marketability using casings to apply smoke, color, flavor and spices, reducing your operating cost and carbon footprint.

Applied Technologies

MEMBRA-CEL® Dialysis Tubing is a hydrophilic material made from regenerated cellulose prepared using the viscose process. Typically this product is used in the laboratory for dialysis purposes. MEMBRA-CEL® is available as tubing and sheets.
