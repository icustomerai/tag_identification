Harbor Distributing

CONNECTING CUSTOMERS, CONSUMERS AND BRANDS EVERY DAY, EVERYWHERE WE OPERATE

Harbor Distributing joined Reyes Beverage Group in 1989 and operates from facilities in Los Angeles, Santa Fe Springs and sunny Huntington Beach, California. Our 400,000 square foot state-of-the-art warehouse located in Surf City, USA acts as our West Coast headquarters. We proudly sell and distribute over 47.9 million cases to more than 13,100 customers annually over an expansive territory from Malibu in Los Angeles County to San Clemente in Orange County.

Who We Are

Harbor Distributing boasts a modern state-of-the-art warehouse distribution center with dramatic central lobby views into the 1 million square foot warehouse and an adjacent 1-acre refrigerated storage zone. Other unique features of this facility are the custom designed bar – Tiger Joe’s – and a health & fitness gym and interior designs that reflect the lifestyle of Southern California. Outside of the Huntington Beach facility, Harbor Distributing also has facilities in Gardena and Santa Fe Springs, California

We’re part of a national network, but we live local and stay true to our values, dedicating our resources to developing our employees and helping communities.
