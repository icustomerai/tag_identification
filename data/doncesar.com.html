The Brand-New Don Is A True Legend

Dramatically perched atop wind-swept dunes with expansive water views in every direction, the fully renovated Don CeSar is the crown jewel of Florida's Gulf Coast. Since 1928, this monument to glamour and leisure has welcomed sun-seekers, luxury travelers and some of America's most famous - and notorious - figures. Become part of the storied history of the legendary Pink Palace®. Iconic. Romantic. Celebratory.  
 

The Gulf Coast's Beachfront Gem Completely Revitalized

Reconnect with sun, sand and sea. Each one of our freshly remodeled 241 guest rooms and 36 suites offers luxurious amenities and sweeping views of the Don CeSar's coastal surroundings. Bask in the Florida sun at the all-new Beacon Pool Bar & Lookout perched above our two heated outdoor pools. Experience breathtaking enhancements at The Lobby Bar and our fine dining restaurant Maritana, transform in 11,000 square feet of seaside bliss at Spa Oceana, or play in our sugary St. Pete Beach backyard as the sparkling Gulf of Mexico is lapping at your feet. With over 38,000 square feet of updated wedding and event spaces, there's plenty of room to celebrate love, life and every other type of special occasion. Discover the new Don for yourself.
