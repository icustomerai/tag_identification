THE LEAGUE by Varsity All Star

The League LIVE Show hosted by Maddie Gardner and Chuck Lott took center stage at JAMfest Cheer Super Nationals! The League is changing the conversation from “Who won the weekend?” to “Who stands at the top this season?” Teams are ranked by their age group, both regionally and nationally, based on the points they earn throughout the regular season. Don’t miss any of the action, catch us LIVE later this season at the UCA International All Star Championship & One Up Grand Nationals!
