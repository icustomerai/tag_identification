Built for you

With a longstanding commitment to our groups and their members, we’re proud to offer access to exclusive savings and policies that are created to match your profession and lifestyle.

  • Exclusive rates

    As a member of an eligible group, you’ll enjoy exclusive home and car insurance rates.

  • More ways to save

    Enjoy even more savings when you do things like bundle your home, car, and travel insurance, insure multiple vehicles, or just by being a loyal customer. There are also many more ways to save!

  • First accident forgiveness

    There’s a first time for everything – even accidents. With our Accident Forgiveness products, your premium won’t be directly increased as a result of your first at-fault accident.

    (Keep in mind that a first at-fault accident could still impact your eligibility for certain discounts, which may affect your premium).

  • Personal property coverage at work

    Receive coverage for personal property you’re required to bring to work as part of your job such as books and laptops.
