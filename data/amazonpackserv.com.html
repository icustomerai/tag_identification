About Amazon Packaging Services

APS, an Ohio based company, has an innovative approach to today's packaging industry.  This approach has allowed the company to properly source individual customers needs to our best manufacturing facilities. Those sources include local, nationwide, and even international manufacturing in countries such as Asia and Central America. 

Amazon’s strengths lie in both industrial packaging products and high-end retail graphics packaging.

In industrial packaging applications, APS works with the best packaging engineers, design teams and testing facilities in the industry to increase our customers' efficiencies and evaluate areas for cost savings while providing the best customer service available.

Through its leadership, APS has also acquired years of experience in  retail and consumer packaging.  By providing innovative packaging designs and the industries most up to date printing processes, we will give your product that POW it needs to get noticed in today's highly competitive instore environment.

APS’s Leadership has worked with many consumer-based industries. Those industries include Food, Beverage, Wine and Spirits, Pharmaceuticals, and Automotive Aftermarket.

As President and CEO, Air Force Veteran Jamie Stevens has used her many years in the packaging industry along with her military background to bring the same core values to APS’s customers:

Integrity First, Service Before Self and Excellence In All We Do. 

Amazon Packaging Services Corp. is a 100% WOMAN owned, VETERAN business with second-generation leadership in the corrugated industry.
