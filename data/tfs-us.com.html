Mechanical

The mechanical services we offer our clients’ facilities projects involves installing pipe, valves, fittings, strainers, pumps, industrial chillers, make-up air handlers and cooling towers.

These are complete with new piping and controls, for our clients’ buildings and advanced technology facilities.

Electrical

In new and existing buildings and facilities we install, upgrade, demo and troubleshoot electrical services for our clients.

Our plant renovation services involve dismantling, relocation and re-installation of equipment and facilities, required electrical equipment, and standby generator systems.

Process Piping

We install high-end process piping systems for advanced technology companies.

We train and certify our personnel on the topics of Safety, Quality, Orbital Welding, ASME Standards, BPE Standards, SEMI Standards, Sloped Piping, Hygienic Piping, and Cleanroom Protocol to meet our client requirements.

Integrated Process Systems for Mission-Critical Facilities

TFS personnel are qualified and trained in: safety, quality, orbital welding, ASME standards, BPE standards, SEMI standards, sloped piping, hygienic piping, and cleanroom protocol. We fabricate and install piping systems for ultra-high-purity piping and critical hygienic piping.

Industry-Leading Commercial Electrical Contractor

Services include plant renovation: dismantling, relocation and re-installation of equipment and facilities, electrical equipment including motors, A/C and D/C drives, instrumentation installation, wiring and calibration, and standby generator systems.
