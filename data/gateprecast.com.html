GateLite, a sole source facade system

GateLites lean concrete skin, design teams can efficiently create a true work of art using architectural precast. The system is finished and unitized with glass and a complete thermal, vapor and air barrier. And it virtually reduces all on-site labor hours, since fabrication and assembly are performed prior to delivery.
