When A Need Occurs

SIS supports site-specific tissue remodeling.

As the body heals, SIS is gradually remodeled and integrated into the body, leaving behind organized tissue that provides long-term strength.
The open, three-dimensional structure of the ECM
permits cells and capillaries
to grow into the matrix.
Cells deliver nutrients and secrete collagen,
gradually renewing
and reinforcing the site
with the patient's own tissue
until completely healed.

Developing advanced biomaterials

Technologies

Cook Biotech specializes in developing advanced biomaterials for site-specific tissue remodeling based on our knowledge of naturally occurring extracellular matrices (ECMs).
As pioneers in ECM technologies, we develop and continuously improve proprietary processing and manufacturing methods to retain the inherent properties of our naturally occurring source materials.
This convergence of nature and technology results in medical-grade devices that help patients overcome serious medical conditions while leaving no permanent material behind.
visit our Technologies page

From Head & Neck to Extremities

Products

Cook Biotech’s advanced tissue-repair products are derived from porcine small intestinal submucosa (SIS) and other ECM-based biomaterials. They are used to manage multiple types of wounds, including burns, diabetic ulcers, venous ulcers, and trauma wounds. Surgical applications include hernia repair, fistula repair, otologic repair, and soft-tissue reinforcement.

Cook Biotech Inc.

About

Cook Biotech Inc. was established in 1995 to develop medical solutions based on the regenerative properties of extracellular matrix materials discovered at Purdue University.

More than 25 years later, our proprietary technologies and manufacturing processes have produced more than 6 million advanced tissue-repair products for global distribution.

We actively seek and welcome partnerships with researchers, distributors, start-up medical companies, and established medical companies to further our mission of serving patients.

We are proud to be part of Cook Group Inc., a global family of socially responsible companies.
