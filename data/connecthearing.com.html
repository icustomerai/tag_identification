Your Hearing Professionals

Connect Hearing is committed to providing industry-leading hearing health care in our nationwide network of hearing centers. Our top-rated hearing care professionals provide expert advice on diagnosing hearing loss and consulting on the latest technology in hearing aids.

World of Hearing

We are excited to announce the opening of our first concept store dedicated to the love of sound. Connect World of Hearing has opened its doors in the Irvine Spectrum Center in Irvine CA. Visitors will find the latest hearing technology and sound innovation with interactive displays and experience rooms. 

At World of Hearing, we offer products ranging from Sennheiser consumer electronics, hearing protection, the latest in hearing aid technology and of course, our top-rated hearing healthcare services. Stop by for a visit if you're in the Irvine area, or explore it virtually through our website.
