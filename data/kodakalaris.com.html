Bringing digital transformation to business & life

 
Kodak Alaris is a global technology company delivering value to businesses and consumers all over the world through two core business units. Our data capture and processing solutions make businesses and government agencies run faster and smarter, while Kodak Moments provides consumers with innovative solutions to preserve and enjoy their most cherished memories.


 



 
Digital transformation starts here.  We combine our award-winning document scanners, data capture software, and professional services to deliver custom solutions that simplify business processes and accelerate results.
