Welcome to Matera, Texas’ Premier Facility Supply Experts. We have provided commercial janitorial supplies, cleaning equipment, and industrial packaging in Houston, TX, San Antonio, TX, and surrounding Texas communities since 1957. Our company serves the needs of businesses and institutions in all sectors with an extensive inventory of more than 3500 products and a fleet of trucks to provide efficient service throughout Texas.

Whether you need industrial cleaning chemicals, laundry equipment, safety equipment, or green products, we have a wide variety of products from many leading manufacturers. Our goal is to provide our customers with the largest selection of paper products, cleaning supplies, and shipping supplies to meet the specific needs of any business or organization.

Our company is a leader in our industry, which enables us to provide unparalleled access to the latest and best products and technologies. Matera serves the needs of hospitals, schools, universities, industrial facilities, contractors, and private businesses for janitorial supplies, industrial cleaning chemicals, and other industrial supplies. A growing number of businesses are concerned about industrial chemicals and implementing green cleaning programs for environmental and health reasons. We offer quality green products to help you achieve that goal.

Matera consistently receives high ratings for superior customer service from our customers. We can help improve efficiency, minimize the number of products required for thorough cleaning, and help your business maintain a professional image. Please browse our website to learn more about our services and find warewash equipment, skin care products, breakroom supplies, liner accessories, and much more. Contact us to arrange an appointment with one of our janitorial product experts.
