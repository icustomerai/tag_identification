Columbus Cardiology Associates

Columbus Cardiology Associates is a full-service, seven physician, cardiovascular medical practice located in Columbus, GA.  Columbus Cardiology Associates is a leader in cardiovascular medical services within the communities we serve. We accomplish this through an experienced, skilled and talented staff while using the most advanced procedures and technology.

Our physicians take pride in our commitment to providing compassionate, courteous and respectful care to our patients and their families. Thank you for your trust and confidence.

Columbus Cardiology Associates – Serving Columbus, GA, and the surrounding areas since 1990.

Leader in Cardiovascular Medicine

At Columbus Cardiology Associates, we are dedicated to preventing, diagnosing, and treating heart disease.

Heart disease is the leading cause of death in the United States and the number one killer of American women. Over one quarter of all deaths result from heart disease. To fight this disease, our cardiologists are trained in the latest cutting-edge cardiovascular treatments.

Some of our services include cardiovascular surgery, coronary intervention and minimally invasive therapies for the treatment of valvular and structural heart disease.
