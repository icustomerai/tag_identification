                      • Cedar Lake Oncology

                        • Cedar Lake Oncology has served patients on the Mississippi Gulf Coast for several years and is a department of Merit Health Biloxi. We are able to serve patients throughout the Mississippi Gulf Coast and surrounding areas in the comfort of their own community. Whether a patient needs a single treatment or 7 weeks of treatment, we believe that they should have a convenient and comfortable setting to turn to for their care.
