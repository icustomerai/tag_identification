Why Choose Seven Hills Hospital

Seven Hills Hospital brings compassionate care to adults, adolescents, and seniors suffering from mental health issues, alcoholism and drug abuse. Our patients receive individualized treatment from our highly-skilled therapists and board certified psychiatrists. At Seven Hills we set a high standard of care and addiction rehabilitation, and are proud to provide a relaxing therapeutic environment where holistic healing can begin.

Comprehensive Services for Every Need

We applaud your courage to take the first step to start your treatment. At Seven Hills Hospital, we understand that can be the hardest step and want to help you get on the right path to recovery. Even if you are just researching information for a loved one, thank you for considering Seven Hills during this difficult time.

At Seven Hills, we offer multiple treatment options for adolescents, adults, and seniors. Our board-certified psychiatrists and around-the-clock medical staff are here to ensure you receive the highest quality of care and all your needs are met. If you or a loved one are struggling withdepression,anxiety, analcohol addiction,drug abuse, or othermental healthrelated illnesses, please do not hesitate to call Seven Hills Hospital today.

We accept phone calls or walk-ins for free, no obligation assessments 24 hours a day, including mobile assessments. See how Seven Hills can help you regain a happy, healthy life.

Why Should I Get Treatment Near Me?

Seeking mental health & addiction treatment close to home provides the convenienceof getting to our treatment centerwithout theadded stressof travel and financial strain associated with travel costs. Instead, you or your loved one can receive the necessary treatment right away and family members can be a much more active part of the recovery process, a fundamental element for long-term success.
