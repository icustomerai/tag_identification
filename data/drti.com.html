Why DRT?

25+ Years of Mission Support

Since 1997, DRT has offered comprehensive SDR solutions to help you manage the deluge of spectrum data, understand relevance of patterns in the electromagnetic spectrum, and act on critical signal intelligence information pre-incident. These capabilities are trusted within the U.S. Department of Defense and Intelligence Community, enabling our country’s most important missions.

Latest DSP & FPGA Technology

We use the latest FPGA technology to provide low power processing engines to address the insatiable demand of higher-speed A/D’s and D/A’s and tougher Digital Signal Processing algorithms.

Advanced Signals Capabilities

Our DSP technology collects, transforms, filters, evaluates, enhances, compresses, and identifies digital signals in order to obtain a signal form that meets the mission critical needs of our technical partners.

Open Architecture

DRT standard interfaces increase quality and reduce integration and testing time. DRT’s new open architecture reduces lifecycle costs for HW and SW development, reuse, maintenance and system design.

SDK & API Available

We provide API and SDK’s to simplify third party integration and provide access to underlying DRT capabilities and features.

24/7 Partner Support

The DRT Support Team is available 24/7 to keep you in the fight. No matter where you are in the world, our dedicated team of patient task masters are standing by to assist.

Three Year Warranty

Reduced maintenance support costs, peace of mind for your organization, no unexpected repair costs, reduced system downtime / mission impact and reduced Total Cost of Ownership (TCO)

FREE Software Updates

DRT systems get more capable with time. Regular embedded software updates are provided that add additional capability and functionality to your hardware at no additional cost.

Flexible SWaP Options

We offer system options to fit various mission profiles and payload limitations.
