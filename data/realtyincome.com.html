PEOPLE & PLACES

Realty Income, The Monthly Dividend Company®, is an S&P *#SP#* company and member of the S&P 500 Dividend Aristocrats® index. For more than five decades, we have invested in people and places to deliver dependable monthly dividends that increase over time. The company is structured as a REIT, and its monthly dividends are supported by the cash flow from *#number-of-properties-rounded#* real estate properties primarily owned under long-term net lease agreements with commercial clients. To date, the company has declared *#consecutive-common-stock-monthly-dividends#* consecutive common stock monthly dividends throughout its *#operating-history-years#*-year operating history and increased the dividend *#number-of-quarterly-dividend-increases#* times since Realty Income's public listing in 1994 (NYSE: O).

AN INDUSTRY LEADER

Realty Income’s CEO, Sumit Roy, recently spoke with Jim Cramer on CNBC’s Mad Money. Sumit provided an update on the company’s operating performance and discussed our track record of dependable monthly dividends. Realty Income has a number of stakeholders, including investors, clients and employees. We are dedicated to making a positive impact on these constituencies, our communities and the commercial real estate industry as a whole.

A TRACK RECORD OF SUCCESS

Realty Income believes in transparency. We operate under the highest ethical standards and work tirelessly to provide long-term value to all stakeholders. Since our public listing in 1994, we have delivered compound average annual total shareholder return of *#compound-average-annual-total-shareholder-return#*, outperforming the US REIT sector and the S&P 500 during that timeframe.
