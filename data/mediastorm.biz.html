Today’s media landscape is not as much about media as it is about business results and open accountability. Media Storm has created an integrated solution of identifying and converting hand raisers, and driving greater ROI through accountable and active media. Our Data Science and Optimization model has replaced traditional planning, allowing for unmatched predictability.

Data Science and Optimization
JubaPlus, our proprietary predictive platform ties custom data science tools to ROI modeling. Through this, we are able to address three fundamental goals for each of our clients: Determine which audiences are most likely to take a desired action, detail a targeting scheme to activate potential audiences and create an efficient media mix to convert potential audiences and maximize their actions.

Insights, Strategy and Ideation
Our syndicated tools are built to work seamlessly with our clients’, and can be used to enhance existing data sets. Combined with our proprietary Data Science tools, they allow for a holistic view of strategic planning and execution. Our teams only deliver high-level, innovative ideas.

Analytics and Reporting
It’s not just about metrics: it’s about real business results. Having all data collected in one place helps our clients understand what decisions should be made nationally, regionally and locally to positively impact their business daily. All sales, transactions, media and brand metrics, as well as flow charts and creative assets are conveniently centralized in a secure, 24/7 online dashboard that can be customized to your needs.

Traditional Media Planning and Buying
Media Storm is known as the ‘Media Agency for the Media’ as many networks are both present and past clients, and we have key executives who hail from them. This gives us access to and knowledge of the space–knowledge that has been earned and cannot be bought.

Digital Media Planning and Buying
We remain nimble and relevant in an ever-changing digital landscape through proprietary best practices developed by our integrated teams and with external partners. This ensures we are always working seamlessly and efficiently while constantly staying up-to-date on the latest trends and certifications with key digital publishers.
