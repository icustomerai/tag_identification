    OUR WORK

    Hudson Yards
    Set atop rail yards, Hudson Yards is altering the Hudson River skyline with its rising skyscrapers. Tilcon provided a large majority of the aggregate stone to this iconic New York project.
    Central Park Tower
    Located steps from Central Park, this new architectural landmark will rise 1,550 feet as the tallest residential building in New York City. Tilcon was proud to have contributed to this landmark project.
    Mario M. Cuomo Bridge
    This twin cable-stayed bridge spans the Hudson River between Tarrytown and Nyack in New York. Tilcon provided stone for this project in addition to paving services on both spans.
    Goethals Bridge
    The bridge spans the Arthur Kill, linking Elizabeth, NJ, to Staten Island, NY. The 140-foot clearance of the bridge permits passage of deep-sea vessels. Tilcon was involved in the replacement which opened in 2017, and the western span which opened in 2018.
    One World Trade Center
    The tallest building in the United States, One World Trade Center is the main building of the rebuilt WTC complex. Tilcon was proud to have been a part of this national landmark.
    JFK Airport
    Modernizing runways for one of the world's premiere airportsTilcon provided aggregates so that airplanes like the Airbus A380 could be utilized.
    Newark Airport
    The redevelopment of Terminal A at Newark Liberty International Airport involved both the supply of stone and recycled concrete in addition to our paving construction services.
