      LEVER HOIST

      The Accolift Lever Chain Hoist, available in 3/4 Ton – 9 Ton, offers a portable, light weight and dependable working tool for one person operation in tight spaces. The lever hoist increases a workers efficiency in pulling, lifting, lowering, moving and skidding objects in a variety of applications including industrial, construction, maintenance, mining, ship building and utility. With an added free wheel operation to reduce working cycle time, this feature allows easy and fast load chain slack removal and adjustment for each unique lift.
