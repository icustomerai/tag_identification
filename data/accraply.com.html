Accraply: A Global Labeling Manufacturer


No matter how your product is packaged – bottles, cans, boxes, flexible packaging and more – Accraply has an industrial labeling machine for you. A Barry-Wehmiller Packaging company, Accraply is a leader in the design, manufacture, and support of automated label application systems as well as converting and finishing equipment for the shrink sleeve label and flexible packaging markets.  Accraply is a trusted name in durable, high-speed labeling machinery. We have an industrial labeling machine to meet virtually any application need.

For nearly half a century, we have provided labeling and shrink sleeve converting equipment, pressure sensitive labelers, roll-fed labeling systems and shrink sleeve labeling technology to our clients across the globe.

Learn why Accraply is a global leader for labeling machinery.

Internationally Known Labeling Equipment Brands

Accraply manufacturers the world's most trusted names in labeling. Our products include the Accraply line, Harland, Trine, Stanford, and Graham & Sleevit solutions.
Our machines service a variety of industries, including Food & Beverage, Pharmaceutical, Consumer Goods & more.
We are well equipped to guide our customers through all aspects of shrink sleeve labeling technology. 

International Labeling Products

Accraply is a global labeling machine manufacturer known for innovation.

As a leading labeling manufacturer, Accraply specializes in designing, building, and supporting pressure sensitive labeling machines, converting equipment for shrink sleeve labels and flexible packaging, shrink sleeve label applicators and tunnels, and roll-fed labeling application equipment.

Trust Accraply for the highest quality flexible packaging technology and custom-engineered labeling solutions.  

Partner with Accraply

We are more than a labeling machine manufacturer. Accraply offers leading engineering and consulting services, helping customers find the right equipment for their unique needs.
