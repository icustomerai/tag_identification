Dialysis Treatment Options

Select a treatment option below to find more information.

IN-CENTER HEMO

At the dialysis clinic, the patient’s blood is filtered and cleaned by a dialysis machine.

PERITONEAL DIALYSIS

The patient’s blood is filtered and cleaned through the lining of their abdomen, also known as the peritoneum, in the comfort of the patient's home.

HOME HEMODIALYSIS

The patient’s blood is filtered and cleaned by a dialysis machine in the comfort of the patient’s home.

TRANSPLANT

All of our patients are evaluated for transplant eligibility. If approved, the patient undergoes a medical procedure to receive a kidney from a donor.

Who We Are

Atlantic Dialysis Management Services

Atlantic Dialysis Management Services, LLC ("ADMS") was establish in 1998 with the purpose of providing new dialysis site development, mananging dialysis services, and organizing related business development activities. Our strategy is to maximize individual site results through consolidated activities. Central to the ADMS approach is the complete control of our clinical services by nephrologists.

Our Vision

We strive to be unsurpassed in the kidney community, to deliver better outcomes and to improve the quality of life for our patients and their families.

Our Experienced Leadership

Our leadership team has over three decades of experience in dialysis. We ensure every patient is afforded high-quality care regardless of their financial status.
