Coating Expertise with Over 30 Years of Experience

Deposition Sciences, Inc. (DSI®), a wholly owned subsidiary of Lockheed Martin, provides innovative, ultra-durable optical coating solutions for aerospace, commercial, and military customers. Applications include automotive, biomedical, astronomy, military, homeland security, aviation, spacecraft, imaging, telecommunications, scientific research, and industrial R&D. Our 144,000 sq. ft. state of the art facility is located in Santa Rosa, California.
