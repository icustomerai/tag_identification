Welcome

When your air travels bring you to the Monroe County Airport in Bloomington, Indiana, choose Cook Aviation—our area’s premier fixed base operator (FBO) for over the past 30 years. Our goal is to be your FBO of choice in Bloomington, offering professional line services and a quick turnaround time. In addition, we’re happy to act as your concierge, facilitating every aspect of your stay in Bloomington.  WiFi is available outside on the ramp area near building.

That means offering both Cadillac courtesy cars and an onsite automobile rental fleet. Booking hotel accommodations. Directing you to area attractions you’ll enjoy. Ultimately, doing anything and everything we can to help you get the most out of your visit.  NO RAMP FEES AT COOK AVIATION.
