    News and Events

    Ward Manufacturing Sponsors FCCB Cancer Classic Golf Tournament

    By Elizabeth Saintours • 31 May, 2023
    May 31, 2023 – It was a warm and sunny day on May 31, 2023, where Brian Nowak (Director of HR), Grant Cole (Industrial Engineer), Bill Thomas (Machine Shop, retired), and Pete Guidi (President/CEO) of Ward Manufacturing (“Ward”) ventured out to Corey Creek Golf Club in Mansfield, PA to attend the annual First Citizens Community Bank (“FCCB”) Cancer Classic golf tournament. This annual event, which started 24 years ago, helps raise funds and awareness for cancer research and the American Cancer Society. Ward Manufacturing (“Ward”) was proud to be a sponsor and enjoyed participating in this event. We look forward to next year! To learn more about First Citizens Community Bank, click here .

    Tioga County Cat Project Receives Donation from Ward Manufacturing

    By Elizabeth Saintours • 30 May, 2023
    At the end of May 2023, one of our favorite local organizations, Tioga County Cat Project, received a hefty $200 donation from Ward Manufacturing, LLC. This money was gathered over the course of the month during the Dress Down Thursday collection run by the main office employees at Ward Manufacturing, LLC. These funds will go a long way to help provide necessary care and materials to the stray, feral, and household cats in need in Tioga County, PA and surrounding areas. Thank you to all staff members for your generous contributions! To learn more about this organization, click here .

    HAVEN Receives Dress Down Thursday Donation from Ward Manufacturing

    By Elizabeth Saintours • 10 Apr, 2023
    In April 2023, HAVEN (Helping Abuse and Violence End Now) of Tioga County was the recipient of a $200 check from Ward Manufacturing (“Ward”). This money was collected every Thursday in the month of April in the main office at Ward Manufacturing where employees can “pay” to wear jeans to work. This money will go a long way helping those in need in unfortunate circumstances who come to HAVEN for safety and comfort. Thank you to everyone that has opened your hearts to those in need. Your generosity is greatly appreciated! To learn more about this organization and how you can help, click here .
