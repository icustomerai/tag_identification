Welcome to Sutherlin Optical Lab!

Sutherlin Optical is a state-of-the-art wholesale optical laboratory focused on providing customers with the highest quality eyewear. We provide premium lens products backed by a full-service, cutting-edge facility. Our lab services are complemented by a world-class customer service team providing a personal touch to your orders.

Our experienced sales team is dedicated to helping grow your practice through extensive product training, creative marketing and a strong business acumen. We look forward to becoming a trusted partner in your success.
