Consumer Confidence Report

Each year, the EPA requires water systems to report the quality of their water to their customers. The report, also known as the drinking water quality report, gives customers an in-depth analysis of water quality and testing results for the year. The most current Consumer Confidence Reports will be issued each year by July 1st, but customers may also review available reports for previous years.
