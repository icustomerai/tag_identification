Medical Rehabilitation to Rebuild Your Life

For over 75 years, Kessler Institute has been a leader in rehabilitation medicine with its unique combination of innovative treatment and cutting-edge technologies that help individuals like you rebuild your life following injury or illness.

We treat diagnoses like spinal cord and brain injury, stroke, heart issues, cancer and more. Our physician-led, interdisciplinary care teams develop individualized, evidence-based treatment plans to return patients to their daily activities and community.

We're ranked No. 4 in the nation and are proud to be the highest ranking rehab hospital in the New Jersey-New York metro area.
