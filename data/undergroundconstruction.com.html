ABOUT US

Underground Construction Co., Inc. has provided efficient gas, power, airport fueling and telecom construction solutions for over 87 years. Since laying our first pipe in 1936, we have successfully completed thousands of governmental, commercial and private sector construction projects throughout the United States. As one of the oldest and most respected contractors in the West, we continue to serve our industries with the highest safety, quality and customer satisfaction.
