In Memoriam: Dean Rostovsky

 
It is with great sadness that Clarion Partners shares the passing of our partner and senior leader, Dean Rostovsky.
Dean had a long and impactful career at Clarion, starting in our New York office both pre- and post-business school. He eventually relocated to Los Angeles where he became a partner of the Firm, co-headed our Acquisitions Group, and helped shape the spirit and culture of that office.
Dean was a remarkable person, a devoted father and husband, and a terrific friend. He was smart, kind, and thoughtful and served as a great mentor to many. Dean was loved and respected, both at Clarion and in the marketplace.
He will be very sorely missed by all.

Why Clarion

Building a more sustainable world

We, in concert with our partners and clients, are committed to owning and operating buildings that seek to provide our tenants with healthy and efficient spaces that add value to surrounding communities.
