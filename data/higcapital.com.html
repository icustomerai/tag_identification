About H.I.G. Capital

H.I.G. Capital is a leading global alternative investment firm with $56 billion* of equity capital under management. For over 30 years, the H.I.G. platform has created long-term value across a range of strategies including private equity, growth equity, direct lending, special situations credit, real estate, infrastructure, and biohealth.
