What we do

We meet the right people where they are through the power of Local TV, building a bridge between brand and location-based marketing, from streaming to broadcast.

Streaming

Combining TV advertising with digital targeting, we reach local consumers watching the most premium streaming TV content. Guaranteed access to high-value ad impressions across 150+ popular streaming TV publishers before programmatic channels.

  • Reach valuable audiences adopting new forms of viewing
  • Fraud free, brand safe environment for advertisers with full control over frequency, recency, and competitive separation
  • Precise targeting & real attribution

Broadcast

Amplify your reach and drive action in specific locations with exclusive access to 300+ broadcast TV stations. We help local broadcast TV advertising buyers achieve their most important business outcomes.

  • 250+ highly trained, long tenured sales professionals aligned with key local markets across the U.S.
  • Ability to customize messaging by individual market and uniqueness of community
  • Exclusive access to a comprehensive collection of data resources, audience insights, and analytics

Political

All politics are local and every voter impression matters. Leverage our decades of expertise in market conditions, spending, and voter cycles to connect with highly engaged voters at scale across premium local video.

  • Access local broadcast and streaming TV inventory in all congressional and legislative districts
  • Find and target engaged voters using geo-location, demographic, behavioral, and voter status data
  • Ensure all political ad campaigns are delivered in full
