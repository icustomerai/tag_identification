What is Locum Tenens?

Locum tenens is Latin for "one holding a place." In healthcare, it refers to the practice of hiring temporary healthcare professionals to fill in for absent or unavailable practitioners. These skilled providers ensure uninterrupted patient care, bridge staffing gaps, and maintain the efficiency of healthcare operations.
