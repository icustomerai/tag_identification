Frequently Asked Questions

Our pest control services at HomeTeam Pest Defense follow our 6-Point Advantage service protocol:

  • Inspect + identify

  • Remove cobwebs & wasps’ nests

  • Apply pest control

  • Treat entry points

  • Protect the perimeter

  • Review & report

For homes that are equipped with Taexx, we utilize a networked home system that runs throughout designated walls of your home to help keep treatment materials effective for a longer period. With a 100% service guarantee, our pest management services also come with inspection and re-servicing at no additional cost if customers have pest concerns in between regular services.

Signs of pest infestation include droppings, property damage, foul odour, visible pests, nests or burrows, chewing or scratching sounds, and increased pest activity. It's crucial to address infestations as soon as possible to prevent further damage and potential health hazards. For more information on our other services, like termite treatment and mosquito control services, call us at (877) 461-7378.

The frequency of pest control treatment varies depending on factors such as the type of pest, the level of infestation, and the environment. Generally, it is advisable to have pest control done bi-monthly or quarterly as a preventive measure, particularly for common pests like ants, spiders, and roaches. 

Seeking expert advice from a pest control company like HomeTeam Pest Defense is recommended to assess your unique situation and recommend a suitable treatment plan. Contact us today to start protecting your home.

Typically, a standard treatment for a low to moderate pest infestation in an average-sized home takes between 30 minutes and 90 minutes. More severe infestations or larger properties may require additional time for treatment. In some cases, multiple visits or follow-up treatments are needed to ensure the pests have been completely eliminated.
