                        Santa Rosa Medical Center

                        Santa Rosa Medical Center is a full-service 129-bed hospital facility, including emergency room services. Located in Milton, FL, the hospital is easily reached from all points in Santa Rosa County. With nearly 650 associates and volunteers and 220 physicians on staff, Santa Rosa Medical Center is well equipped to meet all of your healthcare needs.

                        Santa Rosa Medical Group

                        Santa Rosa Medical Group is a multi-specialty physician practice affiliated with Santa Rosa Medical Center. With clinics located in Milton, Pace and Navarre, Florida, our providers have a focus on both treatment and prevention. Our goal is to provide professional and personalized medical care streamlined to meet the medical needs of each patient.
