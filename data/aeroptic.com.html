WHO WE ARE

We started Aeroptic began a simple notion: leverage our decades worth of global experience with the most advanced, government-grade aerial remote sensing technology, and we could dramatically improve upon the traditional aerial imagery methods of responding to natural disasters, monitoring our precious natural resources, and monitoring infrastructure development.  From enhancing precision agriculture efforts in the Corn Belt to improving crop yields in emerging nations. Aeroptic is focused on helping the world unlock the power of aerial imagery in geospatial information systems.
