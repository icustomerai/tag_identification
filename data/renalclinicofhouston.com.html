About Us

Renal Clinic of Houston, with offices in Memorial City in Houston, Katy, and North Cypress, Texas, specializes in caring for patients who have kidney disease, high blood pressure, or need dialysis for end-stage renal disease. The team of board-certified physicians offers today's most advanced nephrology treatments, delivering the highest quality of care with compassion and respect for each person's unique needs.

As renal specialists, the team treats the full scope of kidney diseases, as well as a wide range of conditions that either cause or arise from damaged kidneys. They frequently diagnose and treat patients with chronic kidney disease, kidney stones, essential (primary) hypertension, and secondary hypertension.

Patients struggling with anemia due to chronic kidney disease, electrolyte disorders, and acid-base disorders receive exceptional care, customized to ease their symptoms and protect the health of the kidneys.

The Renal Clinic of Houston’s extensive services include the diagnosis and treatment of complex kidney conditions, including lupus nephritis, cystic kidney disease, tubulointerstitial disease, and obstructive nephropathy. These conditions get the expert, individualized treatment they require from the experienced team.

Advanced kidney disease demands comprehensive and diligent medical care. The team provides all aspects of your treatment, from dialysis to post-kidney transplant follow-up.

People with questions or who need care for a kidney-related condition can schedule an appointment by calling the nearest office or booking an appointment online today. The Renal Clinic of Houston treats everyone from the age of 18 and older.

A team of healthcare professionals dedicated to providing compassionate, superior quality, and cost-effective care to patients with Kidney Diseases, Hypertension, and Dialysis.
