Red Meat

Dairy Solutions

Eagles advanced inspection systems meet 3-A Sanitary Standards and can detect foreign contaminants in a multitude of dairy products, from raw cheese blocks to packaged yogurts. Our hygienically constructed equipment built to sanitary design can also ensure proper portioning, placement of product, weight and package integrity to deliver the safest, highest quality products.

Fruit & Vegetable Solutions

Fruit & Vegetable Solutions

Fruit and vegetable manufacturers face many inspection challenges. Dangerous contaminants can be introduced at any time, from harvesting through to the processing stage. Whether its detecting glass fragments in glass jars of jelly, plastic in a frozen bag of corn or metal in the bottom of a pickled vegetable jar, Eagle x-ray systems provide outstanding detection of foreign body contaminants. Our systems use advanced dual energy technology to detect contaminants in dense foods, such as dried bulk fruit, and generates easy-to-read x-ray images for enhanced image analysis. Eagle inspection systems can also simultaneously check fill level, verify weight and ensure packaging integrity like making sure a metal lid is placed correctly.
