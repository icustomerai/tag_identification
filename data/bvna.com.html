    Environmental & IH Laboratories
    Do you suspect soil, water, or air contamination; or need to comply with an environmental guideline? Customers concerned with potential soil, water, and air contaminants call on the industry leader, Bureau Veritas. With our Environmental & IH Laboratories, we provide a wide range of scientific support and reliable analytical solutions. Our complementary Green Line Services and Solutions dedicated to sustainability can also help customers comply with environmental regulations and standards to ensure they are operating sustainably and meeting environmental objectives for the future. We leverage our decades of local and international project experience to help you reduce your risk and meet or exceed challenges of quality, health and safety, environmental, and social responsibility.

Our Green Line of Services and Solutions dedicated to sustainability is already helping many of our Oil & Gas clients implement, measure, and achieve their unique sustainability objectives. We understand how critical it is to meet these environmental standards of the future, and our clients can be confident that the tools and expertise we provide in Oil & Gas will lead to transparency and brand protection.

    Management Systems Certification & Training
    Businesses today face a wide range of risks and opportunities. Management system certification enables companies to improve organizational performance and protect reputation. Modern management systems are designed to be flexible and built to the organization’s specific needs. In this context, certification becomes a valuable tool to target continuous improvement in a specific area as well as mitigate risk across the organization. We understand that operating sustainably is critical, too. With our GreenLine Services and Solutions designed to complement our certification capabilities; we help our clients implement, measure, and achieve their unique environmental objectives.
