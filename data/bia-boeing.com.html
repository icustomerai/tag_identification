 The Boeing Company

discover Boeing

The parent organization of BI&A, The Boeing Company, is a global leader in aerospace technology, aircraft development, and defense and weapons systems that employs over 140,000 diverse individuals in more than 65 countries.

Boeing’s defense technologies are shaping the future of national security for the U.S. government and over 150 allied government customers. The company’s extensive line of innovative products provides defense clients with the hardware needed to complete the mission.

At the heart of developing groundbreaking aerospace technology is a vast team of empowered Boeing employees. A commitment to investing in employee success, its workforce’s communities around the globe, and career-focused veteran programs allows Boeing to maintain its status as an industry leader.

Visit the Boeing media room for the latest aerospace updates.
