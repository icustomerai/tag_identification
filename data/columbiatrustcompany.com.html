What's your vision for the future?

Homemattw2023-03-08T13:03:46-08:00

Columbia Trust Company has been serving Northwest families and non-profits since 1966

With over 50 years of experience we understand the importance of high touch, personalized, flexible service. It’s that understanding, combined with our expertise, that allows us to provide a full range of trust services and investment solutions, including non-traditional assets and business ventures.

ARE YOU AN INDIVIDUAL OR FAMILY?

Legacy is about how you, and only you, want to be remembered. This is how Columbia Trust Company thinks about trust services—an opportunity to honor your unique legacy, whatever that might be. We will work hard to understand you, your family and the life you’ve built so that your vision for the future can be realized.

To learn more about how Columbia Trust Company can provide you with trust or investment management services tailored to your family’s unique needs, drop us a note.

ARE YOU AN ESTATE PLANNER?

Columbia Trust Company operates out of five offices located in Washington, Oregon and Idaho, making us a great choice for geographically distributed families. Our seasoned team of professionals manages a full spectrum of sophisticated investment solutions, ranging from securities to family businesses. And clients and estate planners alike appreciate the fact that they can pick up the phone and get an answer almost immediately.

To determine whether we’re the right choice for your client, drop us a note.

ARE YOU A FOUNDATION OR A NON-PROFIT?

While you need an experienced professional investment advisor who can effectively manage your endowment’s resources, you also want to know that those assets are being managed in alignment with your organization’s values. As a fiduciary, Columbia Trust Company takes care to intimately understand your foundation’s mission so we can always act in your best interest. We combine this high-touch approach with sophisticated investment management solutions, helping non-profit endowments fulfill their promises long into the future.

To learn more about how Columbia Trust Company can work in alignment with your values to effectively manage your endowment, drop us a note.
