MISSION

United States Cold Storage is driven to advance, innovate, and serve companies seeking the best service, facilities, and logistics in the cold chain industry.

We put our belief of making the world a better place into practice -- throughout 2020, our ColdCrew donated $250,000, volunteered 500 hours, and served 60 partner organizations.
To learn more, we invite you to read our CSR 2020 Report.
