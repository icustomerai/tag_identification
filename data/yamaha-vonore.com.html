Yamaha Jet Boat Manufacturing

Yamaha Jet Boat Manufacturing is located in Vonore, Tennessee (Monroe County) on the Tellico Lake at the Tellico West Industrial Park about 45 minutes south of Knoxville. We currently employ 700 people.

We offer very competitive pay and a great benefits package that includes, but is not limited to, paid holidays (including MLK day and Presidents Day), STD/LTD and life insurance (paid by the company), medical, dental and vision insurance with very affordable weekly rates to the employee, 401K match of 50 cents/dollar plus profit share , and an employee purchase program on Yamaha products.

Available Positions

Below is a list of positions we are always hiring for however at Yamaha Jet Boats we encourage you to apply for any desired position to which you feel would be a great position for you from Management to Engineering and more! Click the Apply Today to see if you are a great fit at Yamaha Vonore.

ASSEMBLY TEAM MEMBER

This position will work as part of a team having responsibility for assembling a component of a product. Team assemblers can perform all tasks conducted by the team in the assembly process and rotate through all or most of them rather than being assigned to a specific task on a permanent basis.

PI TEAM MEMBER

This position will work as part of a team having responsibility for repairing gel-coat and fiberglass defects on FRP parts. Team members can perform all tasks including but not limited to sanding, buffing and repair work on the FRP parts.

LAMINATION TEAM MEMBER

This position will work as part of a team having responsibility for laminating layers of fiberglass on molds to form boat decks and hulls. Team members can perform all tasks in the lamination process and rotate through all or most of them rather than being assigned to a specific task on a permanent basis.

GEL - COAT TEAM MEMBER

This position will work as part of a team having responsibility for applying gel-coat in the manufacturing process. Team members can perform all tasks including but not limited to maintaining all spray equipment, hand spraying, operation of robot spray, ensuring the quality of the gel-coat process.
