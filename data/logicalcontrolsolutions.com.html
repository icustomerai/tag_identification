We Make Buildings Better

Logical Control Solutions is a customer focused company dedicated to providing the highest quality of energy management solutions in the industry. Our intuitive system provides the tools and resources to analyze your building data in a simple and effective manner to improve and maximize building efficiency.

 

Since 1995, our mission has been to provide exceptional value to our customers across numerous industries, both in the public and private sectors, throughout the Finger Lakes, and Western and Southern Tier regions of New York.

 

Our knowledgeable and dedicated team will demonstrate how we can integrate to any new or existing building automation system without compromising occupancy comfort. Our goal is to help you reduce energy costs while maintaining a reliable and sustainable building environment.
