About Us

A FAMILY OF INSURANCE LEADERS

National Interstate, Vanliner and Great American Insurance Group are united to give producers and insureds an unmatched level of personalized service, expertise and financial assurance in commercial transportation and other specialty industries. We are rated “A+” (Superior) by A.M. Best. 

National Interstate Insurance Company offers an array of insurance products tailored to commercial transportation and adjacent industries.

Vanliner Insurance Company is a leading insurance provider for moving and storage and last mile delivery industries.

Customers have trusted Great American Insurance Group’s niche expertise to protect what matters most to them.
