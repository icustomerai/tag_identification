Building Life's moments

BMD is a building solutions company. For more than 75 years, BMD has been supplying lumber yards, dealers, home centers and hardware stores with the finest quality building products. An employee-owned wholesale distributor, BMD supports and grows our partners by providing unparalleled customer service, and our experienced and talented workforce makes BMD an innovative leader in our industry today.
