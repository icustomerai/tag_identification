The Global Expert in Banknote Security 

Crane Currency, a Crane NXT company, is a fully integrated supplier of secure, durable and well-designed banknotes for central banks all over the world. A pioneer in advanced micro-optics technology, Crane Currency provides a wide range of engaging visual effects in features that increase the level of security and public trust in banknotes. 

The intelligent evolution house note

Introducing RAPID® Vision

Crane Currency presents RAPID Vision, a new and innovative micro-optic security thread for banknotes that brings high-contrast color movement to a new level.

Public First 2023

Central Bank of The Bahamas Recognized with Public First Award

This year’s recipient was recognized for its family of banknotes incorporating novel combinations of technology, sourced from multiple and competing suppliers. On hand to accept the award on behalf of the Central Bank was Deputy Governor Derek Rolle.

Ukraine Commemorative

NBU Presents Commemorative Banknote

The National Bank of Ukraine (NBU) has presented a new commemorative banknote dedicated to the struggle of Ukraine against the Russian invasion. Crane Currency is proud to have been invited to work with the Bank to create a unique security feature for the new banknote.
