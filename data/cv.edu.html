CVCC offers a variety of scholarship options for high school students interested in starting their academic journey at a two-year school. Categories include Academic, Ambassador, Career Technical, Fine Arts, Leadership, Mascot, and Performing Arts.

The scholarships cover tuition and fees and are available to students pursuing a variety of academic programs. Whether the objective is to obtain an associate degree or technical certification, CVCC has a scholarship that can help you achieve your educational goals.

From a Pirate

Antonio Davis, CVCC Alumnus

I was homeless. Me and my mom were struggling to find a place to stay and God placed me here at CVCC in the welding program. I didn’t have any money, so it was very important for me to be able to get the scholarship. Now, I’m in an internship at Ashley Steel company and it’s a game changer for me and my mom. I’m helping her out and getting a lot of experience in welding. I’m basically able to take the knowledge that I got from CVCC and apply it. What really motivates me to stay positive in life and go through all the trials and tribulations is God. He gave me peace that passes all understanding. He gave me courage to continue to keep pushing. At the end of the day, we’re all going to go through a lot in life. It’s all in how you handle it. For me, having this testimony, will inspire others and hopefully let them know that whatever you’re going through, God is able. He’s able to do abundantly and exceedingly in your life if you just trust Him and have faith. And, that’s exactly what I have learned to do.
