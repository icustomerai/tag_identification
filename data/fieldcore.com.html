Top Talent

Collaboration, creativity, ingenuity and profound field experience make FieldCore employees stand out. Our brightest minds and rigorously trained professionals work diligently to deliver reliable outcomes to our customers in all corners of the world.

Finely Tuned Processes

We can connect the dots better than anyone else, optimizing all systems to increase the overall performance of a plant. Because of our robust experience, first-hand knowledge of GE technology and actionable insights from performance data, we have a perspective no other provider can match.

Total Plant Capabilities

We aim to deliver safe, reliable and fast services. The expertise of our teams covers all capability areas for GE’s Fleet360 portfolio, from commissioning and installation to repairs, maintenance and upgrades.

Our Expertise Is Your Advantage

Detailed Knowledge, Bigger Gains

Building off decades of combined experience, the FieldCore team has deep knowledge of GE and customers’ technology. We are the direct connection to GE OEM expertise, advanced technologies and data-driven insights.

Simpler Processes, Faster Results

Things go better when everyone’s on the same page. FieldCore follows a common set of best practices, processes and performance expectations to deliver consistent results for our customers.

Consistent Experiences, Everywhere

By partnering with multiple GE businesses, we continually improve communication, collaboration and standardization for a consistent and optimized field service experience.
