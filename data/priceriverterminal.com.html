YOUR GATEWAY TO NEW ENERGY MARKETS

Optionality, Flexibility, Efficiency

These are attributes of effective businesses and Price River Terminal is perfectly positioned to provide the optionality, flexibility and efficiency to your crude oil operation that will enhance your company’s bottom line.

Oil Production in the US is soaring and the oil fields of the Uinta Basin in Eastern Utah are no exception.  PRT’s crude-by-rail facility is your gateway to new markets for Eastern Utah and Uinta crude.  With service access to both the Union Pacific and Burlington Northern Santa Fe railroads, PRT unlocks previously unavailable markets providing access to refineries throughout the country.  Our close proximity to the oil fields allows for more efficient truck utilization.  PRT’s efficient unit train transloading operation also allow us to ship greater quantities to refineries with faster transit times at lower costs.

Let PRT, the only energy transloading facility in eastern Utah, help you unlock these new markets and provide you with the optionality, flexibility, and flow assurance you need to manage your business.
