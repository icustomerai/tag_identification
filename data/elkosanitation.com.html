About Elko Sanitation

Elko Sanitation is a garbage and recycling company well-known for its energy-conscious, reliable and cost-effective services in Nevada's Elko County. When you trust Elko Sanitation, you not only benefit from the consistency and structure of a major nationwide waste management company, you also get the “mom & pop” feel from the waste management and recycling professionals that live, work, and raise their families in our area.

A Locally Run, Locally Serviced Business

When you put your trust in Elko Sanitation, you’re putting your trust in neighbors who care about our area just as much as you do. We believe that Elko garbage collection and recycling should be done by professionals living and working right here in the community.

In fact, each local customer is assigned a personal Elko Sanitation rep to ensure the utmost in customized, locally-minded service.
