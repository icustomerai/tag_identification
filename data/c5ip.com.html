Meet Core5

Core5 Industrial Partners is a full-service real estate property company with expertise in development and acquisition of Class A inventory and build-to-suit industrial facilities. We settle for nothing less than top-shelf delivery on every assignment. In fact, our commitment to quality is so strong, we named the company for the five core principles we believe are the foundation of any relationship.

  1. We believe real estate is a relationship business.
  2. We believe capable, dedicated employees are the key to our success.
  3. We believe in investing for long-term value.
  4. We believe in excellence to the highest standards.
  5. We always keep our word and follow through on our commitments.
