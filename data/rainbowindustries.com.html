Welcome to our website. We hope you will find the item you need.


For those of you who don't already know us, Rainbow Industries, Inc. is an American company that has been manufacturing tarps and covers in Springfield, Ohio since 1946.

We manufacture covers and tarps in a variety of sizes, colors and shapes to fit the needs of commercial and agricultural industries. With every call we receive, our Customer Service Department uses their years of experience to listen to the customer so a flexible solution can be provided.

Rainbow Industries, Inc. strives to make your life easier. If, after browsing our website, you have not found what you need, we ask that you phone us. We are a custom tarp manufacturer whose catalog is filled with what our customers order most often. Just say "Rainbow", we've got you covered!
