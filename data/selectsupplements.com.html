Welcome to Select Supplements, Inc.

 

Select Supplements, Inc. (SSI) is a privately owned nutritional supplement manufacturer. We pursue advancements and efficiencies in composition and formulation, manufacturing, and quality assurance certification of nutraceuticals and dietary supplements. Our customers are the leading global contract and private label brands.

 

SSI is dedicated to being more than a vendor. By cultivating long-term relationships, we focus our resources - scientific, professional and technical -- on meeting and exceeding our customers' needs for a true partner-supplier relationship.
