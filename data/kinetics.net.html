KINETICS: The most versatile solution provider for critical processes.

All from one source – globally.

KINETICS is a multi-disciplinary company with a truly global footprint that offers all steps of the value chain for critical processes worldwide from a single source. Thanks to our experience of almost fifty years, we have unique know-how of all relevant systems and products, from engineering to facility management.

 

With engineering, production and service capacities on three continents, our more than 2,000 employees around the world provide you with state-of-the-art solutions of the highest quality. 

Chemistry in Motion

Our global team focuses on delivering optimized engineering, equipment and systems where and when they are needed. Our versatility sets KINETICS apart – we create complex processing solutions distinguished by their value and return on investment. This is what we call chemistry in motion. 

Our Market Sectors

Semiconductors

As a reliable partner, we develop, manufacture and install high-purity systems and equipment for chemicals, slurries, gases and ultrapure water.

Semiconductors

Biopharm

From research laboratories to complex pharmaceutical production plants, we plan, build and install plants and process modules.

Labs & Research Institutes

Other high-tech industries

KINETICS Innovation: No-Touch-HMI with Augmented Reality

With our new generation of no-touch HMIs, touchscreens can not only be operated touch-free, they also provide audio-visual feedback in real time. 

KINETICS joins the Semiconductor Climate Consortium

KINETICS is a proud new member of the Semiconductor Climate Consortium (SCC), a renowned association that helps member companies reduce greenhouse gas emissions in industry and other parts of the value chain. The membership underlines our commitment to a cleaner and greener environment.

Fully Integrated Process Solutions

Whether new construction or retrofitting, KINETICS is your reliable partner for plant construction - worldwide. From consulting to production to facility management, you get everything from us from a single source.
