Pegasus

Pegasus is a fully integrated, boutique commercial real estate investment advisory firm providing unparalleled investment sales brokerage, capital markets, asset management and leasing services in connection with high quality, primarily net leased retail property throughout the United States.

Founded as a family office in 1988 and headquartered in Los Angeles, the Pegasus team has grown organically and steadily throughout the real estate market cycles of the last quarter century. Pegasus applies its highly specialized advisory platform to custom tailor real estate investment solutions for its diverse client base of high net worth individuals, family offices and institutional real estate investment firms. The result is unmatched execution capability.
