Hello Spring

A collection inspired by iconic summer days in Malibu. This season is all about maintaining a sense of comfort, while still taking time to make a statement. Our Spring/Summer 2023 collection transports you to those easy summer days, focusing on being present in the moment, but also allowing yourself to thrive. Whether you're hitting up music festivals, spending the day at the beach or just grabbing lunch with friends - we're embracing a sense of normalcy and allowing ourselves to exist in the now. Incorporating our tried and true neutral colorways, with some electric pops of color, our newest collection is meant to be loved by you throughout the season. Fashion forward details, like puffy braids, chunky platforms and pops of print, make these shoes ideal for the woman who loves the ease of summertime, but who also prioritizes style and comfort. Blowfish Malibu shoes are comfortable on your feet, but even better for your soul.
