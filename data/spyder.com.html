Athlete Highlight

Bobby Brown

A true pioneer in the world of freeskiing. From his gold medal never-been-done's at X Games Big Air to jaw dropping backcountry jump sessions, there isn't an aspect of the sport that Bobby hasn't dominated.

Sabrina Simader

The first female skier to ever represent Kenya. Sabrina has pushed herself since first seeing snow in Austria as a child to now standing atop the podium.

Tim Durtschi

Born in Alaska and raised in the PNW, Tim discovered the freedom of the mountain and the ability to make a terrain park out of natural features.. Continually a primary feature in Teton Gravity Research videos Tim can be found most days hot lapping Corbett's in Jackson.

River Radamus

Navigating the vast terrain of Vail Ski Resort taught River to embrace speed at a young age. These days River doesn't hide his thirst for speed and style on the World Cup Circuit. His ever changing hair colors and styles tend to put his personality front and center.
