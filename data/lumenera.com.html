New INFINITY8 Microscope Camera Series
Teledyne Lumenera’s INFINITY8 Microscope Camera Series range in resolution from 2MP to 20MP and use the latest in advanced CMOS sensor technology. These new microscope cameras address the needs of fluorescence imaging, as well as produce vivid color images for use in education and lab settings.
