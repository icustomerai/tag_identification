    Resolve Anxiety
    and
    Depression

     

    Anxiety and depression consume us in terrible ways and make a huge influence on our daily life in almost every way. Anxiety and depression  often work together making it even more difficult getting out of the cycle of stress and hopelessness, sometimes taking one's thoughts to very dark places.

    Families can work together to escape this cycle by identifying the patterns in their life that maintain their condition. With help and guidance they can start taking steps to modify their pattern of behavior and reduce anxiety and depression. Sometimes medication is necessary, but family therapy can help.

    Build Marital Happiness

     

    Marriage is so common and yet so complex. Divorce is becoming more and more common. We know from science that therapy techniques can many times save a marriage in trouble. For example, It is important to avoid four traps that prevent understanding and impede communication. Frank will teach you how to avoid these traps. Poor communication and turning away from each other prevents marital satisfaction. Discover the science of how people talk and develop a healthy form of communication.

    Learn how to resolve problems in your relationship quickly and effectively. You'll create a heightened sense of understanding and the skill to build marital happiness.  

     

    Executive Coaching

    Knowing yourself and your unique style of leadership is so important. We hear it all the time, "know yourself". Through coaching and testing we can identify and enhance your instinctual style of leadership. 

    Learn how to increase your productivity and empower yourself with an arsenal of tools and tactics to combat any stress that comes in your way in your position as an entrepreneur or leader. Being strong and confident in the execution of company goals can be enhanced with special consultation and Executive Coaching.

    Invest in Yourself

    You have taken the first step.

    Now, it's time we make it better.

     

    Life can be so hard on us. We can feel stuck, confused and overwhelmed. We tend to be creatures of habit. And many times our habits transform into troublesome patterns we cannot stop or even recognize.

    Communication is a platform for the relationship. It can either drive us apart or lead to resolving problems. Talking to each other to solve conflict on our own often leads to conflict. Sometimes we have no choice but to avoid one another and stop communicating altogether.

    There is no problem too large or too small to be solved — be it marital conflicts, extra-marital affairs, divorce, porn, addiction or those difficult challenges brought on by teenagers.
