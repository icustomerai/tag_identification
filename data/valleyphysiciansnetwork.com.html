You deserve a doctor who puts you first

Having a doctor who listens and understands you means you get more personalized care. The doctors at Optum Care Network–Valley Physicians, formerly Valley Physicians Network (VPN), get to know you and recommend care based on your health needs.

Optum and its staff are committed to the health, needs and peace of mind of patients in southwest Riverside County. Our focus is to keep you healthy and informed about your care.

At Optum, you’re at the center of a caring team led by your primary care doctor. Everyone works together to make sure you get the right care at the right time, including specialty care, urgent care and lab services.
