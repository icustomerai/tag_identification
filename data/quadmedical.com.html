A Healthy Investment For Your Business and Your Employees

Partnering with you to provide health care for your employees at the workplace — because when they're healthy, your business is too.
  
 

What We Offer

  • Worksite Clinics

    We will work with you to customize your health center for your unique employee population. From the size of the clinic and its location — be it onsite at your workplace or as a standalone center nearby — to add-on services that complement our primary care offering, we design this with your employees in mind. To keep them well, which means a happier, healthier workforce, improved productivity and reduced health care costs. 


      

     

    Explore Our Clinics
  • Onsite Health Services

    With primary care as our foundation, we offer a range of additional services that can be added to the offering to help you, and your employees, get the most out of your investment. From virtual health, occupational health and physical therapy services to chronic condition management, radiology, dental, pharmacy and more.

  • Employee Wellness

    Helping your employees stay well is one of the best ways to maximize your investment and minimize employee downtime. That’s why we also offer wellness programs, including biometrics, coaching, nutrition, fitness, incentive program management and more.

Who We Are

Aggy Lun, MD Physician

"As a physician at QuadMed, I am on the front line providing the medical care that ensures the well-being of our patients. Together with a talented and dedicated team that includes nursing staff and educators, laboratory and pharmacy personnel, physical therapists and support staff, we provide whole-person care that spans the continuum from preventative well-care to chronic condition management of more complex issues. With our patient-centered approach to care, I have the support, tools, and time to coordinate the delivery of comprehensive care to our patients that optimizes their health, safety, and productivity in all aspects of their lives."

Andrew Rumler, PT, DPT Physical Therapist

"As a physical therapist, my job is to maximize function and movement for my patients. I help decrease pain and increase strength, and it all revolves about the patient’s needs and personal goals.  All of our physical therapists use various treatment techniques, and we are fortunate to collaborate closely with our primary medical providers to develop individualized treatment plans. My goal is to be our patients' most cherished benefit."

Melissa McMahon Senior Marketing Strategist

"As a specialist in health care marketing, my role is to help your health center be as successful as possible by communicating effectively to your employee population about all the amazing benefits we have to offer.  From developing mailers announcing a new service or provider, to creating full campaigns around flu immunizations or children's physicals, our team develops strategic marketing plans tailored to your needs -- operating as an extension of you!"

Jim Goard Senior Client Operations Manager

"I ensure our clinic and client teams are getting the full power of QuadMed’s tools and capabilities. Whether it is tools clinicians use for clinical decision-making or benefit plan administrators deciding on the next period of open enrollment. As the key consultant to our clients’ on-site health care benefit, I enjoy building a long term strategy that supports the key reasons why the clinics were introduced originally. One eye on today, the other on tomorrow."

Kathryn Hartman Director of Human Resources

"My team and I serve our workforce by securing exceptional talent, encouraging growth and development, fostering engagement, and advancing QuadMed’s culture and values within the organization!  We take care of our workforce so they can continue to focus on delivering exceptional patient care in our health and wellness centers."

Dionne Green Talent Acquisition Recruiter

"As a recruiter my role is to ensure the success of QuadMed by hiring the best talent to achieve the best business results. I have the pleasure of being the “face” of the organization as I am often the first interaction that applicants have with the organization. It is incredibly rewarding to know that I am making a tangible difference in someone’s career as well as the future of the company’s growth. Every time I walk into a QuadMed facility, it brings me joy to know that I played a part in the way that we deliver health care."

Tim Schuyler Health Center Manager

"A positive health center visit experience for all patients is our expectation. From check in to check out, your interaction with the health care team, various programs and services are my responsibility. As a Health Center Manager, making sure that adequate staffing, resources and supplies are available is vitally important. I am responsible for the care and maintenance of the facility and medical equipment. From personnel management to customer service, I am there to support the daily operations of the health center."

Jill Michalski, CPA Senior Manager, Financial Planning & Analysis

“I know that when employers select onsite health care they are making an important financial investment in the health and well-being of their workforce. My job is to understand our clients’ financial priorities and guide return on investment metrics and reporting as part of the overall success of each health center. This process often begins with feasibility assessments and claims analysis so we can customize services to deliver the best outcomes – financially and clinically.”

Joyce Kurucz Medical Assistant

"As a medical assistant, I am part of the patient care team. I’m committed to delivering a comforting and great experience to each and every patient, whether in-person or on the phone. I am responsible for carefully listening to each patient and thinking ahead as to what their needs may be.  Working in partnership with the provider, I share information that is helpful in ensuring the needs of the patient are always well taken care of."

Matt Kurtzahn Site Analyst III

"As a Site Support Analyst in the IT department, I act as a direct resource to supporting our administrative staff, health center staff and vendors. Daily interaction with end users is paramount to my success. Working closely with IT teams both internal and external, I coordinate and implement new solutions, while supporting existing platforms. My team is also responsible for implementing the technology solutions used in our new health centers, traveling from coast to coast and ensuring technology is a tool and not a barrier."

Beth Maki, MSN, RN, CPNP Nurse Practitioner

"I provide care to our most treasured patients, children.  I am privileged to work with families in ensuring the health and well-being not only of the children, but the entire family unit, in both acute illness or preventive care visits.

I am also involved in formally training our new health providers to use our electronic medical record (EMR).  QuadMed understands the value in receiving training from those in the same role who use the system in practice, and therefore are trained how to maximize the tools available to provide the most comprehensive care possible for our patients."
