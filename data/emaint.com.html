How to use a CMMS for work order management

Work orders are the heart of a maintenance program. Utilizing work order software tools within a CMMS helps organizations stay up-to-date on labor, projects, and resources in the current queue. The work order management tools enable an organization to control work backlog and upcoming work, make constant improvements, boost labor productivity, and remain compliant.
