Commitment to Quality and Service

Nasco Aircraft Brake Inc. is a wholly owned subsidiary of Meggitt Aircraft Braking Systems and Meggitt PLC which supplies aircraft wheel and brake components for the military and commercial aircraft markets. We are unique in being the first company to manufacture and market aircraft wheel and brake components that are qualified under the same US Department of Defense dynamometer and flight testing standards required of the original equipment manufacturer, as well as the first and only company to obtain FAA certification for replacement commercial transport aircraft brake parts as a second source supplier. Nasco customers realize considerable savings on brake components in comparison with OEM products.

Airlines can benefit from the substantial savings and increased attention to customer service that comes from having an alternative supplier of brake components.
Nasco customers are assured of better design, high-quality manufacturing, quicker response to needs and the assurance of on-time deliveries.
