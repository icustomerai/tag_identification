Our Story

IDT has a rich history of creating value through organic growth of its core businesses and investing in new growth businesses, many of which have been spun-off.

2022

net2phone acquires Integra CCS, a contact-center-as-a-service (CCaaS) provider operating in the Americas and Europe, and launches its CCaaS offering.

2020

net2phone introduces its Huddle video conferencing solution and integration with Microsoft Teams, Salesforce and Zoho.

2019

NRS launches NRS Pay, a payment processing service beloved by independent retailers for its transparent pricing and exceptional service.

2017

IDT introduces the BOSS Revolution Money app, enabling customers to send money to family and loved ones overseas from their mobile phones.

2016

IDT Introduces National Retail Solutions (NRS), providing retail stores modern store management technology, including affordable point-of-sale hardware and software.

2015

net2phone evolves into a Unified Communications as a Service (UCaaS) provider with feature rich all-in-one, voice, messaging and chat services for businesses.

2013

BOSS Revolution expands its product portfolio by providing international money transfer to US consumers.

2011

IDT launches the BOSS Revolution Mobile Top-Up Service allowing customers to top-up prepaid cell phones around the world.

2009

IDT launches BOSS Revolution, a revolutionary service, allowing customers to make low cost international calls without using a PIN code.

2001

After launching net2phone, IDT becomes recognized as a pioneer in VoIP as it evolves into the technology of choice for voice services.

1997

IDT launches prepaid phone cards via its vast retail distribution network and establishes itself as a world leader in just a few years.

1996

IDT becomes a public company with a successful Initial Public Offering (IPO) on the New York Stock Exchange. NYSE: IDT

1993

IDT introduces its international voice services with an innovative, low-cost two way international calling solution.

1990

Entrepreneur, Howard Jonas creates IDT, a company focused on providing low cost international calling.
