Welcome to

RSA Media is Boston’s largest, locally-owned billboard company. We offer some of the highest visibility billboard units in Greater Boston. Our focus is only on the Boston market. We operate billboards only in Greater Boston and we are the experts to contact when looking for outdoor units in the Boston area.

When it comes to high-impact advertising, you can’t beat outdoor billboards. Letting us provide a venue for your message virtually guarantees visibility for your brand because billboard advertising in Massachusetts commands the attention of drivers and passersby. Our billboards are seen by people in and around high profile locations throughout Greater Boston including Logan Airport to points west, Massachusetts’ South Shore, Fenway Park and Gillette Stadium.

It’s not only the size of a billboard message that captures your attention – our outdoor billboards are strategically placed on major highways, principle traffic arteries, and other high volume roadways to ensure what is essentially a captive audience. Because a huge audience of receptive people will see your message day after day, billboards represent one of the fastest and most direct ways to build name recognition and to get people thinking about your business.
