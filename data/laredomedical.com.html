                        Laredo Medical Center

                        Laredo Medical Center is your community healthcare provider: a 326-bed, acute care facility with more than 49 specialties and subspecialties, including cardiology, oncology, neurology, obstetrics, orthopedics, urology, wound care, emergency medicine and bariatrics.

                      • Laredo CareClinic
                        • More Information

                        Laredo CareClinic

                        Laredo CareClinic is an after-hours clinic that provides quality, affordable health care for the whole family and offers a wide range of services for minor illnesses and injuries with convenient evening and weekend hours, 7 days a week.

                      • Laredo Physician Associates

                        Laredo Physician Associates

                        Laredo Physician Associates is a complete care medical practice representing a variety of medical specialties. Our physicians and nurse practitioners represent a variety of different specialties.
