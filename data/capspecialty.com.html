Our Products
Healthcare
CapSpecialty offers tailored insurance products underwritten to provide optimal coverage for human services organizations and miscellaneous medical facilities.
Professional Liability
Our professional liability underwriters focus on asset managers, insurance agents and brokers, insurance companies, lawyers, media and film companies, tech companies and other miscellaneous professionals.
Surety & Fidelity
CapSpecialty is recognized as a trusted provider of surety and fidelity bonds in the United States. Our product focus includes commercial and commercial contract surety, fidelity, non-standard and small construction contract.
Specialty Casualty
Our specialty casualty units offer an expansive variety of products tailored to fit the needs of construction, environmental, excess and other specialty risks.
Directors & Officers
Our directors & officers specialists focus on providing excess liability for public, private and non-profit organizations and companies.
