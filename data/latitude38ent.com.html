Latitude 38 Entertainment (L38) is a premium entertainment company located in the Napa Valley, producing world-class events including BottleRock Napa Valley. 

Formed in 2013, Latitude 38 Entertainment (L38) is owned, operated and managed by Napa Valley entrepreneurs Dave Graham, Justin Dragoo and Jason Scoggins, who combine to bring together a unique combination of business acumen, marketing savvy and world class hospitality coupled with their love of music. L38's vision is to blend music, wine, food and community to enhance and elevate the Napa Valley's reputation as a world-class destination to live, work and play. 

 

Our Mission

In 2013, three friends who grew up together in the Napa Valley attended the inaugural BottleRock Napa Valley music festival and were impressed by their experience. It was clear that the Napa Valley had added arts to its reputation as America’s epicenter of great wine and food. In late 2013, Napa Valley entrepreneurs Dave Graham, Justin Dragoo and Jason Scoggins formed Latitude 38 Entertainment and acquired the BottleRock brand. Since becoming the owners and producers of BottleRock Napa Valley, the music, wine, and food festival has become one of the country’s premium annual events, garnering national and international acclaim.
