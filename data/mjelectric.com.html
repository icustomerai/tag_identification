For more than six decades, M. J. Electric, a Quanta Services company, has performed comprehensive electrical construction in the electric transmission, substation, distribution, renewable energy, power, industrial, instrumentation, and civil/foundation sectors.

We welcome the opportunity to work with owners, engineers, and construction managers on utility and electrical construction projects nationwide in various industries. Whether installing, maintaining, or upgrading utility, electrical, or instrumentation systems, we strive to surpass our customer’s expectations.
