About Advantage Fire

Advantage Fire is a full-service fire protection company serving Metro Atlanta and North Georgia for nearly 30 years.

We design, install and service fire protection systems for all types of buildings, across multiple industries, for every stage of a buildings life cycle.

Our team brings the skill and expertise needed to each project to ensure people, property, and critical assets are protected.
