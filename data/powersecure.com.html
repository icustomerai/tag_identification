POWERCONTROL MONITORING

PowerControl Monitoring

PowerSecure’s PowerControl Command Center is manned 24/7/365 by trained analysts ready to respond immediately and proactively to utility outages, generator alarms, emergency events, and severe weather threats.

Our Operation Center monitors and manages the health of each distributed energy resource and microgrid, reporting any change in status to our customers and utilities on a continuous basis. We currently monitor 1,800+ units generating 1.6 GW of energy at 1,797 live sites across the United States.

Our PowerControl capabilities include:

  • Continuous monitoring of key system parameters and peak demand forecasting
  • Encrypted communications network tunnel for ultimate data security
  • Notifications of every operation, warning, and alarm for constant situational awareness and superior emergency preparedness
  • Redundant control centers to ensure that there are zero surprises or service gaps
  • Remote tests to monitor system health diagnostics, with custom reports available upon request
