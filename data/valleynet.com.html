ValleyNet - The Right Telecommunications Solution for Your Business

Our network uses the latest state of the art fiber optic technologies that include DS1, DS3, OC3, OC12, OC48 and OC192 services.
ValleyNet provides services to Inter-exchange Carriers (IXCs)POPs, LEC LSOs, wireless cell sites, data centers as well as on-net building locations.
