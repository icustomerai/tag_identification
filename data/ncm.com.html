Welcome to the Largest Cinema Advertising Network in the US

NCM unites brands with young, diverse, hard-to-reach audiences through the power of movies and pop culture. We provide expansive reach and unrivaled engagement at movie theaters nationwide, connecting with one out of every two 18-34 year-olds each year.

Network & Audience

The scale and reach of advertising in the NCM® movie theater network is unmatched. With a national footprint of more than 1,600 theaters and over 20,300 screens, NCM delivers an audience of hundreds of millions of content-hungry Gen Zs and Millennials to our advertisers.

National CineMedia (NCM) is America's Movie Network. As the largest cinema advertising network in the U.S., we unite brands with young, diverse audiences through the power of movies and popular culture. NCM’s advertising network of movie theatres offers broad reach and unparalleled audience engagement with 20,300+ screens in over 1,600 theaters in 195 Designated Market Areas® (all of the top 50). NCM Digital and Digital-Out-Of-Home (DOOH) advertising networks go beyond movie theaters extending the reach of big screen campaigns into online, mobile, and place-based marketing programs to reach movie and entertainment lovers everywhere.
