Corporate Social Responsibility

Avery Dennison partners with Rotary to support Kerala flood relief.

We partnered with Rotary International to support families who lost their homes during the flooding in Kerala, India in August 2018. The Rebuild Kerala Initiative brought together our employees in India who wanted to give back to a local community, and an international service organization to rebuild homes for nine families in this time of need.
