About iShip, Inc

The Pioneers in Hosted, Multi-carrier, Web-based Shipping

iShip, Inc.'s founders got their start in the shipping business in 1993, when UPS contracted with them to develop UPS OnLine Professional. UPS OnLine Pro became one of the most popular shipping software packages in the world, responsible for shipping well over 1 billion packages per year.


In 1997, iShip.com was founded, with the mission of becoming the "Internet Shipping Service Center." iShip.com shipped its first package over the Internet in 1998 and signed Mail Boxes Etc. as the launch customer for the iShip Service.


In October 1999, Stamps.com purchased iShip.com to create the leading provider of Internet-based mailing and shipping services. Stamps.com introduced the iShip Enterprise Service for corporate customers in May 2000.


Finally, in May 2001, United Parcel Service purchased the iShip business from Stamps.com and the company was renamed iShip, Inc.


Today, the iShip shipping and manifesting solutions are deployed at corporate campuses nationwide and at over 4,500 The UPS Store (TUPSS) locations.


Our Mission

Our mission is to give our customers Shipping InsightTM through Internet-based, multi-carrier shipping services that allow everyone to conveniently make the smartest shipping decisions. The comprehensive iShip service makes it possible for everyone to price, ship, track, and manage shipments over the Internet. The iShip service lets shippers easily compare rates and services among multiple carriers, including DHL, UPS, and the US Postal Service. iShip delivers precise and detailed shipping information - all in easy-to-access, hosted services.
