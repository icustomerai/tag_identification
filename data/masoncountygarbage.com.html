LOCALLY RUN & MANAGED

Mason County Garbage began operations in the early 1950’s. Through several private owners, the company has expanded over the years to provide residential, commercial and drop box services throughout Mason County, WA and the City of Shelton.

In 2001, Mason County Garbage was purchased by Waste Connections, a national leader in residential, construction and commercial waste removal services. As the Waste Connections family has grown, so has Mason County Garbage & Recycling’s ability to take advantage of increasing resources related to safety, route optimization, customer service, employee benefits and environmental responsibility. 

VALUES DRIVEN

Mason County Garbage & Recycling strives to be the premier waste and recycling company in Mason County, Washington by working to meet these core operating values:

  • Safety for our employees, our customers, and the public.
  • Integrity by keeping our promises to customers, employees, and stockholders.
  • Customer Service that ensures satisfaction and the best possible results across the board.
  • Employee Satisfaction via a growth culture that encourages each employee to realize his or her potential.
