Black Soldier Fly Larvae

Tiny Insects with Big Nutrition

EnviroProducts are all-natural, locally sourced black soldier fly larvae (BSFL) grown and processed in the USA. They are highly palatable, nutritious, and require far fewer resources to grow than traditional protein.

For your animals. For our planet.

Poultry
Chickens will naturally forage for insects given the opportunity. BSFL ingredients provide a natural and sustainable solution for the nutritional needs of poultry, such as high-quality protein and calcium for growth and egg development.
Exotics
Many exotic animals naturally hunt and eat insects. EnviroFlight products are trusted by a number of zoos to feed specialized and endangered insectivores, such as anteaters and pangolins. BSFL delivers similar nutrition to what these animals would find in their native habitats.
Young animals
Young animals require a palatable source of high quality protein, energy, and fiber to aid in growth and development. BSFL ingredients provide a natural and sustainable source of amino acids, omega-3s and 6s, lauric acid, and calcium.
Aquaculture
Aquaculture species will naturally scour for insect. BSFL ingredients can extend fish meal and other protein supplies to feed more fish, and ultimately, more people.
Pets
Pets naturally consume insects! BSFL ingredients are a palatable, nutritious, and sustainable source of desired nutrients such as lauric acid and prebiotics.
Fertilizer
Insect frass fertilizer is a natural, chemical free option for fruit and vegetable crops, flowers, lawns, and more. BSFL fertilizer is a sustainable and locally produced source of N-P-K.
