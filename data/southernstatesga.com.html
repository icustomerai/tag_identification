About Us

What we're about

Southern States General Agency ("SSGA") offers a broad range of coverages (including specialty and surplus lines coverages) for personal, commercial property and casualty lines. We work with independent agents in Louisiana, Arkansas, Mississippi and Texas helping them grow and succeed. Southern States General Agency, located in Ruston, Louisiana, was founded in 1962 and is a Scottish American company.
