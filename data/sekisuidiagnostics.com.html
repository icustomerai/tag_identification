Our Range of Products

Improving patient care worldwide starts with quality products and services.

Clinical Chemistry

Enhance laboratory function with accurate, timely results. SEKISUI Diagnostics is a recognized leader in the development, manufacture, and supply of reagents with over 1.8 billion tests performed each year using our products.

View Our Products

Point-of-Care Testing

Help treatment begin before a patient even leaves the doctor’s office. Our point-of-care tests come in a variety of formats to help reduce follow-up visits, promote education/prevention and allow for more precise treatment.

View Our Products

Enzymes

Foster the development and delivery of quality products. For over 40 years, we have been a leader in the manufacturing of high-quality specialty biochemicals and enzymes for diagnostic reagent and biosensor manufacturers.

View Our Products

Pre-Analytic Systems

SEKISUI Diagnostics is a pioneer in the field of plastic vacuum blood collection tubes, and our pre-analytic systems are trusted by blood collection tube manufacturers worldwide.

Biopharma Services

Maximize efficiency, smooth technical transfer, and process scale up while reducing time to market. SEKISUI is a leader in supplying materials for biotherapeutic manufacture, pharmaceutical production, diagnostic reagents, biosensors and medical devices.
