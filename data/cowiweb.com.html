Our Story

Complete Office of Wisconsin started as Bubrick's Office Supply which was founded in 1975 in Germantown, Wisconsin by Howard Bubrick. The Company grew to over $11 million in sales by 2006. It was then purchased by a group of industry executives in 2006 and became Bubrick's Complete Office of Wisconsin. Since 2006, the Company has grown to over $28 million in sales. As of 1/1/2015 the Company will be dropping Bubrick's from their name and be known as Complete Office of Wisconsin.
