Eastland Mall

Eastland Mall, located in Evansville, Indiana, is well-positioned as the Tri-State’s leading shopping destination drawing shoppers from throughout the region. Eastland offers more than 100 stores, restaurants and entertainment opportunities for shoppers including American Eagle, Bath & Body Works, Buckle, Carter's & Dry Goods, BJ’s Restaurant & Brewhouse, Olive Garden, Longhorn, TGI Fridays, Chick-fil-A and more.
