FROM THE CONSTRAINTS OF CONVENTIONAL MOLDING.

Today’s high pressure, multi-phase, complex processing requires deep expertise in high velocity-controlled filling and mastery of pack pressure optimization to construct the ideal process. Unfortunately, when those conditions change, so does your part quality.

A SIMPLER WAY TO MOLD IN CONTRAST TO CONVENTIONAL INJECTION MOLDING

iMFLUX’s adaptive process technology (the Green CurveTM) injects material into the mold through low-constant-pressure. The iMFLUX software extracts feedback from the system throughout the process and adjusts the pressure in real-time, as needed. As a result, you enjoy many competitive advantages.
