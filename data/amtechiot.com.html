Our Areas of Expertise

We bring decades of experience and relationships to craft products and strategies designed to connect people to the systems, properties and places they value. Our services and insights create pathways to better data, health, connectivity and community.

Smart Buildings

Protecting the property and its occupants should be Priority One for any Smart Building. Our Property Relyance system brings Internet of Things technology to multifamily and commercial buildings, monitoring the environmental conditions and security of the property.

Broadband & Wireless

HFC system monitoring and BTS site monitoring share the same overall objective: enabling remote visibility into the operation and condition of outside plant equipment, to ensure network reliability. AM Technologies BOSSc platform is used by Broadband and Mobile operators throughout the world, monitoring more than 100,000 network equipment sites.

Telecommunications

For Telecom field service organizations, the connection of remote IoT sensors to cognitive cloud intelligence enables an unprecedented level of workforce management and productivity. Our AMIoT platform enables customers to deploy limited resources in a more efficient manner with exception management, automated trouble ticketing and geolocation/direction finding.

Data Centers

Rack-level visibility of energy usage and environmental conditions is an absolute necessity in data centers. Our AMIoT platform collects this vital data in real time and monitors for exceptions, facilitating the efficient use of resources.

Featured Initiative: Make Affordable Housing Smart

AM Technologies, in partnership with the JKH Foundation has launched the Make Affordable Housing Smart initiative as part of its Corporate Social Responsibility program.


The initiative is designed to equip affordable housing apartment buildings with first-class sensing and monitoring solutions, aimed at enhancing property management system, adding value to the building, as well and contributing to the sustainability of affordable housing.

Currently, AM Technologies and the Foundation are working with Community League Of The Heights (CLOTH), a nonprofit affordable housing developer in New York to create a demonstration of the efficiencies of AMs Property Relyance system in two apartment buildings, including a special needs building for the formerly homeless.

The initiative offers AMs technology to nonprofit housing developers at a deep discount, and the program includes capacity building and training of the property managers on both the hardware and software aspects of the technology. The initiative has also drawn attention from a number of nonprofit developers nationwide.
