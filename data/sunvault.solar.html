Renewable Energy Ventures, LLC

Renewable Energy Ventures, LLC (“REV”) is a sponsor and financial engineer of renewable energy funding offerings.  REV works exclusively with Solar Energy Ventures, LLC, an emerging renewable energy developer, and Arque Capital, Ltd., a U.S. broker-dealer and investment bank.  Arque Capital has arranged for almost $500,000,000 in financings over the last six years for sponsored alternative investments.

REV designs, creates and distributes unique financial solutions which are designed to provide investors with virtual principal return benefits of up to 75% of the original investment value within the first 12 months of initial investment through the use of (1) federal investment tax credits (“ITCs”), state tax incentives, and/or accelerated depreciation allowances, (2) possible partial prepayment of utility payments, and/or (3) consistent dividend income that may last from 6 – 20 years.

REV financial offerings are typically limited to accredited and qualified investors.  REV financial offerings provide the best benefits to US investors.  REV offers both financial products designed for multiple accredited investors and custom solutions for affluent entrepreneurs and family offices who can invest in excess of $1 Million.  REV product offerings which include federal ITCs provide dollar-for-dollar tax credits for the benefit of US citizens and which are often limited to passive income offsets.   Custom solutions can feature ITCs that may be offset against (a) active and passive income or (b) passive income only.
