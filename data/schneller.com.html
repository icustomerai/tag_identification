WHAT SETS US APART...

It all dates back to 1964, with founder John Schneller's development of a vinyl-coated fiberglass cloth that would provide an attractive and durable headliner for the fabled Douglas DC3 aircraft. It was the beginning of transforming interior aircraft environments and later passenger rail cars, architectural offices and displays.

In response to growing customer needs for interiors that were more attractive, comfortable and compliant with safety regulations, Schneller developed a world class portfolio of engineered decorative materials.

Those materials include: film laminates, reinforced laminates, thermoplastic sheets, utility flooring and an ever-expanding array of custom applications.

Today, virtually all the world's airlines and major aircraft and rail car manufacturers are introducing revolutionary new design solutions with Schneller as their creative partner. From design to product development, to manufacturing, installation and technical service, Schneller is the one resource that surrounds you with solutions. That's what sets us apart.

GLOBAL REACH

Schneller is the global leader in providing comprehensive end-to-end solutions and worldwide on-demand customer service for hard-surface laminates, thermoplastic sheets and non-textile floor coverings for the Aviation and Rail interiors. Schneller is proud to be a qualified supplier to all major OEM airframe manufacturers, including Boeing, Airbus, Bombardier, Embraer, Sukhoi, Mitsubishi, and others.

We work with all major commercial airlines and all major Tier 1 suppliers, and we are a preferred supplier in the Airbus NTF Selection Guide and the exclusive supplier of the Airbus A350 Canvas Catalog. We are also proud to be the Primary supplier of the Bombardier C Series.
