Careers

CONAM continually accepts applications for employment from motivated individuals who would like to build a career in the construction services industry. Five (5) years experience is preferred BUT trainee and intern positions are available. Alaskan residents with remote location experience is also preferred. CONAM offers a competitive wage and benefits package and is an equal opportunity employer.
