WELCOME!

Since being founded in 1960, Nevada Ready Mix has grown to become Nevada's number one concrete company. We have a large number of trucks and multiple batch plants around the Las Vegas metropolitan area. It is our philosophy that our customers are the number one priority, and we strive to provide them with the highest quality product and best service around.

Most of the hotels in Las Vegas are built with Nevada Ready Mix concrete. Along with hotels, NRM has also completed countless residential foundations, public works, golf courses, and heavy highway projects. Our resume proves that Nevada Ready Mix truly is the foundation that Las Vegas was built on.
