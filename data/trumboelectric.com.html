A Glance At Our Company

Trumbo Electric, Inc. is the oldest electrical contractor in western Virginia and one of the pioneers in design/build construction. Located near Harrisonburg, Virginia, Trumbo is still the only contractor in western Virginia licensed to practice electrical engineering. Trumbo Electric was founded in 1948 by Norvell Trumbo. Norvell's vision was to fully integrate electrical engineering and construction into one business. He believed that this approach would provide the customer with an electrical system of superior value. Paramount to his vision was that the company establish the highest quality standards possible. These strategies continue to be keys to our success.
1

Trumbo Electric was the first Virginia contractor to employ engineers (1960's) and practice design/build construction. We have excelled in electrical design and layout for decades and are licensed by the states of Virginia (#0407002199) and West Virginia (C01146-00) to practice electrical engineering. Trumbo has successfully completed thousands of industrial, institutional, and health care projects ranging from several thousand dollars to over ten million dollars in our sixty-plus year history and has never failed to complete a project on schedule.
