The company you are looking for is now part of OPEN Health


OPEN Health was founded in 2011 with the ambitious vision of being the most respected healthcare partner in the world.

Through numerous acquisitions, we have brought together global experts at the forefront of the healthcare industry.

Today, all these brands—and the deep well of human talent and institutional capabilities that they represent—are joined as OPEN Health. Now uniquely equipped to deliver patient-centered, world-class services across the globe, OPEN Health has grown to more than 1,300 dedicated and diversely skilled individuals in 16 offices across 6 countries, namely the US, the UK, the Netherlands, Germany, India, and China.

Together we are established as many, united as one.

Learn more about our history Learn more about our values

Acsel Health joined OPEN Health in 2023

Acsel Health, a New York-based life science consulting firm, joined OPEN Health in 2023. Acsel is focused on commercial strategy, pricing and market access, and commercial excellence, bringing deep industry expertise, scientific rigor, and actionable analysis as a valued partner to life science companies. These capabilities will complement OPEN Health’s existing offering, broadening the range of services it offers to pharma and biotech companies.

Learn more

The CM Group joined OPEN Health in 2022

The CM Group is an integrated commercialization platform enabling biopharmaceutical manufacturers to build market insights and to disseminate scientific information to healthcare providers and patients. The CM Group suite of brands includes decile.ten, the PX Group, and Scientific Commercialization, which together with OPEN Health provide additional expertise in key areas including commercial medical communications.

Learn more

Spirit joined OPEN Health in 2021

Spirit was established as an independent agency in 2006 delivering soul in the science, the humanity in the data, harnessing the power of creativity to deliver communications capable of changing lives. The Spirit team enhances OPEN Health’s publication planning and medical writing and medical education capabilities.

Learn more

ARK joined OPEN Health in 2021

ARK was founded in 2018 as a data-driven creative agency that designs, develops, and delivers measurable experiences by focusing on the customers and the message. ARK has won multiple awards, including The PM Society Digital Awards for Agency Team of the Year and the Creative Floor awards for Charity/Pro Bono work. The ARK team brings extensive experience in omnichannel marketing and commercial marketing expertise.

Learn more

Pharmerit joined OPEN Health in 2019

Pharmerit has a 20-year history of being a global leader in health economics and outcomes research (HEOR). Joining the Pharmerit business with the OPEN Health Group established a new unique entity equipped to be a global leader in HEOR, market access, medical affairs and healthcare communications.

Learn more

Peloton merged with OPEN Health in 2018

Together, they created a multi-disciplinary health communications and market access group equipped to be a leading provider of medical communications services to Medical Affairs departments at pharmaceutical, biotechnology, and medical device companies.

Learn more
