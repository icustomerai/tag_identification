We are Red Thread

We help transform workplaces to engage employees and support their best work by integrating furniture, technology, and architectural systems.

The pandemic has changed the way we work forever. Organizations are defining what hybrid work means to them; hybrid is not one-size-fits-all. People need flexible, tailored environments to fuel innovation, foster connections, and strengthen workplace culture.

Whether it’s delivering equitable experiences with the right technology or creating a destination that earns your employee’s commute – together with your team, we develop a workplace transformation strategy that makes sense for you.

Boston WorkLife

The Steelcase Boston WorkLife is home to Red Thread’s headquarters. WorkLife spaces explore and illustrate the concept of hybrid work neighborhoods designed to support different styles of work and various tasks. Designed with flexibility and employee wellness in mind, it includes a mix of open and enclosed spaces supported by Steelcase products and our community of brands.

How We're Different

We take a holistic approach to the workplace to create the best possible result.

We serve as a single-source vendor for everything you need for your workplace – furniture, technology, demountable walls, flooring.

Our unique breadth of expertise allows us to thoughtfully integrate all of the elements within your innovative work environment, while also streamlining the implementation to deliver cost savings.
