Contract Product Design and Engineering Services

For the Oil and Gas Industry

SensorWise is a full-service contract engineering and product design firm, focused on designing high-tech, mission critical equipment for the oil and gas industry. Founded in 1995, SensorWise has designed state-of-the-art equipment for customers ranging from small start-ups to major to major oil and gas service and equipment providers. Today, the high-tech equipment we design ranks in the top of its class and is critical to our clients’ competitiveness in the international market.

SensorWise provides contract engineering and design services to a variety of firms throughout the oil and gas industry. Our in-house staff of electronic, technical, mechanical, software and systems engineers makes us uniquely equipped to provide turnkey development solutions throughout the industry.

Our capabilities include:

  • Electronics Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Software Engineering
  • Systems Engineering
  • Project Management

Tools Designed for the Downhole Environment

Although SensorWise designs various “mission critical” products, our specialty is designing tools for the challenging oilfield downhole environment.  SensorWise has designed many successful products that incorporate high temperature electronics, sensors, and rugged mechanical assemblies for the high temperature / high pressure downhole environment. We are also very experienced with designing downhole equipment that must perform reliably under high vibration and shock conditions.

Particular areas of expertise in downhole tool design include:

  • Sensor/Instrumentation design and packaging for high temperature applications
  • Data acquisition and real-time processing
  • Mechanical design with moving parts
  • Harsh environment designs

Fully Integrated Services

At SensorWise, we provide integrated, concurrent engineering services designed to maintain the consistency of the product. Our balanced combination of analysis, design, prototyping, and verification ensures cost-effective, successful results.

Whether you are looking for new technology design, upgrades to existing products or to re-engineer legacy products, SensorWise can provide a solution that allows you to optimize your capital investment through product performance, longevity and reliability. SensorWise also can develop automated testing equipment and is a certified National Instruments Alliance partner.
