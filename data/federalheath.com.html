Interior Décor & Graphics

We’ve helped highly successful companies turn their space into brand-aware, sales-centric environments with designs based on the perfect blend of creativity and function.  An immersive brand experience usually exists for one purpose: to make sales. We support that purpose with every design element to maximize the potential for the customer journey you envision.

Exterior Signage

Your sign speaks volumes. Signs that come out of our five state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities always shout out the very best things on your behalf. With Federal Heath’s attention to detail and quality, even the simplest sign will brilliantly communicate the image, brand and message that’s important to you.

Design Services

Our design process is flexible, focused, and tailored to meet the specific needs for your customers and the scope of the project. Open discussion and collaboration with all stakeholders help us create and maintain the project vision. We guide that vision through the design process as we address challenges, take advantage of opportunities and meet the needs of the project.

Digital Signage

With dynamic digital signage, engaging customers has never been easier – but it still takes know-how to avoid disappointments. One size doesn’t fit all. We offer numerous platforms that are custom designed to fit your business objectives and engage your customers from the curb to the counter. Our end-to-end solutions include project management, installation, training and support.

Sign & Lighting Maintenance

Constant exposure to the elements can be hard on a sign, and bulbs don’t last forever. Everyday wear can happen so gradually that you fail to notice. We help you protect your investment in your brand with solutions tailored to individual requirements, including repair, restoration, and ongoing preventative maintenance.

Remodeling Services

When we say single source, we mean it. Federal Heath also supplies schedule-driven, commercial contracting for building and site modifications, image changes and upgrades, and brand conversions for both interior and exterior projects. Yes, we build the best signs in the business. We can also help you rebuild the place where we hang them.
