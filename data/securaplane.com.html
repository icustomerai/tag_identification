Innovation

Wireless control systems, high definition cameras, fully-integrated aircraft security systems, lithium batteries and power electronics for the more electric aircraft- Securaplane has continually brought innovation to commercial aerospace.

HD CMX aircraft cameras

Form-fit replacements for the standard definition line of CMX cameras. Industry leading: 1080p, 30 fps and ruggedized for all helicopter vibration levels.

The Securaplane difference

We provide a complete range of power, motion and security devices.
