Introducing Conniption Kinship Gin

Conniption Gin: Modern, Unrivaled Gins

Born from a passion that began with gin-soaked olives stolen from a grandfather’s martini, Conniption Gin honors the art of traditional gin distillation while simultaneously embracing modern scientific methods. Conniption Gins are now recognized as among the highest awarded and the best distilled in the United States.

Go Ahead. Have a Conniption.

The Best Gin for Martinis

Conniption American Dry Gin earned the pinnacle platinum grand prize winner of the 2022 Martini Competition by Tastings.com Beverage Testing Institute.

Even better? Conniption Navy Strength Gin earned the Gold and 1st place. That's right, Conniption Gins are the best gins for martinis. It's official.
