New Model 589
Simply LEGENDARY

The LEGENDARY Peterbilt Model 589 represents an exceptional blend of tradition and innovation - a true connection to Peterbilt's heritage. The new 589 is built for high performance and maximum uptime and has a road presence that is unmistakably Peterbilt. The 589 features classic Peterbilt styling, chrome and class accents, a spacious and premium interior, enhanced ergonomics and more, making it the most LEGENDARY truck ever.
