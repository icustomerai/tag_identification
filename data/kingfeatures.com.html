ABOUT KING FEATURES

King Features is a premier producer and distributor of the world’s most iconic intellectual properties and a leader in classic character licensing and franchise development.

With one of the longest-running consumer products programs in the industry, our portfolio includes world-renowned pop culture brands such as Popeye®, Cuphead, Flash Gordon™, The Phantom™, Hägar the Horrible, Moomin, Prince Valiant® and Mandrake the Magician.

Headquartered in New York, King Features is a unit of Hearst.
