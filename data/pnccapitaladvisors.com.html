Welcome to PNC Capital Advisors

PNC Capital Advisors, LLC is a fixed income asset management firm devoted to helping solve the complex, evolving investment goals of institutional clients.

As a wholly owned subsidiary of PNC Bank, N.A., we enjoy many large-firm capabilities, while preserving the flexibility and individual client focus of a small investment firm.

We achieve this from a solutions-based perspective—different from the typical product-based approach—so clients benefit from a tailored portfolio that leverages our discipline and risk-focused expertise.
