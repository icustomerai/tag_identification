Flow Technology, recognized in the U.S. and worldwide for providing specialized, high accuracy flow meters and flow metering solutions, offers an unmatched flow measurement resource, including over 50 years of experience solving the most difficult applications.

Flow Technology’s turbine, positive displacement, electromagnetic, and ultrasonic flow meters are used in a wide range of liquid and gas flow sensing applications across numerous industries.

OUR FLOW MEASUREMENT PRODUCTS

Users of Flow Technology’s precision flow metering devices range from major oil & gas companies drilling at the bottom of the North Sea, to leading food and beverage producers, pharmaceutical manufacturers and the NASA space program. Even winning Nascar™ and NHRA™ racing teams use our flow meters. FTI flow metering products are frequently used in mission critical applications, especially within the aerospace, military and oil and gas markets that we serve.

TURBINE FLOW METERS

  • High accuracy
  • Fast response time
  • Liquid and gases
  • Ideally for low viscosities <500 cSt
  • Optional pressure port
  • Custom-engineered on request

ELECTROMAGNETIC FLOW METERS

  • High accuracy
  • No moving parts
  • For conductive fluids
  • Sealed electrodes and coils
  • Bidirectional capability
  • High turndown

IN-LINE ULTRASONIC FLOW METERS

  • For low viscosity liquids
  • Compact lightweight design
  • Low pressure drop
  • High accuracy
  • No moving parts

CLAMP-ON ULTRASONIC FLOW METERS

  • Non-intrusive
  • For piping up to 12 inches
  • High accuracy
  • Simple installation
  • Fast response time
  • Wide rangeability

POSITIVE DISPLACEMENT FLOW METERS

  • Only two moving parts
  • Viscosity insensitive
  • Clean-in-place (CIP)
  • Handles pulsating flow
  • High pressure
  • Very low flow
