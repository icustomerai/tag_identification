Important Notification

Beware of ongoing text scam: We’ve recently noticed an increase in fraudsters posing as Rockland Trust, texting customers about a recent security threat. The fraudsters ask you to click a link that they provided to review and verify your information. This is a scam - do not click any links or enter any of your account information. As a reminder, Rockland Trust will never text, call, or email you asking for your online banking credentials or to verify account information.
