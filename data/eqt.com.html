EQT Corporation is the largest producer of natural gas in the United States.
EQT Corporation is a leading independent natural gas producer with an evolutionary focus on our future. EQT has operations in Pennsylvania, West Virginia and Ohio and is dedicated to responsibly developing our world-class asset base in the core of the Appalachian Basin. EQT is making strides toward becoming the best producer by creating long-term value for all stakeholders, including employees, landowners, communities, industry partners and investors.

Realizing the full potential of EQT
As the largest producer of natural gas in the United States, it is imperative for EQT to be a leader in the industry, and as such, we are embarking on a journey to ensure that we are not just the biggest, but also the best. With a renewed focus on upstream operations, we are working to achieve our mission to realize the full potential of EQT to become the low-cost producer of natural gas and the operator of choice for all stakeholders.

Responsibility

At EQT, our business is about more than just the innovative production of energy resources; it’s also about being a good neighbor. Community

EQT strives to work with and give back to the communities in which we live and operate. We have a renewed commitment to our stakeholders to create long-term value and become their operator of choice. We understand that our ability to operate is rooted in our relationships with communities, especially when it comes to safety and other concerns, and through these engagements, we develop strong relationships that benefit both EQT and our communities.
