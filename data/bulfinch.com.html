At Bulfinch, our seasoned vision enables us to see hidden opportunities, while our agility, passion and creativity allow us to convert these opportunities into value. This is how we collaborate with tenants and investors to create exceptional workplaces.

Cambridge Discovery Park

Bulfinch discovered the opportunity to acquire Acorn Park, one of the first post-war suburban office parks in Cambridge in a core market and transform the outdated park, scattered with open parking lots, into a state-of-the-art life science campus.

See The Project

Life Time Center

Bulfinch had the unique vision to acquire the failing Atrium Mall in an affluent suburb located a short drive from the Longwood Medical Area in Boston and reposition the property into a health, wellness and medical center. The nearly vacant mall presented a rare opportunity to acquire a distressed asset in a core suburban market.

See The Project

117 Kendrick Street

Located in Needham Crossing business park within the N2 Corridor, one of Greater Boston’s leading tech hubs, 117 Kendrick Streets interior was outdated and in need of a face-lift. Bulfinch identified key renovations to the common areas and outdoor spaces to help reinvigorate the property to not only help tenant retention but also attract top-tier tenants.
