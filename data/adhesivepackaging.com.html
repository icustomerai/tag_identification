Welcome to
Adhesive Packaging Specialties, LLC

Adhesive Packaging Specialties, LLC custom packages one and two component adhesives, sealants and resins including acrylic, anaerobic materials, conductives, epoxy, polysulfides, polyurethanes, premixed frozen syringes, UV curable and other polymers. APS is an ISO 9001: 2008 AS9100C registered facility.

APS featured in January Edition of Adhesive and Sealants Industry Magazine
ASI is featuring a story on an exciting new packaging process, developed by APS, for an advanced technology sealant used in the aerospace industry. The properties of this sealant, manufactured by our sister company Royal Adhesives and Sealants, required not only new processing equipment to be developed but also new packaging techniques to properly handle this critical product to meet the stringent demands of the aerospace industry.
