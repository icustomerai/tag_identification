Seneca Meadows, Inc.

Responsible Waste Management for the next generation.

Seneca Meadows, Inc. is a waste management and recycling facility dedicated to the preservation and advancement of the environment. Since our inception in 1983, weve earned the support of national environmental organizations through our exemplary compliance record and our many environmental and sustainability initiatives. Our operations are centrally located in Seneca Falls, New York, making us the primary disposal facility for businesses and communities throughout the State.

Events and educational programs

The Education Center building is gold level LEED (Leadership in Energy and Environmental Design) certified by the U.S. Green Building Council, and houses a laboratory, exhibit room, and office space. Environmental features include regionally harvested timber framing, a geothermal heating and cooling system, daylighting controls, and many other earth friendly components.

To learn more about the Seneca Meadows Environmental Education Center, or to contact our director, please call 315-539-5624.
