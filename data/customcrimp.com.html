 Custom Machining Services, Inc. (CMS) has been producing dies, hose assembly equipment, and hose crimpers since 1979. The Custom Crimp® and ValPower® trade names are distributed worldwide and have established a respected name for quality, accuracy, and unparalleled service. They are used in commercial and industrial applications ranging from farming to oil exploration. All Custom Crimp® and ValPower® components are manufactured in the U.S. and assembled and shipped from Valparaiso, IN USA.

Service, parts, and technical assistance are never more than a phone call or support request away.
