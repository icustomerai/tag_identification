Chemplex® Sample Cup Selection Wizard

The Sample Cup Wizard is configured to lead you directly to the appropriate website page for sample cup options based on one measurement: the inside diameter of your sample cup holder. That measurement will represent the diameter of the sample cup. So take an accurate measurement of the sample cup holder and keep it handy; the Wizard will ask you for it.
