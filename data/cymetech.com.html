Our DCPD Products

Purity, Consistency, & Security

Sold under the ULTRENE™ DCPD trade name, our products are widely recognized for their purity, consistency, supply security, and global availability. Explore our range of ultra-high purity DCPD and MCPDD products.

Explore Our Products

ULTRENE™ 99 DCPD

At 99+% purity, ULTRENE™ 99 DCPD is primarily used in formulations to produce poly-DCPD thermoset structures and is used in fine chemical applications.

ULTRENE™ 97 DCPD

ULTRENE™ 97 DCPD is ideal as an intermediate and monomer for specialty chemical development and innovations.

ULTRENE™ 85 DCPD

ULTRENE™ 85 DCPD was specifically developed for Unsaturated Polyester Resin applications.

ULTRENE™ 99-6 DCPD

Containing 6% Cyclopentadiene (CPD) trimer, ULTRENE™ 99-6 DCPD is ideal for structural applications requiring greater mechanical performance and higher temperature resistance.

ULTRENE™ 94 MCPDD

At 94+% purity, ULTRENE™ 94 MCPDD is used as an intermediate for the production of specialty anhydrides and fuel additives.
