MEPUSA, a subsidiary of Mitsui & Co., Ltd. is an oil and gas exploration and production company headquartered in Houston, Texas, which currently has substantial investments in the Marcellus Shale Gas Project in Pennsylvania, the Eagle Ford Shale Oil & Gas Project in Texas, and the Kaikias Deepwater Gulf of Mexico Project, which is a subsea tie-back to Shellâs Ursa platform.

Additionally, MEPUSA has exploration rights in various high potential offshore basins located in South America. We also market equity and non-equity natural gas through Mitsui & Co. Energy Marketing and Services (USA) Inc., another subsidiary of Mitsui & Co.

Through valued partnerships, MEPUSA is engaged in extracting value from our existing assets and contributing profit and cash flow to the corporation, while at the same time we are focused on the future and committed to continue to transform and grow our portfolio to help serve the energy needs of society in a sustainable and responsible manner.

Mitsui E&P USA LLC invests in innovative energy resources while upholding values and respect for the environment.
