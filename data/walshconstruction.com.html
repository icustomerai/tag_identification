What’s Happening

June 13th, 2023

Team WALSH Meets the Challenge

There is nothing like climbing a mountain together to build camaraderie, collaboration and a lifetime of memories! The WALSH team started in the dark by the light of the strawberry moon and summited the 11,249 feet of Mount Hood at sunrise. Everyone returned to Timberline Lodge safely and much stronger from the experience.

June 12th, 2023

Hobson Place South wins AGC Build Washington award

Hobson Place South has been awarded Moss Adams/AGC Build Washington’s Building Excellence Award in the Private Building: $20M to $50M category for 2023!

June 12th, 2023

Six DJC Top Projects in 2023

We were thrilled to celebrate SIX projects nominated for TopProjects at the Daily Journal of Commerce Oregon event last night! DJC TopProjects is the premier recognition program for the Pacific Northwest’s built environment that celebrates the most outstanding projects completed last year.

May 1st, 2023

Reimagining Space: Revitalizing buildings through thoughtful remodels

Whether creating new affordable homes from an old nursing dormitory, breathing new life and hope into a defunct rehabilitation center, or giving an exterior facelift to a 350,000-square-foot retirement community, we know how to engage in meaningful change in our communities, while working within the confines of existing space.
