We’ll Leave The Light On For You

We believe everyone should have a right to travel, so we give people an affordable, welcoming place to rest wherever their journey takes them. For 60 years, weve been known as the best place to find a clean, comfortable room with great service at a fair price.

Serving Our
Guests

For more than 60 years, Motel 6 has offered travelers a clean, comfortable room with great service at a fair price. Our iconic brand is synonymous with travel value.

Serving Our
Team Members

The team members who work for our organization are special. A willingness to collaborate and work together is a hallmark of our culture. We believe that by openly sharing ideas to drive improvements across the organization, combined with our heart for service, we are making hospitality personal again.

Serving Our
Communities

We are a diverse and inclusive company that is also committed to being a good corporate citizen. We are making strides to be a great partner in our local communities.

Serving Our
Franchise Owners

We created the economy lodging segment in 1962 and we continue to lead it today. And thanks to a strong strategic plan and new goals to propel us forward, the light is still on and burning brightly.

Recent Openings

Euless, TX

New Opening
This newly renovated property is located off SR 183 and is within 10 miles of DFW Airport, amusement parks, Globe Life Field and AT&T/Cowboys. Kitchenette rooms are available should any guests wish to enjoy a personal stove top, microwave and minifridge during their stay. Guests have access to free Wi-Fi for added comfortability, as well as vending machine options should they like convenient access to a late-night or early-morning snack.

North Platte, NE

New Opening
This new construction dual-brand Motel 6/Studio 6 in North Platte is located near the Platte River and the scenic Iron Eagle Golf Course. The location boasts 91 total guest rooms, divided almost evenly between the Motel 6 and Studio 6 design layouts. All 46 of the Studio 6 rooms include kitchenettes, perfect for those long-term guests, and every room on the property includes a memory foam mattress on the bed. There is free Wi-Fi in all guest rooms and in-site coin laundry is available.

Katy, TX

New Opening
This Houston-area new construction extended stay hotel is located right off of I-10, 28 miles west of downtown Houston. Guests will enjoy the latest Studio 6 design that has begun to roll out in select locations throughout the U.S. Located near Katy Mills Mall, guests can take advantage of the full kitchenettes, each including a coffee maker, utensils and cutlery, a mini-fridge, stovetop and microwave. Guests also have access to onsite laundry and free WiFi. Four-legged family members are also welcome as the property is pet friendly.
