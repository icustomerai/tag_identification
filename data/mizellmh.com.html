Welcome to Mizell Memorial Hospital

Here at Mizell Memorial Hospital Our Mission is to Provide the Highest Quality Healthcare, in a Christian Manner with Hometown Compassion to the Communities We Serve.

Through the networking of the various agencies within the Mizell Memorial Hospital, a broad range of healthcare services is available for patients in the medical service area as we strive to fulfill our mission. Providing quality, cost-effective and convenient healthcare services that meet and exceed reasonable customer expectations is what we strive for.
