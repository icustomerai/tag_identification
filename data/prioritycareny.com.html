How It Works

You can hire a family member or friend to provide the care you need in your own home. Your caregiver will be paid and receive benefits through the Consumer Directed Personal Assistance Program (CDPAP). There will be no charge to you.

LEARN MORE ABOUT CDPAP

Who Is Eligible

You may be eligible if you:

  • Live in the State of New York
  • Qualify for Medicaid and a Managed Long-Term Care (MLTC) plan
  • Can make decisions about your care and the caregivers you want

How CDPAP Works

CDPAP is a New York State Department of Health Medicaid program that allows you to control your home care. You can choose, train, and guide your Personal Assistant to provide the exceptional care you deserve. That’s right—family members or friends get paid to be your caregiver!
