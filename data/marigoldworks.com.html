  • Unmatched Innovation

    After 40 years in business it would be easy to rest on our laurels and do things the way they have always been done. But that's not enough for us at Marigold Works. We are constantly seeking better solutions to complicated challenges. Our exclusive PermAlloy® new and rebuild custom overlay process is a great example of our innovation. This trademarked process is the most cost effective rebuild process on the market. It can be applied to ½ inch depth and work hardens to 60+ HRC.

  • Unmatched Experience

    With more than 200 years of collective experience, our expert machinists have the knowledge to take on any challenge related to heavy equipment. We continuously research and develop proprietary metals and processes that increase strength ratios and improve the life of our products. This talented team of designers, machinists, welders, and craftsmen, know that nothing is impossible.

  • Unmatched Capabilities

    With more than 70,000 square feet of manufacturing space, Marigold Works offers a wide array of capabilities through our large scale machine shop, in-house CNC production facility, complete fabrication department, full repair and rebuild department, and our trademarked PermAlloy® custom overlay department. Our on-site logistics department makes it easy for us to ship to and from customers across the world. Our capabilities are as unlimited as your imagination.

  • Unmatched Quality

    At Marigold Works, we believe in manufacturing that is better than new. We call this “repair with enhancement.” The foundation of our business is an unwavering commitment to quality in every product we produce.

From custom fabrication to complete equipment repairs, Marigold Works is one of the most experienced machine shops in the US. Founded in 1975 as B&D Industrial & Mining, we have more than 40 years of experience in fabrication, repair, and enhancement engineering. We cut our teeth on draglines, shovels, rock trucks, and underground mining equipment, and in the years since we have expanded to work with clients in energy, railroad, forestry, and agricultural industries. Today we proudly offer a comprehensive, one-stop shop for all heavy equipment needs.
