About Belvac

Belvac was founded in 1962 in Lynchburg Virginia.

Belvac is a world leader with a commitment to excellence. Belvac’s five business segments: Belvac ceramic Solutions, Belvac Decorating Systems, Belvac Production Machinery, Belvac Container Solutions, and Belvac Service Solutions provide full industry support, a one source solution, and the foundation to your success.

Our Values

Commitment

Belvac’s founding philosophies of quality, work ethic, pride, and craftsmanship as well as new world entrepreneurial spirit are still present today. Belvac continues to hire the best people, use the best techniques and use the best quality material. The result is the best quality product.

Quality

Belvac is dedicated to achieving customer satisfaction by providing high quality products and services delivered on time. We achieve these high standards by fostering a culture of continuous improvement of our processes, improving efficiencies via reduced waste, accelerated throughput, and thus, improved long term shareholder wealth.

Ecology

Belvac’s Machines are designed to favorably contribute to the ecological and environmental needs of the world. For example, the Belvac Necker reduces the amount of organic material needed for each can.
