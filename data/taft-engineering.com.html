A Complete Manufacturer's Services Rep

Taft Engineering is a full-service Manufacturer’s Representative Company providing comprehensive energy solutions for clients across Colorado, Wyoming, New Mexico and parts of Texas. Representing a range of superior HVAC and plumbing product lines, Taft offers premium engineering support and equipment training for industrial, commercial and light commercial projects.

Based near Denver, Colorado, in Centennial, Taft was founded by company president Tracy Wilcox, and our dedicated staff has grown to include internal and external sales engineers, product support specialists, service technicians and equipment training experts. Committed to each valued manufacturer and client partnership, the Taft Engineering team works with mechanical engineers, contractors and building owners to outfit new construction, retrofit existing facilities, manage equipment startups and provide cost-effective maintenance and repair programs.

While Taft Engineering represents a full array of high-efficiency commercial HVAC and plumbing lines from companies like Aerco, Hurst, Bryan, Riello and Shipco, our skilled staff possesses the knowledge and experience to service hundreds of related products and brands. When you need a complete energy solutions provider for schools, hotels, commercial facilities, medical buildings or multi-family housing, trust the Taft team with equipment design, sizing, shipping, maintenance and repairs.
