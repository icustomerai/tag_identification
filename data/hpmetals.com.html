HAMILTON PRECISION METALS AT A GLANCE

Specialty Re-Roller of Metal Strip, Foil and Ultra-Thin Foil

We are experts in re-rolling precision metal strip, foil and ultra-thin foil in nearly any alloy and specialty metal wherever quality, precision and consistency are critical to performance.

Custom Sizes with Short Lead Times

Tightest tolerances and thinnest gauges in the industry from 1.5 mm (.060”) to 1.5 microns (.000060”) in thickness.

Tightly Controlled Mechanical Properties

Delivery of any combination of specific mechanical, physical, electrical and magnetic properties.

Custom Surface Finishes

Our metallurgists work with customers to develop exacting surfaces finishes from ultra-smooth to matte.
