OLYMPIC PETROLEUM CORPORATION

ABOUT OLYMPIC PETROLEUM CORPORATION
Olympic Petroleum Corporation is engaged in the development and production of lubricants, thermal transfer fluids and related additives including motor oil, coolants and antifreeze, engine and fuel products geared towards OEM, professional and end consumers. We provide a complete range of products used by consumers around the globe.
ANNUAL PRODUCTION CAPACITY
Olympic Petroleum has the capacity to blend and package 100,000,000 units and 100,000,000 gallons of products annually. It can manufacture and blend both its Lubricants and Thermal Transfer Fluids in batch sizes as small as 5,000 and up to 120,000 gallons per batch.
+
STORAGE CAPACITY
Olympic Petroleum extensive infrastructure includes over 155 of product storage tanks which can accommodate over 5.2 million gallons of raw and finished products.

+
HANDLING CAPACITIES
Olympic Petroleum compliments its production facility with rail sidings, a barge dock and over 12 bulk loading and unloading terminals that can handle tanker trucks, rail cars and barges to accommodate its customers and production needs.
+
Warehouse & Distribution Facility
210,000 square feet of heated dry storage with 16 loading bays and over 40,000 square feet of pallet racking system to maximum storage capacity and handling. Only minutes from our Cicero manufacturing facility making it the ideal storage and handling for all your 3PL needs.

Laboratory
Fully accoutered full service laboratory testing and blending raw materials and finished goods. The lab has basic wet chemistry analysis capabilities along with spectrophotometry, density, volatility, flash point, pour point, freeze point, cold crank, viscosity and other pertinent testing methodology geared towards lubricants and thermal transfer fluids.
