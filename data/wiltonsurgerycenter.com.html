Why Choose Us?

Our physicians and staff at Wilton Surgery Center are dedicated to providing quality, affordable care to the community in their quest to treat an array of orthopedic, back, eye, oculoplastic, gastrointestinal, urology, plastic surgery and general surgery conditions.

Our goal at Wilton Surgery Center is to ensure your comfort and safety by providing a team of board-certified surgeons, anesthesiologists and nurses whose focus is your comfort and care.

Additionally, our patients have highly rated the care they have received from our doctors and staff, and they say they are likely to recommend our center for outpatient procedures. See what they had to say.

Insurance and Billing

Wilton Surgery Center accepts most major insurance. As a courtesy to you, we will bill your insurance carrier for you; however, we cannot tell you what your policy will cover. Since your insurance policy and coverage are between you and your carrier, we urge you to contact them prior to your procedure. You will be responsible for any co-pays and deductibles. We accept cash, check or credit card.
