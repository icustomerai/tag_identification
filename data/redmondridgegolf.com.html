Located 20 miles from Seattle, The Golf Club at Redmond Ridge is the essence of golf in the Seattle area.

Located 20 miles from Seattle, the golf course layout blends the natural beauty of the area with pristine golf conditions and carefully preserved wetlands. Nestled in the watershed of Redmond, Washington, The Golf Club at Redmond Ridge offers 18 holes of championship golf designed by celebrated architect Gary Panks. The course is a testament to the contemporary Northwest golfing experience blending the natural beauty of the area with pristine course conditions and demanding water features. Winding through a network of dense, native forests, and skirting a series of carefully preserved wetlands during the early part of the round, the course ultimately treats players to spectacular views of the Cascade Mountains as they make their way back to the clubhouse .
