There's always a better way

Artex helps you find it.

Connect with Us

To find a better way to reduce risk and grow business in today's competitive climate, you need to think differently. At Artex, challenging business as usual is our daily practice.

We employ a creative approach that transcends models, procedures and routines. A process that is equal parts insight and imagination. This mindset yields better management of risk, better strategies for cost containment and better solutions for (re)insurance and alternative risk.

Our solutions.

Alternative Risk Management

Our award-winning team offers a wide range of alternative risk financing solutions and services designed for large-scale multinationals and small to mid-sized operations in many sectors across the globe, crafted holistically to help clients find a better way to manage and reduce their total cost of risk.

 

Capital Solutions

Our market-leading team provides expertise and a highly responsive service to efficiently connect capital and (re)insurance risk. Working with a wide range of investors and reinsurance buyers/intermediaries, we're the trusted provider of integrated ILS solutions, covering the full spectrum of requirements from fund administration and risk transformation to independent valuation.

Specialty Risk Transfer

We've developed solutions ranging from guaranteed cost to specialized programs that solve for complex risk transfer needs, allowing clients to better manage capacity constraints, product portfolios and total cost of risk.

Carrier Outsourcing

We provide world-class business process outsourcing services to (re)insurance operations of all sizes and complexity. The advantages of outsourcing to the insurance sector include increased efficiency of an organization, reduced business costs and streamlined operations, allowing clients to concentrate on the core competencies of the business.
