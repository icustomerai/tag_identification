    Invest in Your Future in Financial Planning

     

    Gain the knowledge, skills, and credentials to succeed at every stage of your financial planning career. At the industry-leading College for Financial Planning®—a Kaplan Company, you can pursue a professional certification, complete your CFP® certification education, or earn a master’s degree 100% online, on your schedule.

    Highest Quality

    Stay competitive while preparing to create better outcomes for your clients. Our adaptive curriculum evolves with the industry, bringing you groundbreaking programs in areas such as: behavioral finance; domestic partnerships; sustainable, responsible, and impact (SRI) investments; and more.

    Flexibility for Working Adults

    Pursue your goals in a way that fits your lifestyle and needs. Choose from live online classes with interactive learning or self-paced programs you can complete on your own time and with access to our qualified faculty. Whether you’re launching a career or solidifying your expertise, we’ll meet you wherever you are.

    Deep Experience

    Learn from expert faculty with more than 150 years of combined industry experience. As the College that introduced the CERTIFIED FINANCIAL PLANNER™ certification and an early adopter of online education, our HLC-accredited College draws upon a rich history in financial education to empower you to build a secure and rewarding career in financial planning.
