Learn to speak the language of water

When you flush a toilet or water your lawn, the pressure in your plumbing system changes. Phyn measures microscopic water pressure changes in high definition—240 times a second—to recognize the unique “voice” of each fixture in your home. So when you run a faucet, Phyn knows it’s a faucet. And if that faucet leaks, Phyn lets you know.
