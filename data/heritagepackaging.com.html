Who We Help
 

Our customers have products, goods, or items at risk from hazards like corrosion, bio-deterioration, electrostatic discharge, and more.

Transmission of oxygen, moisture vapor, or other elements into or out of the item’s packaging could significantly affect product integrity as it reaches end-users, so the perfect packaging solution must offer the appropriate barrier properties and the right fit.

From sauce pouches and UGR-E bags that keep food stable in transit or on the store shelf, to glove fit covers for entire aircraft or large power generation equipment; we help businesses protect and preserve their critical products.

Complex solutions for high-risk applications.

Our team loves a challenge! Molded fitments welded into perimeter seals of pouches, woven web handles grommeted to barrier bags, two-piece covers hermetically sealed with airtight zippers; high barrier, insulative air-conditioning ductwork, and more- it’s all in a day’s work at Heritage Packaging.
