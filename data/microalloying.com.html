Microalloying

A leading expert in the provision of metallurgical and support services

Recognized throughout the oil and gas industry for consulting expertise in materials, fabrication, and engineering, Microalloying focuses on critical pipelines, both onshore and offshore, and the fabrication and installation of offshore structures. We work with oil and gas organizations, engineering contractors, fabricators, oilfield service companies, and material producers.

In addition to consulting and R&D, Microalloying offers a wide range of metallurgical and support services including development and review of material and fabrication specifications, material pre-qualification testing and analysis, qualification and optimization of welding processes and procedures, and review of NDE specifications.
