InR Process

We use the same process consistently in working with our clients to optimize their retirement program.

Risk Tolerance

Conservative. Moderate. Aggressive. How much risk are you willing to take on? It correlates to your comfort level.

Time Horizon

At what age do you want to retire? The longer the time, research shows that the expected return diminishes.

Financial Need

Have you decided what you want to do after your retire? Do you know how much money you will need to live the life you want?
