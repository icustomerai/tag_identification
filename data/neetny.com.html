Connecting New York to Clean Energy


NextEra Energy Transmission New York, Inc. (NEETNY) is a subsidiary of NextEra Energy Transmission, LLC (NEET), an indirect subsidiary of NextEra Energy, Inc. (NextEra Energy). NextEra Energy is a leading clean-energy company and one of America’s largest infrastructure capital investors in any industry. 

NEETNY is pursuing opportunities to develop, build and operate new transmission facilities to support New York’s clean energy goals.

EMPIRE STATE LINE

A new 20-mile 345 kV transmission line in western New York. The project enables greater utilization of clean, renewable energy from the Robert Moses Hydroelectric Power Plant. Project construction began in March 2021 and was completed and in-service on June 1, 2022.
