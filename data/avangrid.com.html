Avangrid Foundation supports Red Cross

With $250,000 Donation

The Avangrid Foundation recommitted to the American Red Cross National Disaster Responder Program with a $250,000 donation. These funds proactively ensure the Red Cross is prepared to help meet the needs of people both locally and across the United States who are affected by disasters.

AVANGRIDS Pachwywit Fields

Achieves Commercial Operation

Avangrid has achieved commercial operation at its 162 MWac Pachwywit Fields solar farm in Oregon, becoming the states largest photovoltaic plant. The facility will deliver clean, renewable energy to large customers and municipalities enrolled in Portland General Electrics Green Future Impact program.
