    Our Products

    • HYBRID-WALL®

      HYBRID-WALL® was developed as an alternative to window wall systems. Window Wall systems are architecturally limited and lacking in long term performance. HYBRID-WALL® enables aesthetic flexibility by allowing for larger expanses of glazing, flush external appearance (capless) and a variety of features and infill materials.

      Read More

    • THERMO-3 Series

      THERMO-3 Series is our standard unitized curtain wall system. With increasing demand for energy efficient buildings, SOTAWALL is acutely aware of the critical role that building envelopes play in thermal performance. We strive to provide thermally improved design without compromise in aesthetics or cost competitiveness.
