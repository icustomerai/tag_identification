Who we are. What we do.

Founded in 2002, Cequel III is an investment and management firm based in St. Louis, Missouri, and focused on growth-oriented companies in technology, communications, and business services. We’re active, experienced operators and co-investors with a track record of over $40 billion in successful transactions. Our professional management team has a history of building industry-leading companies through operational excellence and a focus on superior customer service.
