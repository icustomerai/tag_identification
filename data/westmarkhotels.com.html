Warm Northern Hospitality

Westmark offers an assortment of full-service Yukon and Alaska hotels that are perfect places to kick up your feet during a business trip or vacation up north. With premium conference and meeting facilities, unparalleled customer service and an array of amenities, the Westmark collection satisfies even the most selective travelers.

  • Anchorage

    Boasting balcony views of both the harbor and surrounding mountains, Westmark Anchorage Hotel is conveniently located right in the heart of downtown Anchorage.

    Visit Anchorage

  • Dawson City

    Westmark Inn Dawson City offers award-winning accommodations, home-cooked dining, and a lounge to unwind in after your day in the middle of the Yukon wilderness.

    Visit Dawson City

  • Denali

    Stay in comfort at the edge of the Denali wilderness at McKinley Chalet Resort,  featuring modern amenities, quality dining and friendly staff.

    Visit Denali

  • Fairbanks

    Take in the Northern Lights in comfort at the Westmark Fairbanks Hotel & Convention Center located in downtown Fairbanks near the Chena River.

    Visit Fairbanks

  • Sitka

    Located in the heart of downtown near the harbor, the Westmark Sitka Hotel is within walking distance to some of Sitka’s most popular tourist attractions.

    Visit Sitka

  • Skagway

    Experience the charm and Alaska warmth of old-time Skagway at Westmark Inn Skagway, located steps away from Klondike Gold Rush National Historic Park.

    Visit Skagway
