Public & Private Construction

The most common use of steel and associated metals is in construction and building renovation. Pennsylvania Steel is a proud resource of such structural materials for both large and small-scale contracting projects. We serve to benefit our communities’ families and residents by manufacturing necessary building supplies for their school districts and homes.

Government & Defense

A number of government-funded projects require the use of high-quality metals, and Pennsylvania Steel is honored to provide these trusted materials. As a veteran-owned company, we recognize the significance in being a dependable source for our country’s military and defense sectors.

Irrigation & Water Supply

Premium metal products are essential for developing common plumbing equipment such as pipes, tubing, and sprinklers. By supplying durable, corrosion-resistant elements, Pennsylvania Steel helps ensure safe irrigation methods and proper water output.
