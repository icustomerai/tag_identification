FRAC-CHEM, based in Lafayette, Louisiana, is an oilfield chemical manufacturer and wholesale chemical supplier specializing in hydraulic fracturing, stimulation, and coiled tubing chemistries.

We offer a full line of liquid chemistries as well as a growing line of dry chemistries, customized field application equipment, and real-time monitoring capabilities. With facilities located across North America and a team committed to providing unrivaled customer service, we stand ready to meet and exceed customers’ needs and expectations– 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year.
