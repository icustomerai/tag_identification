Welcome to Radiology Associates of Hollywood, Inc.

Radiology Associates of Hollywood is the premier provider of imaging services and radiologic procedures in South Florida. Comprised of 65 subspecialty-trained and board-certified physicians, RAH delivers superior and comprehensive radiologic services including Interventional procedures, PET/Nuclear Medicine, MRI, CT, Ultrasound, Women’s Imaging, and Radiation Oncology.

Members of our practice, based in six of Florida’s leading hospitals, utilize state of the art equipment and cutting-edge technology to diagnose and treat numerous medical conditions. Our mission is to provide the highest quality medicine for our patients and the healthcare community in a considerate, caring, and timely manner. Accuracy, attention to detail, professionalism and a commitment to excellence are the trademarks of our practice.
