AssetWorks solutions enable fleet organizations of all sizes to improve maintenance practices, streamline operations and improve accountability for mission-critical transportation assets.

Enterprise Asset Management

AssetWorks EAM is a web-based and fully integrated solution designed to manage all assets and maintenance in one system. Our integrated approach removes the use of multiple programs for different asset types, thereby saving time for staff and streamlining overall operations.

The all-encompassing view within AssetWorks EAM allows for complete lifecycle management and extensive reporting functionality for all assets without sacrificing security or autonomy. It is a completely scalable solution that manages unlimited assets of any type and unlimited users. The intuitive, user-friendly system supports extensive workflows and functionalities to make the users lives easier.

By centralizing these functions, we enable our customers to streamline their facilities management processes, letting them prioritize on delivering the outcomes required by their campus.

Surplus Property Management

AssetWorks' Recoup is the industrys only all-in-one resource recovery platform exclusively for universities and state surplus agencies. With Recoup, surplus teams can streamline surplus management tasks, increase productivity, and meet reporting requirements by managing transfers, sales/reallocations, contacts, and more all in one place.

Recoup is the only solution that combines a complete lifecycle surplus management system with multiple disposal options, work order management, and an easy-to-use interface. It offers the industry's most advanced tools for managing surplus assets, making it easy for teams of any size to track surplus, sell or redistribute property, and generate reports, all in real-time.

AssetWorks' team of surplus management experts' partner with clients to create innovative, cost-reducing and income generating opportunities from the often-overlooked resource of surplus property. Contact us today for a consultation or to investigate the possibilities Recoup can create for your organization.

Government Property Management
