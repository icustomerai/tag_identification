Industry and Operationally-focused Private Equity

ORIX Capital Partners (“OCP”) is an industry and operationally-focused middle-market private equity team dedicated to utilizing a thematic and industry-driven investment approach and in-house operating expertise to buy and build companies of significant and sustainable value. We provide patient capital, access to transformational industry relationships, and strategic and operational guidance to companies in the industrial services and business services sectors.

About Us

A LEADER IN MIDDLE MARKET PRIVATE EQUITY

OCP*, based in New York City, started investing in 2016, and has deployed approximately $750 million of capital since inception. OCP is a subsidiary of ORIX Corporation USA, the U.S. and Latin American hub for Tokyo-based ORIX Corporation, a publicly traded international financial services company (Tokyo: 8591, NYSE: IX) with operations in 28 countries and over $374 billion in assets under management.**   

OCP’s key differentiators enable a rigorous approach to deal sourcing, underwriting, and investment management:

  • Thematic investment approach that leads to direct conversations with sellers and “less competitive” processes
  • Experienced in-house operating partners and industry advisors provide unique due-diligence, underwriting and investment management support, and emphasize strong and supportive partnerships with management teams to improve operations through strategic value creation programs and tactical enhancements, along with deep add-on integration support
  • Collaborative investment team with experience and expertise in finance, operations and strategy across targeted industries, resulting in strong partnerships with aligned management teams
  • Leverage of ORIX’s complementary global capital solutions and proprietary knowledge platforms creates competitive due-diligence edge, including support from ORIX’s shared services platforms (e.g., accounting, tax, legal, compliance, IT and HR)

* OCP is the private equity team of ORIX Advisers, LLC, an SEC registered investment adviser.

** As of September 2022. 

   

Approach

FOCUSED AND DISCIPLINED INVESTMENT APPROACH

OCP targets control investments in North American middle market companies with $20M to $50M of EBITDA where we can invest $100M to $250M of equity.

We are focused on fragmented and asset-light industries ripe for consolidation, including automation solutions, environmental services, infrastructure & utility services, marketing services, and telecom services.

OCP looks for profitable and scalable businesses with promising organic and inorganic growth potential, differentiated products and services, excellent leadership, positive culture, and strong customer relationships.

Our in-house portfolio operations team works closely with management teams to improve operations and increase revenue and profitability through strategic value creation programs.
