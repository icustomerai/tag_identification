How We'll Help With Your Tacoma Property Management Needs

We provide full service Tacoma property management focusing on Single Family, Multi Family and Commercial properties in Pierce, Thurston and South King counties. Our operational expertise, personal support and integrated maintenance team deliver superior results for our clients.

A Property Manager assigned to You

When working with BCI you will have a dedicated property manager assigned to you who is intimately familiar with your specific needs, your property and your tenants. You will not get the run-around between different representatives who may not be up to date. You will have your property manager's cell phone so that any issues can be resolved right away.

Rental Marketing & Tenant Screening

We market our client's properties through a variety of online platforms, our renter database, and other media. We carefully screen each and every applicant to make sure that they meet our stringent criteria. Screening includes obtaining credit reports, criminal background checks, rental history, and income and employment verification.

Rent Collection

We carefully and systematically monitor on time payments using electronic tools. In case of delinquencies we immediately engage with tenants to work to resolve payment issues quickly.

Maintenance

We have our own maintenance division that handles the vast majority of maintenance tasks. That way we can guarantee value and performance and don’t rely on third parties. We have a 24/7 emergency maintenance line for emergency repairs.

Inspections

We conduct thorough inspections throughout the tenancy so that there are no surprises when a tenant moves out. Inspections are done by our own maintenance division and there is a detailed electronic report with detailed pictures and descriptions.

Enforcement

Your Property Manager is continuously engaged with the tenants, answers questions and provides advice. This includes reminding tenants of lease rules and managing enforcement if necessary. The Team also helps set up utility accounts and monitors utility payments.
