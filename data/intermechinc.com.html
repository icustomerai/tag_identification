Quality Assurance Program 

Intermech’s robust Quality Assurance Program is centered around an ASME NQA-1 program that was established in the early 1980’s to serve the construction of commercial nuclear power plants.

The program is regularly audited by both commercial nuclear customers and operators of government nuclear and chemical weapons decommissioning sites, some of which belong to Nuclear Procurement Issues Committee (NUPIC).

Read more about our Quality Assurance Program. 

CAPABILITIES
