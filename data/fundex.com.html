FUNDEX AND INVESTIA MERGER

On July 1, 2021, FundEX Investments Inc. (“FundEX”) merged its mutual fund dealer operations with those of Investia Financial Services Inc. (“Investia”) via a corporate amalgamation. As a result, Investia and FundEX are becoming one single entity known under the name of Investia Financial Services Inc.

Both FundEX and Investia are proud members of iA Financial Group. With this merger, Investia is strengthening its place in the market by becoming the largest independent mutual fund dealer in Canada providing highly personalized and holistic wealth management solutions to clients and ensuring that they meet their investment goals.

The amalgamation of FundEX and Investia reflects our ongoing commitment to optimizing the efficiency of our operations and ensuring a superior client experience, backed by the strength, stability and trust that iA Financial Group has built over more than 125 years of serving the financial needs of Canadians.
