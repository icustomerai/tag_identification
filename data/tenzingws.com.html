Tenzing to Further Align with Breakthru Beverage Starting March 1

Over the last decade, we at Tenzing have had the honor to represent some of the very best brands in the world, while also working alongside the world-class restaurants, bars, and retail shops that make up our tightly knit community in Illinois.  

We are excited to announce that the next step in our evolution is to further align with Breakthru Beverage to form a new fine wine and craft spirits division within Breakthru called Aspect, beginning March 1st, 2021.

We strongly believe that this new structure will allow us to better serve our customers across the state and provide greater scale to our Tenzing supplier partners.
