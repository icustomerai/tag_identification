Alpha Offers Insulation, Waterproofing, and Fireproofing Services Nationwide

A Cornerstone for over forty years

With over forty years of experience in commercial insulation, waterproofing, spray fireproofing, spray foam insulation, firestop, and restoration, Alpha has served as the benchmark for excellence, peerless quality, and unmatched service. Our commitment to team building and quality craftsmanship, along with our ability to serve our clients in a variety of areas, has allowed us to consistently grow for over three decades. In fact, we can perform all of the scopes of work in Division 7 with the exception of roofing.

Today, we serve hundreds of prominent clients throughout the United States and perform an array of services spanning the fields of commercial insulation, waterproofing, spray foam insulation, restoration, firestop, and spray fireproofing. We have also steadily grown to 14 regional offices — Atlanta, Austin, Charlotte, Cincinnati, Dallas/Ft. Worth, Denver, Houston, Miami, Nashville, Orlando, Raleigh, San Antonio, Tampa, and Tulsa serving the entire Southeast and South Central U.S. markets.

Experience the distinct difference with Alpha. With an illustrious history, a portfolio featuring hundreds of loyal clients, and almost everything you need under one roof, you’ll be glad you did. Get started by finding your local Alpha office.
