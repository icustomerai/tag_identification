  Welcome to Suncrest Health Care , A skilled nursing facility located at 22nd Street and Southern Avenue in Phoenix, AZ.  Our Facility has been in the business of caring and healing for many years. We have 24 hour Nursing Care, In-House Laundry Services, In-House Rehabilitation Services, and Home-Cooked Meals.  Please consider our facility for your loved ones. We provide resident-centered care with timely and effective comprehensive services. Our goal is to treat the whole resident and his or her individual needs, helping them achieve their highest possible level of functioning and independence. Our Facility cares for age population ranging from ages 18 and up.
