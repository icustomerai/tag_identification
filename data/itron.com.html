Who We Serve

Combining experience and innovation to deliver results.

Our proven portfolio of smart networks, software, services, meters and sensors helps our customers better manage energy and water for the people they serve.

Improving Multiple City Services in the City of Lights

The City of Lights is deploying a city-wide canopy network, connecting cabinet-based controllers for more than 200,000 street and traffic lights across the city.

Goal: Cut public lighting energy use by 30% over 10 years

Improving Reliability and Efficiency

"The smart grid program is delivering on its promise to generate efficiencies and we are pleased to pass along those savings to our customers by decreasing their electricity delivery costs."

48% reduction in outage duration

Wiser Water Management

A “horrible, horrible” read rate—along with estimates standing in for accurate usage reads on half of its 22,000 water meters—prompted an AMI and analytics overhaul. Read rates soared, and so has Bismarck’s ability to detect and prevent leaks and waste.

Read rates soared to 99.98%

Enhancing Safety by Detecting Methane

"These detectors could help save lives by using smart technology to communicate directly from a customer’s home to our emergency department."

First responders on scene within 30 minutes
