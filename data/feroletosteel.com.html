Welcome to Feroleto Steel Company, Inc.

The Feroleto Steel Company, founded over 50 years ago, is a wholly owned subsidiary of Toyota Tsusho America, Inc. Our company is managed locally with a philosophy that focuses on Quality and Service. Feroleto offers precision rolled strip to meet the custom requirements of our customers. We have grown to be one of the best equipped service centers offering sophisticated and specialized production capabilities. Feroleto currently has plants strategically located in Bridgeport, Connecticut; Weirton, West Virginia and Knoxville, Tennessee to service a broad based clientele.
