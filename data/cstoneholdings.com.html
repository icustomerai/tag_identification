About Cornerstone < back to home

Cornerstone Holdings is a private investment company backed by the Family Office of Tom and Bonnie McCloskey. Its mission is to create long-term wealth for the family by making selective investments into operating businesses and real estate ventures. Due to its discretionary capital, Cornerstone is incredibly opportunistic and capable of being very flexible in how it structures its investments.

We welcome the opportunity to discuss potential investment opportunities and partnerships with professionals with similar goals and mindset.
