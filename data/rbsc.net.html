SOUTHWEST FLORIDA’S LEADING BUILDING SUPPLIER

Since 1957, Raymond Building Supply has provided lumber, trusses, garage doors, windows, residential doors, commercial doors, cabinets, custom millwork and more to building industry professionals.
Our culture is rooted in small business principles and a quality assurance program that ensures customer satisfaction.

Raymond serves the Florida communities from warehouses and showrooms located in Lee, Collier, Sarasota, Hamilton, Broward and Hillsborough counties.  Whether you are in Bonita springs, Fort Myers, Sanibel, Punta Gorda, Boca Grande, Sarasota or Marco Island you’ll find a full line of building supply materials and appreciate our custom installation services.
