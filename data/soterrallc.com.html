About Soterra

Soterra uses advanced silvicultural techniques to improve the productivity of our timberland, which increases the yield of forest products over a period of time. We sell our timber to a variety of customers who manufacture products such as paper and building materials that people use in everyday life.

We pride ourselves on using modern technology, proven research methods and sound management techniques. We are dedicated to providing services that will increase your property’s wildlife, timber and overall land value.
