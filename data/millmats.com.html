Millennium Masters Program

Millennium begins it's internal partner training program, Millennium Masters, which aims to incentivize partners to master their job and the jobs of those around them. With each training and each test, partners can move into the next certification level. Once they achieve Master level partners are given a raise and are seen as someone who can do every job in the department
