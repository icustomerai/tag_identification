A Relentless Obsession to Serve Our Customer

Metal Fabrication Equipment

Welcome to Mac-Tech! We’re your one-stop-shop for metal fabrication equipment. We carry both new and used metal fabrication equipment for sale. From new or pre-owned machines to parts, service, and maintenance, we’re happy to be your partner!

Among our metal fabrication equipment, you can find plasma cutting systems, hydraulic press brakes, fiber lasers, tube cutting machines, metal folders, cut-to-length, deburring and finishing machines, and more. While we have top-of-the-line metal fabrication equipment for sale, we also service every piece of equipment we carry, and then some. No need to rely on manufacturers for service—we are often able to respond much more rapidly to service requests than manufacturers can.

Browse our selection of metal fabrication machinery for sale, or contact us for service.
