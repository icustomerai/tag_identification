Why Choose Bound Tree?

The Right Products

Bound Tree Medical is a national distributor of prehospital emergency medical supplies, equipment, and pharmaceuticals for EMS providers, including First Responders, EMTs and Paramedics. From everyday disposable items to extensive capital equipment, we offer thousands of products across all clinical categories.

Quick links: Curaplex by Bound Tree | Pharmaceutical Backorder Report | Kitting Solutions

The Right Services

Bound Tree offers valuable services to increase efficiency and accuracy, reduce liability and positively impact your bottom line. Services such as inventory and asset management, online ordering for controlled substances, and emergency disaster support can save time and reduce overall costs.

Quick links: Asset Management Solutions | Controlled Substance Ordering | Emergency Disaster Support

The Right Support

Bound Tree is dedicated to providing best-in-class support. This includes a dedicated Account Manager and a team of highly-knowledgeable Customer Service Representatives as well as additional educational resources and continuing education through Bound Tree University.
