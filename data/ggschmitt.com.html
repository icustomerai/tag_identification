A Streamlined Process to Bring Your Ideas to Life

1

Design

Based on the design you provide our team with, our engineering team will break it down to understand what it would take to bring your design to life.


2

Engineering

Once our engineering team has worked to determine if we can make your design concept a reality, we check to make sure it would not only be functional, but up to industry standards.

3

Quote

The quote includes what it costs to develop the prototype model for your product and the production of product thereafter.


4

Prototyping

The prototype is the first tangible version of your design. This will eventually be the model for all future production, but we want to make sure you are happy with your design.

5

Approval

We will work together closely during the prototyping phase to get things just right. Once that is complete you will give final approval for your product to go into production.

6

Production

We will use your prototype to fabricate the first few versions of your product then have you install them to reaffirm the fit, form, and function before we fabricate the entire order.
