Custom PIT Tag Monitoring Solutions

Biomark specializes in the design, construction, and installation of custom PIT tag systems for terrestrial and aquatic use. Our experience allows us to design and test numerous antenna sizes and configurations to meet the needs of unique monitoring applications. Whether your site is in a metropolis or on a remote mountaintop, we can provide a monitoring solution. We have been building customized PIT tag detection systems for nearly 30 years. No project is too big or small and our carefully selected components collectively result in a robust and reliable system. Take a look at a few of our terrestrial, aquatic and custom system solutions or contact our team [email protected] to discuss your project needs.
