HOLDINGS

Wasp began with the desire to provide easy, straightforward, and error-free tracking for small to medium-sized businesses. Because most tracking systems were designed for large businesses, small companies were left to manage asset and inventory information manually. Wasp recognized this as an opportunity and quickly moved from providing only barcode hardware to offering complete barcode systems.

Wasp provides asset, inventory, and time & attendance systems that are fairly priced, easily implemented, and ready-to-use after installation. More than 500,000 customers have purchased Wasp hardware and software systems since 1994 and over 2000 resellers throughout North America, Europe, and Australia supply products that are distinctive because of their outstanding quality.
