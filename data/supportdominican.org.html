Welcome

Please look around our site to learn about the many ways we support Dominican Hospital. 

As a non-profit hospital, Dominican Hospital relies on voluntary contributions to help meet the changing healthcare needs of our community and keep pace with the most advanced technology.

Donations, large and small, enable the Foundation to:

  • Build and support health education and outreach programs
  • Help provide the very best medical equipment and technology
  • Provide access to emergency services
  • Support hospital key service lines
  • Make available critical care for life-threatening illnesses
