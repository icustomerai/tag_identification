Economics of the Lockdown System

337.839.0900

If total rig operations cost $5,000/hr.

Allow 2.5 hrs. to rig down bell nipple system and nipple up a companion flange to the top of the annular B.O.P.

Allow 2.5hrs. to nipple down companion flange and put bell nipple system back in place.

The cost in down time when using a conventional companion flange lubricator:
Total of 5 hrs. down time @ $5,000/hr. = $25,000 each login operation – lost

The Lockdown Lubricator System installs in just minutes because the Lockdown Flange is already installed as part of the rig’s bell nipple system. The Lockdown Lubricator is simply lowered through the bell nipple until the Lockdown Mandrel lands in the flange. The mandrel is then locked into the flange. The lubricator is now anchored and the annular B.O.P. can effect a seal around the annular seal extension of the mandrel.

$25,000 each login operation – saved
