Welcome to Lawyers Title

Lawyers Title of Cincinnati, Inc. has offered responsible, reliable Real Estate representation for the commercial and residential real estate industry since 1936. We are the largest independent title agency in southwestern Ohio – handling more residential closings than any other independent agency in the region.

We offer the highest quality service available for complete residential and commercial closings, escrow, title, and title insurance services. Every client gets personalized attention, and our due diligence ensures your transaction process is as seamless as possible.

The experience and expertise of our title professionals enables Lawyers Title of Cincinnati, Inc. to handle every size and type of real estate project. Our national contacts with Fidelity National Title Group, First American Title Insurance Company, Stewart Title, and through the law firm of Griffin Fletcher & Herndon, LLP empower us to assist our commercial and residential clients with transactions throughout the United States.
