Featured Job Roles

For more than two decades MeasureUp has helped millions of students reach their goals. Now, we want to help you enter the job market, with concrete paths to the most in-demand IT Job Roles.

IT Administrator

You will be in charge of implementing, managing, and monitoring your company’s cloud environments and solutions. Get the skills and knowledge needed to build your career as a successful IT Administrator.

get started

Software Developer

You will be responsible for designing, installing, testing, and maintaining software systems. The programs you create will help your company be more efficient and provide a better service.

get started

Solution architect

You will be in charge of evaluating your company's business needs and determining how IT can support those needs by leveraging software, hardware, and infrastructure.

get started

IT Engineer

You will be responsible for testing, configuring, and troubleshooting hardware, software, and networking systems to meet the needs of your company. Get the skills and knowledge needed to build your career as a successful IT Engineer.

get started

Functional Consultant

As a Functional Consultant, you will be in charge of creating and configuring apps, automations, and solutions. You will act as the liaison between users and the implementation team.

get started

Business Analyst

As a Business Analyst, you will be responsible for helping your company understand the current state of its business by interpreting a wide range of data. You will work with data to help your company make better business decisions.

get started
