Chilled & Frozen Meat

Our Meat Group has expertise in the procurement, transportation and export of chilled and frozen pork, beef and other meat products to various food processing companies, other distributors and grocery retail chains primarily in Japan and other Asian countries.

Learn More
 

Agricultural Products

The Agricultural Group is also an international and domestic importer and distributor focusing on a variety of frozen vegetables including sweet corn, precooked frozen french fried potatoes and other agricultural products. The Agricultural Group also exports frozen berries and tropical fruit, sourced from various suppliers in North and South America.

Tamaki Rice

Exclusive domestic and international distributor of Tamaki brand rice an award winning premium short grain rice milled by the Tamaki Rice Corporation in Northern California.

Japanese Grocery Products
