About Coastal GasLink

The 670 kilometre long Coastal GasLink Pipeline will transport natural gas to the approved LNG Canada facility near Kitimat. The pipeline route was determined by considering Indigenous, landowner and stakeholder input, the environment, archaeological and cultural values, land use compatibility, safety, constructability and economics.
