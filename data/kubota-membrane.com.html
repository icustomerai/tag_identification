Kubota Membrane USA Corporation is a Membrane Bioreactor (MBR) system supplier utilizing the Kubota Submerged Membrane Unit® (SMU). The Kubota SMU was developed to treat municipal, commercial and industrial wastewater simply and successfully. Click here to learn how using our SMUs in an MBR system lets you achieve a reuse quality effluent in a compact footprint.
