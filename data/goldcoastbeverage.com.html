Gold Coast Beverage

Connecting Customers, Consumers And Brands Every Day, Everywhere We Operate

Gold Coast Beverage joined Reyes Beverage Group in 2015 and operates from facilities in Doral, Pompano and Rockland Key. We are the largest beer wholesaler in Florida and one of the largest beer distributors in the U.S., offering domestic, import and craft brands. Located in Southeast Florida, our territory covers Palm Beach, Broward, Miami-Dade and Monroe counties and services more than 30 million cases to 11,720 licensed beverage accounts.

Who We Are

We’re huge fans of beer and the outdoors. We cover territory as north as Tequesta, as south as Key West, as west as Weston and as east as the beautiful beaches up and down the coast! We know what South Floridians love to drink while at the beach, on the boat, at a BBQ or out on the town. We’re part of a national network, but we live local and stay true to our South Florida values, dedicating our resources to developing our employees and helping surrounding communities.
