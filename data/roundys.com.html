Roundy’s, Inc., (Roundy’s) is a leading grocer in the Midwest. Roundy’s is a wholly-owned subsidiary of the Kroger Co. (NYSE:KR), located in Cincinnati, Ohio. Founded in Milwaukee in 1872, Roundy’s operates 149 retail grocery stores under the retail banners Pick ‘n Save and Metro Market in Wisconsin and Mariano’s in Illinois.
The two largest banners are Pick 'n Save, with over 100 locations throughout the State of Wisconsin, and Mariano's, with over 40 locations throughout the Chicagoland area.
