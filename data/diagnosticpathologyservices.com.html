OUR MISSION

Our mission at Diagnostic Pathology Services is to deliver timely diagnoses to patients and physicians utilizing cutting-edge methods available to modern laboratory specialists.

Pathology Team

All DPS pathologists are board certified in anatomic and clinical pathology, and the group includes specialists in dermatopathology, cytopathology, breast pathology, gynecological pathology, immunopathology, gastrointestinal pathology, molecular pathology, and hematopathology.
