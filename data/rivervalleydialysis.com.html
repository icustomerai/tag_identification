Services

Our Nurse Manager is a Registered Nurse (RN) and a Certified Dialysis Nurse. Our nurse to patient ratio is one nurse per three patients.

In addition to our nursing staff we have a Dietician and Social Worker available to meet all of your dietary and social needs. All dialysis care is overseen by our Medical Director Dr. Fadi Yacoub from Internist Associates of Iowa.

Setting

We have a caring, homelike setting.

Each of our dialysis chairs has an individual television set with cable for your enjoyment.

We also offer free wireless internet within the unit.

Hours

We currently provide dialysis on Mondays, Wednesdays, and Fridays in the mornings and afternoons.

We are Medicare Certified.
