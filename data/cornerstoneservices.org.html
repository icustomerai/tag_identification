Services for the disabled

Providing the opportunity to live and work in the community

Advocates in Will and Kankakee County

Promoting choice, dignity, and opportunity to people with disabilities.

Last year we served over 1,000 people with disabilities, provided homes to over 200 homeless, and found jobs for 172 jobless. Cornerstone Services provides progressive, comprehensive services to people with disabilities promoting choice, dignity, and the opportunity to live and work in the community. Since 1969, Cornerstone has been a leader in meeting the unique needs of individuals with developmental disabilities, mental illnesses, physical disabilities and sensory impairments.
