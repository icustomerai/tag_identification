From search and placement to strategy, and everything in between, the ECL Group of Companies has the expertise and years of experience to deliver professional talent management solutions throughout every step of your business’ evolution.

We are strategic advisors

Offering the services and support you need to attract and retain the talent who drive your success. We're dedicated to learning about every aspect of your business and providing you with long-term guidance and solutions to conquer any organizational challenge.

Our goal is simple: to provide the professional, expert support that enables you and your team to achieve your vision.

Developing an organization’s strongest asset—its people—has been our focus since we opened our doors in 1974. From our foundations as an entrepreneurial recruitment firm, Executrade Consultants, we have evolved into a multi-divisional company that provides exceptional people solutions, customized to meet our clients’ needs.

Over the years, we’ve grown to become one of Canada’s most comprehensive people firms. Now, the ECL Group acts as a strategic partner, working alongside each of our clients to find the integrated talent management solutions that work across their businesses.

Our Companies

ECL Group includes five specialized companies, each focused and dedicated to specific talent management needs.

Executrade is Canada’s leading professional recruitment firm.

As a dynamic team, we can manage a broad range of staffing requirements and at the same time provide niche-specific expertise and focus.

Our team offers professional recruitment services for permanent, temporary, and contract positions, with a level of quality and tenacity unmatched in the professional recruitment marketplace.
