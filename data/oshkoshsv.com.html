Your Partner in Mobile Training Solutions

Since 1946, Oshkosh Specialty Vehicles (OSV), a division of Pierce Manufacturing, Inc. has produced thousands of mobile training stations and special purpose vehicles to advance military, broadcast, government, and display missions.

Our ability to design, manufacture, integrate, and field specialized mobile solutions around specific mission demands is unequaled.  With 100,000 square feet of dedicated manufacturing space certified to ISO 9001:2015 standards and Safety Management System – Level III, each mobility unit is crafted under the most stringent standards in Clearwater, Florida.  Whatever you require – a customized vehicle body, expanding trailers and shelters, containers, or extensions and retrofits, our design engineers have the expertise and capabilities to get it exactly right.

  • State-of-the-art 3D modeling tools for precision design and analysis of all critical components
  • EMI enclosures
  • Slide-out sections expand interior space
  • Completely customized electrical system for maximum operator control
  • Communication system design and integration
  • Equipment programming and full mechanical and systems training
  • 10-year structural body warranty

OSV is proudly owned by Oshkosh Corporation Company (NYSE:OSK), a publicly traded Fortune 500 company and one of the largest automotive truck builders in the USA. As an Oshkosh Corporation company, our foundation is rock solid, which positions us in having a unique ability to leverage manufacturing, procurement, and engineering resources across the breadth of Oshkosh Corporation, with the ability to maintain a small company focus and the flexibility to better serve clients in our niche markets.
