FALL SEMESTER

Learn more about how to become a student for the Fall Semester. Whether you just graduated from high school or have been out of the classroom for a few years, SUNY Schenectady is here to help you find your path.

HEALTHCARE TRAININGS

Build your career in healthcare with the Office of Workforce Development and Community Education's healthcare training courses. There is a wide variety of trainings to choose from, including the new Certified Clinical Medical Assistant program.

SUMMER NON-CREDIT COURSES

Registration is now open for summer non-credit courses offered through the Division of Office of Workforce Development and Community Education. From computers to fitness, archaeology to courses for kids and teens, there is something available for everyone.

DEGREES AND CERTIFICATES

Explore all the degrees, certificate, and microcredentials that SUNY Schenectady has to offer. Whether you're looking to enroll in a program or just take a class or two, SUNY Schenectady can help you find your path.
