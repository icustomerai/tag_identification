INVEST IN THE PLANET

Investing in the planet has never been more important.

We aim to respond to society‚Äôs main needs through the provision of sustainable infrastructure solutions that will have a positive impact on people and the planet for generations to come.

    ACCIONA has been delivering sustainable infrastructure solutions in Canada since the year 2000. Our leadership and experience span the entire value chain, from research and development to the design, financing, construction, operation and maintenance of large-scale, complex projects.

    From the Saint John Safe Clean Drinking Water Project in New Brunswick, which supplies 75 million liters of safe drinking water to the community every day, to the award-winning Royal Jubilee Hospital Patient Care Centre in Victoria, B.C., to the Broadway Subway Project currently under construction in Vancouver that will take cars off roads and reduce greenhouse gas emissions, our goal is to have a positive impact on the community and environment with every step.
