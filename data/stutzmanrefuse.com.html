Providing commercial waste removal and residential trash collection

Whether you need a roll-off container for your business or construction site, front load trash bins for your special event, twice a week commercial waste and recycling services, weekly single or multi-family residential waste collection and recycling pickup, or even a compactor for your restaurant, Stutzman Refuse Disposal offers a variety of affordable services and plans that can be customized to suit your waste removal needs.


Plus, when it comes to residential recycling service and commercial recycling service, Stutzman Refuse Disposal is the only single stream recycling plant in all of Kansas to accept and process glass on site. We grind the glass down to a sand-like form and haul it to Johns Manville in McPherson, Kansas.


Stutzman Refuse Disposal is a locally operated Central Kansas waste management company owned by Waste Connections, a national leader in residential trash collection, single-stream recycling and construction and commercial waste removal services. Dedicated to our customers and the communities in which we live and work, we proudly serve Reno, Rice, and Barton Counties including the municipalities of Hutchinson, South Hutchinson, Buhler, Lyons, Pretty Prairie, Sterling, Nickerson, Hoisington, and many more.
