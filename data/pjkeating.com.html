Products

P.J. Keating is a leading supplier of quality crushed aggregates, hot mix asphalt and recycled products throughout Massachusetts, Rhode Island and Southern New Hampshire.

P.J. Keating supplies the raw materials needed over a vast network of project and market types. Our products can be found throughout the construction industry from the ground up, from general site construction to finished retail products.

Services

From interstate and local highways to commercial parking lots, P.J. Keating Company's Paving and Cold Planing Division is a proven and award-winning leader. With main-line paving crews, hand work crews, grading crews, milling and cold planing crews, traffic control crews, and quality control services, P.J. Keating Company can meet every paving requirement in Southern New Hampshire, Massachusetts or Rhode Island. P.J. Keating Company is equipped with the latest state-of-the-art pavers, rollers, graders and cold planers.

Welcome to P.J. Keating

Our modern facilities, veteran paving crews and large investment in plants and equipment make us one of the largest and most efficient producers in the state. We are MassDOT and RIDOT prequalified and have worked on millions of dollars of MassDOT and RIDOT projects in our area. We are proud of our work at airports, shopping malls, industrial sites and subdivisions throughout Massachusetts, Rhode Island, and southern New Hampshire.
