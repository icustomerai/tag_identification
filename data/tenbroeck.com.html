Why Choose Ten Broeck?

Behavioral health and addiction treatment is vital to our communities - now more than ever. We provide a healing path for anyone that struggles with these challenges. Together we educate our communities, and address the stigma surrounding behavioral health.

Ten Broeck's mission is to improve the health and well-being of the people we service by creating comprehensive, community-based behavioral programs that are integrated with primary care services. Everyday, our talented and compassionate professionals help individuals and families in crisis to improve their mental health - in the most clinically appropriate and cost-effective ways possible.
