Who We Are

AJD Forest Products LP in Grayling, Michigan, is the original Lakes States industrial hardwood lumber sawmill. We produce high-grade red oak, hard and soft maple, aspen, ash, and basswood. Since 1974, we've been the producers of the best quality industrial lumber in the Lake States.

For more than 40 years, our reputation for quality has never wavered. Many of our customers have been with us for more than 20 years. When you want a good product to process in your plant and not have to deal with a lot of waste, you need to buy from us.

Contact us in Grayling, Michigan, to request more information about our lumber.
