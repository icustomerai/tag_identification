Welcome to Foley RV Center

And Airstream of Mississippi

As the only Airstream RV Dealer in Mississippi, Foley RV Center and Airstream of Mississippi is located in Gulfport, Mississippi between New Orleans, Louisiana, and Mobile, Alabama.

Nearly Half a Century in the Business

We have been in the automobile industry for over 40 years. Since 2011, our family owned and operated RV dealership has served the Gulf Coast with a focus on building lasting relationships. Our staff is knowledgeable and experienced with all RV makes and models. We are a community-centered dealership conveniently located for both local and out of town customers whether they are in need of service and parts or the perfect RV.

Endless Options

Foley RV Center and Airstream of Mississippi is proud to have an extensive inventory for our customers to choose from, and we can even customize designs to meet your specific needs. As a premier Gulf Coast RV dealer, we stock a large variety of sizes and models of Airstream, Thor, Heartland, and Winnebago brands. Our extensive stock of Airstream models includes Eddie Bauer, Bambi, Flying Cloud, Classic, Sport, International Serenity, and International Signature. Peruse and test drive our many makes and models.

Knowledgeable Service and Parts Department

Our team can accommodate nearly all types of service issues you may encounter. We specialize in stock and high-performance fifth wheels, motorhomes, travel trailers, and more. We also carry a full line of generators. Our service department will provide you with all of the essential services to maintain your RV as well as any parts you may need. We also honor all warranties and recalls.

Conveniently Located

We serve a 300-mile radius around the Gulf South, providing quality RVs to the entire coast, from Gulfport to Biloxi to Jackson, and every city in between. With our location just 1 mile north of I-10 on Hwy 49N, our sales, service, and parts departments are easy to get to from Destin and Panama City, Florida; Atlanta, Georgia; Birmingham, Alabama; and New Orleans, Lafayette, and Baton Rouge, Louisiana.

Come see us today! We will help you find the RV, travel trailer, or camper that is right for you!
