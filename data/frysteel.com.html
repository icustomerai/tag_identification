HISTORY

A Reputation Like
No Other

The metal company began in Los Angeles as the Allen-Fry Steel Company. In 1971, John Fry bought out Bill Allen to become what is now the Fry Steel Company. Today, Fry Steel relies on more than 70 years of experience and passion for customer satisfaction to make sure they are the first steel supplier people call on for metal solutions and specialized steel products.

1948

William Allen and John Fry created
The Allen Fry Steel Company

1971

John Fry Buys out Steve Allen and
rebrands the company as Fry Steel

1979

We relocated our steel business to Santa Fe Springs
(Our Current Location)

2002

Built an additional warehouse facility
next to our main building to support our customers’
growth and our expanding inventory of steel metals (31,500 SQ FT)

2019

Fry Steel was bought
by Reliance Steel & Aluminum Co.

 
 

A Vision for
the Future

Fry Steel Company was family-owned until 2019. The company was acquired and now operates as a wholly-owned subsidiary of Reliance Steel & Aluminum Co. But the mission remains. Fry Steel Company is committed to providing customers with superior customer service, high-quality products, and innovative solutions, in order to be a top metal supplier in the United States.
