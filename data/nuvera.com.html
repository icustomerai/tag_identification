      Nuvera China

      China has become one of largest fuel cell markets in recent years. Nuvera has established an in-country presence in conjunction Hyster-Yale Group and with local partners.

      The Nuvera China team is poised to work with local and international companies to integrate Nuvera® fuel cell engines into medium- and heavy-duty electric vehicles and zero-emission platforms. Our offerings in China include the following:

      • Fuel cell engine products
      • Application assessments and consulting services
      • Integration support
      • Fuel cell systems training and certification
      • Aftermarket service capabilities
      • Market development for fuel cell vehicles and other equipment
