About DCMC

At Dallas County Medical Center we strive for excellence in everything we do. We provide quality care to all citizens of all ages. We are always striving to meet the changing health care needs of Dallas County and the surrounding area. At DCMC, we are committed to every patient receiving high quality health care, close to home!
