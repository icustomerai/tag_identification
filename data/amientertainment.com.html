IT ALL BEGINS WITH GREAT MUSIC

We help bars and restaurants create engaging customer experiences.

At AMI, music is at the heart of everything we do. We’re a digital jukebox music network and customer engagement platform that tens of thousands of commercial businesses turn to today, to elevate their atmosphere.

EXPLORE OUR LATEST JUKEBOXES

Available now through our nationwide network of Amusement Operators

Powered by strong partnerships with major and independent labels, the AMI music catalog enables patrons to access every album and song imaginable or just the select few that best soundtrack a venue’s brand.

Supporting the needs of businesses looking to stand out from the crowd, AMI remains the only digital jukebox company offering music video capabilities to U.S. Amusement Operators and the locations they serve.

With nearly half a billion songs served per year, the AMI network continuously rocks around the clock, routinely processing over 150,000 transactions per hour and engaging over 2 million app users.
