Millennium Surgical Center is an advanced outpatient surgery center focused on the specialties of orthopedics, spine, pain management and podiatry. Our state-of-the-art ambulatory surgery center opened in October of 2007 with the goal of providing patients with the highest quality of care in a safe and convenient environment. Our facility is equipped with state-of-the-art operating rooms and is staffed by highly qualified personnel.

The facility is designed to meet the needs of our patients and physicians. Our goal is to provide patients with convenient, friendly and high quality care. We think you're going to like being treated at Millennium.  Ambulatory surgery centers (ASC's) have continued to rise in popularity over the years thanks to the convenience, efficiency and quality of care they offer. Some of the additional benefits of ASC's include improved accessibility, an emphasis on personalized attention and reduced infection rates when compared to acute care hospitals.

Patients are referred to the Millennium Surgical Center for treatment by their physician. Contact your primary care doctor or call (856) 751-4555 for additional information.

Millennium Surgical Center is located at 2090 Springdale Road, Suite A in Cherry Hill.

Joint Commission accredited

The Millennium Surgical Center is proud to be an accredited ambulatory surgical center by The Joint Commission. The Joint Commission accreditation and certification is recognized nationwide as a symbol of quality that reflects our commitment to high performance standards of patient care.

The Joint Commission is an independent, not-for-profit organization that accredits and certifies more than 15,000 health care organizations and programs in the United States.

The mission of The Joint Commission is to continuously improve the safety and quality of care provided to the public through the provision of health care accreditation and related services that support performance improvement in health care organizations.
