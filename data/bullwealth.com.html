Risk Attitude Questionnaire

BULLWEALTHâs Risk Attitude Questionnaire helps us make an objective assessment of your attitude towards risk. By completing BULLWEALTHâs Risk Attitude Questionnaire, we can assess what your optimal asset allocation is, then build a portfolio to target your risk/return objectives to achieve your overall financial goals.

Risk can be split into two parts: Risk Capacity â The ability to take risk and Risk Attitude â The willingness to take risk.

Risk Capacity relates to your financial circumstances and goals where the higher your level of wealth and income, and the longer your horizon, the higher your risk capacity, while Risk Attitude is more to do with your psychology than your financial circumstances. BULLWEALTHâs Risk Attitude Questionnaire focuses on your risk attitude and your comfort with volatility. The more risk you are willing to take, the higher the volatility and potential return of your portfolio; however the higher your risk, the greater the potential for loss. Lower risk offers greater stability but lower potential returns.

BULLWEALTHâs Risk Attitude Questionnaire produces a score ranging from 0 to 100, where 0 is most risk-averse and 100 is most risk-tolerant.
