Commercial Electrical Design, Installation and Support

Comprehensive solutions for electrical construction, Solar PV, telecom, prefabrication and procurement

Whether you have a new, remodel, or tenant improvement project, EIG focuses on delivering exceptional results for your technically challenging and sustainable projects nationwide. Our core markets include: Advanced Technology, Life Sciences, Healthcare, Higher Education and Commercial.

Our team of highly skilled and well-trained technical builders and technicians adhere to the highest standards of quality. From conception through implementation and post-installation support, we work with you to ensure your design goals and expectations are met and exceeded.

What we do

  • Electrical Construction - Utilizing Virtual Design and Construction (VDC) techniques and more, meet the needs of all through our best in class design, installation and maintenance of electrical systems.

  • Solar PV - Through consulting, design, engineering, procurement or a full design build, we can take a blank rooftop, lot or piece of land and commission a full PV system that meets your needs.

  • Prefabrication - Increase your project control, quality and safety with standard assemblies created in a controlled environment. Speed to market with efficiency, schedule control and flexibility.

  • Procurement - Stay on track to meet milestones through proactive material management.

  • Telecom/LV - Background, installation and structured cabling for a variety of projects including small cable plants to campus wide backbone fiber optic installations. Horizontal Cat 6A, Cat 6, Cat5e, copper and more.

  • Service - LED retrofits, energy assessments, emergency services, maintenance services and contracts, panel prefabrication, electrical service upgrades, temporary power and more.

With the help of Evergreen Innovation Group (EIG), LLC's prefabrication team, customers can have more control and flexibility in their building projects with a customizable modular electrical room. The engineered-to-order prefab electrical room includes floor, walls, interior finishes, interconnections, and internal wiring and can range in size from a full administration room to a smaller room.
