From Engineering to Execution

We work with you to support your business objectives, productivity, quality and environmental standards. We work with you to achieve success and sustainability.

Our 123,000 sq. ft. facility in York, Pennsylvania meets the highest environmental standards offering state of the art repair services for industrial equipment. Our facilities have modern large capacity machine-tools to meet all your overhaul and maintenance challenges.

  • Thermal spray application
  • Welding repairs
  • Inspection and NDT

  • Bearing babbitting
