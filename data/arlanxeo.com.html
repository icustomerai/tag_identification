AUTOMOTIVE

Whether it’s under the hood, in the dashboard, or in the tires, synthetic rubber gets vehicles going. The butyl rubber X_Butyl®, the butadiene and styrene-butadiene rubbers Buna® optimize tire sidewalls, treads, and inner liners. The EPDM rubber Keltan® seals windows and doors. And hoses and lines with Levapren® EVM rubber and Therban® HNBR rubber in the sheathing withstand heat and oil.

Read more

Industrial Goods

Hidden all-rounders – technical rubber products are inconspicuous but very effective. Be they in a special cable high up in a windmill or deep down in a submarine. Or as a seal in pumps and oil pans or in conveyor belts for coal, letters, or luggage. Or as drive belts in printing machines, sawmills, and motors or as dampers for ship couplings and rail vehicles.
