Growing Forward

Winslow Capital is a growth equity investment firm grounded in fundamental, long-term investing.

In all of our strategies, a dedication to serving the needs of our clients is the key principle that drives our business. Through a disciplined approach and deep fundamental research, we aim to identify compelling opportunities in public and private markets globally. By identifying and investing in growth companies that aspire to transform industries, sectors, customers and even society, we can deliver on our clients goals.
