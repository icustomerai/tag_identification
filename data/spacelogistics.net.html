Welcome to Space Logistics


Space Logistics is a company specialized in providing door to door transportation, distribution and logistics services between Mexico, USA and Canada (Nafta). We are the most competitive option for door to door LTL (Less Than Truck Load) and parcel shipments within the NAFTA market. Furthermore, we offer FTL (Full Truck Load) transportation service and additional value added logistics and distribution services, such as merchandise consolidation, sourcing, order assembly, pool distribution, warehousing, pick and pack, local transportation among other services through our warehouses in Guadalajara, Jalisco, Mexico and Laredo, TX, USA. We are proud to offer a service which is simple and accessible for your company and your shipments.

Our Advantage


Effective communication, service, flexibility and reliability are the reasons why this company works everyday to offer you high quality transportation and distribution services, and to continually be your best logistics option for all of your shipments within the NAFTA Market (Mexico, USA and Canada).





Service Features


For your international shipments (within Nafta, Mexico, USA and Canada), Space Logistics offers a wide arrangement of door to door services with which you will be able to cover in an optimal way, all your transportation needs.
