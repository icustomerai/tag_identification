Welcome to CRC Canada Co.

CRC Canada Co. has been providing quality chemical maintenance products for the Canadian Automotive, Marine, and Industrial markets for the past 30 years. Our retail customers include Grainger, NAPA Auto Parts, Fastenal, and Canadian Tire. We also provide chemical products and solutions for all organization types.  CRC Canada's head office is located in Toronto, Ontario, with regional offices in Calgary, AB, and Montreal, Quebec.  We also have a network of representatives in British Columbia, Manitoba, Nova Scotia and New Brunswick.

CRC Canada Co. is a leader in the automotive and car care industry supplying the number one, original brake parts cleaner, Brākleen® to professional technicians and DIY’ers across the country. CRC’s broad automotive product offering includes specialty chemicals for these major service categories: brakes, electrical system, fuel system, engine, cooling system, transmission, and power steering system. CRC also supplies general purpose lubricants and penetrants, cleaners and degreasers, corrosion protectants, tools and shop supplies, and appearance products. We have a large line of products for diesel and heavy truck needs.

CRC's marine and boat care product line offers an extensive selection of professional grade marine maintenance chemicals that includes boat cleaners, fogging oils, corrosion inhibitors, lubricants, fuel stabilizers, mildew stain removers.  Our top selling ON & OFF Hull & Bottom Cleaner, as well as Salt Terminator® Engine Flush Cleaner & Corrosion Inhibitor, Stor-X™ Commercial Grade Fuel Stabilizer; amongst other premium products.

CRC Canada’s Industrial Division supplies professional grade specialty products, oils and chemicals for  Industrial & Electrical, Mining, Food & Beverage, Oil & Gas, Utilities, Commercial & Institutional, and Aviation.  We also specialize in CFIA approved chemical solutions, such as food grade oils and products in the food processing and services industries.  Our product offerings include contact cleaners, aqueous, water based cleaners, heavy duty degreasers, Smartwasher parts washing systems, industrial strength lubricants, corrosion inhibitors and oils and greases.

In addition to quality chemical products, CRC Canada offers problem solving solutions to our customers. The MRO Chemicals Risk Reduction Partnership Plan™ is specifically designed to help organizations save money and hassle on the acquisition, use, and disposal of maintenance chemicals. The StopLight™ Food Safety Program is a colour coding system provides the factory floor control of their chemicals, and ensure the right product is used for food industry applications.
