Naoya and Marie Yoshida’s Winchester Farm features the best the Bluegrass has to offer your Thoroughbred horses: the same soil, grass and water that nourished racing greats BOLD FORBES and MARKET BOOSTER and a convenient location less than 10 minutes from world-class equine hospitals, stallion farms and sales venues. Add in the owners’ impeccable pedigrees—heavy on equine medicine and global expertise—and you have a truly special Thoroughbred operation.

Winchester Farm provides outstanding care for domestic and international broodmares, weanlings, yearlings and sales prospects. The Yoshidas also act as agents at sales around the world, upholding the highest level of integrity in their business dealings.
