Experience and Expertise

At Whippany Actuation Systems, we put our experience and expertise to work designing and manufacturing specialized electromechanical actuation components, systems, and subsystems for commercial and military applications.

Actuation Systems

Whippany Actuation Systems supports flight critical and auxiliary electromechanical actuation requirements across all military and commercial applications, including passenger aircraft, combat UAVs, missile, eVTOLs and helicopters.

Flight Control

Primary and secondary flight control components

Landing Gear

Landing gear actuation components for manned and unmanned commercial and military aircraft

Utility

Utility motion control components including doors, latches, valves, and blade folding

Land System

Military land system actuation components including leveling, boom extension, radar array, and azimuth drive gear systems
