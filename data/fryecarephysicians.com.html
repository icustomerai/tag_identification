About FryeCare Physicians Network

The members of the FryeCare Physicians Network are proud to offer quality health care in a compassionate environment - close to home. Our network serves patients throughout the region, covering seven counties across Western North Carolina. We are committed to getting to know each patient as an individual in order to create a partnership in care designed around your specific needs.

Thank you for your interest in the FryeCare Physicians Network. We are honored and proud to be your first choice when you need health care.

FryeCare Physicians Network

It is the policy of FryeCare Physicians Network to admit and treat all residents without regard to race, color, national origin, sex, age or handicap.
