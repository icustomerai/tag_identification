Jordan's Furniture – not just a store, an experience! 

Jordan's Furniture offers the largest selection of quality name-brand furniture and mattresses in New England. Visit any of our locations in Connecticut, Massachusetts, Maine, New Hampshire or Rhode Island and experience the Jordan’s difference. Stroll through hundreds of designer-inspired living room furniture setups with sofas, sectionals, accent chairs, recliners, coffee tables, entertainment centers, and even ottomans. Choose from thousands of fabric, leather, and wood finish options that fit your style. Create your ideal dining space by perusing our dining room furniture. Choose a table, side chairs, server, or an entire dining room set to suit your unique style. We have an extensive selection of bedroom furniture, including dressers, nightstands, armoires, and more for everyone in the family. Let our Sleep Lab Technicians help you select the perfect mattress from the best brands in the industry. Our display of home office furniture will give you the urge to be productive. Our desks, file cabinets and bookcases are both stylish and functional. Have fun selecting the perfect pool table or gaming table for your family room. Select a bar, pub table or maybe even theatre seating to enjoy with family and friends. If you're looking to create the ultimate backyard retreat, explore our collection of outdoor furniture. Whether your style is rustic country, traditional or contemporary, we have the ideal furniture to complete your space. Jordan's Furniture offers the best prices every day on all of our furniture, area rugs, and more. Each store offers a unique experience for the entire family to enjoy. Have an adventure at one of our three ropes courses in Reading, New Haven, or South Portland. Visit one of our furniture stores and see for yourself why Jordan's Furniture is not just a store, it's an experience!
