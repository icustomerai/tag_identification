FOR 30 YEARS, WE HAVE BEEN HELPING LEADING BRANDS, MANUFACTURERS AND RETAILERS STRENGTHEN THEIR RELATIONSHIPS WITH CONSUMERS BY CREATING BREAKTHROUGH PRODUCTS AND EXPERIENCES VIA THE LICENSING MODEL.

 
 

The power of licensing

Trademark licensing has emerged as a major brand communications strategy. It has become integral to many major organizations’ marketing efforts, motivating consumers to invite a brand into their lives and to become brand loyalists. It is a powerful marketing tool that effectively increases engagement with consumers, drives brand loyalty and value, and creates new revenue streams.

In trademark licensing, a brand owner grants a third party (manufacturer or retailer) permission to develop a product using the brand’s logo, attributes, and intellectual property in exchange for a royalty (percentage of sales) or fee.

 

our services

 

Specialist Services

Consulting

Our consulting division advises companies on how to extend their brands through innovative and commercially-viable brand extension strategies as well as advises on licensing best practices and conducts consumer research studies.

Manufacturer Representation

Our Manufacturer Representation service leverages an unparalleled, global network of contacts with hundreds of leading brands to partner manufacturers with the right brands to bring the best new products to market.

Royalty Auditing

We offer specialized global Royalty Auditing services that ensure licensee royalty compliance using a non-adversarial approach to strengthen the licensor/licensee relationship.

Cultural intelligence

Our Cultural Intelligence service identifies category, retail and consumer behavior trends and purchase drivers to inform brand extension strategies.

Legal & Finance

Our Legal and Financial Management services provides in-house legal services, as well as integrated technologies and customized financial and data management systems to support the management of licensing programs.
