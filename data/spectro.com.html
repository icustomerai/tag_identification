SPECTRO AT A GLANCE

SPECTRO is one of the worldwide leading suppliers of analytical instruments, employing optical emission (stationary and mobile Arc/Spark OES, ICP-OES) and X-ray fluorescence spectrometry (XRF) technology, used for the elemental analysis of materials in industry, research and academia. SPECTRO is a business unit of AMETEK, Inc. 

Information about MC-ICP-MS, HR-ICP-MS, GD-MS, IRMS, TIMS and Noble Gas technology can be found at our AMETEK sister company Nu Instruments.
