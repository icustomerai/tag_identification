About HIMCO

Hartford Investment Management Company (HIMCO) has been providing investment advice for over 40 years. With more than 125 investment professionals and over $110 billion in assets under management across the fixed income, equity and alternatives markets, we deliver tailored strategies to insurance, sub-advisory and other institutional clients.
 
HIMCO’s investment philosophy is centered on the belief that markets often overreact and risk can become mispriced at the global, interest rate, inflation, sector and security levels. Identifying and exploiting these relative-value opportunities can be key to delivering consistent risk-adjusted returns. 
 
We utilize a balanced top-down, bottom up investment process, with macro strategy, asset allocation and security selection as the three main sources of performance. A consistent quantitative framework in conjunction with qualitative inputs and disciplined fundamental analysis inform portfolio construction. Finally, risk management is integrated into our process to help drive consistent outcomes.
 
Our broad capability set  allows HIMCO to customize strategies for each client's needs while navigating various market environments. We offer holistic as well as sector-specific capabilities to complement your portfolio.
