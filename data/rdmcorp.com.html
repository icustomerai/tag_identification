A trusted technology innovator

We are an innovative technology company that has helped standardize the global check printing and processing industry since 1987 with solutions for MICR & print quality control, and affordable desktop check scanners. As a result, we have become a trusted payments industry resource worldwide, to financial institutions, government agencies, security printers, check scanner, MICR printer and toner manufacturers, and corporate in-house check printing operations.

The RDM brand of multifunctional check scanners by Deluxe are available in two platforms: network scanners (driverless) and traditional (driver-based) scanners.

RDM EC9600i and EC9700i Network Series Scanners


The RDM network series scanners, are built on RDM’s Independence Platform Technology; an intelligent, self-contained, secure architecture that uses standard network protocols.

This industry-first design provides secure USB and Ethernet connectivity across multiple operating systems, networks and virtual environments without installing drivers – keeping your scanner current and compatible as industry standards and operating systems evolve.

RDM network scanners are the industry’s only check scanners to offer true USB “Plug-and-play”. By leveraging the drivers built-in to Windows, macOS and Linux, no drivers or software needs to be installed on local workstations – enabling users to start scanning checks within a few minutes of unboxing.

Alternatively, the standard Ethernet connection available on most models, enables the scanners to be securely shared over a network or used in virtual environments like Citrix or VMware.

The EC9600i and EC9700i network scanners are ideal for remote deposit capture, remote lockbox, teller and branch capture, check cashing and in-person payments and are available in a range of single-feed and multi-feed multifunctional models with speeds from 30 to 100 documents per minute to accommodate a variety of payment types and volumes.

RDM EC9100i Series Scanners

RDM EC9100i series scanners are an ideal fit for Windows based payment applications such as remote deposit capture, remote lockbox, teller capture, check cashing and in-person payments. The scanners are available in single-feed and multi-feed models, with speeds up to 90 documents per minute. Unlike RDM network scanners, these scanners require the installation of a USB driver, similar to traditional check scanners from other vendors.


Simplified Application Integration

Our simplified scanner programming interface “RDM SCI”, enables application developers to quickly integrate and certify any RDM scanner within only a few days – unparalleled in the industry. The same interface also supports our legacy EC9000i, EC7500i and EC7000i series scanners with no additional coding. RDM scanners are also certified with Silver Bullet Technology’s Ranger interface.

RDM MICR & Print Quality Control Solutions

Deluxe manufactures the world’s only quality control testing equipment for the global check printing and banking industries.

The RDM QCX and RDM QCV line of testers are relied upon by check printers (including Deluxe) and financial institutions world-wide to ensure checks are printed to the respective country standards for MICR, OCR and image print quality.  They provide technical analysts with the “tools-of-the-trade” for detailed testing and reporting while automating the testing functions with consistency of results to ensure compliance with the standards.

Financial institutions report saving over 50% on their scanner-related implementation and support costs.
