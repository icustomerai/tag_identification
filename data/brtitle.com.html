About Baton Rouge Title

Baton Rouge Title is the oldest and largest title company in the Baton Rouge area with convenient locations so that we are never too far to handle your individual closing needs. From residential sales, construction mortgages, refinances and second mortgages, to deferred tax exchanges, to the largest commercial transactions, we have the experience and knowledge to service all real estate closings.
At Baton Rouge Title, we believe that nothing is more important than your real estate transaction. That is why we have the best title staff in the Baton Rouge area, including experienced real estate attorneys, notaries, and abstractors, to provide quick, hassle-free closings resulting in safe, secure land titles.
