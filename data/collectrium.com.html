What We Do

Collectrium provides a comprehensive solution for your collection management and organizational needs.

Collectrium is a secure, visual, and comprehensive tool to help collectors track, report on, and interact with their collections digitally. The platform is cloud based and supported by both web and iOS which means as long as you have an internet connection you can access your account from your computer or mobile device. 

With unlimited capacity for images and attachments, Collectrium allows subscribers to move away from cumbersome paper documentation and keep all the information about their collections in one place. From object Acquisition to Expenses, or Valuation to Sale and beyond, our comprehensive transaction options allow users to see the lifespan of an object within your collection in the greatest of detail.
