Nutra Manufacturing, based in Greenville, South Carolina, is a full-service contract manufacturer and private labeler of dietary supplements. We provide complete turnkey products from our tablet, two-piece hardshell, and soft gelatin dosage forms to our bottling, cartoning, multi pill sachets, and blister packaging options.

Nutra Manufacturing is an IVC company and manufactures vitamin, mineral, herbal, sports nutrition, and diet and energy products. The dietary supplements produced by Nutra Manufacturing for IVC and our contract customers are sold in various countries worldwide.

Today, Nutra Manufacturing is comprised of two sites with combined square footage of 950,000 in Greenville, SC and Anderson, SC.  These locations support the complete manufacturing, warehousing, packaging and distribution processes in production of over 11,000 dosage formulations each year including tablets, hardshell capsules and softgels.
