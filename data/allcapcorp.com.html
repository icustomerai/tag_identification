The trusted advisor of business owners ready to take the next step.

You’ve poured your heart and soul into creating and growing a successful business. And when the time comes to sell your company, raise capital to help take it to the next level or acquire another, we’re here to help you generate exceptional results.

Allegiance Capital specializes in mergers and acquisitions of privately-held, closely-held middle-market, family-run and entrepreneurial driven businesses. Since 1998, our team of experts has helped countless business owners protect their legacy, optimize their ROI and face the future with confidence.

Whatever transition you’re considering for your business, protecting your best interests is our #1 priority.

Allegiance Capital served as the exclusive financial advisor to RPM Software representing the company in its acquisition by NASDAQ public company, ScanSource (SCSC)

The RPM acquisition allowed ScanSource to expand their offerings, branching out into software, and allowing ScanSource to offer more products and services to their already well-established telecom customer base.
