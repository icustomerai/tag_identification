#WEAREQUANTA

Realtime Utility Engineers is an operating company of Quanta Services, Inc. (NYSE: PWR). We provide industry leading high voltage power delivery technical solutions to Utilities, Contractors, and Developers throughout North America. The energy industry makes life possible, but few realize how intertwined the electric power industry is in our daily lives. We engineer and test the infrastructure that powers your world.
