Introducing the Polaris Lyra

Providing precision navigation in confined surgical or clinical suites, the Polaris Lyra delivers exceptional accuracy, an optimized measurement volume, and a small form factor.

Explore Polaris Lyra

Together with our innovative partners, we’re exploring new possibilities for integrating accurate and reliable optical and electromagnetic tracking solutions into OEM medical devices.

Optical Navigation Technology

The Polaris Vega and Polaris Lyra, optical measurement solutions, can provide precise wireless tracking and navigation of OEM surgical instruments and patient and OR equipment position tracking within a large measurement volume.

Learn More

Electromagnetic Tracking Technology

The Aurora and 3D Guidance electromagnetic tracking solutions can provide accurate tracking and navigation of incredibly small OEM medical instruments through complex and hard-to-reach anatomy, without requiring a line of sight.

Learn More
