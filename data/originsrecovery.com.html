Comprehensive Addiction Recovery

Learn more about the residential and outpatient recovery services offered by Origins Behavioral HealthCare. We provide specialized addiction treatments for patients and their families. Your first step towards recovery starts with Origins.

What We Treat

Substance use and mental health disorders take many forms and can affect all aspects of your life. Your relationships with friends and family suffer; work becomes unmanageable, and your health starts to deteriorate. All journeys are different, so we are equipped to target specific disorders - such as alcohol, cocaine, and opioid addiction - as well as mental health concerns such as depression, anxiety, and PTSD. We also diagnose and treat co-occurring medical illnesses during your stay. More

How We Treat It

We have a wide variety of residential treatment programs. Our specialized inpatient services include gender-specific treatment, age-specific treatment, and trauma-informed recovery. No matter the circumstances, Origins has the perfect program for you or your loved one. Each one is staffed with mental health experts who are here to help, beginning with the initial medical detox all the way through outpatient recovery and continuing care. We make sure no one goes through recovery alone. More

Refer a Patient

Are you a referring medical professional seeking more information about Origins Behavioral HealthCare? Our Admissions Specialists will collect the information necessary to verify your patients health benefits. We look forward to answering your questions and involving you in your clients care as much as you both desire. More

What Are Co-Occurring Disorders?

If someone has both addiction and a mental health disorder, they are classified as having co-occurring disorders (a.k.a. dual diagnoses). These disorders impact nearly every aspect of an individuals mental, physical, and spiritual life. Read more >
