Steel Trading

Nucor Trading is an international trading company active around the world in ferrous products such as finished and semi-finished steel.

We pride ourselves in our ability to respond quickly and flexibly to our customers' needs and in efficiently managing the risks in moving steel around the world.

Steel Procurement

As part of the Nucor group, we have access to one of the widest and deepest product mixes on the market. In addition, we have privileged relationships with other major steel mills.

Our relationships with Nucor mills and third-party suppliers all serve just one purpose: to provide our customers with the steel they need when they need it.
