HULA POST: YOUR ONE STOP POSTPRODUCTION FACILITY

RENTALS

Navigating an ever-evolving digital postproduction landscape is challenging. Therefore, Hula Post offers multiple solutions under one roof, remotely or as a hybrid one. If your project needs editing equipment and storage, creative space, and finishing, we can do it!

We believe workflow design and equipment rental solutions go together. You need experts you can depend upon to help navigate the maze of choices available for your unique project. Our technical staff prides itself on understanding our clients' workflow and offering options, available products, and solutions that best fit your needs and budget. You can depend on our many years of experience to assist you in making the best choices technically and economically. That is why studios, networks, streaming platforms, independent producers, and other content creators worldwide rely on Hula Post as a trusted partner and a source of excellence and innovation.

We feature a robust inventory of Avid and Adobe Premiere non-linear editorial systems and a vast array of local and networked storage systems such as NEXIS. We can set up your custom-designed equipment and workflow in one of our facilities or deliver and install just one or multiple at your location, anywhere in the country, or worldwide. All systems are prebuilt and tested before delivery to ensure reliability and consistency. In addition, we are known as the industry leader in technical support, delivered 24/7/365 by highly trained engineers.
REMOTE EDITING USING HULA POST EVERYWHERE

We launched our remote editing solution, Hula Post Everywhere, in 2020, primarily developed to help our clients get back to work safely during the COVID-19 pandemic. Hula Post Everywhere allows feature, streaming, and television editors to work anywhere. If you need a hybrid solution, we can install a remote system in your editing teams' homes and in one of our locations or yours. In addition, we provide the latest tools in review and approval technology so that creative teams to collaborate remotely from anywhere on their projects.

Creative Space

Hula provides spacious, modern editing suites equipped with the latest Avid editing systems configured to the technical specifications of the individual project and personal preferences. We provide round-the-clock tech support and ultra-fast connectivity, as well as kitchen facilities and other amenities available on site.

FINISHING

We offer audio and color finishing for feature, television, and streaming at our Burbank, CA headquarters on South Victory Blvd. Our color suite features DaVinci Resolve, both our audio suites have Pro Tools, and our in-house editor uses Avid Media Composer with Symphony. In addition, we can accommodate any format your project requires. We offer a complete 4K workflow to meet demanding delivery requirements. Combining leading-edge technology with some of the industry's best creative talent, we offer a handcrafted approach toward theatrical, television, and streaming finishing.

We also provide a host of media-related services, including dailies transcoding and archival services, deliverable creation, and data archiving of your final elements. In addition, we can create encrypted and non-encrypted DCPs at a cost-effective rate.
