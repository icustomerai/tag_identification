about abilene motor express

Abilene Motor Express is recognized nationwide for our prompt and reliable motor freight services to destinations throughout the continental US. Customers appreciate our flexibility and prompt response to their needs. They know our courteous and attentive drivers, supported by our behind-the-scenes team of experts, ensure the most professional level of services for them and their own customers.
