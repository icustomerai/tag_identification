About

i-Health, Inc. – a wholly owned subsidiary of dsm-firmenich – is a global consumer health and wellness company. We develop, market and distribute branded products that support health and wellness. Our dedication to quality is a commitment solidly backed by quality ingredients, stringent quality control measures and continuous improvement through research and innovation. We believe in best in class formulations and rely on strong science to support our brands.

Our Commitment to Sustainability

All our products will use FSC certified paper by 2025.

We are working to reduce our carbon footprint with the goal of being Net Zero by 2050.

We are moving towards a fully sustainable portfolio.
