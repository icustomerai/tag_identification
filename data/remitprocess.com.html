    Plain and simple, you do what you do best and let Alliance Payment Solutions do what we do best.     Lockbox Processing

    Lockbox facilitates the process of retrieving and processing customers payments sent through the mail to a specific PO Box.

    Electronic Lockbox

    E-Lock Box consolidates the bill payments and generates a data file. This file is automatically integrated with the lockbox posting data file.

    Statement Printing

    Statement printing service is a multi-step process. We print, fold, apply postage and mail each to your clients.

    Document Imaging

    Access provided to an automated web-based portal for researching documents and transactions captured.

    Check Printing

    A comprehensive suite of private-label high-quality lockbox services along with a full array of payment solutions.

    Smart Returns

    Allows you to choose when to re-present returned items.

    Electronic Bill Presentment

    Allows you to send invoices via email.
