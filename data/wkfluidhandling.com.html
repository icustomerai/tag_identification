White Knight Pumps & Pump Systems

Pumps, Debubblers, Pressure Regulators & More!

Bellows Pumps

White Knight pneumatic bellows pumps feature PTFE and PFA flow paths. These reliable, leak-free pumps have high flow rates, steady pressures and stable temperatures. They do not contain metals or elastomer O-rings. They are dead-head capable, and offer suction lift.

Bellows Pumps

Centrifugal Pumps

White Knight high-purity centrifugal pumps feature a magnetically levitated impeller that is rotated independent of its stabilization functions. It offers continuous, steady flow without pressure pulsation. It features a process-safe PTFE/PFA flow path and precise controls.

Centrifugal Pumps

Diaphragm Pumps

White Knight pneumatic diaphragm (AODD) pumps feature PTFE or UHMW polyethylene (UHMWPE) corrosion-resistant fluid paths. They are ideal for corrosive or toxic liquids such as acids, fuels, glues, paints, resins, inks, or slurry.

Diaphragm Pumps

Metering Pumps

White Knight pneumatic and electronic metering pumps accurately dispense up to 100 ml and 50 ml, respectively. Their fully-supported PTFE rolling diaphragms maximize pressure capabilities and ensure safe, reliable chemical dispense.

Metering Pumps

Mini-Pump

Mini-pumps offer up to 300 ml/min flow rates and 160 psi discharge pressures. They feature PTFE and PFA fluid paths, and spring-loaded checks enable them to mount in any direction.

Mini-Pump

Debubblers

White Knight debubblers reduce bubbles from chemical delivery and wafer process systems. They feature chemically-resistant PTFE and PFA fluid paths for high-purity chemical applications.

Debubblers

Pressure Regulators

Automatically maintain and accurately control pressure for circulation loops or multiple dispense points without additional circuitry or feedback. Pressure regulators are remotely piloted and feature fully-swept PTFE and PFA flow paths.

Pressure Regulators

Filter Housings

Universal, compact, multi-chamber filter housings with PTFE and PFA fluid paths for high-purity chemicals. Dual-bowl and quad-bowl filter housings are available in parallel or in-line (series) models.

Filter Housings

High-Purity Closed-Loop Pump Systems

Automatically maintain laminar flow or steady pressure. White Knight closed-loop systems feature metal-free pumps with PTFE and PFA flow paths. They provide stable temperatures, dead-head, and suction lift.

Control your high-purity chemical processes and delivery systems. Simplify process automation to save time, resources and reduce costs.

  • ≤ 140 lpm (36 gpm) flow rates
  • ≤ 7 Bar (100 psi) pressures
  • ≤ 210°C (410°F) temperatures
  • Maintain stable temperatures
  • No metals, no corrosion
  • No elastomer O-rings, no leaks
  • No electric motors, no heat rise
  • No impellers, no microbubbles
