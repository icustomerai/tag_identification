    OUR KNOWLEDGE. YOUR POWER.

    GCube is the specialist provider of insurance services for utility-scale renewable energy projects around the globe.

    With over 25 years of experience in the renewable energy sector, we understand the unique exposures and risks of developing and operating these power generation projects.

    We help wind, solar, wave, tidal, hydro and energy storage asset owners to identify, quantify and mitigate risk efficiently and cost-effectively.

    Our policies provide comprehensive coverage to asset owners at all stages of the project lifecycle, throughout transit, construction and operation.
