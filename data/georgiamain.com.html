Our History

 

 

1903 marked the beginnings of the H.Y. Louie Company at the corner of Georgia and Main Street in Vancouver’s Chinatown.

 

For over 100 years, the family business has been nimble and adaptable with the energy and foresight to grow and thrive in the highly competitive grocery industry.

 

The name "Georgia Main" is a nod to our rich heritage and our beginnings.

A Local Food Company

As the parent company for two of BC’s most trusted and loved food brands, IGA and Fresh St. Market, Georgia Main Food Group is fresh in ideas and filled with an invigorated vision.

 

IGA is the simple one-stop-shop and the perfect place to nourish a busy day. Proud to deliver simple goodness to its communities since 1955.

 

Fresh St. Market is where a local market meets everyday shopping, it’s where fresh ideas meet the pursuit of what is new and delicious. It’s where fresh meets everyday.

 

Georgia Main Food Group has always been proud of its B.C. roots and supports local in everything we do. That's why we're committed to sustaining the environment and supporting our community in everything that we do, from sourcing products, to managing waste, to choosing responsible packaging and everything in between.
