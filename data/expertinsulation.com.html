Welcome to Expert Insulation!

Expert Insulation began serving the Rochester area with blown-in fiberglass and batt insulation. Thanks to the support of our community, we expanded into the Brainerd area and have grown in manpower and in the services we can provide to our commercial, builder, residential, and agriculture customers. In addition to batt and blown fiberglass, we are also proud to provide eco-friendly spray foam insulation, waterproofing, air sealing, and retrofit services for all our customers’ projects.

Our dedication to customer service and quality products have positioned us as the regional choice for architects, distributors, and contractors on commercial projects, as well as builders and homeowners who are building or renovating. No matter what type of job we’re on, our goal is to provide the insulation that is the most effective, efficient, and sustainable for your needs and budget.
