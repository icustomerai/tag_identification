Full-Service Solutions for Pipeline Drag Reduction

Industry Leading Technology

LSPI invented and commercialized the first pipeline drag reducer in 1979, and remains the global market leader in DRA technology. LSPI offers the strongest portfolio of patented products and is committed to continued innovation.

Quality Production

LSPI is the largest manufacturer of pipeline drag reducing agents (DRA), under a strict quality system that encompasses every aspect of the business. LSPI is certified to ISO 9001:2015 quality standards.

Technical Support and Consulting

LSPI technical professionals are recognized authorities at evaluating, implementing and maintaining successful DRA field applications. LSPI experts interact with all levels of their customer’s organization, from pipeline modeling for business development to optimization for field operations.

Reliable Supply Chain

LSPI recognizes the business critical nature of supply chain reliability, and invests in providing the most extensive and capable DRA logistics infrastructure in the world. Utilizing both in-house and trusted business partners, LSPI is able to deliver product safely and on time to customers around the globe.

Injection Equipment & Field Service

LSPI ensures reliable field operations by offering an integrated systems approach. Equipment is designed to optimize LSPI product injection reliability, while a network of field service representatives provides preventative maintenance at injection sites.
