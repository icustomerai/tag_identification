Welcome
The Center for Kidney Disease and Hypertension, LLC. is dedicated to serving patients with kidney disease and hypertension. We care for patients with acute and chronic kidney disease, patients on dialysis, and patients waiting for or having received kidney transplants. We see patients at Pinnacle Health Hospitals and Holy Spirit Hospital, long term and acute care hospitals, rehabilitation centers, outpatient dialysis centers, and our office.

Our goal is to provide optimal nephrology and hypertension care to all our patients. Our doctors are nephrologists – a nephrologist is a medical doctor who specializes in the care of patients with kidney disease. Nephrologists are also called kidney doctors.
