50 Years Providing Optical Solutions

Precision optical solutions made from a broad range of infrared optical materials have always been a key product for Janos Technology, LLC.  Since 1970 we have specialized in design, manufacture and coating.  From the beginning, we have been an industry leader in infrared and precision optics performance.  

We support both commercial and defense and surveillance applications from our own product designs to delivering specific customer solutions.

  • Cooled Lens and Lens Assemblies
  • Uncooled and Athermal Lens and Lens Assemblies
  • Assembly Accessories
  • IR Coatings
  • IR Mirrors    

Janos Technology, LLC continues to develop new solutions to help our customers push their system performance in the non-visible range.
