Why Choose Premier Building Supply?

Founded in American Fork, Utah, in 2009, Premier Building Supply offers quality building products and exceptional installation services to Wasatch Front, Utah County and Salt Lake County.

We specialize in residential and commercial insulation, including fiberglass and spray foam insulation. We also offer professional garage door installation, repair and maintenance for homes and businesses along The Wasatch Front.
