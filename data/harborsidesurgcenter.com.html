What sets us apart?

Harborside Surgery Center is a state-of-the-art facility for surgeries that do not require hospital admission. Nearly 60% of surgeries in the US are performed in an ambulatory setting. Harborside offers patients a modern setting that is more cost-effective and creates a more caring and comfortable environment than you would typically find in a hospital setting.

At Harborside you will receive services in a setting where the surgeons themselves select the highest quality staff and equipment and have complete control over the surgical process. As a Harborside Surgery Center patient, you will receive the exemplary care you deserve with one-to-one nursing care throughout your recovery. Our center meets the highest industry standards for top-quality health care, is certified by Medicare, licensed by the state, and fully accredited by the Accreditation Association for Ambulatory Health Care (AAAHC).
