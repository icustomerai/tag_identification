Build your security solution

With an Exacq video surveillance solution you can connect cameras, add access points, and select the storage that fits your needs. Additionally, exacqVision software allows you to customize deep learning analytics to match your business needs. 

Use Existing Cameras or Choose Something New

Keep your existing IP or analog cameras or upgrade to something new.
