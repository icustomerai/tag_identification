Don’t Get ‘Burned’ by Poor Fertilizer Application Timing This Summer

22 Jun, 2023
It’s common knowledge that applying too much fertilizer containing high percentages of readily available nitrogen and water soluble nutrients can result in burn damage to plants, but did you know that damage can still occur even if your application rate is correct? Applying the right product at the right rate, but at the WRONG time can have disastrous consequences.

ANNOUNCEMENT: Turf Care Supply, LLC Announces Vice-President, Blend Division

07 Mar, 2023
Brunswick, OH. (Mar 1, 2023) – Turf Care Supply, LLC (“Turf Care” or the “Company”), the leading formulator and blender of coated nitrogen and other fertilizer products to the turf & ornamental market, is excited to announce Dave Swanigan has been named Vice-President, Blend Division. Dave will oversee all blend sales and be the point person for national accounts. Dave has an extensive sales and leadership background in both the golf and lawn & landscape industry. Previously to Turf Care, Dave was the Key Accounts Manager for Bayer Environmental Science, handling both regional and national accounts. Prior to that role, Dave was a top area sales manager across the southeastern portion of the United States where he earned multiple achievements in excellence. Dave started his career in LESCO, working within the Georgia and Carolina markets. Dave also worked on opening the first Golf Super Store concept in Myrtle Beach, SC. At LESCO, Dave was a top sales performer and received many outstanding sales recognitions within his tenure at the company. With his impressive industry knowledge and experience, Dave is a tremendous addition to the Turf Care Supply team.
