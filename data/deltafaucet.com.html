DELTA® RECERTIFIED

A SECOND CHANCE AT CHANGE

Recertified products are returned, unused items that are inspected to meet the quality standards of new Delta products and are backed by a 10-Year Limited Warranty.

Recertified products aim to reuse the original packaging to reduce waste. Packaging may have stickers, minor flaws or get completely repackaged, but we promise the contents are like new.

Our annual donation to Habitat for Humanity is made possible, in part, by Recertified product purchases.

All orders $49+ ship free, and include a hassle-free, 90 day return policy.
