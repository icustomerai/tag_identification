Leading Global Industrial Raw Material, Polymeric Resin, and Additive Supplier

Founded in 1988, Mitsui Plastics, Inc. is a prominent distributor serving plastic and chemical industries worldwide. Our products include a diverse range of engineering and commodity grade resins as well as polymer additives, minerals and semi-finished goods. Through our global network of partners and affiliates, we also supply various engineering compounds, masterbatches and compounding services.

Tapping into Mitsui Global Raw Material Network, we can source almost any raw material, providing global resource as a local solution. The combination of our product portfolio, logistics management and locations in Europe, Asia, North and South America enables Mitsui Plastics, Inc. to provide localized service to our customers around the world, who rely on us for just-in-time material solutions.
