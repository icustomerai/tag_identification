We've Moved!

Our headquarters office has moved to a new, more modern location at 55 Park Drive in Lawrence, PA. Now our offices, warehouse, and fabrication shop are under one roof, helping us streamline services, so we can continue to provide outstanding electrical, fire protection, and commercial HVAC solutions across the Pittsburgh metro and surrounding area.

Safety Award

At this year's Mechanical Contractors Association of America (MCAA) Convention, we were presented with an MCAA/CNA Safety Excellence Award! The award is one our industry's most prestigious recognitions of safety and health programs, safety innovations, and achievements in safety. Congratulations to our team for their hard work!
