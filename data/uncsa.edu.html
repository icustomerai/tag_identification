The Hollywood Reporter ranks UNCSA School of Drama No. 3 in the world

The Hollywood Reporter has ranked the School of Drama at UNCSA No. 3 on its list of the "Top 25 Drama Schools in the World," up from No. 4 last year.

Don't miss these 23 UNCSA alumni connections to 2023 Tony Award-winning productions

With connections to "Leopoldstadt" and "Parade," which took home top prizes, musical thriller "Sweeney Todd," musical comedy "Some Like it Hot" and more, UNCSA made a strong appearance in 2023 Tony Award-winning productions.

UNCSA Provost Patrick J. Sims honored by Black Business Ink

UNCSA Executive Vice Chancellor and Provost Patrick J. Sims was named one of Black Business Ink magazine's Power 100 during the publication's 20th anniversary awards ceremony at the Steven Tanger Center for the Performing Arts.

Alumni Success

From backstage to the limelight, discover how UNCSA alumni are making their mark in the world.

 
Alumni
Visual Arts alumna Hunter Schafer (H.S. '17) worked as a model and visual artist for just two years before landing her first acting role as Jules Vaughn in "Euphoria" on HBO.
Alumni
School of Design & Production alumnus Paul Tazewell (B.F.A. '86) has designed costumes for nearly two dozen Broadway shows, including the smash hit "Hamilton: An American Musical."
Alumni
School of Dance alumna Camille A. Brown (B.F.A. '01) has been everywhere from Broadway to Abu Dhabi — choreographing Tony Award-winning shows and leading her own company, Camille A. Brown & Dancers.
Alumni
School of Music alumna Jessica McJunkins (B.M. '09) has shared the stage with symphonies, ensembles and music royalty — including Beyonce, Jay-Z and Stevie Wonder.
Alumni
School of Design & Production alumnus Randy Rees' current career is out of this world. No, literally. The 1999 graduate is helping SpaceX revolutionize space technology and is using his UNCSA training to do it.
Alumni
Every day, visual Arts alumnus Connor Matz (H.S. '12) blends together his love of fashion and visual arts at work as visual windows and interior director for Saks Fifth Avenue’s flagship store in Manhattan.
Alumni
School of Filmmaking alumnus Danny McBride (B.F.A '99) delivers laughs and thrills as a multi-faceted filmmaker. His recent work includes executive producer of the "Halloween" reboot and creator and star of "The Righteous Gemstones."
