About us

    For more than 25 years, Livex Lighting has been creating extraordinary decorative lighting, focusing on detail and craftsmanship. Each design is infused with an unparalleled passion for the craft as well as an undeniable creativity.
    With more than 4,000 fashionable styles to choose, Livex Lighting provides solutions for any space. Each piece features meticulously hand-applied finishes and is developed with the most current design trends and technology. Available in many sizes suitable for various applications, Livex Lighting complements design aesthetics that range from chic and contemporary to timeless and transitional.
