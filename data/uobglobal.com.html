UOB Global Capital

UOB Global Capital (UOBGC), founded in 1998, is the global asset management affiliate of the UOB Group (UOB), one of Asia’s premier financial institutions.

Our philosophy is to combine excellence in investment capability with flexibility in product design and distribution in order to meet the needs of institutional investors worldwide.

Managed by a team of experienced investment executives, UOBGC has a proven track record of  creating competitive products worldwide through joint ventures, alliances and affiliations.

Our Investment Focus
  • Creating appropriate risk-adjusted reward products in all asset classes for institutional investors
  • Partnering with exceptional talent to provide performance orientated investment products
  • Focused on understanding and meeting client needs worldwide
  • Capabilities in listed, alternative and direct investment products
  • Experienced team with successful records in both fund management and fund raising

UOBGC’s Mission Is Two-Fold:

To provide integrated global asset management solutions with a comprehensive product range that serves institutional investors worldwide.

To bring UOB’s Asian regional investment strengths to clients outside that region who are seeking exposure there.
