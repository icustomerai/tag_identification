    Lighting Simplified

    We understand that you want more than attractive outdoor lighting. You want safety, security and cost savings that come from effective lighting solutions. You also need responsive service and maintenance. Miami Valley Lighting is responsible for more than 60,000 lights in West Central Ohio. And we’ve got some bright ideas for your business too.

    Parking lot and security lighting

    Miami Valley Lighting can develop custom plans to install safe, secure and well-lit parking lot lighting systems from the ground up. But we can also improve existing outdoor lighting, offering energy-saving LED solutions that will reduce your energy bills.

    Street lighting

    We understand that local governments have an ever-growing list of operational duties, so why not leave the lighting to us? Miami Valley Lighting provides a full spectrum of street lighting services, from design to installation and maintenance.

    Decorative lighting

    Every community has its own special character, so curb appeal certainly shouldn’t end at the curb. Miami Valley Lighting offers an array of decorative lighting options to complement the aesthetics of your area.
