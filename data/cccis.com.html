    TECHNOLOGY

    AI-enabled solutions to advance how you operate

    Our proven AI solutions power better decisions at scale. Paired with IoT and telematics solutions, these technologies provide unprecedented clarity, improve business performance, and elevate the customer experience.

    INSIGHTS

    Data-driven insights for better decision-making

    CCC makes big data and insights from connected car data, claims, photos, videos and hundreds of other sources actionable. Our IoT solutions provide the infrastructure to activate and assess that data at scale to better inform decisions across the insurance and automotive industries.

    NETWORK

    Access to a vast network to accelerate innovation

    Our technology is powered by an extensive partner network of insurers, repair facilities, OEMs, lenders, dealerships and parts suppliers—all working together to deliver an excellent experience for your shared customers.
