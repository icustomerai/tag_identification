Our Investment Franchises

Our clients choose from investment strategies managed by 12 highly specialized Investment Franchises, each with an independent brand and investment approach. Our investment professionals leverage a centralized operations and distribution platform, so that they can focus  their efforts on serving clients and delivering results. We also offer a Solutions Platform, featuring an array of multi-asset, multi-manager, quantitative and rules-based strategies as well as our innovative VictoryShares ETFs.

Fixed income professionals, specializing in total return and convertible securities strategies. Team’s process is independent, comprehensive and repeatable.
