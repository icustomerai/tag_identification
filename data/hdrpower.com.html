AMETEK HDR Power AT A GLANCE

Headquartered in Columbus, OH, AMETEK HDR is a recognized world-class manufacturer of electronic equipment for specific markets, including, but not limited to Electrical Heat Treating, Furnace and Coating, and special applications. Our SCR Power Controllers are high quality, industrial grade products designed to operate reliably for many years in process environments. Our expert staff are dedicated to providing custom engineered products in a short time period.

Industrial Applications from Fiberglass to Nuclear
