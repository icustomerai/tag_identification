Welcome to Piggly Wiggly®!

Piggly Wiggly®, America's first true self-service grocery store, was founded in Memphis, Tennessee in 1916 by Clarence Saunders.

Today there are more than 500 Piggly Wiggly® stores serving communities in 18 states. Piggly Wiggly® Stores are independently owned and operated under license of Piggly Wiggly®, LLC and C&S Wholesale Grocers, LLC
