Tower Over Your Challenges and Empower Connectivity

TowerCo owns, builds, and manages a portfolio of wireless infrastructure assets and properties for colocation. We work in close partnership with everyone involved; and use our knowledge of site acquisition, zoning, and construction to facilitate a smooth process. We’re a nationally approved vendor for all major wireless operators and have extensive experience working with state agencies, municipalities, schools, and private landowners. Asset classes we serve include towers, small cells, in-building solutions, and edge data centers.

Working With Us

We understand that cell tower colocation can be a confusing topic. That’s why when you work with us, we will break everything down so that you are kept in the loop. It’s important to us to work with our clients closely. If you ever have any questions, don’t hesitate to reach out and ask. With more than 10,000 assets, we are prepared to meet your needs. Before you reach out, spend some time looking around our website. You might be able to find some of the important information before you even call.

Sell Your Ground Lease

If you are a landowner or building owner currently receiving rent due to a wireless cell tower site or wireless rooftop installation being located on your property, TowerCo has a lease buyout program whereby we will pay you a one-time lump sum amount in exchange for your rent payment. You can use this money to invest in your business or pay down your mortgage. This is a unique opportunity to “monetize” your recurring payments.
