ABOUT US
OUR STORY

FRESH FROM THE FARM

Since 1935, Cass-Clay Creamery has been providing fresh and delicious dairy products to families in North Dakota and surrounding areas. We start with the freshest milk from local farms and manufacture a full line of milks, premium ice cream, sour cream, cottage cheese, and more. Our manufacturing facility is located in Fargo, North Dakota. 190 employees proudly work to make sure your family can enjoy wholesome and delicious Cass-Clay dairy products.
