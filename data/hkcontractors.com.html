About Us

As a member of the CRH family of companies, HK Contractors maintains deep roots across northern Idaho and beyond. Starting in the 1950s, we’ve developed a loyal customer base through our innovative products and services, which are backed by our world-class teams, all with a commitment to safety and sustainability. The value we hold isn’t just in our bottom line, it’s in the families we support and in the customers that make it all possible. We look forward to your partnership!
