Why Dark-Hued Finishes Are The New Designer Go-To

Dark-hued and black fixtures and fittings have been a finish of choice in kitchen and bath spaces for several years now. Whether for a tone-on-tone, monochromatic effect or to create a striking juxtaposition, these finish selections make a strong design statement in any setting.
READ LUXE INTERIOR'S ARTICLE HERE

MATTE BLACK VS GUNMETAL

What we refer to as a living finish, Gunmetal reveals blue-hued tones with time. Matte Blacks deep, velvety color will remain over time as a durable, powder coat finish. Find a showroom to find the right dark finish for your next renovation.
