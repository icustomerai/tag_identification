Cans Get You Cooking

Cans Get You Cooking began in 2013 as a multi-year consumer communication campaign paid for by some can manufacturers like Silgan. As the communication campaign proved itself, it has attracted broader support from more can manufacturers and some contributions by coaters and steel manufacturers.

The communication campaign continues in 2019 that utilizes multiple social media platforms. This video is one of the key elements that will be shared across influencer platforms as we continue to educate consumers about the canning process.

Cans Get You Cooking is a reminder that cans seal in nutrition, freshness and flavor, naturally.

Children and adults who ate 6+ canned food items over two weeks were more likely to meet or exceed their recommended daily allowance for 17 essential nutrients. That’s because canned foods help make eating healthy more convenient: when canned produce is incorporated into diets in addition to fresh, people eat more fruits & vegetables.
