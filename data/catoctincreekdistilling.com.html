  • In 1607, colonists in Jamestown, Virginia, began distilling rye whisky.

  • By 1790, rye whisky was the most popular spirit in America.

  • Throughout the 1800s, rye whisky was the spirit of choice in cocktails.

  • And in 2000, nobody knew any of this.

  • In 2009, Scott & Becky brought back 400 years of Virginia rye history...

  • ...in every bottle they make.
