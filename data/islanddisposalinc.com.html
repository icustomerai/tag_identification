    Island Disposal Inc.

    Island Disposal is honored to serve the residents and businesses of unincorporated Whidbey Island, the City of Langley and the Town of Coupeville. We specialize in garbage pickup, recycling, and dumpster rental solutions. Our team is more than happy to work with you so you can find the ideal waste management solution for your business or home.

    We provide safe, reliable, environmentally conscience residential, commercial trash and recycling collection services. Locally operated, we value the people, environment and relationships we share in the community. With our commitment to customer satisfaction and years of experience in the field, you can rest assured that your garbage disposal needs are taken care of quickly and securely.
