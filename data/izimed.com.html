Vertefix® HV
Bone Cement

Better Control. Better Care.

Infused with Insite™ tracking beads

For vertebroplasty and kyphoplasty procedures, knowing how much and where bone cement is flowing gives you a greater sense of control.

Vertefix HV cement is infused with Insite tracking beads for real-time flow visualization, with high viscosity and long working time, giving you the power to perform and the confidence to deliver better care.
