    ABOUT US

    ®

    GAMPAC Logistics is an arm of US Foods  , specializing in the handling of food and beverage-related products for Fortune 500 companies, as well as many more of the nation’s food and beverage manufacturers.

    We serve customers across the nation from our offices in Tempe, AZ; Rosemont, IL; and Indianapolis, IN, utilizing our national network footprint. The GAMPAC Logistics leadership team has 100+ years of collective experience in the transportation industry.

    The company was founded in 1986 with the purchase of Pacific Gamble Robinson, which then became GAMPAC – a combination of ‘Gamble’ and ‘Pacific’ that reflects the company history and legacy.

    Today, we provide transportation services for multiple industries, using multiple modes of transportation – including Temperature-Controlled, Dry Van, Flatbed and Cross Docking Operations throughout the U.S. and Canada.
