Where can I buy?

Comfortex dealers are product specialists who work with you to help you find the best window fashions and treatments for your home.

Visit one of our dealers in their store, set up an in-home consultation, or schedule a virtual meeting. They will work with you to offer design and product advice, take measurements of your windows and many of our dealers even handle the installation process. Let Comfortex solve all of your custom window fashion needs today.

Made in The USA

To ensure Color Lux® products meet the highest standards for quality and craftsmanship, we design, manufacture, and assemble each shade in our Upstate New York State production facility. It’s our commitment to support U.S. manufacturing and maintaining a strong American economy. 

Sustainability

Comfortex offers numerous energy saving and environmentally friendly window treatment products. Comfortex is actively dedicated to building greener houses, buildings and communities through our custom sustainable window treatments.
