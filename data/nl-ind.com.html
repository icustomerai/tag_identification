Corporate Profile

NL Industries, Inc. is primarily a holding company. NL Industries operates in the component products industry through its majority-owned subsidiary, CompX International Inc. (NYSE American: CIX).  NL Industries operates in the chemicals industry through its noncontrolling interest in Kronos Worldwide, Inc. (NYSE: KRO).  NL Industries is based in Dallas, Texas and is a subsidiary of Valhi, Inc.
