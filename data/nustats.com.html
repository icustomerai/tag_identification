NuStats is a full service survey research consultancy specializing in complex and large-scale social research studies and data analytics. For more than 30 years, the firm's reputation has grown within the transportation research industry, fusing the science and art of research with technological innovations and advanced practices. Headquartered in Buda, Texas; NuStats provides technological solutions for data collection and analysis to clients throughout the United States, and has an international consulting presence through its virtual call centers.

NuStats is a leading travel and transportation research consultancy since 1984. The company specializes in project surveys and studies across multiple disciplines including Travel Behavior, Transit, Health, Education, Social Policy, etc.

NuStats President is headed by Dr. Martin Kunzmann. The Company's in-house information technology team has produced software toolsets for web & mobile platforms to assist and expand business research & user groups, data collection quality, and operations cost optimization.
