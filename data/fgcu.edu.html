Discover FGCU

At FGCU, we measure success one student at a time. Our 22:1 student-to-faculty ratio and average class size of 33 students ensures a more personal education experience. Professors know their students by name. We also provide academic support services and flexible financial aid and scholarships to make your degree affordable.
