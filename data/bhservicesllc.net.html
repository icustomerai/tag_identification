BH Services LLC is the sales arm of CONTROLS INTERFACE Ltd in the UK. Controls Interface Limited is the former HEATHWAY Ltd management team which now services the previous supplied equipment built by HEATHWAY. BH SERVICES LLC is run by the former HEATHWAY Inc. Vice President Bob Halbreiner.

Together this team brings to the table over 50+ years of experiences in Fiber Optic Process Equipment Manufacturing. Controls Interface Ltd can built Fiber Optic Draw Towers to a height of 9 Meters and as short as 3 meters both in double and single side structures. These towers have been used for Silica, Chalcogenide, Fluoride and Structured Glasses drawn in to either fibers and canes. Controls Interface Ltd has also supplied Preform Manufacturing Equipment, Fiber Proof Test and Re-Wind Equipment. Any of this equipment can be provided for new installations, as well as upgrades or modifications to your existing Draw Towers can also be performed.

The control equipment used is an Omron plc and motion controller. Hardware and Software can be included for access via the internet by our engineers to resolve problems and make minor changes without a site visit.
