Denali Incorporated acquired by NOV

 

We are proud to announce that Denali Incorporated has been acquired by National Oilwell Varco (NOV). With this acquisition, the Denali Incorporated brands of Belco, Containment Solutions, Ershigs, Fabricated Plastics, and Fibra will join the Fiber Glass Systems business unit within NOV.

 

Fiber Glass Systems is a leader in composite piping systems, fittings, and structures for the oil and gas, chemical, marine, offshore, fuel handling, and mining industries. This acquisition provides many new and exciting opportunities within our markets.

 

During this acquisition our day-to-day business will continue as usual and your current CSI contacts will remain the same. Combining expertise in manufacturing, knowledge of fiberglass/composites, and a passion to serve will allow us to rise to the top and create the best product and service offerings to our customers.
