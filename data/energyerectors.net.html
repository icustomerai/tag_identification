Project Profiles

Energy Erectors’ projects include public- and private-sector initiatives to construct substations and switchyards for non-renewable energy as well as solar and wind power. Energy Erectors’ highly trained crews work with static VAR compensators, GIS, series capacitor banks and HVDC converter stations.

When NV Energy decided to upgrade security around its Crystal Substation northeast of Las Vegas, they chose Energy Erectors.
