SUPERIOR TUBE AT A GLANCE

Superior Tube Manufactures High Quality, Small Diameter Tubes

Here at Superior Tube we're passionate about manufacturing precision, small diameter thin wall tubing, and our experience has made us a global leader.

Custom-made to the highest quality

Our engineering experts work with our customers to produce custom-made tubes for the most demanding applications, whether that’s in space, under the sea or inside the human body.

Engineering Expertise

Over the past 80 years, we have developed the unique capability to manufacture seamless and welded and drawn (Weldrawn®), in a wide range of high-performance stainless steel, nickel, titanium and zirconium alloys.

Perform without failure

Because of the innovative culture within our company, customers around the world turn to Superior Tube for high quality tubes that excel in the toughest conditions where failure is not an option.
