A trusted leader in fertility testing and research

Always striving to find answers to the most challenging questions of reproductive health, ReproSource provides cutting-edge diagnostic testing and unparalleled client services to fertility specialists, and clinical research organizations around the world.

Unique diagnostics tests like the Ovarian Assessment Report and the @Home collection for Semen Analysis provide clinicians, patients, and researchers with information about egg supply and sperm health that can be used for diagnosing fertility issues, planning for future pregnancy, and developing drugs that improve reproductive health.

The turnaround time has been excellent and the lab has always been responsive to our questions.

Empowering clinicians & researchers with
state-of-the-art testing

Fertility specialists, OBGYNs and Urologists depend on accurate, timely, and innovative testing to counsel patients through existing reproductive health issues, as well as proactive family planning options. Clinical Research Organizations require laboratory service partners with documented expertise in their fields and a service delivery capability that is second to none. Since 2008, ReproSource has served clinicians, researchers, and patients worldwide by providing unique diagnostic solutions and quality delivery services that improve reproductive outcomes and provide for timely & cost-effective drug development.
