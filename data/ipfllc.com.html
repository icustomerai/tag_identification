Quality and Precision

Indiana Precision Forge, L.L.C. (IPF) supplies a wide variety of precision, high quality cold forged and machined parts to the automotive industry. Using the latest in forging technology, we currently have the capacity to produce more than thirty million cold forged parts per year. To provide additional value to our customers, we also have the capability to provide finished, machined parts, or semi-machined parts, after the forging process.

Backed by Industry Leaders

Established in 1996. We utilize only the highest quality raw material supplied by first class steel mills. The combined resources and technological capabilities of Nippon Steel Precision Forge, Nippon Steel Corporation, and Indiana Precision Forge makes us a leading supplier of cold forged parts to North America.
