Community Involvement

Safran Optics 1 is committed to supporting the communities in which we operate. We focus our outreach efforts in two major areas – giving back to our troops, veterans and their families, and investing in next generation talent through STEM education. We are proud to provide support both by means of financial donations, and through the generosity and efforts of our employees. Stated simply, taking care of our community and its members is an integral part of who we are – as a company, and as a team.
