Whitepaper: ePaper for Sustainable Universities

The sustainability challenge is complex, requiring multiple initiatives and new levels of innovation. One emerging solution that universities are deploying is signage made with E Ink ePaper, which requires approximately 99 percent less power than the LED/LCD technology common to TVs and mobile devices. Top universities from Wisconsin to Australia to Northern Europe are installing everything from E Ink real-time bus stop signs to lecture hall and meeting room signs.

Whitepaper: Digital Paper for Healthier Screen Time

Anyone who’s ever been told, “Don’t sit so close to the television,” is attuned to the idea that watching screens may not be good for your eyes. The constant barrage of artificial light—specifically the blue light spectrum—is far worse for the human eye than most people are aware. And today’s hand-held devices are bringing that light closer to our eyes, for longer durations, than ever before.
