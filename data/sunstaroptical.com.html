Welcome to Truckee-Sunstar Optical Lab!

Truckee Meadows Optical and Sunstar Optical have joined forces to provide you with the highest caliber optical products at prices that make sense for you and your patients. But we do not stop there. Our wholesale optical lab works every day to provide you with unparalleled service and support. 

We want you to think of us as a reliable and knowledgeable business partner, intent on developing a long-term relationship that adds value to the care you provide to your patients. We know that when we provide you with this kind of support, you can devote more time to your patients. With our optical laboratory, your staff will have direct access to our entire team. That way, you can rest easy, knowing we hold ourselves directly accountable to you. 

If you are a current customer, we thank you for your business and your continued faith in us. If you are not, we look forward to showing you a truly different level of service and attention. Either way, we are looking forward to providing you with off-the-chart service for years to come!
