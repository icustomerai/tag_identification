SHELF LIFE, EXPIRY DATE _ Differences worth knowing

SHELF LIFE, EXPIRY DATE

Differences worth knowing

The term Shelf Life refers to the shelf life of a product stored in its original packaging under the storage conditions established by the manufacturer, without any chemical or physical changes that compromise its optimal performance.

The term Expiry Date means the time limit beyond which the product, even if stored in its original packaging under the storage conditions established by the manufacturer, could undergo chemical-physical variations that alter its parameters making it unsuitable for standard use.
