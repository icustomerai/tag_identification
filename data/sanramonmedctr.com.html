Medicaid Resources

If you are enrolled in a Managed Medicaid plan, we encourage you to learn about health plan options and next steps to switch plans on your state website.

In California, you can choose to change your Medi-Cal health plan at any time. If you would like to join a different health plan, you can call Health Care Options, toll free, at 800-430-4263 or log into the Your DHCS Medi-Cal Managed Care account.
