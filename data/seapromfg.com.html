Bay Boats

The Sea Pro bay boat has quickly become a leader in the industry with every size and model we offer. Each one is designed with a tremendous balance of serious fishability and family fun.

The large bow and stern casting decks provide spacious and insulated storage,livewells and rod holders galore, coupled with lockable rod boxes that can be accessed without even having to board the boat. The in-floor lazarette behind the leaning post provides easy access to all critical components while keeping the weight distribution low and in the middle to promote the best ride characteristics on the market. As with all Sea Pros, the sharp entry at the bow along with the “Carolina flare” we are known for make these rigs worthy of dry comfortable rides even on days where rough chop would stop a good day of fishing or boating.

The many seat configurations that we offer including some that are stowable and removable, leave the best set-up for your family needs entirely up to you for each outing. We load the boats with standard features such as power assist steering, premium sound system, 24/7 monitoring from your cell phone and Simrad electronics to make your only choice which model suits your needs.

Deep V Boats

The entire Sea Pro Deep-V center console line up were designed and built around the needs of an avid fisherman. From the open cockpits,easy access livewells, numerous insulated fish boxes to the toe rails and bolsters. All while having the ability to transform into the perfect cruise and sand bar rig for the family.

Comfortable seating with armrests and loungers, insulated coolers/storage, removable picnic tables with cupholders, power assist steering, 24/7 boat monitoring from your cell phone and awesome premium sound systems that are standard on every model. All Sea Pro models are sharp on bow entry to knife waves and have that “Carolina flare” create a soft, comfortable and dry ride.

The only decision that’s needed is which one best fits your family’s needs and budget.
