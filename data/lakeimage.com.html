Welcome

Celebrating our 28th year as a world leader in the design, development and manufacture of print inspection systems for the global commercial print, labelling and mailing industries. By reading and processing the variable data now commonplace on many printed items our systems inspect, verify, track and report on millions of documents, labels, plastic cards and packages daily using codes such as OCR, barcodes, 2D codes, postal barcodes, RFID and much more.
