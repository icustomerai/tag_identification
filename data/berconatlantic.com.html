Bercon Atlantic Ltd is a supplier of high density plastic bottles used for packaging in the dairy, bottled water and fruit juice industries. for over 10 years the company has been recognized as a leader in the blow molding of proprietary plastic bottles in the Atlantic Province's.

Bercon Atlantic can provide customers with a complete supply of high quality 4 litre, 2 litre, 500 ml, 350 ml and more. We keep a large inventory and can usually ship orders out within 24 hours of receiving purchase order.

At Bercon our employee's take pride in their work and are continuously doing quality assurance to ensure that our products reach the highest of quality before leaving our plant. This kind of quality assurance can give our customers assurance of the product they have purchased, which enables them to virtually have no incoming inspection at their site.

Bercon Atlantic is located in the Grandview Industrial Park in the city of Saint John in the beautiful province of New Brunswick, Canada.
