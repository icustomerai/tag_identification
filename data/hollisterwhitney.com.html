HOLLISTER-WHITNEY: A HISTORY OF QUALITY SINCE 1899

For 120 years, Hollister-Whitney has invented and manufactured solutions for the vertical transportation industry, with an emphasis on cutting-edge technology and the highest quality products.

Founded on a commitment to unmatched quality and technology, we supply elevator contractors and operators with a wide range of reliable elevator equipment. Our line of products, including elevator hoist machines, platforms, slings, safeties, guides, and governors, are built to ensure all passengers are moved quickly and securely.

As a key member of the Vantage family of suppliers, we also have the ability to serve our customers with a comprehensive range of additional products and services. Take a look at the various brands and service centers here.
