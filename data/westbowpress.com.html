Who We Are & What We Do

As a division of Thomas Nelson and Zondervan

  • We offer publishing, editorial, and marketing support to help Christian authors spread the Good News to as many people as possible.

  • We specialize in books with Christian morals, inspirational themes, and family values.

  • We adhere to the highest editorial standards established by HarperCollins Christian Publishing.

  • We connect authors with experienced industry professionals for guidance and support throughout the publishing process

  • We provide Christian authors the opportunity to be discovered through Thomas Nelson and Zondervan.

  • We recognize and reward notable books and encourage their continued success.

Our Editorial Standards

We strive to serve others who want to share His Word through publishing.

  • All of our titles adhere to our WestBow Press Editorial Standards, established by HarperCollins Christian Publishing, in collaboration with WestBow Press.

  • By publishing with us, our authors ensure their titles are associated with a self-publishing company that shares their Christian values and beliefs.

WestBow Press is a self-publishing alliance of HarperCollins Christian Publishing and Authors Solutions. Books published through WestBow Press are offered author support, editorial, marketing, and bookselling services through publishing packages managed by Author Solutions. HarperCollins Christian Publishing utilizes WestBow as a resource to discover emerging new voices, for Thomas Nelson and Zondervan, which have a proven market demand. Information on top performing WestBow Press titles is provided to the acquisition teams at Thomas Nelson and Zondervan for consideration. For more information visit our History page.
