Samaritan CrisisCare closed on Monday, April 11, 2022. If you are in need of crisis services, please call Crisis Now at 1-833-580-2255

We would like to thank the countless CrisisCare staff who served our community over the years, and the thousands of people who put their faith and trust in CrisisCare at the most difficult times of their lives. CrisisCare served the Montgomery County Community for over 25 years providing 24-hour crisis hotline, walk-in, and mobile crisis services.

For over 50 years, SBHI has provided behavioral health services to people of all ages, from birth to seniors. Samaritan Behavioral Health continues to provide mental health, substance use, and physical health services to individuals and families throughout Montgomery, Preble, and Miami Counties.

To find a location and service that meets your needs, please call 937-734-4310 or 937-734-4311.
