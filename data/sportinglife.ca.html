Meet Morgan Cooper - Arc'teryx Women's Design Director

Morgan has been with Arc’teryx since 2019. After earning her BFA from Parsons in New York and working in the luxury market for many years, Morgan is thrilled to be based in the Coast Mountains of BC where she’s able to combine her two passions – a love of the outdoors and designing highly technical apparel that is both refined and functional.

Meet the Sporting Life Arc'techs

The Arc’techs are Sporting Life staff members who share the Arc’teryx ethos with customers and fellow staff members. Through product-focused experiences, the Arc’techs bring Arc’teryx to life in our stores. They create inspiring and informed educational opportunities for their colleagues and memorable Arc’teryx product moments for our customers.
