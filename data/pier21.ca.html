Special Exhibition

Revealing Chignecto

May 13 to July 23, 2023

Parks Canadas exhibit, Revealing Chignecto: The Stories Within, looks deeply at the history of this region and focuses on its archaeology: vestiges that represent millennia of Mikmaw communities, Acadian villages, and 18th century forts. Memories live here, across Chignectos lands and waters.

Special Exhibition

In My Yesterday

May 13 to July 23, 2023

Artwork, historical objects and documents reflect artist JJ Lees Chinese Maritime familys immigration experience in this new temporary exhibition. The mixed media installation tells the remarkable story of her familys multi-generational emigration from China, between 1916 and 1957.
