What Is a Home Warranty?

A home warranty covers (or helps pay) the costs for repairing or replacing systems and appliances when they stop working. The service contract for a home warranty is one year. Home warranties are meant to complement your homeowners insurance to cover things that insurance doesnt, such as improper maintenance and normal wear-and-tear.

A home warranty from American Home Shield offers even more benefits than a typical home warranty. Members enjoy perks such as discounts on new appliances and services like pre-season HVAC maintenance, tech set-up and installation, and more.

Why should I have a home warranty?

Its inevitable. At some point, your appliances and home systems will likely break. And American Home Shield is one of the best ways to protect you when it happens. If an item can be fixed, we help cover the cost of repairs. If it cant be repaired, we replace it. Our plans are a great way to save money, as well as time spent looking for help.

What type of things do home warranties cover?

American Home Shield covers your important systems such as the major parts of plumbing, electrical, heating, and air conditioning systems. We also cover laundry appliances, kitchen appliances, and more so you have more protection and value than a single appliance warranty.

Is it worth having a Home Warranty?

Take this into consideration. If you have an air conditioning unit that needs to be repaired or replaced, the costs from your pocket can be up to $3,800 if you dont have a home warranty. However, if your air conditioner is covered by American Home Shield, your costs would only be your pre-determined Service Fee $100 or $125. Or we will replace it if the unit cannot be repaired, based on the home service plan coverage you selected.
