What We Do

Industry Leader

Whether you know it or not, you have probably used our products when designing or building a house. Providing dealers with high margins and quick lead times, builders with tremendous value-add products, manufacturers with easy distribution, and homeowners with beautiful finishes, our collection of products sit atop each of their market niches.

Technology Driven

Using proprietary technology, we have changed the way millwork products are manufactured, marketed, and distributed. From the development process, to the manufacturing floor, to quoting jobs, to traditional and untraditional distribution, all the way to installation and finishing, our innovative practices separate our brands from the pack.

Amazing ROI

Each of our products is specifically designed to add exponential value to your projects. With the addition of Endura-Thane moulding, Lifetime vinyl shutters, Timber Craft gable brackets, or products from any of our other brands, your project instantly increases in value.
