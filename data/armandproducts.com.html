    Unsurpassed Strength

    Armand is one of the world’s largest manufacturers of potassium carbonate, and is backed by two of the industry’s biggest brands.

Armand Products was incorporated in 1986 through a joint partnership agreement between Church & Dwight Co., Inc. and Occidental Chemical Corporation (OxyChem), two of the chemical industry’s largest brands. While we might be big – we’re one of the world's largest producers of potassium carbonate and the only producer of potassium bicarbonate in the U.S. – our approach to customer service has a very personal touch. Simply put, we view our customers as partners. We know their businesses and support their needs 24 hours a day, seven days a week. Whatever you may need – from fast turnaround orders to troubleshooting a technical issue – when you’re an Armand customer, we have you covered.
