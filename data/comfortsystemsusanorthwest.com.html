ABOUT US

Comfort Systems USA Northwest is a leading design-build, full-service provider for mechanical and plumbing building systems in the Pacific Northwest. Our steady growth and committed customer base give testimony to the success of our company philosophy – provide a quality job at a competitive price.

Our dedicated and professional project crews and service technicians take great pride in the projects we’ve completed and the solid customer relationships we have developed.

As part of the Comfort Systems USA family of companies, we are publicly traded on the New York Stock Exchange and you can look up our complete financials anytime you want (NYSE:FIX).

We have the financial stability and bonding capabilities to deliver almost any type or size of construction project.
