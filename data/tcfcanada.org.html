The Citizens Foundation, Canada is a CRA registered charity (Registration Number: 844142273RR0001) incorporated with the objective of providing educational opportunities for underprivileged children. It achieves this goal with the help of its intermediary The Citizens Foundation (TCF). TCF is a professionally managed, non-profit organization set up in 1995 by a group of citizens who wanted to bring about positive social change through education. 28 years later, TCF is now one of Pakistans leading organizations in the field of education for the less privileged.

Vision

Equality of Opportunity

To provide a level playing field that facilitates upward social mobility for the underprivileged
to result in personal dignity, independence and better citizenship.

Mission

Quality Education

Provide quality education to open minds and enrich life through the power of good values and intellectual curiosity for a better future.
