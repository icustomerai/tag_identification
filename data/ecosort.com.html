Welcome to EcoSort

EcoSort plays an important part in our area’s recovery of recyclable material. EcoSort is a Material Recovery Facility (MRF) designed to accommodate large quantities of items that can be recovered from the waste stream.

Eco Sort is open to the general public and waste haulers, accepting debris from construction sites and demolition projects when measured at 6 yards of material or more. This debris is sorted and prepped for recycling.

Ecosort also receives demolition material from many construction/demolition companies and building contractors in the Eugene/Springfield area. (6.91MB PDF)
