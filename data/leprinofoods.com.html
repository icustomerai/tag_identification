Welcome to Leprino Foods Company

A global leader in mozzarella cheese and dairy nutrition products.

Cheese Nutrition

A Passion for Excellence

Our commitment to excellence runs decades deep, starting over 65 years ago as a small, family-run cheese business. Today, Leprino Foods is proud to be the world’s largest mozzarella cheese maker and top producer of whey protein and dairy ingredients. Still family-owned, we pride ourselves on our reputation of being a dairy industry pioneer, supplying the highest-quality products to food and nutrition companies around the globe.
