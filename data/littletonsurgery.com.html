About

Welcome to Littleton Day Surgery Center

We understand preparing for your upcoming surgery may be an anxious time for you.

We are looking forward to your visit and would like to familiarize you with our facility to help make sure you have a pleasant experience. Our center is comprised of a pre-operative area, operating rooms, a laser room and a recovery area all designed for your comfort and care. The center has a spacious waiting room and plenty of free parking right at our front door. We welcome all patients who wish to tour our facility and meet the staff prior to surgery. Please contact us in advance to schedule time for a tour.
