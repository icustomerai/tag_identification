Company Overview

METGLAS® Inc., located in Conway, SC, is a
world leading producer of Amorphous Metal Ribbon.

In 1970’s, Metglas, Inc. (then Allied Signal located in Morristown, NJ) pioneered the development and production of amorphous metal, a unique alloy that exhibits a structure in which the metal atoms occur in a random pattern.

The key to Metglas Inc.’s proprietary manufacturing process is the rapid-solidification of molten alloy at a rate of approximately
one million degrees Celsius per second.
