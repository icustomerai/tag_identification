Who We Are

We Produce the Chemicals That Become Everyday Products — and So Much More.

At Chevron Phillips Chemical, we like to say our work improves lives around the world, all the time. In the necessities that keep people safe from harm, and in the packaging that protects food and other products. In the processes that improve efficiency and value. And in our commitment to our communities and the planet.
