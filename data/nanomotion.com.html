About Company

Nanomotion Technology

Nanomotions patented Ultrasonic Standing Wave motors provide unlimited linear and rotary motion. The standing wave transmits silent motion with the ability to function in many challenging environments and achieve the utmost in precision positioning. There are a wide range of motor sizes that allow for operation in a closed loop servo system, driving motion control systems and a family of small, low power motors that are optimized for use in optronic applications. Nanomotions motor technology supports motion requirements for applications in Semiconductor, Medical and Optronic markets.
