Specialty Films

Presto is a market-leading supplier of products ranging from private label food and disposer bags to packaging closures to soil stabilization materials to specialty stretch films. With entrepreneurial beginnings as a manufacturer of plastic bags and wrap, we are now a business of Reynolds Packaging Group. This combination gives us the strength and agility to provide leading-edge products and services that help our customers compete.
