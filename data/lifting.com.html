    FAQ

    Who is Bishop Lifting? >

    Bishop Lifting, founded in 1984, is the most trusted name in the lifting world. Bishop Lifting has the deep expertise, services, and support to solve both routine and complex lifting challenges. They have the above and below-the-hook inventory customers need, when they need it. And they have a culture of service and support that puts customer satisfaction first.

    Where and to whom does Bishop Lifting sell? >

    Bishop Lifting is Headquartered in Houston, Texas with additional branch locations throughout the United States. Our Headquarters also sells our lifting and rigging products worldwide. We sell directly to end-users, resellers, and also to the U.S. government for related projects.

    How does Bishop Lifting ship orders to customers? >

    Our standard methods of shipment are UPS and Motor freight (for orders over 150 lbs.). Bishop Lifting ships worldwide to meet the needs of our customers. However, for speedier delivery, we offer Hot Shot services. In addition, we also offer inspection, documentation, and completely Incoterms 2000 compliant shipping.
