About Us

Leading Industry Experts in 4G/5G Telecom Domains
  • Served Tier 1 Telecom Carriers in USA, such as AT&T, T-Mobile, Verizon and BSNL, BBNL India, etc over the past 1.5 decades since 2007 with industry standard certifications TL9000 & ISO27001, ISO14001 & ISO45001.
  • Offers Design & Engineering, Build & Deploy, Test & Integrate, and Operate & Maintain NW Engineering Services (NES) for NextGen Wireless, IP-OTN and IoT networks for Tier-1 operators.
  • Company has Domain specialists, Subject Matter Experts and Technology Advocates with a team of ~600 RAN/Optical/IP NW professionals.
  • Has proven and time-tested credentials in SDN/NFV, Cloud architecture based 4G/5G Network Solutions.
  • Achieved cumulative revenue of US$ 370 M over 4 years with Indian & Overseas lines of NES business.
  • Company focus is on the Digital WiGig Broadband era of Products along with home-grown software Tools by Best-in-class Talent.
  • QuadGen provides Network solutions in Multi domain, Multi technology on Multi OEM platforms, thus enabling customers with best-in-class quality, cost efficient and faster go to market strategies for improved network capacity, coverage and optimised network performance.

Arogyaswami Paulraj

Dr. Arogyaswami Paulraj

Professor Emeritus - Stanford,
USA, Inductee in NIHF, USA, Padma Bhushan Awardee

  • Paulraj is an Emeritus Professor at Stanford University, USA and is regarded as the inventor of MIMO (Multiple
    Input Multiple Output), which is the core technology in all modern wireless systems from 4G, 5G and WiFi.
  • He is known as the father of 4G wireless (WiMAX that later evolved to LTE).
  • Padma Bhushan awardee by the Govt of India
  • An inductee in the US Govts National Inventors Hall of Fame
  • Recipient of Marconi Prize (equivalent of Noble Prize in Telecommunications), IEEE Alexander Graham Bell
    Medal, (Worlds Highest Honour for Engineering) and the Friendship Award (Govt. of PR China).
  • He has over 400 archival research publications, 80 US patents and several textbooks in MIMO wireless.
  • Prof Paulraj served in Indian Navy for 25 years and led the development of APSOH sonar during 1977-83, the
    worlds leading sonar technology at that time.
  • Founder of Center for Artificial Intelligence and Robotics (DRDO) (First Generation AI Scientist in India), Central
    Research Labs (Bharat Electronics, Bangalore) and co-founded the Center for Development of Advanced
    Computing (CDAC) for SUPER Computing Development in India.
