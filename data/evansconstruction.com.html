About Us

Evans Construction Company is located in Jackson, Wyoming & was founded by Monty & Sherry Evans. The company was started in the fall of 1968 by purchasing an old D6 Dozer. Monty & Sherry grew the business from that one dozer through the years, entering into aggregate production in 1972, the ready mix business in 1976, and the asphalt business in 1989. Evans Construction serves the greater Teton County and is the leading integrated construction materials provider in the area, specializing in heavy civil construction, asphalt paving, aggregate production, and ready-mixed concrete supply. Projects range from small residential to city streets and highways to multi-million dollar developments.

Evans Construction is a drug-free company with a full-time Safety Officer, DOT Specialist, and Quality Assurance Professionals on staff. We are committed to the safety and well-being of our employees and the motorists on the road. Our Quality Control and Quality Assurance program ensures that the quality of all of the products we offer are the very best available and that they meet and/or exceed specifications. You can rest assured that when you hire Evans Construction, the work will be done with safety and quality in mind.
