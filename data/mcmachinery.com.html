    ONE STOP. ONE SOURCE. ENDLESS EXPERTISE.

    MC Machinery Systems is North Americas premier supplier and servicer of Mitsubishi lasers, EDMs and press brakes, along with fabrication and milling equipment from OPS Ingersoll, Roku-Roku and ADIRA.

    Technologies and solutions

    Our expertise spans virtually every aspect of metalworking - from simple fabrication to CNC-driven, automated manufacturing cells. Each machine is backed by our expansive regionalized support network - a team of MC Machinery experts that you can rely on for training, maintenance and on-site service.
    AUTOMATION

    Automation can be the key to consistent part quality, shorter lead times and decreased operational costs. MC Machinery offers laser, EDM, press brake and milling automation systems.

    LEARN MORE
    EDM

    MC Machinery offers wire and sinker EDMs that are accurate, cost effective and provide the best overall speed and performance. Our wire EDMs feature user-friendly controls and internal designs that minimize downtime and reduce wear and tear for lower maintenance costs.

    LEARN MORE
    LASER

    MC Machinery supplies and services Mitsubishi fiber lasers, 2D CO2 lasers, multi-axis CO2 lasers and laser automation, including the GX-F ADVANCED Series of artificial intelligence-enabled fiber lasers and the ASTES4 High Speed ADVANCED laser automation system.

    LEARN MORE
    MILLING

    MC Machinery offers milling machines that are ideal for high-speed and tight-tolerance jobs. Every machine is inspected to the highest standards.

    LEARN MORE
    PRESS BRAKE

    Our press brakes range from 40- to 2,000-ton capacities with anywhere between 3 and 11 axes, featuring tool layout, collision check, bend reports, back gauge editing and DiamondSoft software with auto tooling.

    LEARN MORE
    REMOTE MONITORING

    MC Machinerys remote360 remote monitoring system for lasers and EDMs monitors real-time performance, performs diagnostics and predictive maintenance and accesses remote support.

    LEARN MORE
    SHEARS

    MC Machinery offers hydraulic shears that are cost-effective, customizable, and rugged enough to handle many different types of materials and thicknesses.

    LEARN MORE
    CONSUMABLES

    Using the right consumables can increase machine productivity and longevity. Our Consumable Products Group offers OEM consumables for milling machines, EDMs and lasers, including nozzles, optics, wire and much more.
