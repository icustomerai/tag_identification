Road Machinery LLC |

68 YEARS OF SERVICE

Since 1955, Road Machinery has been committed to its customer's success by supplying product support solutions to the mining, quarries, production, and earthmoving industries. We started out selling street sweepers, motor graders, and air compressors from our 7th Street, Phoenix, Arizona location. Today, Road Machinery partners with world-renowned brands, including Komatsu, providing equipment sales, rental, parts, and services through its 12 strategic locations across Arizona and California, including world-class remanufacturing facilities for Diesel Engines, Transmissions, Electrical Rotating Devices, and Hydraulic Cylinders.
