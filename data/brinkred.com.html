Electrical Transmission Leaders

As a nationally engaged and commissioned electrical contracting expert serving the western half of the United States, we are leaders specializing in high voltage transmission lines, distribution lines, fiber optic line installation, storm damage repairs, substation construction, and electrical contracting. Headquartered in Rapid City, South Dakota, we boast an excellent track record of ongoing relationships and past projects completed with nationwide utility clients.
