Why Washington National?

1911

For over 100 years, Washington National has been helping Americans protect themselves from the financial hardship that so often comes with critical illness, accidents and loss of life.

$5.6B

We’ve returned more than $4 billion in premiums to our policyholders and have paid out more than $2.8 billion in claims to our supplemental health and life insurance customers.
