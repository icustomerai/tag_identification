About Farrell Lines

Farrell Lines is a U.S. flag roll-on, roll-off carrier that provides international end-to-end transportation services to the U.S. government and commercial customers. Our modern fleet and experienced colleagues ensure that you receive exemplary service every time you ship with us.

» Services

We offer ocean carriage, inland transportation, and logistics services for your rolling stock and oversized cargo.

» Customer Relations

Our knowledgeable team is available anytime to make global transportation of your cargo smooth and simple.

» Fleet

Our U.S. flagged fleet offers the reliability and efficiency of new vessels and flexible deck configurations to handle your rolling stock cargo.
