TOP NOTCH® family consists of 100% solution dyed polyester fabrics, providing superior colorfastness along with superior strength, abrasion resistance, and dimensional stability. Products in the family include 11.5 ounce (Top Notch), 9 ounce (Top Notch 9), 6.5 ounce with a clear coating on one side (Top Notch 1S), and flame retardant (Top Notch FR) versions.

Learn more about
the Top Notch family of products

TOP GUN® family consists of premium coated fabrics, providing superior tear strength, abrasion resistance, and all around performance. Products in the family include 11.5 ounce (Top Gun), 9 ounce (Top Gun 9), coating on only one side (Top Gun 1S), and flame retardant (Top Gun FR) versions.

Learn more about
the Top Gun family of products

ODYSSEY® family consists of economical coated fabrics, providing a cost effective option for many applications. Products in the family include 6.5 ounce (Odyssey), 5.5 ounce (Odyssey Lite), 5.5 ounce water permeable (Odyssey Open), and flame retardant (Odyssey FR) versions.

Learn more about
the Odyssey family of products

When needing an abrasion resistant fabric, SOFTOUCH® is the fabric you want. This fabric is a combination of an acrylic-coated woven polyester and a soft non-woven backer of 100% polyester.

Learn More about Softouch

AQUA-TITE® GREEN is a premium grade water repellant that also provides stain protection. Unlike most other available water repellants, AQUA-TITE® GREEN has a very low VOC level, meeting all known country, region, state and area regulations.

Learn More about Aqua-Tite Green

Coated and Laminated Fabric Products

Marlen Textiles manufactures coated, laminated, and solution dyed polyester fabrics for use in a wide range of applications. Our polyester fabrics are the strongest there is and are treated to be resistant to water, mildew, weather, rot and abrasion, and can be treated for fire resistance. We also have complete capabilities to do custom coating, toll coating or custom laminating. Look below for more details on what we have to offer.
