Introducing the new

Faxitron® Sciences & NDT

Hologic’s increased portfolio dedicated to life sciences and non-destructive testing, allows for scientists and researchers more timely discoveries and effective ways to ensure the integrity of their work in areas of pre-clinical, agriculture, and forensic imaging and non-destructive testing.

We develop solutions to help you see the world with more clarity.


Pre-Clinical Imaging

Small animal models are commonly used to study conditions from metastatic cancer to obesity and osteoporosis. Hologic’s Faxitron cabinet x-ray systems allow scientists and researchers to screen, track, and evaluate disease and structural changes in longitudinal studies.

Learn More


Agricultural Imaging

Our imaging systems are used by seed banks, government agriculture and forestry services, botanical gardens, and seed producers to optimize plant and seed health.

Learn More


Forensic Imaging

High-resolution images are important in forensic applications, such as determining cause of death through excised bones and organs; gun, bullet, and firing analysis, and counterfeit items. Our systems allow for scientists and researchers to unveil the truth in the past and present with more efficiency and accuracy.

Learn More


Non-Destructive Testing

Hologic Faxitron systems are widely used in a variety of light NDT applications, from electronics to gems, R&D, small batch QC and defect analysis. Our range of cabinet sizes, x-ray sources, and detector imaging areas ensure a solution for almost any cabinet-based NDT application.

Learn More
