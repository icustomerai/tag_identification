      Sealing & Flow Control Engineering

      Our know-how

      In addition to the versatile standard products that we offer in our catalogue, our customers often have individual requirements, whether in the field of hydraulics, automotive, or other areas of application. After clarification and definition of these requirements, we work out solution options in close coordination with the customer, which work throughout series production and introduction, as part of our certified project management.

      With the introduction of the agile project method in 2021, we now have the ideal work process for continuously involving all participating trades and the customer from an early stage.

      Material selection

      When it comes to selecting the right material, we leave nothing to chance. While observing the applicable legislation and in cooperation with universities, research institutes, and the know-how of our suppliers and experts, we have developed comprehensive material specifications that satisfy the physical properties in order to meet even the most demanding requirements.

      Latest methods and processes including

      During the product development phase, we ensure, among other things, that all relevant departments make their timely and qualified contribution through agile project management, whether in SCRUM or KANBAN.

      From the first concept, which is accompanied and secured using FEM or flow simulation methods, to modern and high-quality analysis and test methods, we ensure the correct processes on the way to the secured product over the course of development. Of course, we make consistent use of quality assurance tools such as FMEA throughout the development stages and different prototype stages.
