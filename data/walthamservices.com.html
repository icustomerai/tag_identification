Scary to pests. Helpful to our neighbors.

We’ve been providing quality pest control to local home and business owners since 1893.

Why Waltham?

We are proudly celebrating our 130th anniversary as the original pest control company in the New England area. That’s a lot of years dedicated ourselves to providing fast, quality pest control to homes and businesses in Massachusetts, Connecticut, Maine, Rhode Island, New York, and Vermont. With that many years under our belt, you can be sure you’re getting the best pest control services around.

Satisfaction Guarantee

We’re here to get rid of pests in as few visits as possible. Our experience and expertise allows us to guarantee your satisfaction.

On Staff Entomologists

Our Board Certified Entomologists continually implement the latest knowledge and technology in our programs.

Safety
