About Western Stevedoring

Recognized as a leader in cargo handling innovation and operational excellence, our diversified stevedoring and marine terminal operations safely and efficiently handle forestry, steel, grain, autos, cruise passengers and project cargos to and from global markets. Our business was built and still stands on a foundation of exceptional service, integrity and respect – for our colleagues, clients and our communities.

Western Stevedoring is a division of Western Group.

Our Services

Expertise that matters

Terminal Services

We operate strategically located terminals in B.C. including facilities in North Vancouver, Squamish, Nanaimo and Victoria to drive cargo movement through the supply chain.

Learn More

Stevedoring Services

We provide a full range of stevedoring services handling forestry, steel, grain, bulk, autos, cruise passengers and project cargos to and from global markets.

Learn More

Cruise Services

We operate cruise facilities in Victoria, acting as Agent/Manager for the Greater Victoria Harbour Authority. Our subsidiary Pacific Northwest Transportation Services connects passengers to shuttle bus services and tours.

Other Services

Our expertise extends to other value-added marine services including off-dock operations, inland logistics facilitation and marine transload facilitation.
