About Iris Power

Iris Power, founded in 1990, offers sensors, monitors and diagnostic solutions for the assessment of the actual state of generators, motors, dry-type transformers and air-insulated switchgear, so that the risk assessed and the necessary maintenance can be planned. In 2010 Iris Power LP was acquired by Qualitrol Corporation, a global leader for condition-based monitoring.
