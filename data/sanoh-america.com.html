    Environmental Policy

    Sanoh America, Inc. and Sanoh Canada, Ltd. (collectively Sanoh) are dedicated to being responsible corporate citizens in Environmental Health and Safety through Management involvement and a Compliance Plus philosophy. Every employee of Sanoh is responsible for achieving this mission as an integral part of his or her duties.

    Principles & Practices

    Sanoh will strive to achieve its environmental mission through:

    • Continuous improvement, in all areas of environmental protection, pollution prevention and human environmental health & safety by setting objectives and establishing a meaningful set of metrics to measure progress.
    • Education and motivation of all employees to conduct their activities in an environmentally responsible and safe manner.
    • Eliminating or minimization of environmental pollution from the conduct of Sanoh operations.
    • Reducing, recycling and reusing natural resources and energy consumption, to the best of our ability in our daily activities, in our products and in our services.
    • Identifying and complying with all applicable environmental legislation, regulations, industry standards and internal customer requirements, including these principles and practices
