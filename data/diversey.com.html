Taking care of what's precious since 1923

Our story began a century ago in Chicago. We have since expanded to over 100 countries around the world, and are physically present in 80+ countries; creating innovative cleaning products that meet the needs of a resource-challenged world. At Diversey, Taking care of what’s precious means going beyond clean and taking care of life, of people, businesses, but also local economies, growing industries, and the planet we call home.
