Doing Business with IWA

A General Insurance Agent and Specialty Insurance Agent working with local agents offering coverage to Main Street Businesses.  IWA is a general insurance agent giving local agents access to commercial insurance through admitted carriers without a carrier appointment.  Check out some of the industries we service on our Program’s Page.  Our main carrier, is QBE, although we have access to Travelers, Hartford, Liberty Mutual and more.  You decide what carriers we approach on your behalf.  

Since we opened our doors in 1946, Irving Weber Associates (IWA) has been dedicated to serving thousands of business owners throughout the United States with a wide range of products, including Business Owner’s Policies, Workers’ Compensation, Business Auto, Umbrellas, Cyber, EPLI, Site Pollution Liability, and more.   Our goal is to cater to each customer’s specific needs, offering a competitive rate as well as building long-lasting relationships with our brokers and insurance carriers.

You DON’T have to be Appointed to write business with us!  Just send in your submission, our professional underwriters will review it, and we go from there.  Once you are ready to bind your first account with Irving Weber Associates, Inc, we will ask you to complete a Producer Agreement and submit a copy of your E&O and License.  It’s that simple to do business with Irving Weber Associates, a National General Insurance Agent & Specialty Insurance Agent.  At IWA, it is our mission to provide outstanding and efficient service to our agents and their customers.  Through IWA our agents have access to a host of admitted, A-Rated, companies.

IWA’s Approach

As a General Insurance Agent and Specialty Insurance Agent, IWA gives local agents access to admitted carriers (AM Best) without the need for a carrier appointment for retail, LRO, manufacturers & more.  We work with local insurance agents to customize an insurance program that addresses their client’s risk profile while also aiding in mitigating risk and minimizing losses. An integral part of our success, in fact, is our valuable risk management tools – from loss control to claims handling and educational resources. Throughout our seven decades ensuring local, main street businesses, a lot has changed in the industries in which we specialize because of technological advances, evolving consumer demands, diversification, regulations, and other factors. One constant is IWA’s commitment to stay abreast of these changes and emerging trends and ensure that our Commercial Insurance programs continue to reflect the risks our customers face today and into the future. Considering IWA’s longevity, experience, and focused expertise as the foundation of our organization, you can trust our commitment to superior customer service. At IWA, our goal is to make working with us easy for the local insurance agent.  That’s why we don’t require a producer agreement until you are ready to bind your first account with us.  Simply send in your submission on one of our many insurance programs.  We will review it and send you a quote.  Once you are ready to bind your first account, that’s when we’ll ask you to complete the Producer Agreement and submit a copy of your E&O and License.
