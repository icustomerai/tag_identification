What We Do

Injection Molding

Western Container utilizes the latest technology in two stage injection molding to manufacture preforms. We currently operate 37 injection molding systems with outputs of up to 1.6 million preforms/day on a single machine.

Blow Molding

Western Container utilizes the latest reheat blow molding technology to convert preforms into bottles. We currently operate 15 blow molding systems with outputs of up to 1.4 million bottles/day on a single machine.

What's Our Mission?

Western Container is a packaging company dedicated to maximizing value for our Coca-Cola Bottler shareholders.

  • Lowest Cost
  • Highest Quality and Service
  • Assurance of Supply

Western Container is built around our Core Values of Integrity, Personal Responsibility, Professional Behavior and Mutual Respect. We achieve our mission through these values and our consistent investments in training and investing in the latest technologies.
