Garbage In, Garbage Out

No one wants to deal with trash, but it must be contained and hauled away. Out of sight and out of mind are our goals, and Toter meets these challenges with the most convenient and durable carts on the market. Our products are guaranteed to meet or exceed the needs of the municipal, retail/residential and pro/specialty markets.

Finding Treasure in Trash

Toter gets our customers the products and services they want, when they want them. We offer numerous services, including project planning, data-driven cart management, assembly and delivery, and cart maintenance to help you start or enhance a collection program. Our Customer Support Group is a real treasure — and we’re here for you!
