Our Academic Dental Software Products

axiUm has been on the forefront of revolutionary healthcare software since 1995. axiUm Dental Software caters to both Academic and Enterprise operations. Our Academic edition is a comprehensive dental education solution that helps with business administration, clinical efficiency, and student management. Our Enterprise edition offers a scalable solution to improve operations and increase profitability for larger dental service organizations.

LEARN MORE

Ascend Academic is the first cloud-based academic dental software on the market. Ascend Academic was built upon the trusted private-practice Dentrix Ascend software and specifically designed for the academic market (dental and hygiene schools). Ascend Academic is responsive to fit your needs securely within your environment. With cloud-connectivity, youre able to lower IT costs and streamline updates and support.

Discover the Benefits of Revolutionary Academic Dental Software

Exan Software ensures the solutions we create not only benefit our clients, but make an impact in the dental industry as a whole.

Data Driven Decisions

Data that can be sourced through Exan Software solutions will allow your enterprise to tweak operations, become more efficient, and generate reports that assist your clinicians to see trends in diagnostics and procedures.

Guaranteed Standards of Care

Ensure your patients get the best care. Exan makes this possible with solutions that automate basic functionalities for providers. In addition, our solutions allow admin and clinical directors to set rules and alerts, ensuring clinic rules are followed and nothing falls between the cracks.

Scalable and Customizable

Built with the flexibility to accommodate your ever-changing organization, Exan Software solutions adapt to your needs. Our software can handle an unlimited number of locations and providers and is scalable to any size.

Advanced Integration and APIs

Our solutions are built to integrate with a variety of tools your organization requires to run efficiently. Interface or integrate with Hospital Information Systems, Digital Imaging Systems, and more. Exan Software is flexible enough to work with your existing or future systems.

Clinical and Operational Efficiency

Your academic dental institution is comprised of a variety of people, positions, and tools. Our software joins these together. Make data entry a snap with Exans single-point-of-entry Electronic Health Record (EHR) forms that can easily be distributed.

Compliance and Security

Exans Software solutions are one of the only dental software solutions with features that allow for true HIPAA compliance. In fact, from user access, to field level auditing, to encryption Exan Software security features are in a league of their own.
