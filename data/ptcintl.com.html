PTC International Inc.

Create & Grow

PTC International, Inc. is an international sales and marketing company based in Baltimore, MD with offices around the world, including China and South Korea.


We have provided innovative solutions for our customers since 1980, and pride ourselves on our abilities to apply marketing concepts to real world projects which focus on long term growth, creativity and accomplishment.

About

We consider both our suppliers and our customers as our partners and value each and every relationship.


Our Baltimore office employs men and women from various cultures, backgrounds and ethnicities. We are a diverse company, curious and knowledgeable about our local community and the global economy.
