Forged Railway Wheels and Axles Standard Steel is proud to say all of our products are "MADE IN USA."
Recycling Starting from recycled scrap metal, we melt the steel needed for our operations at our Burnham, PA, facility.
Forged Wheels Each wheel is forged, heat-treated and machined right at our facility and undergoes at least five quality tests before it is shipped.
Forged Axles Each axle is hammered into shape using our axle forging machine. Each ingot of steel produces two axles, which are torch-cut, heat-treated and machined at our facility.
Award-Winning Customer Service Standard Steel maintains the highest level of service to its external customers. By listening and reacting to customer feedback, we can better serve the needs and desires to build lasting business relationships with our customers.

TTX SECO Award Winner - 28 YEARS

The SECO program measures each supplier in the areas of Delivery Performance, Cost Competitiveness, Total Quality Management, Customer Service and Finance and Administration. Standard Steel is the only TTX supplier to receive this prestigious award each year since it started in 1991 - 28 consecutive years.
