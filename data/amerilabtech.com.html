Amerilab is a full turnkey manufacturer of customized effervescent tablets and powders and non-effervescent powders. We develop, produce and package these custom products for our clients.

An industry leader for 25+ years.   Contract manufacturing done right!

 

Amerilab has two patents around effervescent tablets!

Effervescent Tablets That Include Crystalline Sugar Binder and Methods of Making the Same

Patent 10,966,920

  • Disintegration time < 4 minutes, 20 seconds
  • Crystalline sugar binder
  • Natural sweetener
  • Clean label flavor carrier – gum acacia

Rapid Disintegrating Effervescent Tablets and Methods of Making the Same

Patent 10,912,733

  • Disintegration time < 4 minutes
  • Natural lubricant
  • Crystalline sugar binder
  • Natural sweetener
  • Clean label flavor carrier
