Colonial Oil Industries, Inc.

Colonial Oil Industries, Inc. (COI), serving the region since 1921, is one of the largest independent oil companies in the Southeast.

With strategic ocean terminals in Savannah, GA; Charleston, SC; Jacksonville, FL; and Wilmington, NC as well as numerous terminals along the pipelines, COI has an extensive network to supply gasoline and diesel fuel to the Eastern United States.
