The leader in perforated metal solutions for over 95 years

Ferguson leads the perforating industry in producing exceptional products, custom designs, manufacturing capabilities and flexibility, custom support and quality (ISO 9001, AS9100, NADCAP certifications).

Our dedicated engineering, manufacturing, sales, and quality teams strive to excel in every aspect to satisfy your requirements and build our partnership.

Ferguson believes in developing long-lasting customer relationships and strives to exceed everyone’s expectations. Our customers are the focal point of our business.
