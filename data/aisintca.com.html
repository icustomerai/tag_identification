Message from ATC-A

Aisin Technical Center of America (ATC-A) was founded on the necessity to establish strategic product development capability, localization and customer support for the Americas. ATC-A’s goal is to lead product development activities for the American market. We have been an independent entity within the Aisin Group since July 2008 and merged with AW Technical Center of America in June 2021 as well as Shiroki North America in July 2022.

The automotive industry is entering a “once-a-century” era of dramatic change. To survive, thrive and continue to grow, ATC-A must continue finding new value proposals for our customers, refining them together.

ATC-A has taken on more local engineering responsibilities and now manage the complete product lifecycle, thus making us a more nimble operation, able to better engage and serve our customers. The solid foundation built by achieving Aisin Group Philosophy and Aisin Group Way will aid in our pursuit of our next Vision.

Our ambitious next Vision includes developing unique products that meet our customers’ needs for today and in the future. This enormous pursuit is only possible through a global team and a harmonious effort. ATC-A collaborates with our global partners, but more importantly, we have assembled here a deep knowledge and skills base, and our team members represent a strong, local foundation.

Aisin Group's ultimate goal is to contribute to the enrichment of human life, for our associates by providing a work environment that offers development opportunities and initiatives so they can leave a positive legacy; for our customers through offering an innovative, world-class quality product line that delivers ever increasing value; and for our local communities by promoting, sponsoring and participating in various social activities.

In our brief history as an independent corporate citizen, ATC-A has fostered strong ties with our neighbors and local communities and will continue developing these relationships.

Thank you for your interest in our company.
