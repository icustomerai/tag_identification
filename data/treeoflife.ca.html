TREE OF LIFE Tree of Life has been growing brands in Canada for over 70 years. We have unparalleled category expertise in our industry, serving brand partners, customers and consumers, “Helping Canada Eat Well and Live Well”.

We are a flexible, full-service route to market with access to over 95% of where food is sold and served across Canada. Selling, marketing and logistics are core areas of expertise. FRESH & DELI A fresh perspective

As one of the largest holders of import cheese quota in Canada, we know fresh. We call on all deli’s and cheese counters across the country with a unique portfolio of products. SPECIALTY & GOURMET Refined products meet refined palates

Tree of Life is synonymous with specialty. Retailers look to us for unique and innovative food offerings that engages their most profitable shopper. NATURAL & ORGANIC Excellence comes naturally

We consistently monitor insights and trends in the health food industry to offer natural and organic products that meet the needs of the Canadian health-conscious shopper. Innovation is a constant. Your Food service solution As an industry leader,

we travel the globe to bring you the latest on-trend products. With over 30 00 international food products to choose from, Tree of Life adds innovation to the menu.

About Us

Helping Canada Eat Well and Live Well. It’s What We Do.

Here at Tree of Life, our passion for brands and making a difference in the lives of Canadians across the country is at the core of what we do. Together as a team, we are meeting the needs of today’s health conscious consumers living a wholesome, healthy and ethically responsible lifestyle.

With three distribution centres and 4 sales offices across Canada, we have the ability to reach over 90% of where food is sold and served.
