WE ARE THE KENTUCKY CEMENT PRODUCER

Kosmos Cement Company has been an industry leading supplier of cement across Kentucky since 1905.

Kosmos Cement Company is one of the few remaining American-owned cement producers with our main operation located in Kosmosdale, Kentucky. We also operate seven cement terminals in Kentucky, Ohio, West Virginia, Pennsylvania and Indiana. Our sister company, Fairborn Cement Company, is located close by in Fairborn, Ohio, and helps provide our market area with top quality MIAMI masonry cement. We operate under the traditional values of hard work, honesty and experience and our teams are motivated by their passion of the products and dedication to customer service. As a major producer of high quality portland cement products, and a distributor of MIAMI masonry cement products, we partner with distributors, contractors and businesses throughout Kentucky and surrounding states.

We are continuously exploring new ideas and product innovations to contribute to the construction of better schools, emergency response facilities, roadways, bridges, commercial and industrial facilities and residential housing to name a few. We have a significant impact on Kentucky’s economy and jobs which gives us great pride in being a local supplier and community partner.
