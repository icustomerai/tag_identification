Certifications & Quality Approvals

United Performance Metals is an AS9100 and ISO9001 certified organization, with select locations also certified to the ISO13485 Medical standard. United Performance Metals is regularly audited to the AS9100, ISO9001, and ISO13485 standards by the 3rd party certifying body, NSF-ISR. We purchase only prime materials from industry leading mill sources. Materials are certified to popular industry standards like ASM, ASTM, MILS and others. In addition, UPM holds a unique portfolio of OEM approvals spanning a wide range of industries from Aerospace and Space to Medical and Power Generation, setting us apart from the competition.
