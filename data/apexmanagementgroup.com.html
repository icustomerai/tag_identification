Apex Management Group (Apex) was formed in 2014 to meet the complex needs of the Patient Protection Affordable Care Act (PPACA). By partnering with industry leaders, Apex provides comprehensive health benefit solutions for companies seeking to comply with the evolving PPACA. Apex provides Minimum Essential Coverage (MEC) plans for members who typically cannot afford a comprehensive Major Medical Plan.
