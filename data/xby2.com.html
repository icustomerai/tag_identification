What We Do

Business and Technology Transformation for Healthcare and Insurance.

X by 2 has served leading healthcare and insurance companies for over 20 years. This focus has resulted in rich insights, a record of successful initiatives, and a deep understanding of the unique challenges facing healthcare organizations and insurers.

Healthcare Transformation for Payers and Providers.

Our innovative yet pragmatic approach to the complex challenges facing the healthcare industry allows payers and providers to transform the way they do business, while focusing on what matters most, patient care.

LEARN MORE

Driving Change for Property and Casualty and Life Insurers.

We help property and casualty and life and annuity insurers embrace industry disruption with transformational solutions that address the changing needs and increased demands of both agents and policyholders.

HOW WE HELP

Healthcare and Insurance Solutions Across the Journey.

Highly capable, our team engages at your level of need leading, supporting and collaborating with your team at any and every stage of your transformation journey.

Strategy

Strategy

Bilingual in business and technology, our domain experts will work with you to develop a strategic vision aligning people, process and technology that delivers your future state vision. Strategy in flight? Well provide an unbiased assessment of the approach, your organizations readiness to implement, and alignment to business goals.

Technology Options & Evaluations

Technology Options & Evaluations

Technology is a strategic investment. The wrong decisions can impact your business goals and your bottom line. Deeply technical, our team can evaluate your needs, align them to resources and goals, and create best-fit technology options to achieve success. We believe in developing practical solutions that not only achieve results, but are adopted across the enterprise.

Mobilization

Mobilization

Identifying and organizing the right resources is the secret sauce to an initiatives success. Without proper mobilization an initiative is sub-optimized from the start. We know how to check egos at the door, identify the best resources irrespective of title or position, and organize for success. Thats why you hired us.

Implementation

Implementation

Its an unfortunate fact that 25% of IT projects fail outright, while nearly 50% need significant rework once implemented. Our approach is to do things right the first time around. X by 2 brings the planning, leadership, experience, expertise, creativity and persistence required for implementation success.

Change Management

Change Management

Changing technology is easy compared to getting people to change their ways. We get that, and we also get that the most successful initiatives have broad buy-in and support. We know how to align people and projects to achieve optimal change adoption and impact. Theres no magic formula it happens when proven experience combines with empathy and communication.
