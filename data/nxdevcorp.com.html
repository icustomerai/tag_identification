Transforming Brain Cancer Surgery Through Innovation

At NX Development Corp, we are changing the way brain cancer is treated. Our product, Gleolan is used to help neurosurgeons remove malignant tissue during surgery in patients with high-grade gliomas. Our hope is to improve the lives of those affected by this devastating disease.

Revolutionizing Brain Tumor Visualization With Gleolan™

The team at NX Development Corp is dedicated to revolutionizing the field of fluorescence guided surgery with our groundbreaking imaging agent, Gleolan™.

As a leading life sciences company, we are committed to developing innovative solutions that benefit cancer patients and their surgeons.

About Us

NX Development Corp was founded as a life sciences company dedicated to the commercial launch of Gleolan™ in the United States as a real-time imaging agent to assist surgeons in visualizing brain tumors during surgery.

FDA Approval and Market Authorization for Gleolan™

In 2017, NX Development Corp received FDA approval for Gleolan™, a real-time imaging agent that assists surgeons in visualizing brain tumors during surgery.

As the market authorization holder for Gleolan™ in the United States, we take pride in offering this revolutionary product that has transformed how brain cancer surgeries are performed.

Gleolan™: A Breakthrough in Neurosurgery

Our flagship product, Gleolan™, is a groundbreaking addition to the field of neurosurgery.

By enabling fluorescent visualization of glioblastoma, Gleolan helps surgeons identify and remove cancerous tissue with unprecedented precision.

Empowering Doctors With Novel Diagnostic Options

We believe every patient deserves access to the best possible care, which is why we are committed to developing and advancing new imaging options for brain cancer.

Through our work in brain cancer solutions, we hope to unlock the potential of innovative technologies to provide brain cancer patients with hope and a better chance at recovery.

Pioneering the Future of Brain Tumor Resection

As a leader in the area of fluorescence guided brain surgery, we are constantly exploring new avenues for diagnosing and treating this disease by pushing the boundaries of what our product can do. Get in touch with us to find out more.
