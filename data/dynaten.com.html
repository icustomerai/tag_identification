James Belota

Vice President of Skilled Trades

James Belota serves as a Vice President of Skilled Trades for DynaTen. With more than 42 years of experience in this profession and over 23 years at DynaTen, James also sits on the board for the Construction Education Foundation (CEF) and works as a mentor for upcoming craft professionals and leaders in the industry. He has completed a four year apprenticeship program through Tarrant County College of Business, is an HVAC State License Holder, and holds certifications in Sub-Class 100 Clean Room and Leadership Training from Comfort Systems USA. When away from work Mr. Belota enjoys golfing, biking, cooking, and family time with his daughter.

When away from work, Mr. Faulkenberry enjoys spending time with his family, raising cattle, and hunting.

Wesley Baker

Vice President of Construction

Wesley Baker brings over 40 years of experience in the construction industry to DynaTen. Wesley began his career as an Apprentice Pipefitter and served in several capacities, including roles in Project Operations, Project Executive, Vice President of Operations, Business Unit Leader and Vice President of Manufacturing Operations. Wesley has completed the UA Local 100 Pipefitter Apprenticeship and holds a State of Texas HVAC “A” license as well as State of Texas Master Plumbers license with Med-Gas endorsement. He has completed Executive Leadership Programs from TEXO, UT McCombs Executive Program, MCAA Executive Program, as well as Executive Leadership training in Servant Leadership. Wesley is a Six Sigma Greenbelt and has taught multiple Lean Training Principles programs. He served as President of MCA Dallas, MCA Texas Board of Directors, UA JACT Apprenticeship Committee, and many other trade organization committees. Away from work, Wesley enjoys real estate investing, watching the grandkids play sports, live theater with his wife, and enjoying family fun time around the pool. He is married, to Pamela, and has seven kids and six grandchildren.
