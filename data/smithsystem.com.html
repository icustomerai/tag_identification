Built for Learning.

 the collaborative classroom experience.
We make school furniture specifically for pre-K-12 teachers and students. Everything is tailored to how they work, how they think and even how they fidget. When you’re ready, we’ll partner with you to curate the best possible spaces – school by school, classroom by classroom.

We don’t make educational furniture as part of what we do. It’s the only thing we do.

As a school furniture company committed to improving and enhancing the learning experience, we design wholesale school furniture for all learners. With a century of experience, we’ve learned what makes education different from any other environment (spoiler alert: quite a lot), and have made it our business to cater to those unique qualities. About Smith System, the most innovative school furniture company.

Seating

Our seating school classroom furniture includes the Flowform®, designed for outdoor learning spaces, and the Oodle® which provides multidirectional rocking motion.

Teachers

Our teacher furniture line includes desks, shelves, files, lectners, and seating options, designed to help teachers in their demanding jobs.

Tables

Tables from our school classroom furniture collection adapt to any and every classroom space. Smith Systems offer more than 150 table options for every place and purpose.

All new movement optional seating.

The school classroom furniture option designed for every body type. Oodle® provides both flat and rocking settings, and facilitates collaborative learning and common areas seating. Designed exclusively for our school furniture company by award-winning designers Joe Ricchio and Charlie Patterson of Ricchio Design.
