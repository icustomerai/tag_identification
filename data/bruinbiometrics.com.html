Who we are

Bruin Biometrics LLC is a pioneer in sensor based medical devices and is committed to the development of assessment devices for early identification and monitoring of chronic, preventable conditions.

Our mission

Our mission is to modernize care pathways through developing and collaboratively integrating new-to-the-world biometric-sensor based medical devices.

Our lead product is the Provizio® SEM Scanner, a hand-held wireless device that identifies increased risk of pressure injuries/ulcers 5 days* earlier than visual skin assessment1 regardless of skin tone2.

Scan and collect patient data in real-time, site-by-site and patient-by-patient.

When placed in the Provizio Charging Hub, the collected data automatically transfers to the comprehensive Gateway Dashboard for patient, ward and facility management purposes. Data can be analysed via the Dashboard or downloaded to a CSV file.

The Provizio SEM Scanner is commercially available via our Exclusive Distribution Partner Arjo AB to find out more or contact your local Arjo representation please go to www.arjo.com.
