WHO WE ARE

We are a closely-held, diversified, on-highway, transportation services company whose subsidiaries operate in a variety of industry segments, including retail automotive, truck leasing, transportation logistics and professional motorsports. Penske Corporation manages businesses with consolidated revenues of more than $39 billion, operating in over 3,200 locations and employing more than 70,000 people worldwide.
