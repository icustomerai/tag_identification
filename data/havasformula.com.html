We Make Brands Braver

Havas Formula is an award-winning, mid-sized, national public relations agency with offices in New York, Los Angeles, San Diego, Chicago, Denver and Miami. We believe that in order for a brand to truly differentiate itself in the marketplace it has to be brave. It takes bravery to push through the clutter to emotionally connect with consumers, who want to understand the story behind your brand and where they fit in. This requires moving beyond traditional thinking and traditional marketing approaches to build that narrative and establish an ownable brand position.

Brave brands become passion brands. And passion brands become beloved brands.
