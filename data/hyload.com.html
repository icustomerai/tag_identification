Building Envelope Systems

Commercial Roofing Solutions Hyload roof membranes are high performance; thermoplastic roofing membranes compounded with a proprietary combination of either coal tar pitch, and Elvaloy® KEE resins (H150E) or PVC and Elvaloy® KEE resins (AlProof). Both membrane types are reinforced with dispersed polyester fibers and manufactured without liquid plasticizers permitting the use of stronger more resilient raw materials and a unique bonding adhesive system. In addition, both membrane types can be fully adhered to the substrate offering excellent resistance to wind uplift.
 Waterproofing Coatings Solutions The most energy-efficient, watertight, light reflective, fluid applied, Energy Star solutions! Learn more about our roofing and waterproofing solutions. Waterproofing Membrane Solutions The HyProof waterproofing membrane is a coal tar elastomeric (CTEM) waterproofing membrane with the primary components being coal tar pitch, PVC, and DuPont ® Elvaloy ® KEE resin. These membranes are tough, durable and chemically resistant against various environmental contaminants.
