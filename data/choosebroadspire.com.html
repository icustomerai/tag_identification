The right care for claimants.
The right processes for you.

Providing the right care doesn’t mean costs have to be out of control. Working with Broadspire means tapping into a meticulously crafted set of solutions for the results your business needs and the care your claimants deserve. Every claim is managed by an industry-leading team of medical experts that not only ensures patients are receiving the right care, but also that processes are efficient and costs are accountable. By integrating innovations in technology and analytics into daily processes and workflows, we enable efficient recovery that benefits both the claimant and the payer.

Driving performance in the program, captives & alternative market space

Whether you represent an MGA/MGU, carrier program, group captive, or a risk retention group, we have the experience and expertise to meet your unique needs. We understand the nuances of this complex market, the risks associated with it, its service demands, and its specific reporting needs.

Learn how we can apply our compassionate medical management, world-class technology, and insightful data analytics to build a completely customized program that meets your specific needs.

Better measurement leads to better management

We believe that data becomes valuable when it informs and drives program improvement. Behind each of Broadspire’s services is a base of industry-leading data and analytics used to shape and improve service delivery throughout the life of a claim and implemented across entire programs by our client service teams. With a company-wide focus on continuous improvement, we are geared towards looking for advanced ways to analyze data, find new insights, and share those findings with our customers.
