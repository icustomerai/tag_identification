Metroplex Energy
is a trusted and reliable fuel supply partner

Metroplex Energy is an Atlanta-based wholesale fuel company that secures bulk fuel to supply rack sales and delivery of gasoline, diesel, and biofuel products by pipeline, rail, truck, barge, and vessel.

Distribution System for unbranded gasoline & Diesel fuel

Metroplex provides high-quality supply with timely delivery at competitive prices. We move over 3 billion gallons annually of unbranded gasoline and diesel through a system of marine and pipeline-connected terminal assets with access to Magellan South, Explorer, TEPPCO, Colonial, Plantation, and Central Florida pipelines. Our integrated systems provide live, real-time information, and our exceptional team offers extensive experience in trading, supply, and logistics.
