ABOUT CASTLE

Getting its start in the Midwest in 1995, Castle Contracting has worked to build a reputation as a premier sitework contractor. Driven by a solid understanding of the possible issues existing beneath the surface, the team is known for providing exceptional quality in extraordinary situations – often with unusually tight schedule expectations.
