Higher Intelligence for Industrial Automation Systems.

Plug it in. It works.

Holjeron designs, engineers and manufactures brains for industrial automation equipment and material handling systems—including conveyors and motor-driven rollers. Original equipment manufacturers (OEMs) and system integrators appreciate that every Holjeron control device and component is plug-and-play for fast installation, minimal maintenance and expanded diagnostics.

Each of our innovative products is based on industry-standard technologies, including Controller Area Network (CAN) technology, to support DeviceNet®, Smart Distributed System™(SDS), CANopen, Ethernet /IP® and AS-i  protocols in industrial automation.

As the in-house designer and manufacturer of our entire product line, your control device or component can be customized specifically to meet the unique requirements of your application—while providing maximum return on your investment. We offer full support for all of our products, including legacy Holjeron components that have been retired, but still provide critical functionality in the systems they support.

CONTROL PRODUCTS
MotorWorX® Custom Motor Controls

MotorWorX customized direct current (DC) motors and controls for OEMs are specially engineered to handle difficult control application requirements of machinery, conveyor/sortation equipment, material handling system and vehicle builders.

Custom OEM Controls

Application-specific control devices engineered to interface to machines in a number of ways, including custom PCI cards for network control, machine controls for high voltage interfaces, position controllers, embedded logic controllers, wireless networks and environmentally hardened devices.

Conveyor Controls

Low-cost ZoneLink3® 4-zone controllers include devices for zero-pressure accumulation (ZPA) applications and run a range of conveyors, including 24-volt direct current (VDC) motorized roller, pneumatic, variable frequency drive (VFD), or standard alternating current (AC) motor driven systems. Specialized modules for merge/divert/transfer (MDT) applications are also offered.

Process Controls

Analog and digital input/output (I/O) products incorporate built-in diagnostics, presets and deadbands—creating the flexibility needed when putting a distributed network in the field.

Microrollers/Smartrollers

Microroller® internally-powered, motor-driven conveyor rollers lower the total cost of conveyor ownership by optimizing system throughput, reducing installation and maintenance costs, and using less overall power.

CIP Network Technologies

Many control products can be built with standardized Common Industrial Protocol (CIP™) network technologies, including EtherNet/IP™, DeviceNet® and ControlNet™.
