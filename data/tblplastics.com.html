Now Part of SaniSure®

TBL Performance Plastics is an ISO 9001:2015 registered manufacturer of non-metallic single-use products tailored for bio-pharmaceutical manufacturers, CMO’s, drug development companies and related OEM’s. We offer industry leading expertise in developing non-metallic fluid transfer and storage products with a heavy focus on meeting the specific needs of our clients.

Our manufacturing capabilities include tubing, fittings, and single-use assembly. With the integration of all three divisions, our customers benefit from faster delivery times, less supply chain risk, added variety, innovation, and ultimately lower costs.

TBL specializes in working with many polymeric materials including silicone, Kynar (PVDF), ADIF Polypropylene, TPE’s, TPV’s, phthalate-free Vinyl, Hytrel, Nylon, PTFE, FEP, PFA and many other engineering polymers.
