Aisin Canada, Inc.

ACI is the only Aisin plant in Canada, which is located in Stratford, Ontario, Canada. ACI supplies both Toyota plants in Canada with various parts. The plants are in Woodstock and Cambridge, Ontario.

About Us

ACI manufactures a range of quality products for Toyota and Lexus models. These products include sunroofs, doorframes, moldings and seating and small components.

ACI stands behind the core beliefs in placing safety and health above all else, harmony with society and nature and continuous growth of values, cultures and customs.

Aisin Canada welcomes team members, clients and visitors with disabilities and promotes a safe, diverse, and inclusive working environment that is free from all forms of violence, harassment, discrimination, and reprisals. Service dogs are welcomed in all office spaces. Team members, clients and visitors who require support person(s) or technology support will be accommodated to the best of our ability. ***The full accommodations policy is available upon request***

Aisin strives to provide products and services that are accessible to all. We are compliant with the Accessibility for Ontarians with Disabilities Act (AODA) requirements and strive to continually improve our services to better serve the world.
