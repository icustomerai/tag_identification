About the DFW Metroplex

The DFW region is the 4th largest metropolitan area in the United States, and the region is home to more than 7.5 million people. The DFW Metroplex is home to the major Texas cities of Dallas, Fort Worth, and Arlington. Dallas, founded in 1844, is the 9th largest city in the United States, and Fort Worth is the 13th largest city in the country. The DFW area is in North Texas, a region known for its sweeping hills, cowboy culture, beautiful botanical gardens, and delicious BBQ.
