Franchising for Dummies, the leading book on franchising, was written by our founder and Managing ​Director Michael Seid and the late Dave Thomas, founder of Wendy’s International. It does not replace the need for professional business and legal advisors, but gives the reader a solid grounding in the world of franchising that will make them better decision-makers, business managers, and operators.
