FAST SDD® Detectors

The FAST SDD® represents Amptek’s highest performance silicon drift detector (SDD), capable of count rates over 1,000,000 CPS (counts per second) while maintaining excellent resolution.

70 mm2 FAST SDD® Detectors

Amptek has developed a 70 mm² FAST SDD® Detector in a TO-8 package. Triple the count rate vs. the 25 mm² SDD with the same performance.

1mm Thick Detectors

1mm thick detectors improve efficiency above 10 keV, improve sensitivity and detection limit at higher energies, and all while using the same TO-8 package and pinouts.

OEM Solutions

Amptek offers a complete line of OEM detectors, preamplifiers, digital processors, and power supplies, and can work with you to help develop custom solutions unique to your needs.

Si-PIN Detectors

Amptek’s Si-PIN Detectors are still the workhorse of the XRF industry because of the combination of good performance and low cost.

Charge Sensitive Preamplifiers

Get the best performance with solid state detectors, proportional counters, photodiodes, PM tubes, CEMs, or MCPs by using Amptek Charge Sensitive Preamplifiers.

Digital Multichannel Analyzer

Compromising nothing in performance, the MCA-8000D is a low power, light weight instrument which is exceptionally versatile and easy to use.

Amptek AT A GLANCE

Amptek is a high technology company and a recognized world leader in the design and manufacture of state-of-the-art nuclear instrumentation for the satellite, x-ray and gamma ray detection, laboratory, analytical, and portable instrumentation industries.

I The XRF method

What is XRF?

The process of emissions of characteristic x-rays is called “X-ray Fluorescence,” or XRF. The XRF method is widely used to measure the elemental composition of materials.
