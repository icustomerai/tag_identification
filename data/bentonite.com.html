ALPHASIL®

ALPHASIL® is a unique, specialized Hydrated Sodium Calcium Aluminosilicate (HSCAS) selectively mined from bentonite reserves in Wyoming.  ALPHASIL is manufactured at our Safe Feed/Safe Food certified facility in Lovell, WY and is recognized as the highest quality bentonite in the world. 

Explore ALPHASIL

EXPANDA® Bentonite for Cosmetics and Personal Care

EXPANDA products are composed of natural bentonite clay and are used as a stabilizer, viscosifier, suspension aid, binder, and anti-caking agent.  Color ranges from very bright white to gray and viscosity from low to high.  

Explore EXPANDA

Organoclay - Oil wettable clays

Bentonite Performance Minerals offers multiple organoclay products for use in environmental remediation, animal feed, and oil-based emulsions.
