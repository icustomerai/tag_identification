Family of Locations

Family of Locations

Liebovich represents an integrated family of full-line metals service centers across the Midwest, providing an expansive inventory of ferrous and non-ferrous steel, aluminum and coiled metals and a comprehensive range of metals processing, supported by our 100+ fleet of flatbed trucks that deliver daily.

The Liebovich family of companies includes Liebovich Steel & Aluminum Company with full-service locations in Northern Illinois, Wisconsin and Iowa; Hagerty Steel & Aluminum Company in Central Illinois; and Good Metals Company in Michigan.

Liebovich operates as a wholly-owned subsidiary of Reliance Steel & Aluminum Co. (NYSE: RS), a leading diversified metal solutions provider and the largest metals service center company in North America.
