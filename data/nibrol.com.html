Nibrol is a state of the art manufacturing facility, located in Lancaster, SC, whose primary product line consists of textile components for the window coverings industry. These products include cord, laddertape, bead and ball chain, cloth tapes and decorative edge bindings. All cord and ladder products are produced to very strict tolerances from high tenacity polyester yarns to ensure minimum shrinkage and elongation.

Nibrol has over 30 years of experience in the window coverings industry and is also known for its superior customer service, on-time deliveries and innovative product developments.
