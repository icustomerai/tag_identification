Who is Net Health?

Net Health is a leader in healthcare innovation, providing integrated software solutions that empower restorative caregivers in the vast healthcare landscape. Specializing in EHR software and healthcare data analytics, Net Health is revolutionizing the way healthcare providers manage patient information, streamline operations and improve patient outcomes.

Net Health's comprehensive electronic health record (EHR) systems allows clinicians to focus on what truly matters. Our customers enjoy customized EHR software and predictive analytics that enhance patient engagement, improve patient outcomes, and eliminate the complexities of documentation.

Innovative Restorative Care Solutions

Net Health is at the forefront of developing software for restorative care professionals in rehab therapy and wound care, providing advanced software solutions that support recovery and rehabilitation.

Our restorative care software assists healthcare providers in creating personalized care plans, monitoring progress, and adjusting treatments as necessary, all to ensure the best possible patient outcomes.
