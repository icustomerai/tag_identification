We empower people to live financially healthy lives.

Strategic Financial Solutions operates at the crossroads of technology, information, and empathy, with a range of brands that provide effective solutions for the financial needs that arise throughout our customers' lives.

Our Brands

Strategic Financial Solutions operates several independent products offering loans, debt relief programs, and budgeting tools for consumers hoping to improve their financial health and wellness. What all our brands share is a commitment to cutting-edge technology that delivers a seamless customer experience, as well as a deep belief in the value of personal connection. Our highly trained sales and customer service representatives work tirelessly to help our clients understand and take control of their financial future.

Since Strategic was founded over thirteen years ago, we have used this unique approach to successfully fund loans and resolve more than $1 billion in debt for more than 100,000 consumers across the United States.

Strategic Financial Solutions

For over 14 years, Strategic Financial Solutions has been dedicated to helping people live financially healthy lives. Through our family of brands, we empower and advocate for our clients while helping them navigate a brighter, financially strong present and future. In doing so, we deliver superior financial results to all of our stakeholders.

Our Core Values

At Strategic Financial Solutions, our Core Values are an integral part of who we are. Since our founding, we have grown to over 800 team members in New York City and Buffalo, New York, and our Core Values have guided us along the way. By living our Core Values, every day, we continue to attract the best and brightest talent, achieve unsurpassed employee satisfaction, and deliver incredible results for our clients.

  • Innovation

    Always look to improve our talent, processes, and products. Be open to new ideas.

  • Results

    Commit to achieve the best outcomes for our clients. Continuously track and measure everything and use it to always be improving. Be proactive, not reactive.

  • Teamwork

    Work together to reach common goals. Mentor your peers.

  • Integrity

    Act professionally and positively. Build trust by doing what we say. Be upfront and communicate thoroughly.

  • Opportunity

    Reward results. Encourage individual growth. Recognize positive attitudes.

  • Empathy

    Be sensitive to clients’ difficult situations. Partner with the clients to change lives.

Strategic Dreams

Strategic Dreams is a unique and innovative program that allows the employee-owners of the company to submit dreams that they would like the company to help grant. The work we do on behalf of our clients is incredibly important and we believe that we should also do good on behalf of our team.

Strategic Dreams allows us to meaningfully give back to our hard-working teams. Each quarter, we grant dreams such as help with engagement rings, all-expense vacations, home remodeling, tuition payment, and many more.

Strategic dreams is in its fourth year and has granted over 170 dreams.
