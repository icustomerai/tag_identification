To Our Valued Customers

First and foremost, we want to thank you for continuing to trust Raven Transport with your business. We take our obligation to provide timely and efficient transportation for your products seriously and have taken steps to ensure the health of our work force.  Both our drivers and our office team in Jacksonville, Florida are taking steps to ensure that the continued and uninterrupted flow of services continue.

In light of the current outbreak of COVID-19, we would like to provide an overview of the precautions we are taking here at Raven Transport to ensure our ability to provide the highest level of service to our customers as we prepare to limit our employees’ potential exposure to the virus.
  
Our Business Continuity Plan covers a wide variety of measures that we are taking. These include team members working remotely as well as team members in our office in Jacksonville.  We anticipate no disruption to operations or service levels currently as we remain fully staffed. We are committed to providing capacity and moving the essential products our nation needs to keep moving forward.   So, in short, we are open for business and will continue to provide critical transportation services.
  
We have implemented the following precautions to protect our employees and our customers from potential exposure to COVID-19:    

  1. We are actively monitoring the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), the World Health Organization (WHO), and local public health departments for recommendations and best practices.
  2. We have worked with building management in all of our facilities to increase cleaning and disinfection processes.
  3. We have limited the size of internal meetings and require remote access to be provided to all participants.
  4. We have implemented travel restrictions on all non-critical domestic travel and fully restricted international travel.

We will continue to monitor the development of this situation. Rest assured we will continue to implement best practices during these challenging times.  

Thank you for your continued trust in Raven Transport. We remain committed to serving your transportation needs.
