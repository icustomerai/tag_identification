We’ve been providing Service to
Central Wisconsin for over 40 years

Ron’s Refrigeration & Air Conditioning, Inc. has been providing Central Wisconsin (Wisconsin Rapids, Plover, Stevens Point, Marshfield, Nekoosa, Port Edwards, Vesper) and surrounding communities with refrigeration, heating, air conditioning, and electrical service for 40 years. No matter how large or small your system is, Ron’s Refrigeration places it’s number one priority on timely, efficient maintenance and repair.

And when we say 24 hour on-call service we mean it. We have at least two trained service technicians scheduled for after hours availability. Call us any time at 1-800-236-0005. It doesn’t matter if it’s July 4th or Christmas Eve, our service technicians will be available for you.

Our technicians have professional training that is unrivalled in the industry. We are proud of our work, our reputation, and the satisfaction of our customers. We offer free estimates and we look forward to exceeding your expectations.
