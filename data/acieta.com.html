Robots Help Manufacturing Companies Meet Challenges

  • Robots do the dirty, dangerous and menial tasks so humans can assume more challenging and satisfying jobs.
  • Robots keep working without vacation time, lunch breaks or sick days.
  • Robots increase productivity and quality, helping companies grow and become more competitive.
  • Robots can be programmed easily and quickly by trained and skilled workers.
  • Robot production today has reached the point at which they can be purchased and installed in less time and for less money.
  • Robots create jobs in robotics, programming engineering and integration. Other jobs are created as the higher quality and increased productivity lowers costs and stimulates demand for everyone.

Not All Industrial Automation Solutions or Robotic Integrators Are Equal

Industrial robotic automation services require much more than simply adding a robot. You need an automation integrator: a robotic automation company that ensures that your entire manufacturing process maximizes your robotic equipment and ROI. Acieta believes in partnering with customers to ensure optimization of all processes, not just one component. We are more than just an industrial robotics company that installs an out-of-the-box implementation. We examine your manufacturing flow from raw material receipt to finished product shipment to ensure total efficiency.

By examining your upstream and downstream operations, we provide the highest possible financial return on each project — with lower cost per part piece and increased throughput quantities in shorter timeframes.

Customers who partner with Acieta can be assured that we are committed to their success, and we help mitigate all risks. While other integrators can deliver robot systems, we stand behind our integration systems and we have the capacity to design, install and support multiple systems simultaneously because of our size and financial strength.
