PAYER PORTAL

Already works the way you work, just faster.

  • Securely upload and handle real time or batch payments
  • Automated data workflow with XML templates - no need for paper or manual processes
  • Real time payment status and reporting
  • International payments

PAYEE PORTAL

Add self-service functionality.

  • Secure payee registration with invitations
  • Centralized identification management
  • Real time payment information and access to full payment history
  • Online access to centralized tax document center

Increase your efficiency

Improve and manage security

Personally Identifiable Information (PII) is never made available to your company, which alleviates compliance requirements on your business.

Provide transparency to customers

Unique reports and dashboards for payers and payees. Share data that helps answer most questions before they are asked.
