    Welcome to safe, reliable shipping solutions by Mid-Atlantic Transport

    Mid-Atlantic Transport President, Jerry Shields, has been in the trucking and transportation industry since 1987. He saw an opportunity to enhance client customer service by creating a team of experienced drivers and knowledgeable internal staff. With great experience in the field, he founded Mid-Atlantic Transport in 2018. Jerry is committed to providing a work environment that treats its team with the utmost respect and loyalty.
