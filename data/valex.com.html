As the industry leader in ultra-high purity stainless steel distribution system components, Valex has been helping customers succeed and thrive since 1976.

Uncompromising Quality

Our product quality is the result of 40-years of accumulated experience in supplying the highest grade stainless steel piping components to the most demanding of industries, including the semiconductor, TFT/LCD, and solar markets.

Comprehensive Product Range

From stainless steel tube, fittings, and valves, to manifolds & assemblies, our product portfolio spans sizes as small as 1/8" tube, up to NPS 24 diameter pipe - available in various standards, and wrapped in a multitude of finish options.

Global Service & Support

Our team consists of a network of factory manufacturing locations, and distributor support centers located throughout the world. Our presence is global, so that we can deliver service to you that is local.
