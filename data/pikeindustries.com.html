  • Pike Peaks Interest for Runway Rebuild Project

    The leadership team from Pike Industries welcomed U.S. Senator Jeanne Shaheen, D-NH, who wanted to see firsthand the work taking place at Portsmouth International Airport. Barry Duffy, President; Alex Phelps, VP Construction; Larry Major, Government Relations and Frank Tilton, Construction Manager shared how vital it is to have our construction industry safely working to support our local and state economies. Crews at Pike have been working through the pandemic to make progress on the $24 million project for the 11,322 feet long and 150 feet wide runway which was last reconstructed in 1997.

The Company has doubled in size in the last ten years and currently employs over 800 people throughout Maine, New Hampshire and Vermont.
