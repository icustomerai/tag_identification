THE KOMATSU HERITAGE

Komatsu Forklift Factory-owned Dealerships are wholly owned by Komatsu Forklift USA. Unlike a local dealership, we are backed by the exceptional financial resources of a large global organization. Strong and stable, with a national network of stores, we’re in it for the long haul, working every day to build long-term partnerships with our customers.

From new and used sales and aftermarket support to service, parts, operator training and more, our experts will keep your forklifts operating at peak performance—and help take your business to a higher level. Call or stop by your Komatsu Forklift Factory-owned Dealership today and see the difference for yourself.

Komatsu USA

Komatsu Forklift U.S.A. markets, sells, and provides aftermarket support for Komatsu branded forklifts in the United States, Canada, Mexico, and Latin American countries.  Komatsu forklifts are sold and serviced by a dedicated dealer network with over 200 locations within the regions they serve.
