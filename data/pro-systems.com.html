Automated Solutions for the Plastics Industry

Lincoln Electric Automation is an industry leader in the design and construction of automated machines and systems for the plastics industry. Through cutting edge design, professional project management, and our foundational philosophy to provide consistent and ethical business practices, our company has carved out a unique niche within the automated machinery industry. We take great pride in our forward-thinking capabilities. By embracing new technologies, fostering growth, and promoting innovation, we have developed key strengths that differentiate us from the competition.

Our automation experts will work closely with you to design and develop a solution that meets your specific project needs. Whether the solution is described in a detailed statement of work, or by implementing a creative out-of-the-box solution, Lincoln Electric has the product knowledge and expertise to help you succeed.
