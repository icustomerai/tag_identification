About First Colony Mall

Shopping in Sugar Land

First Colony Mall is the ultimate shopping destination in Sugar Land, TX, for upscale brands in apparel, home furnishings, entertainment, and dining in the Houston market. Plan an entire weekend shopping and dining at one of six sit-down restaurants and exploring Constellation Field, the Smart Financial Performing Arts Center and the Sugar Land Heritage Museum located near the shopping mall.
