Creating sustainable food & travel experiences

deSter is a leading provider of innovative food packaging and serviceware concepts to the aviation and food service industries. By combining over 40 years of industry experience with a passion for design and service trends, we deliver bespoke, customer centric and impactful innovations.

Through dedicated research and a close collaboration with our customers, we keep our finger on the pulse of today’s market. This way we’re able to deliver functional & attractive products that do not only deliver on today’s requirements but also anticipate the needs of tomorrow.
