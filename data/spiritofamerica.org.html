Take Ukraine’s Side. Defend Freedom. Save Lives.

Working with US military personnel, Spirit of America has delivered 200+ tons of protective, nonlethal aid — body armor, helmets, medical supplies, surveillance drones, vehicles, food and more — to Ukrainian soldiers and civilian volunteers on the front lines. We’re meeting urgent frontline needs and providing targeted humanitarian support with speed. Your gift can save more lives and help Ukraine win.

We are citizens serving the nation, dedicated to preserving the promise of a free and better life.

Spirit of America is the only nonprofit that works alongside our US troops and diplomats to provide critical assistance where freedom and democracy are threatened.
