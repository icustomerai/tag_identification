The Premier Aluminum Die Casting Company

Madison Precision Products, Inc. maintains a position of leadership through innovative aluminum die casting technology and quality. We use the most advanced production methods and machining technology available. Our 231,170 sq. ft. facility offers state-of-the-art aluminum die casting. We have continued technological advances in the field of die casting since our inception in 1987. Our facility casts and machines high quality aluminum parts for the automotive industry, in addition to exploring new technology for the use of aluminum die cast products.
