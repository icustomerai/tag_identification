About Us

Berkley Oil & Gas offers unique insurance products and services tailored to the needs of the energy industry. We understand the highly specialized needs of businesses that operate in this industry and have built total insurance solutions to address these needs.

With well over 500 years of combined energy experience, our objective is to provide total account solutions to our customers. This includes specialized coverages, risk services that complement existing safety practices, and claims management that reflects our in-depth knowledge of hazards and losses normal to this industry.

Industry Experience in Risk

At Berkley Oil & Gas, our energy risk specialists all have hands-on experience working in the energy industry. This knowledge of the industry helps our policyholders integrate safety in a manner that supports operational productivity and quality excellence. Their experience and industry knowledge also helps our customers reduce their risk and manage their losses by providing industry-specific training, best practice guides, and technical advice. The experience of working exclusively within this industry enables us to identify and share proven best management practices and risk management solutions.
