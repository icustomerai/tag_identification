Trinity Health System Twin City Medical Center

 

 

Trinity Health System Twin City Medical Center offers you and your family a trusted resource for healthcare. Our professionals are prepared 24 hours a day, seven days a week to treat even the most serious illnesses and injuries. Choose us as your partner in health. 

Our hospital is a locally-operated, not-for-profit, Catholic, critical access hospital. We provide a full range of acute care services, including a group medical provider practice known as Trinity Medical Group. Our hospital is fully accredited by The Joint Commission and equipped with the latest in digital health technology to meet patient needs. The hospital is part of CommonSpirit Health, a nonprofit, catholic heath system dedicated to advancing health for all people.

At Trinity Health System Twin City Medical Center, our priority is to provide quality, patient-centered healthcare. Our staff of more than 250 friendly and experienced professionals is united in the common belief of caring for the whole person—physically, emotionally, and spiritually. You and your loved ones can confidently place your trust in Trinity Health System where the Christian values of Reverence, Integrity, Compassion, and Excellence are carried out every day.
