    Continued care and service that you can count on

    Phone: 800.793.1261  |  Fax: 800.962.1611

    As the coronavirus pandemic continues, our commitment to helping people breathe easier remains strong. We’re here for you and are prepared to continue to support individuals who rely on airway clearance therapies like the Philips InCourage system or Philips Respironics CoughAssist T70.

     

    Please visit our forms and resources page for prescription forms, instruction manuals and other useful tools.

    Forms and resources

    Philips Respironics announced a voluntary recall for continuous and non-continuous ventilators (certain CPAP, BiLevel PAP and ventilator devices, including the Trilogy 100 ventilator, which is serviced by RespirTech) due to two issues related to the polyester-based polyurethane (PE-PUR) sound abatement foam used in these devices.

     

    For information on the Recall Notice, a complete list of impacted products, potential health risks and how to register your device for replacement, visit philips.com/src-update. For recall-related questions, call 877.907.7508. Please note that Philips InCourage vest therapy and Philips CoughAssist T70 are not affected by this product recall.
