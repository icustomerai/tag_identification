Hydrogas® InArm®

A further development on the Hydrogas® design is the InArm® version which incorporates the pneumatic spring and damper within the road arm itself thereby requiring the minimum space claim.

Hydrostrut® Suspension

Hydrostrut® is an external suspension solution for wheeled and tracked vehicles. The suspension unit combines both the spring and damping functions in a lightweight self-contained design which has the added advantage of having a small volume footprint.

Integral Rotary Dampers

Integral rotary dampers wrap around the road wheel pivot shaft for new tracked vehicles and are designed to work in conjunction with a torsion bar spring. They offer the advantage of fitting into the running gear and are integral to the vehicle hull.

 Find out more

Linear Dampers

Horstman Linear Dampers offer rugged low cost damping solutions for new and existing applications on tracked and wheeled vehicles. Sometimes referred to as Shock and Coil, Horstman provide solutions from the lightest All Terrain Vehicle (ATV) to the heaviest  8×8 and tracked vehicles such as the FV430 Bulldog.

Global Footprint

Recognized by armoured vehicle designers worldwide as the benchmark for excellence in the field since the 1920’s, Horstman today works with more than 18 blue chip prime contractors and government research centres. With export sales to 28 countries we provide unrivalled mobility to vehicles ranging from ultra lightweight-wheeled 4×4, battlefield 6×6 and 8×8 wheeled, through infantry and engineer tracked vehicles and up to the heaviest of main battle tanks.

Horstman, a Company of the RENK group, with more than a hundred skilled employees in the UK, US and Canada, we are an agile, creative and growing business. Our core high value added, innovative engineering business has a global footprint in both customer base and supply chain that provides a quality, technically excellent offering to solve customers’ problems, throughout the product life cycle.
