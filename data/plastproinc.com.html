New Craftsman Series

The Craftsman Series pulls design inspiration from 19th and 20th century architecture, featuring flat panels and a traditional styles. This series offers a selection of ovolo profiles, simple Shaker profiles, and sleek, direct glazed options. Explore this series if you're in the market for a modern door with classic charm.

New Nova 90 ¾ Lite Models

The Nova 90 3/4 Lite is a new addition to the Plastpro family, combining the luxurious detailing of a panel design and 3/4 lite with the sleek look of our direct glazed glass and smooth skin. Explore the beautiful glass options we offer in this new design.

What Makes Us Unique

Since 1994, Plastpro has always believed in providing a superior product with the best value, technology, and commitment to our customers. We know how important it is to provide quality service and valuable resource to those in the industry.

HydroShield Technology®

Our exclusive technology protects our doors against moisture and humidity infiltration on all six sides.

PF™ Door Frames

Polyfiber (PF) Door Frames are an innovative new alternative to traditional door jambs.

Plastpro Quality

We do our part to create products with the best technology, energy efficiency, and are environmentally friendly.

Full Length LVL

Plastpro’s full length LVL allows any type of lock to be placed for increased security.

Decorative Doorlites

Our exclusive decorative door glass designs improve the look and value of your home.
