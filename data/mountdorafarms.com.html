Mount Dora Farms

Mount Dora Farms processes jalapeño peppers at its plant located in Comayagua, Honduras. We process nearly 25 million pounds of fresh peppers annually. Mount Dora Farms is equipped to process jalapeños in a variety of forms for food service operators. The bulk of the product is sold within the United States and Europe for later production as “jalapeño poppers.”
