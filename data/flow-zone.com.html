Flow-Zone is the premier distributor of Pipe, Valves, Fittings, Instrumentation, Controls and Measurement equipment for the Upstream, Midstream and Downstream Markets in Texas, New Mexico and Colorado. Serving the oil and gas industry for over 20 years with eleven strategic locations, in the Permian Basin, Delaware Basin, and Eagle Ford Shale with our Corporate Headquarters located in Houston, TX. We provide comprehensive service and support in all areas we are located. Flow-Zone maintains an extensive inventory of pipe, valves, fittings, actuators for automation and fluid transfer products. Our on-demand delivery fleet provides free deliveries to customers within our trade area.
