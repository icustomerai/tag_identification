WELCOME TO PHASOR ENGINEERING

Leaders in Design, Engineering and Automation

Phasor is the leading substation design and automation specialist in Western Canada. We provide top quality engineering from concept development through to construction, panel fabrication, and commissioning to energy companies, municipalities and utility providers. As substation innovators, Phasor works with all major technology brands and has a complete team of in­ house experts in engineering, design, fabrication, testing and commissioning.

Phasor is a recognized expert in IEC 61850 substation automation through the integration of protection and SCADA systems. Our automation project teams consist of experts in SCADA, HMI, protection settings, and commissioning with extensive hands­ on experience.
