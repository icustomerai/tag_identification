Siskin Steel

We are the Southeast’s premier metals service center company and your steel and metals supply chain partner. We have six locations in three states and deliver to over thirteen states daily. We stock and process a full line of carbon steel, stainless steel, and aluminum products in various forms including long products, sheet, and plate. We can custom cut your material with our in-depth processing capabilities.

Processing Services

We have state-of-the-art processing capabilities companywide to serve your needs.

  • laser cutting
  • plasma cutting
  • oxy-fuel flame cutting
  • combo plasma & machining
  • shearing
  • tube laser cutting
  • robotic 6-axis plasma cutting
  • t-splitting & straightening
  • saw cutting

Extensive Inventory

We maintain a large line inventory of flat and long products in carbon steel, alloy steel, stainless steel, and aluminum.
