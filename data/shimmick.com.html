The Shimmick Story

What is the inherent value of history? A deep-rooted culture built upon the legacies of Morrison Knudsen, Washington Group, and Shimmick. Today’s Shimmick is the result of multiple entities becoming one — the people, resources, and experience to carry on a tradition of excellence. We bring a diverse and geographically distributed team. A single-source solution to meet the many needs of infrastructure.
