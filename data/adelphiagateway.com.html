About Adelphia Gateway

Adelphia Gateway, LLC is a FERC-regulated interstate pipeline delivering safe, reliable, locally produced natural gas to constrained energy markets in southeastern Pennsylvania.

Access to Abundant, Low-cost Domestic Natural Gas

Adelphia Gateway LLC provides access to domestic natural gas to more than 250,000 mid-Atlantic households each year. This 84-mile former oil pipeline was converted to natural gas and connects locally produced, low-cost energy to the greater Philadelphia market.
