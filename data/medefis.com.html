Manage contingent healthcare labor with a personalized, vendor-neutral approach and easy-to-use centralized platform.

Contingent labor
management simplified.

The Medefis Vendor-Neutral Managed Services Program (MSP) combines a personalized, hands-on managed service experience with our easy-to-use vendor management system (VMS) platform. This provides a streamlined, centralized approach to contingent labor management, giving you access to millions of healthcare professionals, from hundreds of Medefis-vetted staffing agencies with real-time market rates. It’s that simple.

Strategic, personalized customer support

The Medefis team helps you navigate every aspect of your clinical and non-clinical staffing process, including technical support that ensures you have 24/7 access to the candidates you need.

National network of vendors

The combination of our first-class support team, a vendor-neutral approach, and our coast-to-coast vendor network ensures the most qualified candidates at the most competitive rates.
