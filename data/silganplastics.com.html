Looking to purchase a plastic bottle, jar or closure?

Silgan Plastics sells direct to customers that meet a volume requirement of approximately 500,000 units+ per year with minimum releases of 100,000 each. If you can meet these order requirements, please contact our inside sales at 1-800-2-SILGAN (1-800-274-5426).

 
If your quantity requirements are less than 500K per year, please contact one of our certified contract distributors. Your closest Silgan Plastics distributor location can be found HERE.  
 
If you are specifically interested in PET bottles, we have a catalog of select PET items HERE.   These items are typically stocked and available for purchase in case quantities and for immediate shipment out of our Penn Yan, NY facility.
