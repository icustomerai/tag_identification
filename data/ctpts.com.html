Get Better, Stay Better

Become a Healthier You with Connecticut Physical Therapy Specialists!

We are physical therapists that are trained in advanced orthopedics, foot orthoses prescription, medical exercise therapy, and manual [hands-on] therapy and the developers of our new Posture & Alignment Performance Fitness Training Program utilizing Redcord. We view our clients as life-long partners in their physical well-being.

Redcord

Suspension exercise is among the fastest-growing trends in home fitness, personal training, and physical therapy.

Orthopedic Manual Therapy

Manual therapy is a safe, hands-on technique physical therapists use to move stiff joints and soft tissue.

Performance Training

It was developed to address the high frequency of injuries and limited results gained from typical personal training.

ACL Injury Prevention

ACL prevention include targeted exercises that address muscle strengthening, muscle recruitment patterns, and more.
