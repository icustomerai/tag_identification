Home Decor & Art Gallery

As a tribute to Mary's father who had been in the furniture industry for over 30 years, WebbMarsteller was established in 1993 as a wall-covering showroom at ADAC. Over the course of two decades, and moving to The Galleries of Peachtree Hills, the showroom has grown into a full-scale home furnishings store.

With classic furnishings and interesting objets d’art, WebbMarsteller remains a staple to the Atlanta design community. The store layout is room like vignettes in which transitional yet sophisticated styles mix for an overall look that is classic with a twist. One-of-a-kind accessories are one of the showrooms strongest selling points. Accessories including case goods, mirrors & art are of the quickest moving items. We pride ourselves that the store is constantly evolving.
