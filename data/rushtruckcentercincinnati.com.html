Ford Dealership in Cincinnati, OH

Rush Truck Centers was founded in 1965 and holds a storied history. We specialize in high-quality commercial Ford trucks such as Chassis Cabs, the E-Series, and F-450 Super Duty pickups. Above our targeted and curated selection, our service and attention to detail have made us a cornerstone for businesses in need of commercial Fords.            

An Enduring Vision

In 1965, the automobile market was much more fragmented, and truck sales were still in their infancy, leaving customers no choice but to travel to many noncommercial dealers all around town to see only a handful of truck options each time. It was this situation that inspired Marvin Rush and his two business partners to open a commercial truck-focused dealership in Houston, a city with constant demand due to its east-west truck traffic. His success inspired him to build a network of truck dealerships in the Southern Belt, including in Cincinnati.            

Marvin eventually became the sole owner of Rush Enterprises, which continued to grow and prevail until it became the largest collective truck dealership in the country, with more than 140 locations in 23 states.            

Why You Should Shop With Us

If you are in need of a commercial vehicle, or several, Rush Truck Centers should be the first place you visit. Specifically, Rush Truck Centers - Cincinnati deals in new and pre-owned Ford models of appropriate condition for commercial work. Why trust the one or two commercial trucks from a noncommercial dealer to last, when vehicles for the public are clearly their specialty? You'll save time looking through our lot and can be confident that our sales team understands commercial Ford trucks and the needs of their owners.            

One-Stop Shop for Commercial Ford Truck Owners

We operate as a single hub for all our customer's needs. We can order aftermarket parts to specialize your purchases, and our service and body shop departments will handle all repair and maintenance matters quickly and professionally. Our parts team can help hunt down, order, and install anything you ever need to be replaced or upgraded to save money in the long run or better serve your clients, customers, and employees. All of this on top of an expert financing staff that also handles diverse matters beyond sales, such as rental use and leasing.            

Rush Enterprise dealers like Rush Truck Centers - Cincinnati also sell and provide service for commercial refuse, crane and heavy towing vehicles, tire service, CNG fuel systems, vehicle telematics, and even upfitting critical aspects of models or customizing them with attractive accessories. Whatever your business needs, we'll do our best to provide the perfect trucks and services to help it expand and thrive.            

Today, our dealership in the Queen City continues to be a valuable part of what Marvin created, maintaining the same standards of excellent service and fairness that got us this far. If you're shopping for a Super Duty or commercial-class Ford truck, Rush Truck Centers - Cincinnati should be at the top of your list of dealers.
