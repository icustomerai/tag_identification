    Welcome to Advanced Surgical Hospital

    When you come to Advanced Surgical Hospital, your goal is to go back to the life you love, doing the things you love to do. We want the way you feel when you first visit with us to be as affirming as the one you have long after you leave here.

    Our physicians are board certified, specializing in orthopedics, ortho-spine, as well as interventional radiology and pain management. Each member of the Advanced Surgical team takes the time to form a relationship with you and your loved ones is one of our core values – that’s why we were named one of the top-rated hospitals in the entire country for overall patient experience.

    Advanced Surgical Hospital has even been recognized with a Healthgrades America’s 100 Best Hospitals for Joint Replacement Award™ as the #1 hospital in Pennsylvania for total joint replacement.

    If you’re ready to receive one-on-one care from the top orthopedic surgeons Washington, PA has to offer, reach out to us today.
