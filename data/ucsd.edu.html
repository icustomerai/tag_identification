10 Spaces to Find Serenity On Campus

From rugged coastline and sweeping views of the Pacific to eucalyptus groves and public art installations spread across 1,200 acres of coastal woodland, there’s inspiration to be found around every corner at UC San Diego. When you find yourself in need of some peace, take the time to reset at one of these soul-filling spots.

FIND SERENITY ON CAMPUS

New Mentorship Program Expands Inclusivity in Business

The Department of Economics and the Rady School of Management have recently formed the Business and Economics Mentoring Program, which provides students with access to academic support, peer-to-peer mentoring and more. The program’s goal is to expand the diversity of students in the Business Economics major launched in 2021.
