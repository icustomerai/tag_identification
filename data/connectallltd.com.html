20 YEARS

of service, innovation and quality

CONNECTALL LTD., established in 1994, is a leading manufacturer of stainless steel corrugated hoses and tubular braids, metal and rubber expansion joints, hose and fitting assemblies as well as a wide range of fluid conveying products.

Thanks to its highly qualified personnel with proven expertise in the field of conveying and connecting products, CONNECTALL Ltd. supplies specialty products for a variety of industrial applications.
