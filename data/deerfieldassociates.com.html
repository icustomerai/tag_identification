Deerfield Associates Executive Search, Inc. recruits and places exceptional candidates for senior level administrative positions at colleges, universities and independent schools, both nationally and internationally. Established in 2001, the firm has successfully conducted and completed 600+ searches. Over 92 percent of our 156 clients return to us for additional search work.

Deerfield Associates is a search firm that recruits on a national basis and has offices based in Wellesley Mass, Amherst Mass, Charlotte North Carolina, and Miami Florida.

We are committed to providing our clients:

  • A strategic overview of the hiring profile.
  • A first rate, creative, energetic and pro-active recruitment process.
  • A professional and in-depth assessment for each candidate presented.
  • Constant attention to finding the best candidate.
  • Assurance that our clients and candidates receive the highest level of personalized service.
  • The resources necessary to get the search done in a timely manner.

Each college, university, and independent school has unique qualities and needs. Find out how Deerfield Associates Executive Search, Inc. can select the best professional for your organization.

Deerfield Associates Covid and Vaccination Alert

Deerfield Associates Covid and Vaccination Alert

Deerfield Associates conducts executive searches for leadership positions at top ranked independent schools, colleges and universities across the country.

As the COVID-19 pandemic persists, our top priority is safety and compliance with the various protocols our clients have put into place to protect faculty, staff and students at their respective institutions. It is our policy to comply with those protocols and requirements.

Consequently, we request that every candidate review available health requirements of any institution at which the candidate seeks placement, subject to disability and religious accommodation exceptions under federal and state law. Our general understanding is that all employees of these institutions must be fully vaccinated against influenza and COVID-19 unless otherwise exempted. Again, we refer you to the policies of each potential employer, particularly in light of the rapidly changing nature of responses to the pandemic.
