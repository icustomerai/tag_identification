CLINICAL APPLICATIONS

Cook MyoSite creates specialized treatments for patients. These treatments use the patient’s own muscle cells in a unique procedure that begins and ends with the patient’s own body. Currently, this technology is being investigated in clinical trials and is not commercially available.
