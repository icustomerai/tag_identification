Jennings, LA

Opening the Jennings, LA, fabrication facility in 2022 is an exciting addition to Greenberry’s Gulf Coast and National capabilities. With 104,000 square feet under hook, barge access, and over 100 acres of laydown space for fabricating heavy industrial modules and structural steel, this facility supports our clients in the petrochemical, oil & gas, semiconductor, manufacturing, renewables, and power industries.
