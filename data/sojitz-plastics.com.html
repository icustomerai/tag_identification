Sojitz Plastics America Inc. (SPA) was founded in 1999 as a center of the North American plastics-related business of Sojitz Corporation, one of Japan’s largest global trading and investment companies.

SPA and our professional team offer many years of experience in both packaging films and engineering specialty resins. In addition, we have invested considerable resources in the areas of new products and business development. Our team is committed to providing you with a total service approach, and your ideas and inquiries are always welcome.
