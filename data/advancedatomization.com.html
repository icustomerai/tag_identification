Fueling the Future

Advanced Atomization Technologies designs, produces, and supports fuel nozzles for some of the most powerful and efficient GE, CFM, and Engine Alliance gas turbine engines in the world. The company is uniquely competitive with design, assembly, test, and aftermarket support capabilities.

The requirements for today’s aircraft engines have never been greater in terms of power, efficiency, emissions, and noise reduction. One of the key contributors to fuel burn and efficiency, as well as the reduction of emissions, is the fuel atomization nozzle. This is the technology provided by Advanced Atomization Technologies. Our advanced nozzle and combustor integration has made us the world’s leading manufacturer of aero engine nozzles.
