EHV Power is the electricity industry’s leading team for designing, installing and maintaining high-voltage underground transmission cable systems. Fleet of high voltage cable installation and removal equipment. Cable fluid degasification tools and equipment. High quality and safe installation of high voltage cable accessories.
Services

With experienced technical resources, EHV Power can provide a full range of design, installation and maintenance services and tailor their delivery to meet with specific customer requirements.

SELF CONTAINED FLUID FILLED (SCFF)/LOW PRESSURE OIL FILLED (LPOF)

Also known as Low Pressure Oil Filled (LPOF) consist of cables, joints and terminations with oil-impregnated paper dielectric insulation.

Learn More
HIGH PRESSURE FLUID FILLED (HPFF)/HIGH PRESSURE PIPE TYPE (HPPT)

Consist of paper insulated cables encased in steel pipes and filled with dielectric oil maintained at high pressure (typically 200-300 psi).

Learn More
CROSS-LINKED POLYETHYLENE SOLID DIELECTRIC
(XLPE)

Solid dielectric insulation technologies were developed during the latter part of the 20th century, as an alternative to paper/oil insulation for cable systems.

Safety

Health, Safety and the Environment is a top value at EHV Power. We maintain the highest standards for safety to mitigate and eliminate incidents and injuries.

Keeping people safe, day in and day out, is the most important thing we do. Beyond all other pursuits, we keep a safe work environment. Because we insist on high safety and technical standards, EHV Power has one of the best safety records in the industry. We’re committed to providing a quality of workmanship second to none, as our track record shows.

In all phases of its endeavours, EHV Power is guided by an established health and safety policy. It is based on a sincere desire to eliminate employee injuries and occupational disease, EHV Power will make every effort to provide a safe, healthy work environment.

Safety shall not be sacrificed for production but rather considered an integral part of quality control, cost reduction, and job efficiency.

EHV Power is honoured to be recognized by ISN as a RAVS Plus Participant. The ISN Review and Verification Services (RAVS) audits an organization’s Health, Safety and Environmental program for compliance. The audit process included interviews and online evaluations to assess our employee’s level of knowledge of health and safety policies.

EHV POWER IS PART OF
THE USi GROUP OF COMPANIES.
The USi Group of Companies provides engineering services and advanced technology products for the electric power delivery industry. The USi Group is unique: in addition to designing, fabricating, installing and maintaining custom engineered products including high voltage cable accessories and pressurizing and cooling plants and associated controls, our staff provides the expertise to integrate with and support customer’s existing monitoring and control systems.
