Design and Manufacture of High Current Conductors

Watteredge, LLC is a leading manufacturer of  High Current Conductors including: Battery and Custom Bus Bar Copper Connectors, Engineered Furnace Secondary Power Connectors, Bus Bar Systems, Laminated Shunts, Water Cooled Power Cables, Resistance Welding Cables, High Current DC Switches, Industrial Rubber Hose and Tubing, and a wide variety of custom engineered products.

For over 66 years, we have served many industries including Automotive, Chemical Processing, Energy and Power Generation with tough, dependable and highly efficient products.

Copper & Aluminum Bus Bars

We offer a complete line of high current secondary AC and DC custom Copper & Aluminum Bus Bars and Grounding Bars for every application.


Copper Bus Tubes

Our custom designed Air and Water Cooled Bus Tubes are the most efficient in the industry for minimizing AC resistance.


Flexible Power Cables

Our capabilities include high performance water cooled and air cooled cables for just about all industrial and resistance welding applications.

Our designs deliver the lowest possible inductive reactance of any flexible power cable available.


Experienced Engineers and Modern Technology Make the Difference
We utilize innovation and experience to bring you products that perform and customer service that's quality driven. Our products are tough, dependable and efficient. From our proprietary power cable designs to our exclusive press weld designs, we are leading the innovation efforts in maximum efficiency and cost effectiveness.

Watteredge's advanced design and testing technologies include CAD and Solid Works for 2D and 3D design, CNC machining for bending, milling, turning, and punching & forming.

Unparalleled Service, Repair and Delivery
Our experienced engineering team can assist with design verification, part rebuilding or welding and welding repair services. We can determine the optimal cost effective solution to maintain your equipment within your budget.
