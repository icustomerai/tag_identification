Engineering the Skies since 2002.

Regulus Group has been partnering with the FAA and the aviation community for over 15 years to solve complex problems with creative, high-caliber, and cost-effective services. We have an outstanding talent base with an unparalleled level of engineering expertise, air traffic operations knowledge, and program management experience. This depth of experience integrated with a broad set of program management, finance, and operations research capabilities has enabled Regulus to become the premier systems engineering and program management services provider for the FAA’s most challenging programs.
