Telemedicine Service

In response to the COVID-19 (coronavirus) pandemic, we are now offering telemedicine services to patients with existing and future appointments. These remote visits allow us to proceed with caution and ensure patients receive timely care while also abiding by the appropriate social distancing protocols set forth by the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC).

Southeast Perinatal Associates

Southeast Perinatal Associates has provided Maternal-Fetal Medicine consultation and co-management of high-risk and complicated pregnancies to the South Florida area since its establishment in 1993. Southeast Perinatal Associates has 3 convenient locations in Broward County. Southeast Perinatal Associates is committed to turning high-risk pregnancies into low-risk deliveries.
