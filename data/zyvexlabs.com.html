    Enabling Quantum Technology

    Zyvex Labs is developing ‘Atomically Precise Manufacturing’ to create society-changing devices—devices in which control of the location of individual atoms at the single-lattice-site level yields unprecedented capabilities. APM makes possible such transformational devices as Quantum Computers, Analog Quantum Simulators and Atomic Electronics. While the promise of nanotechnology through manipulation at the atomic level has been on the horizon for decades, Zyvex Labs is making it a reality.
