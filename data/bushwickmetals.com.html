About Intsel Steel East – Steel Suppliers

As the Northeast’s leading steel service center, Intsel Steel East offers an unsurpassed inventory range, an extensive collection of value added services, and customer service that is second to none. Even with over a century in business, Intsel Steel East isn’t content to rest on its laurels. With an eye toward constant innovation and a focus on customer satisfaction, we consistently roll out new value-added services, improve upon our premier next-day delivery service, and expand our extensive product lines.

All of these efforts support one goal, to help customers grow, control expenses and succeed.
