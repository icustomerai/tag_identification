Software Solutions for Civil Construction

AGTEK provides Dirt Simple Solutions for the construction industry to accurately takeoff and estimate construction quantities, model efficient construction processes and measuring progress throughout the construction life cycle, ensuring you stay on track.

AGTEK’s collaborative tools put data in the hands of those who need it, in a format allowing easy decision making, if you are walking on site, in a truck or an office. Data can be shared via the cloud and visualized on mobile phones or desktop platforms removing the need to carry paper plans. Data can be sent to all major Machine Control systems including Leica, Trimble and Topcon.

Stay on top of the project as it progresses by measuring changing conditions through UAV / Drone flights or traditional surveys. Ensure your fleet is being put to work effectively by tracking machine movements and utilization every step of the way.

AGTEK’s Dirt. Simple. Solutions help accurately bid and get the job done effectively and efficiently.
