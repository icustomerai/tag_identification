Recent Court Ruling Supports Momentive’s Protection of Intellectual Property

NISKAYUNA, N.Y. (April 6, 2023) – Momentive Performance Materials Inc. (Momentive) announced today that a criminal court in China reached a ruling that supports Momentive’s protection of its intellectual property. The ruling determined that four former employees committed trade secret infringement by misappropriating Momentive’s proprietary textile-enhancer and antifoaming technologies and using the information to produce and sell competing products.

Momentive Performance Materials Launches HARMONIE™ – Innovative Derived-Naturals for the Beauty and Personal Care Industry

NISKAYUNA, New York – March 21, 2023 – Momentive Performance Materials, Inc. (Momentive), a global high-performance silicones and specialty solutions company, has launched HARMONIE – a pioneering range of high-performance derived natural and natural ingredients for the beauty and personal care industry.
