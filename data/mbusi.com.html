Welcome to Mercedes-Benz U.S. International, Inc. (MBUSI)

Welcome to Tuscaloosa County Alabama, U.S.A, the assembly location of the Mercedes-Benz GLE SUV, GLS SUV, GLE Coupe, and the Mercedes-Maybach GLS.

Mercedes-Benz U.S. International (MBUSI) started with a clean “sheet of paper” in terms of vehicle concept, in the way it would produce Mercedes-Benz quality in the design of the factory and in the establishment of a young corporate culture. The result is Mercedes-Benz vehicles that are continually setting the standard in the industry.

We assemble superior quality vehicles here in Alabama using Mercedes-Benz and MBUSI’s original production system which is a combination of American, Japanese, and German automotive best practices. This process contributes to the quality of our vehicles in a safe, efficient, and predictable way.

With the arrival of our Mercedes-Benz vehicles – the dream has become a reality. Make your dreams come true! For more information about purchasing a Mercedes-Benz vehicle, please visit Mercedes-Benz U.S.A., and Mercedes-Benz.
