The Obvious Choice for Charleston Amenities

Welcome

Snee Farm Country Club is located in the heart of Mt. Pleasant, SC on the beautiful, 200 year-old Snee Farm Plantation. The private club is home to a George Cobb designed championship golf course, a newly renovated clubhouse, a full service restaurant and bar, a fitness room, 18 tennis courts and two newly renovated swimming pools. Members are also privy to a variety of social events including dances, cookouts, holiday parties, trivia nights, live music, kids club and more.

Experience Membership Reciprocity Around the World

Snee Farm Country Club offers traditional memberships, but Dual Club Memberships are also available with RiverTowne Country Club. Both RiverTowne and Snee Farm are affiliated with the ClubCorp network, which grants members access to over 200 private golf and country clubs worldwide.

Dual and Triple Club Memberships Available

We are proud to offer dual and triple club golf memberships with our sister courses, RiverTowne Country Club and Dunes West Golf Club. RiverTowne features the only Arnold Palmer Signature golf course in the Charleston area, a dual-directional practice facility with chipping and putting greens. Dunes West is a Arthur Hills designed course, a charming clubhouse overlooking Wagner Creek and some of the area’s most spectacular scenery into a truly world-class and one of a kind experience.
