We are an aviation management and consulting business offering industry leading and individualized turn-key flight department operations for clients in the Puget Sound. Focused on safety, we remain committed to clients through a personalized approach. Whether we are searching for your perfect airplane or overseeing a scheduled maintenance visit, our attention to detail is never compromised.  Our mission is to provide professional and efficient aviation management services while always conducting operations to the highest of safety standards.

Vector Management is a proud member of the National Business Aviation Association (NBAA), Pacific Northwest Business Aviation Association (PNBAA) and Flight Service Bureau OPSGROUP.
