Leadership

Ray Landry – President
Ray brings over 15 years of management and leadership experience within the industrial and commercial instrumentation and electrical industry. Ray started his career in 1982 as an Electrical Apprentice, then served as Project Manager at sister EMCOR company Ardent Services. He has held several operational leadership roles including Vice President of Loenbro I&E and Vice President of Capital Projects for Ardent Services, before joining our team.
