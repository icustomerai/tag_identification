LIGHTWEIGHT

Kids can easily pick the e-bike up, and manage it on their own regardless of its position.

LOW SEAT HEIGHT

Inspires riding confidence by allowing the e-bike rider's feet to be firmly planted on the ground.

A lower center of gravity balance bike offers greater stability.
