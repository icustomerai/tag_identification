Effective January 1, 2021, Physician Alliance of the Rockies officially became the Optum Care Network of Colorado (OCNC). OCNC is an independent practice association (IPA) that supports contracted primary care physicians across all of Colorado in their efforts to manage the care of select United Healthcare Medicare Advantage members. We provide administrative, quality, coding, and medical management support to these providers so they can focus on delivering high-value care to these patients.

Our website has changed. Please visit us at https://professionals.optumcare.com/ to learn more about partnering with OCNC.

If you have general questions regarding OCNC, please email us at OptumCareNetworkofColorado@Optum.com.

If you are an existing OCNC provider and are trying to submit a referral or prior authorization, please do so via the Mountain West Provider Center Portal at https://providers.optumcaremw.com/. You may also call our referral/prior authorization department at 303-716-8040 or fax in your request to 844-206-5736.

Additional resources for existing OCNC providers can be found on the Mountain West Provider Center Portal or at https://professionals.optumcare.com/resources-clinicians/colorado-clinician-resources.
