Investing in the future of Legal Finance

As a founding member of ILFA, The International Legal Finance Association, the UK’s Association of Litigation Funders, ALF and Litigation Funding Working Group, Therium is committed to advancing the development of the legal finance industry across the world. Therium also invests in AI and big data firms that are developing tools to further advance the legal finance industry’s use of technology to value claims.
