Welcome to
Elite Spray Foam of Las Vegas

We are the leading spray foam insulation company serving the Las Vegas Metro Area.

Spray foam is the best insulation available in the industry for sustainability, energy efficiency and long-term product quality. In addition to keeping your home or building warm in the winter and cool in the summer, spray foam also protects against allergens, insects, mold, pollen and unwanted noise.

Our mission is to give the customer the fact about what needs to be done, offer the best solution for their unique needs and complete the job with the utmost professionalism. Our expert technicians offer elite service and premier quality for every spray foam insulation project.

Our spray foam insulation products include hy-fi hybrid systems, fiberglass batts and open and closed cell spray foam. We can also tackle waterproofing, wine rooms and containers, trailers or box trucks with our closed cell foam.
