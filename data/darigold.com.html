Leading Dairy Forward

We are proud to have served our communities high-quality dairy products for over 100 years. Our values of leadership, stewardship, and perseverance continue to guide us as we create value for our customers and farmers. Looking toward the next 100 years, we want to create a world where people, animals and the planet can prosper. We’re committed to doing our part through the communities we serve, the work environment we create and by practicing stewardship within the realm of responsibilities we are fortunate to have.

Earth Day 2022

We partnered with the U.S. Dairy Export Council to demonstrate how we are delivery high-quality food to over 30 countries — all while continuing to strive for our goal of reaching greenhouse gas neutrality by 2050.

Darigold World Cow

Darigold commissioned Vermont-based artist DJ Barry to paint the world’s largest World Cow mural at our Sunnyside plant—to honor our Northwest Dairy Association farmer-owners, their employees, and our Darigold team members, and to bring joy to all those who see and experience it.
