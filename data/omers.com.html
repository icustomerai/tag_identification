With the OMERS defined benefit pension plan, you can confidently retire knowing that you will have income for life.

We are the stewards of our members’ financial future, and we approach our work with the passion and care that comes with this humbling responsibility. Our world-class team carefully invests your income in high-quality assets, diversified around the world, to meet the pension promise of a secure retirement.

The OMERS Story
