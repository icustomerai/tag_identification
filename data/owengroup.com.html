DESIGN

Owen is an award winning multi-disciplinary design firm providing design services for buildings, parking structures and infrastructure for all types of project delivery methods. Owen provides comprehensive services in a unique, single-source setting. Featured Project: UC Irvine Health Gavin Herbert Eye Institute, Irvine, CA

ENERGY & MEP

Our energy system design methodology is supported by a foundation of energy efficient, sustainable, and workable solutions. We provide a comprehensive array of energy efficient services including Title 24 for the new energy code, solar and wind design, central utilities plant, commissioning and energy management systems. Featured Project: World Trade Center Transportation Hub, New York, NY

PROJECT MANAGEMENT
