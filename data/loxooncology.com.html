Loxo@Lilly Development Approach

Identify

the right targets

Direct resources to high confidence areas of cancer biology

Choose targets with the greatest unmet need based on insights from:

  • Clinicians
  • Scientific Advisors
  • Academic Researchers
  • Patients

Build

the right medicines

Rationally design
compounds that:

  • Selectively inhibit the target
  • Deeply inhibit the target
  • Exhibit favorable medicinal properties

Employ varied discovery strategies:

  • In-house R&D
  • In-licensing & Acquisitions
  • Discovery Collaborations

Study

the right patients

Explore medicines in
well-defined patient populations

  • Identify early response signals
  • Accelerate development through novel clinical and regulatory strategies
  • Engage key stakeholders throughout the process
