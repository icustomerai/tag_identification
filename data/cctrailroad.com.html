Port of Stockton 

Located in Stockton, California. CCT Railroad provides switching services for over 40 port based customers. Switching services available Mon-Fri.

Central Valley Branch - Lodi 

CCT Railroad's Central Valley Branch provides switching services for 6 Stockton and Lodi California based customers.  Switching services available Mon-Fri. 

Interchanges 

CCT Railroad interchanges with both the UPRR and BNSF at the Port of Stockton and Central Valley Branch.
