Heat Pump Services

Heat pumps are an innovative option for both heating and cooling your home. Air source heat pumps draw heat from the air and either bring it into the home or send it out, depending on the setting.

Were equipped to handle maintenance and repairs for your air source heat pump. Whether you use a heat pump as your primary source of heating and cooling, or this is a supplementary installation, well help you optimize your operation so youre enjoying affordable comfort throughout your home.

Reliable Heating Solutions

Maryland, Virginia, and Mid-Atlantic harsh winters make an efficient heating system a must. Griffith services all types of heating installations, including:

  • Air source heat pumps
  • Boilers
  • Gas furnaces
  • Oil furnaces

When you call our experienced technicians, youll have a wealth of experience and knowledge on your side. Were committed to providing fast heat pump, boiler repair or boiler replacement, as well asfurnace repair or furnace replacement so youre never left in the cold.

Remember to schedule maintenance for these systems once a year to keep them in prime condition. Regular maintenance means fewer repairs, lower operating costs, and a longer life span for your heater. When its time for an upgrade, well help you evaluate your options.
