A Global Firm Built Over Generations on a Foundation of Client Service

In a world of accelerating change, innovative thinking drives success. That’s why for 175 years, clients around the world have relied on Lazard for trusted, independent advice and solutions to their most important strategic and financial matters.

A Business Founded on a Simple and Powerful Model

 

Lazard is one of the most respected names in global financial services, standing for excellence, integrity and innovation on behalf of clients.

Our mission is to provide the best advice and investment solutions to our clients through two businesses that are global in scale: Financial Advisory and Asset Management.
