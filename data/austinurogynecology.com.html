Austin Urogynecology: Sharp Focus and Supportive Staff

At Austin Urogynecology, we want you to be comfortable with your health, your body, and with our expertise. Our practice is highly specialized in female pelvic medicine and reconstructive surgery.

What We Do

A Urogynecologist is expertly trained in female pelvic reconstructive surgery and the treatment of pelvic floor disorders. This expertise lends to greater experience and knowledge of the conditions we diagnose and treat. These are the common conditions and procedures we offer at our practice:

  • Rectocele

Our Clinicians

Our staff might seem impressive on paper, but it’s even better to get to know in person. Here’s who you’ll meet when you visit us:

George Shashoua, MD

Founder of Austin Urogynecology, Dr. Shashoua is board certified in female pelvic medicine and reconstructive surgery. He specializes in female pelvic floor disorders and advanced surgical procedures, such as pelvic organ prolapse and da Vinci sacrocolpopexy.

Read more

Julianna Monceaux, FNP-BC

Julianna is a Board Certified Nurse Practitioner with over a decade of experience in the field. She works with Dr. Shashoua to treat urogynecological conditions and assist with robotic surgeries.

Read more

Kristin Longshore, ANP-BC

Kristin is an adult health, board certified nurse practitioner. After seven years as a travel nurse then as a vascular nurse practitioner, she joined our practice in 2016.

Our Facilities

Our offices may house the most advanced technology and high grade equipment, but they are designed to make you feel at home. Our comfortable, clean, and modern waiting room is the first thing to greet our patients. The healing experience should begin as soon as you walk through the door.
