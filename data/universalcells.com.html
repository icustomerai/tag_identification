Making Stem Cells Work for Everyone

We are a Seattle-based company that creates engineered, allogeneic stem cells that can be differentiated into many types of therapeutic cells.

Editing Stem Cells To Cure Disease

Universal Cells edits genes required for immune recognition to create universal donor, pluripotent stem cells. We use, nuclease-free, recombinant Adeno-Associated Virus (rAAV) gene editing technology to produce off-the-shelf, stem cell-based products.
