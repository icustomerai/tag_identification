The courage to disrupt our business even when things are going well.

For over 50 years InVue has been one of the few companies that has consistently spotted emerging market trends and then reinvented itself to take advantage of the next major market opportunity.

1972

Richard Sankey forms Alpha Enterprises as a product design company.

1984

Alpha transforms itself into a product marketing company selling audio cassette security packaging to record chains and video cases to video rental stores throughout the United States.

1992

Alpha introduces a complete range of packaging and security products for compact discs.

1993

Alpha Consumer Products develops a complete line of home storage products for pre-recorded media and home office accessories.

2002

Alpha Security Products introduces the S3 key, a storewide single key concept operating a broad line of retail security products. The S3 key eliminated the retailer’s problem of managing multiple keys to service customers.

2006

Alpha Security Products launches the innovative POD [Protection on Demand] providing retailers the first easy-to-use, above counter, alarming security device for handheld electronics. The POD concept featured the new IR Key with enhanced security functions.

2007

The belief that the IR Key provided retailers a superior value over magnetic keys, and anticipating the growth of handheld electronics, Alpha sells the S3 product line to Checkpoint Systems and forms InVue.

2012

The IR2 Key and OneKEY ecosystem™ are introduced providing retailers a storewide single key security platform designed to decrease theft and increase sales.

2012

InVue launches Smart Lock, an electronic lock and key system for easy access to merchandise locked in cabinets and display cases.

2015

InVue launches a full line of business tablet solutions to secure and enable tablets used for business.

2017

InVue releases a full software suite developed to connect and enhance our security platform and provide retailers critical data and analytics.

2020

InVue Point of Sale launches the NE360 mobile payment system. NE360 provides the complete freedom to combine any tablet or mobile device, any payment terminal, and any operating system to create a flexible mPOS solution.

2021

InVue moves into Critical Infrastructure security with multiple solutions providing security, access control and real-time visibility.

2022

InVue CE Brands division is renamed Strategic Partnerships positioning us to provide merchandising, access control, IoT, and security solutions to a broader audience of brands and enterprise customers selling thru retailers.
