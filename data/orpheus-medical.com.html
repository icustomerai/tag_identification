Advancing what's possible in clinical media management

Our vendor neutral clinical media management platform allows for easy capture, storage, streaming and sharing of clinical video and images, providing an efficient workflow, easy collaboration and enhanced interoperability.

Request a demo

Enhance physician capabilities with a clear image to streamlined care

The digitally integrated operating suites, procedure labs and ambulatory surgical settings require a standardized way to capture, stream and collaborate with clinical media across all perioperative settings.

To gain a clear path to streamlined patient care, you need:

  • Versatility to capture, store and stream video, audio and images from any source.
  • Real-time collaboration and engagement of teams.
  • Immediate access to results across the care continuum and completing the patient record.
  • A video-based clinical quality management system for case reviews and training.

Enhance collaboration with colleagues, patients and referring physicians

Orpheus enables broader collaboration between clinicians by automating the distribution of videos and images across multiple specialties to drive workflow efficiencies.

Patient and referring physician interactions can be enriched by empowering physicians to share videos, images and annotations on any device.

Capture

Versatility to capture, store and stream video, audio and images from any source.

Archive

Instant storage and sharing of images with PACS and EHR.

Stream

Ability to collaborate over video in real-time for consultation and case observations.

Treasure

Captured videos are accessed from any device to enhance collaboration, teaching and clinical performance.

Security, Plug & Play, Standard

Orpheus is a scalable enterprise solution fully integrated with the electronic medical record and promotes interoperability. Orpheus applications help providers and covered entities to comply with privacy requirements. Risk is mitigated by using secure and auditable methods to access clinical media.
