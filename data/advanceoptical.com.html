Welcome to Advance Optical

Serving our valued customers since 1922!

2022 marks a very special milestone at Advance –our 100th anniversary! Advance was founded back in 1922 as a wholesale optical frame supplier and has grown to become the full-service ophthalmic laboratory that you know today. We remain committed to our founding principles of delivering quality products with precision workmanship and providing friendly and knowledgeable customer service. We are pleased to launch our new logo, representing 100 years (and counting!) of providing visual solutions to the eyecare community. We take your decision to entrust us with your patients’ Rx orders very seriously and we continue to work towards bettering our processes and flow to ensure that your patients’ orders are delivered to you accurately and timely, all with the support of helpful customer service and communications.
 

People matter to us. When you call Advance Optical, an actual person answers the phone because that’s just a part of who we are here at Advance. We value that your patients trust you with their visual needs and we take ownership in our part of maintaining that trust. 

We hope that you find this website to be a valuable resource. Be sure to Register for access to pricing and promotions. If there are any additional features you would like to see, please email us at advance@advanceoptical.com.
