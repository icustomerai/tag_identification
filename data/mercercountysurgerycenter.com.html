Mercer County Surgery Center

is a state-of-the-art freestanding, outpatient ambulatory surgical center offering a broad range of surgical procedures. Our highly skilled physicians specialize in a variety of field including breast surgery, gastroenterology, neurosurgery, orthopaedics, pain management and podiatry. We believe in compassionate medicine in which we provide each of our patients individualized focus and care.

AAAHC Accreditation

Our surgical center is certified by the Accreditation Association for Ambulatory Health Care (AAAHC) - the leader in developing patient care guidelines. This accreditation was achieved by our physicians and staff members providing the highest quality of care to the community based on nationally recognized standards of care as determined by an independent, external process of evaluation.

Best Ambulatory Surgery Center

MCSC is named the number one Ambulatory Surgery Center in New Jersey and the fifteenth best Ambulatory Surgical Center in the United States by Newsweek Magazine!

This prestigious ranking is based on a reputation score which includes the following: Recommendations, quality of care, COVID-19 response and the KPI data score which is all Inclusive of the overall quality measures set forth by the surgery center in relation to other Surgical centers in the country.

Safety Precautions Matter

Here at Mercer County Surgery Center, we are continuing to monitor the ongoing COVID-19 health crisis impacting the United States. As we continue to follow guidance of the CDC and NJ Department of Health, we want everyone to understand your health and wellness is our primary focus. We, as an organization, are moving forward to proactively responding to the COVID-19 crisis. 

Our center has always adhered to both state and federal infection control requirements, but they become even more important during a world wide pandemic. Because your safety is always our main concern, we have implemented additional infection prevention measures to provide you, as well as our physicians and staff, with a safe clinical environment for your procedure.
